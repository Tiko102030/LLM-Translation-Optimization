However, such a general сопоставление of the concepts of property rights can be misunderstood because these rights largely limit the rights of other people. Property rights give individuals state-approved privileges to monopolistically manage resources, the ability to contact authorized police agencies in order to limit the activities of other people. Thus, ownership significantly restricts some advantages because government authorities largely support property owners rather than other citizens. Therefore, not a debate about the principles of natural freedom and justice, but discussions of property regulation make it necessary to choose whose property needs to be protected and whose property rights have advantage in the struggle for them. Discussing the issue of democratic rights and their regulation has become so unclear because within the structure of the legal system of the United States, legal issues have been repeatedly discussed at various levels of government. Thus, according to the federalism system, one Federal Government is formed, and each state establishes its own legal system. Also at each level of governance there is a clear division of executive, legislative and judicial powers, each of which is intended to respect and enforce the norms of the Constitution, in which they find their interpretation.
