If actions go "too far", then compensation for damage will be necessary in this case; however, it will be up to the judges, taking into account the existence of public needs and special circumstances, to determine what "too far" means. Neither private property owners nor the public have absolute rights; in each case, judges must carefully evaluate and balance the competing interests of both parties.
