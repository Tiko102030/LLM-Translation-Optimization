Data Collection for Training Selecting data for training the network and their processing is the most challenging stage of solving the problem. The training dataset must meet several criteria: The original data is transformed into a form that can be input into the network's inputs. Each record in the data file is called a training pair or a training vector. The training vector contains one value per network input, and depending on the type of learning (supervised or unsupervised), one value per network output. Training the network on a raw dataset typically does not yield high-quality results. There are several ways to improve the network's 'perception'.
