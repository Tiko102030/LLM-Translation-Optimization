The market of free trade will lead to the same efficient result regardless of property rights distribution and who holds rights to air and water. The Coase theorem has had a significant impact on economic theory, resulting in many economists abandoning the idea of income distribution and focusing on the values of efficiency various legal regimes bring about; however, there are numerous questions associated with these statements. Separate consideration of price and income distribution takes place only when income distribution will affect both prices and the structure of production. This occurs when economic subjects have varying preferences and scale returns either increase or decrease. Additionally, as Coase himself noted, his theory fails to take into account transaction costs or expenses related to negotiating and enforcing contracts. This can be very important in entering a trade agreement that involves external effects particularly, it is often problematic to incentivize a large number of people to bribe the one polluting the environment. Definition of property rights is frequently viewed as controversial in liberal democracies much like Americans have both personal freedom and the right of the majority to govern the country (see Horowitz 1992; Demsetz 1964; Dillon, 1895).
