The market of free trade will lead to the same efficient result regardless of property rights distribution and regardless of who owns the rights to air and water. Coase's article had a huge impact on economic theory, causing many economists to abandon the idea of income distribution and focus on the values of different legal regimes' efficiency. However, there are several questions related to these statements. Separate consideration of price and income distribution occurs only when income distribution affects both the level of prices and production structure. This will happen in cases where economic subjects have various preferences and when returns to scale increase or decrease. Furthermore, as Coase himself noted, his theory does not take into account transaction costs or expenses related to negotiating and enforcing contracts.
