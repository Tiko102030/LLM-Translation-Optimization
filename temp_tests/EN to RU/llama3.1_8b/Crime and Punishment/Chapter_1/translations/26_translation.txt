"It must be the top drawer," he thought to himself. "So she carries the keys in a pocket on the right. All in one bunch on a steel ring.... And there’s one key there, three times as big as all the others, with deep notches; that can’t be the key of the chest of drawers... then there must be some other chest or strong-box... that’s worth knowing. Strong-boxes always have keys like that... but how humiliating it is."

The old woman returned. "Here, sir: just as we say ten kopecks per rouble a month, so I need to take fifteen kopecks from one and a half roubles for the month in advance.
