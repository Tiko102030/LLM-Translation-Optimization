But how won't the portfolios sink and float? Yes, of course that way. Everyone will see it. And in any case, people already look at each other like this when they meet, they glance over, as if only one thing matters to them. "Why so, or maybe I just think so," he thought. And he was suddenly amazed: how did he spend whole half hours wandering in despair and anxiety, and in dangerous places, but this he couldn't come up with before! And that's why he wasted only whole half hours on such a reckless thing that it had already been decided to do in a dream, while still delirious.
