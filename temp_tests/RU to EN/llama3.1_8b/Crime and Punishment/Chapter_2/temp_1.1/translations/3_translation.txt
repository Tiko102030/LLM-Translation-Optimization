What's this? He quickly rushed to pull them out and throw them onto the table. Having taken everything, even rummaging through pockets to make sure nothing was left behind, he moved all of that pile over to the corner. There, in the very corner, at the bottom, in one place the wallpaper hanging away from the wall had been torn: immediately he began shoving everything into this hole under the paper: "in it's gone! All out of sight and my wallet too!" - he thought with joy, standing up straight and stupidly looking into the corner, into the hole that was protruding even more. Suddenly he jerked in fright at an awful thought: "My God," - he whispered despairingly, "what's happening to me? Is it really hidden? Do they hide things like this?"
