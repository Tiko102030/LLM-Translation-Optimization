Nothing and no one; nobody looked in. Not even Nastasya touched it. But, good Lord! How could he have left all those things here yesterday? He dived into the corner, reached under the wallpaper with his hand and started pulling out things and loading them into pockets. There were eight items altogether: two small boxes with earrings or something of that kind - he didn't take a close look; then four small suede cases. One chain was just wrapped in newspaper. There was also something else wrapped in newspaper, seems like an order...

He put everything into different pockets, in the coat and into the remaining right pocket of the pants, trying to make it look as unobtrusive as possible. The wallet was taken along with things too. Then he left the room this time even leaving it wide open.
