The call was so strong that he had already stood up to answer it. "Shouldn't I think about it for a minute?" there flashed in his head. "No, better not to think at all and let go of my shoulders!" But suddenly he stopped dead in his tracks: Nikodim Fomich spoke with passion to Ilia Petrovich, and his words reached him:

— It cannot be that both will be released! Firstly, everything contradicts it; judge for yourselves: why call the janitor if this was their business? To report on themselves, like? Or out of cunning? No, it would have been too cunning already! And finally, student Pestyakov was seen by both janitors and the townspeople near the gates at that exact moment when he entered; he went with three friends and parted from them just near the gate and asked about living arrangements from the janitors while his friends were still there. So, would they be asking about living arrangements if they had such an intention?
