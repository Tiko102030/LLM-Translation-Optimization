He suddenly thought to himself, "I'll put on my socks! It will rub off even more dust and the tracks will disappear." But as soon as he did, he immediately ripped them off with disgust and horror. He ripped them off but then realized there was no other choice so he took and put them back on again – and burst out laughing once more. "Everything is conditional, everything is relative, everything is just forms," he thought briefly, a mere glimmer of thought, while himself shaking all over his body – after all he did put it on! After all, he ended up putting it on!" The laughter was immediately replaced by despair. "No, not possible...” he thought to himself. His legs were trembling. "From fear," he muttered to himself. His head spun and hurt from the heat.
