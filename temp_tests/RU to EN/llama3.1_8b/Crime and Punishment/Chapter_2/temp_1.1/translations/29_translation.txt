—"And you, such-and-such and that, he suddenly shouted to the top of his lungs (the sorrowful lady had already stepped out), what happened to you last night? Ah? Again shame, producing a riot on the entire street. Again fights and intoxication. You dream of a straitjacket! Because I already told you, because I already warned you ten times that in eleven I won't let you go! And again, and again, such-and-such is you like that! The paper had even slipped from Razumikhin's hands, and he looked wildly at the lavish lady being so cavalierly handled; but soon, however, he realized what was going on, and right away this whole story began to please him very much. He listened with pleasure, so much so that he wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh... All his nerves were jumping like that." —Il'ya Petrovich!
