It became very unpleasant for him to contemplate this even frightfully. "Nastasya, why are you silent?" he said finally in a weak voice. "This is blood," she replied at last quietly and as if to herself speaking. "Blood! What blood?..." He mumbled, blanching and moving back towards the wall. Nastasya continued to silently watch him. "No one has beaten the mistress," she said again in a strict and resolute voice.
