He muttered to himself, "Rags of torn hem won't arouse suspicion; it seems like that, yes, like that!" and with an intense agony-filled attention he began to search around again, on the floor and everywhere else, if he had forgotten something? The certainty that everything - even memory, even simple consideration - was leaving him started unbearably tormenting him. "Is it really starting already, is this really judgment coming? Look, look, it's really so!" Indeed, there were shreds of hem which he cut off from his pants just lying on the floor, in the middle of the room, for anyone to see! "What's going on with me?" - he screamed again like a lost soul. It then came to him an absurd thought: maybe all his clothes are bloody, and there may be many stains, but he doesn't see them because his consideration is weakening, torn apart... mind clouded... Suddenly he remembered that the wallet also had blood on it. "Ha! So, I guess there must also be blood in the pocket too, because I stuffed the still wet wallet into my pocket then!" He immediately rummaged through the pocket and - yes, it's really so - there are stains, spots on the lining of the pocket! "So therefore I haven't completely lost my reason yet, so there is some consideration and memory left, since I managed to remember and figure it out myself! " - he thought triumphantly, breathing in deeply with his whole chest and feeling joyful and relieved. "It's just a feeble illness, delirium for one minute," - and he yanked the entire lining out of the left pocket of his pants.
