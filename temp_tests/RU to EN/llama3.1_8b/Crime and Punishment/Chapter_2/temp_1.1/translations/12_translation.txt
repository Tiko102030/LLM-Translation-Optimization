Later, thinking about it, he remembered that when he was half-asleep in the heat, he gripped everything tightly in his hand and fell asleep again. — And look at the ragtag mess he's collected and sleeps with it, precisely like a treasure... Nastasya burst out laughing her unhealthy-nervous laugh. He quickly stuffed it all under his coat and stared intently at it through his eyes.
