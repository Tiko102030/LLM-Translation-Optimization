It's already been three hours!" He sat on the sofa - and suddenly it all came back to him! All at once it all came back to him! In that first moment he thought he was going mad. A terrible chill gripped him; but there was also the fever, which had started in his sleep long before. Now a sudden shiver struck him, making his teeth rattle and everything inside him tremble. He opened the door and began listening: the house was absolutely quiet. With amazement he looked around himself and all over the room, not understanding how it could have happened that he had come in yesterday without locking the doors on the hook and flung himself onto the sofa, not only undressed but even still wearing his hat - which had slipped off and was lying right next to the pillow. "If someone came in, what would they think? That I'm drunk, but..." He rushed over to the window. The light was enough, and he quickly started looking at himself all over, from head to foot, checking if there were any signs of it.
