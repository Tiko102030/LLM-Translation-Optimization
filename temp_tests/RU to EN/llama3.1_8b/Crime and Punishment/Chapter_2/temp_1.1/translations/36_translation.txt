Oh, please forgive me, captain, he began very freely, suddenly addressing Nikodim Fomich - take an interest in my situation... I am even prepared to ask them for apologies if I had any shortcomings on my part. I'm a poor and sick student, depressed (he said exactly that: "depressed") by poverty. I used to be a student, because now I cannot support myself, but I will receive money. My mother and sister live in the — gubnernia. They'll send me some, and I... pay back. My landlady is a kind woman, but she got so angry that I lost my lessons and have not paid for four months, that she doesn't even send me food... And I don't understand at all what this promissory note is! Now she's demanding from me by this loanable letter, what should I pay her? Decide yourselves!

- But this isn't our business...

- Allow me, allow me, I completely agree with you, but let me explain my side - took up Rascolnikov again addressing not the writer, but all to Nikodim Fomich, but trying in every way also address it and to Ilia Petrovich, though that stubbornly made to look through documents and disdainfully didn't pay attention to him - allow me and from my side explain that I live with her already for about three years, since the arrival from province and before...before... by the way, why should I not admit in turn, from very beginning I gave a promise, that I would marry her daughter, a verbal promise absolutely free... It was a girl... by the way, she even liked me... though I wasn't in love... one word, youth, that is to say, my landlady made then many credits for me and I lived partly such a life... I was very thoughtless...

- From you they are not demanding such intimacy, charitable lord, and there's no time - roughly interrupted Ilia Petrovich with triumph but Rascolnikov stopped him warmly though it became very hard to talk to him. - But allow me, please, let me tell it a bit... how the matter was and in its turn... although it is superfluous to tell - agreed by you, - but last year this girl died from typhus, I remained a tenant as was, and my landlady moved to current apartment said... said kindly that she completely trusts me and all... but that wouldn't like for me to give her this promissory note in one hundred fifteen rubles, the whole that she considers by me debt. Please: she exactly said, that if I would give that document, she again will credit me whatever you want and that she never - her own words were - will use this paper until I myself pay back... And now, when I have lost my lessons and I don't have anything to eat, she puts forward for claimment... What should I say?

- All these sensitive details, charitable lord, do not concern us at all - cynically interrupted Ilia Petrovich, - you must give an opinion and a commitment, and what you were in love with and all those tragic places, it has nothing to do with our business.
