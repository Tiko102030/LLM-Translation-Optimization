No one noticed him. In the second room sat and wrote some clerks, dressed a bit better than him, looking like an odd kind of people. He approached one of them. "What's it to you?" he asked. The clerk showed him a summons from the office. "You're a student?" he asked, glancing at the summons. "Yes, former student," he replied. The clerk eyed him over, but without any curiosity. This was some especially disheveled person with an unmovable idea in his gaze.
