He looked at her, barely breathing. "I heard myself... I didn't sleep... I was sitting," he stammered again. "I listened for a long time... The assistant guard would come by... Everyone gathered on the stairs from all the apartments..." 

"No one came. But there's blood crying out of you. This happens when there is no way out and your insides will start to roast, and it starts to appear... Will you exist, then?" He didn't answer. Nastasya was standing over him, staring at him intently and not leaving.

"Give me a drink... Nastasyushka." She went down and came back two minutes later with water in a white clay cup, but he already didn't remember what happened next. He remembered only how he took one gulp of cold water and spilled some from the cup on his chest.
