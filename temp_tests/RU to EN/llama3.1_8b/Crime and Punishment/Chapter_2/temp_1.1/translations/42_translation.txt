The house is Noah's Ark – noticed the clerk, listening from his place. – It's clear, it's clear! – fervently repeated Nikodim Fomich. – No, it's very unclear – supported Ilya Petrovich. Raskolnikov raised his hat and walked towards the doors, but he didn't make it to the doors... When he came to, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that someone was supporting him from the right, another man stood beside him on the left with a yellow cup filled with yellow water, and Nikodim Fomich stood in front of him and looked at him intently; he got up from the chair. – What is this, are you ill? – asked Nikodim Fomich quite sharply. – They signed as they barely moved the pen – noted the clerk, taking his seat again and starting to work on the papers. – And how long have you been sick? – yelled Ilya Petrovich from his place and also rummaging through papers.
