If asked, he might say, he thought, approaching the office. The office was a quarter of a verst away from him. It had just moved to a new apartment, in a new house, on the fourth floor. He had been there briefly on his old apartment, but very long ago. Entering through the gates, he saw a staircase to the right, which an older man with a book in his hands was coming down: "it's a janitor, so this must be the office", and he started going up to the fourth floor randomly. He didn't want to ask anyone anything. "I'll go in, kneel down, and tell everything...". He thought, entering on the fourth floor. The staircase was narrow, steep, and covered with trash. All kitchens of all apartments on all four floors opened onto this staircase and stood there almost all day.
