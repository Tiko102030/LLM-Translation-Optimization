Yes, Mr. Captain, and what a noble guest is this, Mr. Captain, in the noble house...

—Hush, hush! Enough! I told you before, I said it already, I've been telling you...

—Ilya Petrovich! — The scribe spoke again with emphasis. The lieutenant quickly glanced at him; the scribe slightly nodded his head. ...So this is for you, esteemed Lavis Ivanovna, my final word, and this will be the last time, — the lieutenant continued. — If in your noble house there's even just one more scandal, I'll get rid of you as a pawn, as it's said in high language. Did you hear? So a writer, an author, five kopecks for a "fald" in the "noble house"? Here they are, writers!
