It's better to get rid of it! — he repeated, getting up on the sofa again — and now, right away, don't delay!.. But instead of that, his head leaned against the pillow once more; again an unbearable shiver ran down him; again he pulled himself together in the greatcoat. And for a long time, several hours, it still kept flitting into his mind in bursts that "it would be good now, without delay, to go somewhere and get rid of everything, so as to finally banish it from sight, quickly, quickly!" He was seized with an urge to get up from the sofa several times, he wanted to stand up but couldn't. A strong knock on the door finally woke him up. — Open up, are you alive? And what's all this sleeping around! — Nastasya shouted, knocking her fist on the door, — whole daytime days like a dog, sleeping all day long!
