It seemed to him as if he had cut himself off from everyone and everything with scissors in this very moment. He came back home already by evening, which means he had been gone for six hours altogether. Where he was and how he got back, he couldn't remember anything about it. After undressing and trembling all over like a worn-out horse, he lay down on the sofa, put on his greatcoat, and immediately fell into a stupor...

He woke up in full darkness from an awful scream. Good Lord, what a scream! Such unnatural sounds, such yelling, shouting, scratching, sobbing, cursing, and threats - he had never heard or seen anything like it before. He couldn't even imagine something like that for himself. In horror, he sat up on his bed, frozen in terror with every moment, writhing. But the fights, screams, and curses were getting stronger and stronger. And then, to his greatest amazement, he suddenly heard the voice of his landlady.
