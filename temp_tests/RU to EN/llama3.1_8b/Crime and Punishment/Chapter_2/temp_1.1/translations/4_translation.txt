— Is that how they hide it? Truly, my mind has left me! In despair, he sat down on the divan, and the unbearable shiver shook him instantly again. Mechanically, he dragged the student's winter coat, which was lying next to him on the stool, warm but already almost in tatters, covered himself with it, and sleep and delirium enveloped him once more. He dozed off. No more than five minutes later, he leapt up again and, in a state of frenzy, rushed to his shirt once more. "How could I fall asleep again when nothing has been done? Just so, just so: the loop under the armpit is still not removed! I forgot, forgot about such things! Such evidence!" He pulled off the loop and quickly began to tear it into pieces, shoving them under the pillow in his clothing. "Pieces of torn fabric won't arouse any suspicions; it seems that way, it seems that way!" — he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with intense pain, he began to search around again, on the floor and everywhere else, if he hadn't forgotten something yet?
