Nastasya, it turned out, wouldn't be able to notice anything from afar, thank God!" Then with trembling he unfolded the summons and began reading; long he read and finally understood. It was a common summons from the district to appear today at half past ten in the district inspector's office. "But when could this possibly happen? I have no dealings whatsoever with the police! And why exactly today?" he thought, plagued by bewilderment. "Lord, hurry up, would you!" He had thrown himself on his knees to pray, but even he laughed at himself - not at the prayer itself, but at his own foolishness. He hurriedly began getting dressed. "I'll fail so miserably, anyway! Put on my sock!"
