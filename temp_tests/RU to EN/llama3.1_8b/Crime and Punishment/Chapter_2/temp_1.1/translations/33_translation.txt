There they are, writers! - and he threw a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov - Third day in the tavern there was an incident: ate, but refused to pay; "I'll write about it," he said, "in satire for that". On the steamer too, last week, he insulted with foul language a respectable family of a civil servant, his wife and daughter. The other day they threw out of a confectionery one person into the crowd. Behold them - writers, literati, students, heralds... shoo! Go away! I'll go visit you myself... then watch yourself! Did you hear? Louisa Ivanovna started bobbing up and down in all directions, bobbing as she went towards the doors, but bumped her bottom on a prominent officer standing in the doorway, with an open fresh face and magnificent dense blond whiskers.
