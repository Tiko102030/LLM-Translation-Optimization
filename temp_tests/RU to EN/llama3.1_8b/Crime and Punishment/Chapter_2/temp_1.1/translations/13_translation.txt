Though he could hardly have fully comprehended at that moment, he felt that one wouldn't behave with a person like that when they came to take him away. "But... the police?"

"Want a cup of tea? If you'd like, I'll bring it; there's some left..."

"No... I'll go: I'm going right now," he mumbled, standing up on his feet. 

"Go ahead then. She went after the janitor." Immediately he rushed over to examine the sock and the hem: "There are stains, but they're not very noticeable; everything got stained, blended in, and has already faded. Anyone who didn't know beforehand wouldn't be able to spot it. Nastasya was lucky she couldn't see it from a distance!"

With trembling hands he opened the summons and began reading; he read for a long time before finally understanding.
