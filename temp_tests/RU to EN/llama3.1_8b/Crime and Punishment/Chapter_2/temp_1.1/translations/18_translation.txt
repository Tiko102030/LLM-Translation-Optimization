The air was thick with a dreadful stench. Up and down, the janitors with notebooks under their arms and various people of both sexes who were visitors would go in and out. The door to the inner office was also wide open. He entered and stopped in the entrance hall. Here everyone was standing and waiting for some men. There too, an incredible stench hung in the air and besides, a nauseating odor hit his nose from the fresh, not yet dried paint on the old linseed oil used to newly repaint the rooms. After a moment, he decided to move forward again, into the next room. All the rooms were tiny and low-ceilinged. A terrible impatience drove him further and further. Nobody noticed him.
