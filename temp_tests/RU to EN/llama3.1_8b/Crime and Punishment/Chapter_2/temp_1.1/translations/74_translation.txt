Then he tried to get up from his place, wanted to run away, but someone always held him back by force, and he fell into powerlessness and unconsciousness again. Finally, he completely came to himself. This happened in the morning at ten o'clock. At that hour of the morning, on clear days, the sun was always passing through a long strip along his right wall and illuminating the corner near the door. By his bedside stood Nastasya and another person, who was very curiously looking at him and completely unknown to him. This was a young man in a caftan with a beard, resembling an artisan from appearance. The hostess was peeking out of half-opened door. Raskolnikov sat up. - Who is this, Nastasya? - he asked, pointing to the young man.
