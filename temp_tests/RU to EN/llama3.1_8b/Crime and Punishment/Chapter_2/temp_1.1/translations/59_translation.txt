Leave me be! — Wait a minute, pipe-fitter! You're completely mad! As far as I'm concerned, you can do what you like. Look at it: I don't have any lessons either, and to hell with them anyway; I've got a bookseller friend called Hervimov on Tolkuchy Street, and that's its own sort of lesson. I wouldn't swap him for five merchants' lessons. He produces these little publications and brings out scientific pamphlets – they sell like hotcakes! Some titles are just priceless! You're always saying how stupid I am; by God, brother, there are people stupider than me! And now he's useful too; he doesn't even know what nonsense he's publishing, but naturally I encourage him.
