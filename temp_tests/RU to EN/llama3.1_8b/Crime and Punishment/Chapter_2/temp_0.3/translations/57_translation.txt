"Don't bother," he said, "I came... well, I have no lessons... I wanted to... but I don't need any lessons anyway." 

"You know what?" Razumikhin observed, watching him closely, "You're just rambling!" "No, I'm not rambling..." Raskolnikov stood up from the sofa. As he rose to face Razumikhin, he hadn't even thought about the fact that he would have to face him directly. Now, in an instant, he realized, on his own experience, that he was least inclined at this moment to meet anyone face-to-face in the whole world. All his bile rose up in him. He almost choked with anger at himself for having just stepped into Razumikhin's place. "Goodbye!" he said suddenly and headed towards the door.
