— And you, such-and-such and so-and-so, he suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the mournful lady had already left), what happened to you last night? Ah? Again a scandal, making a ruckus all over the street. Again fights and drunkenness. You're dreaming of being put in a straitjacket! I've told you ten times already, I've warned you, that I won't let you out on the eleventh! And you again, again, such-and-such and so-and-so! The paper even slipped from Razumikhin's hands, and he looked wildly at the plump lady who was being treated with such disrespect; but soon, however, he realized what was going on, and this whole story began to please him very much. He listened with pleasure, so much so that he wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh... All his nerves were jumping like that. — Ilya Petrovich!
