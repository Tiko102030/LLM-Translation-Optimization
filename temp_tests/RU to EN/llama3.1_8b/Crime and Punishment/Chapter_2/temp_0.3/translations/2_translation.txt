The light was on and he quickly began to inspect himself from head to toe, checking his clothes for any signs of blood. But it couldn't be done like that: shivering with cold, he started taking off everything and examining it again around him. He turned everything over, even the last thread and shred, and not trusting himself, repeated the search three times. But there seemed to be nothing, no signs at all; only on the place where the pants had torn and hung in fringes, the fringe itself left thick marks of dried blood. He grabbed a large folding knife and cut off the fringe. There seemed to be nothing else now. Suddenly he remembered that the wallet and things he had pulled out from the old woman's trunk were still all in his pockets! He hadn't even thought to take them out yet, let alone hide them! He didn't even think of it while inspecting his clothes! What is this?
