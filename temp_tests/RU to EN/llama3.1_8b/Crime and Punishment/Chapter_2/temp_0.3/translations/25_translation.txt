Don't shout! I'm not shouting, I'm speaking quite calmly, and it's you who are shouting at me; but I am a student and won't allow myself to be shouted down. The clerk was so flustered that in the first minute he couldn't get a word out, and only some sort of spittle flew from his lips. He leapt up from his seat. "Go ahead and s-s-scream!" he shouted. "You're present! Don't p-p-perjure yourself, sir!" "And you are present too," Raskolnikov shouted back, "and besides, what's more, you're smoking a cigar, so we're all equally guilty."
