— Do they hide it like that? Truly, reason has left me! In exhaustion he sat down on the sofa, and at once an unbearable chill shook him again. Mechanically, he pulled over to himself the winter coat, which had been lying next to him on a chair, his student's warm but already almost in tatters, covered himself with it, and sleep and delirium seized him once more. He dozed off. No more than five minutes later, he jumped up again and at once, in a frenzy, rushed back to his clothes. "How could I fall asleep again when nothing has been done! It's true, it's true: the noose is still under my arm! I forgot, I forgot such a thing! Such evidence!" He pulled off the noose and quickly began to tear it into pieces, stuffing them under the pillow in his clothes. "Torn strips of fabric will not arouse any suspicion at all; it seems like that, it seems like that," he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with a painful attention began to search around again, on the floor and everywhere, if he hadn't forgotten anything else?
