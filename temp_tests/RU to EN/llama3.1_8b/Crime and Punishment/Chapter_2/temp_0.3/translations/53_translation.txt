"Oh, for Pete's sake! - he thought suddenly in a fit of boundless spite. - Well, it's started, so let it start, damn her and the new life with her! How silly, Lord, how silly... And what a fool I've been today, making a show of myself and playing up to that scoundrel, Ilya Petrovich! But what's the point anyway? I don't care about them all, or even about my own foolishness. Not at all, not at all..."

He suddenly stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question struck him like a blow and filled him with bitter amazement:

"If indeed this whole thing was done consciously, not by accident, if you really had a definite and firm goal, then how on earth did you not even glance into your wallet and don't know what's been given to you, for which you've suffered so much and deliberately gone down to such a vile, disgusting, lowly deed?"
