His entire room was so small that you could hook a hook without getting up from bed. And indeed: the janitor and Nastasya are standing there. Nastasya somehow strangely glanced at him over her shoulder. He looked at the janitor with an impudent and desperate expression. The janitor silently handed him a grey, folded in half piece of paper sealed with wax from a bottle. — It's a summons, from the office, he said, handing him the paper. — From which office?... — To the police, so it means they're calling you to the office. We know which office. — To the police!
