What is this? He immediately jumped to take them out and throw them onto the table. Having picked up everything, even rummaging through pockets to make sure there was nothing left behind, he carried all of this pile over to the corner. There, in the very corner, at the bottom, in one place, the peeling wallpaper had been torn away from the wall: immediately he began to stuff it all into this hole under the paper: "it's gone! Everything is out of sight and so is the wallet!" - he thought joyfully, standing up and staring blankly into the corner at the even more gaping hole. Suddenly he shuddered with horror: "Good God," he whispered in despair, "what's happening to me? Is it really hidden? Do they hide things like this?"
