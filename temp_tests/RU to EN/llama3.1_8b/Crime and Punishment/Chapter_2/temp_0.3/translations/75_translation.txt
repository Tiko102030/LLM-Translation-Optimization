Guessing that he was awake, the mistress, who had been peeking through the door, immediately closed it and hid herself. She was always shy and endured conversations and explanations with difficulty; she was forty years old, fat and plump, black-browed and black-eyed, kind due to her weight and laziness; and even very slovenly in appearance. Shy beyond necessity. "You... who are you?" he continued questioning, addressing the foreman himself. But at that moment the door creaked open again, and, slightly leaning forward because he was tall, Razumikhin entered.
