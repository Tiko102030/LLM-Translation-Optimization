He thought triumphantly, taking a deep and joyful breath with his whole chest, "Just a weak, feverish delirium for a minute," and he pulled out the entire lining from the left pocket of his pants. At this moment, a sunbeam lit up his left boot: on the toe that was sticking out of the boot, as if signs appeared. He took off his boot: "Really, signs! The whole tip of the toe is soaked in blood"; probably he carelessly stepped into that puddle... "But what to do with this now? Where to get rid of this sock, balm, pocket?"
