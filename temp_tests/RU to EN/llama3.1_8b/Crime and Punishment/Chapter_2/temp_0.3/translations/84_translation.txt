I will bring it. Raskolnikov looked at everything with deep amazement and a dull meaningless fear. He decided to remain silent and wait: what would happen next? "It seems I'm not in delirium," he thought, "it seems this is really...". 

After two minutes Nastasya returned with soup and announced that now tea would also be served. Two spoons, two plates, and the entire set of tableware appeared: saltshaker, pepper shaker, mustard for beef, and other things that had not been seen in such an order for a long time before. The tablecloth was clean. "Not bad, Nastasya, if Praskovya Pavlovna would send two bottles of beer," I said. "We'll drink it." "Oh, you're quite the connoisseur!" Nastasya muttered and went to carry out my command.
