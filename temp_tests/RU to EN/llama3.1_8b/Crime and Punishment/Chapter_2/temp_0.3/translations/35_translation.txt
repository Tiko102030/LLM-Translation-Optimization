Poverty is not a vice, friend, but really, what can you do? It's known that he couldn't bear an insult. You probably offended him somehow and yourself didn't hold back, continued Nikodim Fomich, addressing Raskolnikov with courtesy - but it's pointless: the most non-blasphemous, I'll tell you, person, but powder, powder! He blew up, boiled over, burned out - and that's it! And everything passed by! And as a result, only gold in his heart! They even nicknamed him "lieutenant-powder"...

— And what an army unit was! — exclaimed Ilya Petrovich, very pleased that they had been so pleasantly teased, but still rousing Rascolnikov. Suddenly Raskolnikov wanted to say something unusually pleasant to them all. — Forgive me, Captain, he began very freely, addressing Nikodim Fomich suddenly - take an interest in my situation... I'm even ready to ask them for forgiveness if I made any mistakes on my part.
