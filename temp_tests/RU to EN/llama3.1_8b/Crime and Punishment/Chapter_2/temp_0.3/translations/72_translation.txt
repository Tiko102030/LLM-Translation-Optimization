He looked at her barely breathing. — I heard it myself... I didn't sleep... I sat, he said more timidly. — I listened for a long time... The assistant warden came... Everyone rushed to the staircase from all apartments...

— No one came. And your blood is screaming in you. This happens when there's no way out and you'll start to feel like being cooked in your liver... You'll be, won't you? He didn't answer. Nastasya stood over him, staring at him intently and not leaving.

— Give me a drink... Nastasyushka. She went down and came back up with water in a white clay cup two minutes later; but he already didn't remember what happened next. He only remembered taking one cold gulp of water and spilling it from the cup onto his chest.
