He thought to himself: "I'll put on the sock! - and it will only get dusty even more, and the tracks will disappear." But as soon as he put it on, he immediately pulled it off with disgust and horror. He pulled it off, but realizing there was no other option, he picked it up again and laughed. "Everything is conditional, everything is relative, everything is just forms," he thought briefly, only a corner of the thought, but himself trembling all over the body - after all, he put it on! After all, that's what he ended with! The laughter was immediately replaced by despair. "No, not within my powers..." - came to mind. His legs were shaking. "From fear," he muttered to himself. His head was spinning and aching from the heat.
