"Rags of torn hem will not arouse any suspicions; it seems so, it seems so!" he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with a painful attention began to search around again, on the floor and everywhere, had he forgotten something else? The certainty that everything, even memory, even simple consideration left him, was beginning to torment him intolerably. "What, is it really starting, is this really the execution?" He exclaimed, pointing to the remnants of braid that he cut off from his pants lying on the floor in the middle of the room for anyone to see! "What's happening to me!" he cried out again like a lost soul. Then a strange idea came to him: maybe all his clothes are bloody, maybe there are many stains, but he just can't see them, he doesn't notice because his consideration is weakened, fragmented... his mind is clouded... Suddenly he remembered that the wallet was also bloody. "Ah! So it means there must be blood in the pocket too, because I put a wet wallet in my pocket then!" He turned out the pocket at once, and - yes, on the lining of the pocket there are stains, spots! "So it means I haven't lost all reason completely, so there is still consideration and memory, since I remembered myself and figured it out!" he thought triumphantly, taking a deep breath with his whole chest, full of joy. "Just a weak moment due to fever, just a brief madness," - and he ripped off the entire lining from the left pocket of his pants.
