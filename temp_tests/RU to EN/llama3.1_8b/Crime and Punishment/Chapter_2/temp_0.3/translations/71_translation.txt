It became very unpleasant for him from this consideration, even frightening. — Nastasya, why are you silent? — he finally whispered weakly. — It's blood, — she replied quietly as if speaking to herself. — Blood! What blood?... — he mumbled, paling and stepping back against the wall. Nastasya continued to silently look at him. — No one beats the mistress, — she said again in a strict and resolute voice. He looked at her, barely breathing.
