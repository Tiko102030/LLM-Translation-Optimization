If they ask me, I might say so, he thought, approaching the office. The office was a quarter of a verst away from him. It had just moved to a new apartment, in a new house, on the fourth floor. He had been there briefly once before, but very long ago. Entering through the gates, he saw a staircase to his right, down which a man with a book in his hands was walking: "the janitor, so it's here that the office is", and he began to go up to the fourth floor at random. He didn't want to ask anyone anything about anything. "I'll enter, fall on my knees and tell everything..." - he thought, entering the fourth floor. The staircase was narrow, steep, and covered in filth. All the kitchens of all apartments on all four floors opened onto this staircase and stood there almost all day. That's why it was terribly stuffy.
