No one noticed him. In the second room sat and wrote some clerks, dressed a bit better than he was, but looking like some strange people altogether. He approached one of them. — What do you want? He showed him a summons from the office. — Are you a student? — asked that one, glancing at the summons. — Yes, an ex-student. The clerk looked him over, however without any curiosity. This was some especially disheveled person with a fixed idea in his gaze.
