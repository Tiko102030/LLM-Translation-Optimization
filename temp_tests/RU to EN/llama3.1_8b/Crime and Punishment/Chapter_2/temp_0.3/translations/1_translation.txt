"Third hour already!" He sat on the sofa, and suddenly he remembered everything! In one moment, he remembered everything! At first, he thought he was going mad. A terrible chill gripped him; but the chill was also from the fever that had started in his sleep a long time ago. Now, a sudden shiver hit him so hard that his teeth almost fell out and everything inside him was churning. He opened the door and began to listen: the whole house was completely asleep. With amazement, he looked around himself and all around the room and didn't understand: how could he have done it yesterday, entering without locking the doors on a hook and throwing himself onto the sofa, not only not undressing but even wearing a hat: it had slipped off and lay right there on the floor near the pillow. "If someone came in, what would they think? That I'm drunk, but..." He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began to look himself over from head to toe, all his clothes: were there any signs?"
