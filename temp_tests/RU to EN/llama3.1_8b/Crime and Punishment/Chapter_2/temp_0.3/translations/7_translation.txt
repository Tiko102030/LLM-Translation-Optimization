There weren't even any matches. No, it's better to go somewhere and get rid of everything. Yes! Better to get rid of it all! — he repeated, sitting down on the couch again, — right now, this very minute, without delay... But instead of that his head leaned back onto the pillow once more; the unbearable chill struck him once more; he put his greatcoat back on once more. And for a long time, several hours, it still seemed to him in fits and starts that "if only I could go somewhere right now, not putting it off, and get rid of everything so as to be rid of it all quickly, quickly!" He tried to get up from the couch several times, wanted to stand up, but couldn't. A strong knock at the door finally woke him up. — Open up, are you alive or dead? And he's sleeping like a log! — Nastasya shouted, knocking on the door with her fist, — whole days of daytime sleep, like a dog, sleeping away!
