He felt as though he had somehow cut himself off from everything and everyone with scissors in this moment. He arrived back home already by evening, so it took him only six hours. Where and how he went back, he didn't remember anything about that. Undressed and shaking all over like a horse, he lay down on the divan, put on his greatcoat, and immediately fell asleep...

He woke up in full darkness from an awful scream. God, what kind of scream was this! Such unnatural sounds, such howling, wailing, screeching, sobbing, beating, and cursing he had never heard or seen before. He couldn't even imagine such savagery, such frenzy to himself. In horror, he sat up on his bed, freezing in terror with each moment. But the screams, wails, and curses were getting louder and louder. And then, to his greatest amazement, he suddenly made out the voice of his landlady.
