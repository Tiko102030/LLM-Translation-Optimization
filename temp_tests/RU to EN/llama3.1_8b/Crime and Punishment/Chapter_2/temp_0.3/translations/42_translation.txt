The house is Noah's Ark, observed the clerk who had been listening from his place. - The matter is clear, the matter is clear! - Nikodim Fomich repeated heatedly. - No, the matter is very unclear, - Ilya Petrovich confirmed. Raskolnikov raised his hat and went towards the doors, but he didn't reach the doors...

When he came to, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, being supported from the right by some man, with another man standing to the left holding a yellow cup filled with yellow water, and Nikodim Fomich standing in front of him looking at him intently; he stood up from the chair. - What is this, are you ill? - Nikodim Fomich asked fairly sharply. - They signed it as if they were barely moving their pen, - the clerk observed, sitting down on his place and starting to work with papers again. - And how long have you been ill? - Ilya Petrovich shouted from his place, also rummaging through papers.
