The clerk smiled as he looked at them. The hot lieutenant was visibly perplexed. — This is none of your business! — he finally shouted somehow unnaturally loudly, — but please do submit the review that they are demanding from you. Show it to him, Alexander Grigorievich. Complaints against you! You're not paying money! And look how well the clear hawk has flown out! But Raskolnikov was no longer listening and greedily grabbed for the paper, hastily searching for a solution. He read it once, twice, and didn't understand.
