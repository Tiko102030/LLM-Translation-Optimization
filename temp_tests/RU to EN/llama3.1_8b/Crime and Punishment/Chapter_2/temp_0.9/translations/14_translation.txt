Then he trembled as he unfolded the summons and began to read; he longed over it for a long time before finally understanding. It was simply a notice from his neighborhood to appear at the office of the local overseer today, at half past ten. "When did this ever happen? I have no business with the police on my own! And why now especially?" he thought in tormenting bewilderment. "God, let it soon be over!" He had flung himself down on his knees to pray, but even he laughed at himself - not at the prayer, but at the very thought of praying.
