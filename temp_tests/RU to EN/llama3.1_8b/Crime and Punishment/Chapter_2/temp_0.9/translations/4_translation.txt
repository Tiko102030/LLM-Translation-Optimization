— Is it really hidden this way? Honestly, my reason has left me! In exhaustion, he sat down on the sofa, and immediately an unbearable chill shook him again. Mechanically, he pulled out the student's winter coat that was lying nearby on a chair, warm but already almost in rags, covered himself with it, and at once sleep and delirium overcame him once more. He dozed off. Not more than five minutes later, he jumped up again and immediately, in a frenzy, rushed to his clothes once more. "How could I fall asleep again, when nothing has been done! It's true, it's true: the noose is still under my arm until now! I forgot, forgot such a thing! Such evidence!" He pulled out the noose and quickly began to tear it into pieces, stuffing them under the pillow in his underwear. "Torn fabric scraps will not arouse any suspicion at all; that's how it seems, that's how it seems!" he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with intense attention started looking around again, on the floor and everywhere, if he hadn't forgotten something else?
