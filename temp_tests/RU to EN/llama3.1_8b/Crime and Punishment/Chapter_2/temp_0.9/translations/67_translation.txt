He felt as if he had somehow cut himself off with scissors from everyone and everything at that moment. He returned to his place already in the evening, so he had only passed six hours. Where and how he went back home, he couldn't remember anything. Having undressed and trembling all over like a horse in heat, he lay on the divan, put on his greatcoat, and immediately fell asleep... He woke up in full twilight to an awful cry. God, what was that scream! Such unnatural sounds, such a howl, wail, scrape, sobbing, beating and cursing, he had never heard or seen before, nor could even imagine it himself. In horror, he sat up on his bed and froze every moment, suffering. But the fights, screams and curses only grew louder and louder. And then, to his greatest amazement, he suddenly caught a glimpse of his landlady's voice.
