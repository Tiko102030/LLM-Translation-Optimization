If they ask me, I might say maybe, he thought, approaching the office. The office was a quarter of a verst from him. It had just moved to a new apartment, in a new house, on the fourth floor. He was at the old apartment only briefly, but very long ago. Entering through the gates, he saw the staircase to the right, down which a man with a book walked: "the janitor, that means; that means this is the office," and he started going up randomly to the fourth floor without asking anyone anything about it. "I'll go in, get on my knees and tell everything... "- he thought, entering the fourth floor. The staircase was narrow, steep, and covered with filth. All kitchens of all apartments in all four floors opened onto this staircase and stood like that almost the whole day. Because of this, there was a terrible stench.
