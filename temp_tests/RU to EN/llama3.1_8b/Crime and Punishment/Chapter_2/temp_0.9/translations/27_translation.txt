He read it once or twice and didn't understand it either. - What is this, then? - he asked the scribe. - This is a demand for payment from you with all expenses, interest and other fees due to the loan note. You must pay immediately with all costs, or give a written response stating when you can pay, and at the same time an obligation not to leave the capital until the payment has been made and not to sell or hide your property. The creditor is free to sell your property and act according to law towards you. - I... I owe no one anything! - It's none of our business. We received a notice for collection of a loan note worth 115 rubles, issued nine months ago by the widow Zarytinskaya, a collegiate assessor, to which it had been transferred upon payment from the late Zarytinskaya to State Councilor Chebarov, and we invite you therefore to respond. - But she's my mistress!
