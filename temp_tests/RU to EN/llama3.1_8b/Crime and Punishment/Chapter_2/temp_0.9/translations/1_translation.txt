The third hour was already gone! He sat on the sofa and suddenly remembered everything in an instant! In the first moment he thought he would go mad. A terrible chill gripped him; but the cold was also from the fever that had begun with him during sleep a long time ago. Now, however, such a shiver hit him that his teeth were almost falling out and everything inside of him went numb. He opened the door and began to listen: in the house everything had fallen completely asleep. With amazement he looked around at himself and everything around him in the room and did not understand: how was it possible for him yesterday, upon entering, not to fasten the doors on a latch and throw himself onto the sofa, not only without undressing, but even wearing a hat: it had slipped off and lay immediately on the floor close to the pillow. "If someone had come in, what would he have thought? That I'm drunk, but...". He rushed to the windowpane. There was enough light, and he quickly began to look himself over from head to toe, everything about him: are there any signs?
