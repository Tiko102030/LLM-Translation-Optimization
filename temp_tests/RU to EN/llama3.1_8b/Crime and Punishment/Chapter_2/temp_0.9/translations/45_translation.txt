It was strange. — Well, okay, concluded Ilya Petrovich, we're not going to detain you. Raskolnikov left. He still managed to catch the start of a lively conversation that had begun on his exit, in which Nikodim Fomich's interrogatory voice stood out most... On the street, he fully regained his senses. "Search, search, it's just a search! - he repeated to himself, hurrying along; 'robbers! they suspect me!' Again, an old fear enveloped him all over, from head to toe. II… What if there was indeed a search? What if I'd catch them at their own place?' But now his room. Nothing and no one; no one had looked in.
