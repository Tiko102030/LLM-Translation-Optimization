Nothing and no one; no one looked in. Not even Nastasya came near. But, Lord! How could he have left all these things here yesterday? He darted to the corner, stuck his hand under the wallpaper and started pulling out things and stuffing them into pockets. There were eight pieces altogether: two small boxes with earrings or something like that - he didn't look closely; then four little leather cases. One chain was simply wrapped in newspaper. Something else was wrapped in newspaper too, probably a medal... He put everything into different pockets, in the coat and in the remaining right pocket of his trousers, trying to make it unnoticeable. He also took the wallet along with the things. Then he left the room again, this time even leaving it wide open.
