And he was opening the window over the gutter and peering out into it like a little pig squealing; and that's shame. And how is it possible to look out of the window onto the street like a little pig squealing; and that's shame. Fie upon it, fie upon it! And Karl was dragging him from behind by his frock through the window; and here, indeed, sir captain, he tore off his silver epaulet. And then he began to shout that he had been fined fifteen real for a dirty mouth. And I myself, sir captain, paid five real for his epaulet. And this is an impolite guest, sir captain, causing all sorts of scandal!
