The summons was so strong that he had already risen to fulfill it. "Shouldn't I think for a moment?" flashed through his head. "No, better not to think at all and get rid of it!" But suddenly he stopped as if rooted to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was talking with passion to Ilya Petrovich, and the words reached him:

— It cannot be that both will be set free! First of all, everything contradicts; consider this: why would they summon a janitor if it were their business? Would they report against themselves, for cunning's sake? No, it was already too cunning! And finally, student Pestrakov was seen near the entrance by both janitors and a woman from the neighborhood at the very moment he entered: he walked with three friends and parted from them right near the entrance, and while his friends were present, he inquired about their living arrangements. Would such a person ask about living arrangements if he had that intention?
