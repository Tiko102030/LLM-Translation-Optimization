The scribe began to dictate to him the usual form of such a statement, namely that he could not pay and would do so sometime later, that he would not leave the city, that he would neither sell nor give away his property etc.

- You cannot write, your pen is slipping from your hand, - observed the scribe, looking at Raskolnikov with curiosity.

Are you ill?

Yes... my head is spinning...

Go on. The scribe took the paper and attended to others while Raskolnikov gave him his pen but instead of standing up and leaving he placed both elbows on the table and clasped his hands around his head. It felt as though they were hammering a nail into his skull.

A strange idea suddenly occurred to him: get up now, go over to Nikodim Fyodorovich, tell him everything that happened yesterday in every detail, then go with them to their apartment and point out the things that are there in the corner in the hole.
