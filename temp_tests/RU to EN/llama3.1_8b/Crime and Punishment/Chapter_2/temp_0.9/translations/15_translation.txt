He thought to himself suddenly: "I should put it on, but then I'll rub myself even more in the dust and the tracks will disappear." But as soon as he put it on, he immediately took it off with revulsion and horror. He took it off, but realizing there was no other option, he picked it up again and put it back on – and laughed again. "Everything is conditional, everything is relative, everything is just one form –" he thought for a moment, with only a corner of his mind, while his whole body trembled – "since I've already put it on! Since I ended up putting it on!" His laughter was immediately replaced by despair. "No, not within my power..." – he thought to himself. His legs were shaking. "From fear," he muttered to himself. His head was spinning and throbbing with heat.
