The air was terribly thick with moisture. Uphill and downhill, the janitors were climbing up and down carrying notebooks under their arms, officials and various people of both sexes - visitors. The door to the office was also wide open. He entered and stopped in the hallway. Everyone there stood waiting for some men. Here too, the air was overpoweringly thick, and on top of that, a fresh, still wet paint smell from newly painted rooms hit his nose like nausea. After waiting for a bit, he decided to move forward again, into the next room. All the rooms were tiny and low-ceilinged. The awful impatience kept drawing him further and further along. No one noticed him.
