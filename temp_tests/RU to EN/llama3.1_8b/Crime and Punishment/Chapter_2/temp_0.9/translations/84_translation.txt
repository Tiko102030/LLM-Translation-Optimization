To the soup came two spoons, two plates, and all the utensils: a saltshaker, a pepper mill, mustard for beef, and other things, which before, in such order, had not been seen for a long time. The tablecloth was clean. "Not bad, Nastasya, if Praskovya Pavlovna ordered two bottles of beer.
