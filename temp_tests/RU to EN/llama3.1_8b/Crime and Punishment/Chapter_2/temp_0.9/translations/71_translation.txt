It became very unpleasant for him from this consideration, even frightening. — Nastasya, why are you silent? — he finally spoke in a weak voice, hesitantly. — It's blood, — she answered at last, quietly and as if speaking to herself. — Blood!... What blood is it?... — he muttered, blanching and edging away from the wall. Nastasya continued silently looking at him. — No one hit the mistress, — she said again in a strict and resolute voice. He looked at her, barely breathing.
