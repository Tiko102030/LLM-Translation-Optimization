There they are, writers! - and he threw a scornful glance at Raskolnikov. - There was also a story in the tavern three days ago: he had dinner, but doesn't want to pay; "I'll describe you, allegedly, in satire for that." On the steamer too another one, last week, a respected family of a civil servant, his wife and daughter, he insulted with vile words. Just yesterday someone was thrown out of the confectionery in a fit of rage. Here are what writers are like, literati, students, heralds... ugh! Go away! I'll come to you myself... then watch out! Did you hear that? Louise Ivanovna began to curtsy all around and, curtsying, reached the door; but she collided with her backside on one prominent officer in the doorway, with a fresh open face and excellent thick blond whiskers.
