“Ah, what the devil is all this! — he thought suddenly in a fit of boundless spite. — Well, it started, so be it, may God have mercy on her and on her new life! How stupid it is, Lord... And how many times I've talked nonsense today! How disgusting I was leaping around and flirting with that scoundrel, Il’ya Petrovich yesterday! But what's the point of all this anyway? It doesn't matter to me one bit, nor does it matter what I did or who I flirted with. Not at all, not at all...”

He suddenly stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question knocked him off balance and shocked him painfully:

“If indeed everything was done deliberately, not by accident, if you actually had a definite and firm goal, then how on earth could you still not have looked in the wallet and not know what you received, because of which you've been going through all these torments and consciously went to such a vile, disgusting, lowly thing?”
