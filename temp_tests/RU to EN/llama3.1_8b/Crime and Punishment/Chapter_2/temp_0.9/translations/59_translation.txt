Leave me alone! — Yes, just wait a minute, telephone repairman! Completely crazy! You see: I don't have lessons, and I don't care, but there's this bookseller Hervimov on Tolchukhinskaya Street, that in itself is a lesson. I wouldn't trade him for five merchants' lessons now. He makes such oddities and publishes natural science booklets - and how they sell! Some of the titles are just priceless! You always said I was stupid; by God, brother, there's someone stupider than me! Now you're also useful in direction too; not knowing a thing yourself, but I naturally encourage it.
