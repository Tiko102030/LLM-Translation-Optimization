But how will the boxes not sink and float instead? Of course, that is what will happen. Everyone will see it. And they already look at each other like that, greeting one another, taking a glance, as if their only concern was him. "Why so, or perhaps I'm just imagining things," he thought to himself. Finally, it came to his head that maybe it would be better for him to go somewhere along the Neva; there are fewer people, and it's less noticeable, and at any rate more convenient, most importantly - far from these places. And he was suddenly surprised: how is it that he had wandered for a whole half hour in distress and anxiety, in danger, but couldn't come up with this on his own? And so he only wasted a whole half hour on such an imprudent business, which had already been decided long ago in a dream, in delirium! He was becoming extremely distracted and forgetful and knew it.
