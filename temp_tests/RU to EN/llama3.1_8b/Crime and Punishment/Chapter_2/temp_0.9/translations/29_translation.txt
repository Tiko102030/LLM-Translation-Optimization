— And you, such a one and so on, he suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the mourning lady had already left), what happened to you last night? Ah? Again, you're making a scandal, creating a commotion all over the street. Again, fighting and drunkenness. You dream of being locked up! For I've warned you ten times before, that I won't let you out on the eleventh. And yet again, such a one and so on! The piece of paper had fallen from Razumikhin's hands, and he looked wildly at the pompous lady who was being so discourteously addressed; but soon, however, he realized what was going on, and that very moment this whole story began to please him greatly. He listened with pleasure, even laughing to himself, wanting to laugh, laugh, laugh... All his nerves were jumping like that. — Il'ya Petrovich!
