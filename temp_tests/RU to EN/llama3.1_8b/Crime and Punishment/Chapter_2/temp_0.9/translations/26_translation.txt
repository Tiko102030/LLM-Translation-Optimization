The clerk looked at them with a smile. The hot lieutenant was clearly flustered. — This doesn't concern you! he finally shouted somewhat unnaturally loudly — but do be so kind as to submit the report that is required of you. Show him, Alexander Grigorievich. Complaints against you! You don't pay your debts! Ah, how well you have flown the coop, like a clear jay! But Raskolnikov was no longer listening and greedily grabbed for the paper, hastening to find an explanation. He read it once, twice, but did not understand.
