The house is a Noah's Ark, observed the clerk, who was listening from his seat. It's clear what it is, it's clear what it is! - Nikodim Fomich exclaimed hotly again. Not at all, but very unclear - Ilya Petrovich emphasized. Raskolnikov raised his hat and went towards the door, but he didn't make it to the door...

When he came to, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, being held by some man on his right side, another man standing on his left with a yellow cup filled with yellow water, and Nikodim Fomich standing in front of him staring at him intensely; he got up from the chair. - Are you ill? - Nikodim Fomich asked quite roughly. They signed just barely touching the paper with their pen - observed the clerk, sitting down again and resuming work on his papers. How long have you been ill? - Ilya Petrovich shouted from his seat and also rummaging through his papers.
