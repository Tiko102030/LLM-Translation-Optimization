"Money? What money?" thought Raskolnikov, "but... I suppose it can't be!" And he shuddered with joy. It suddenly felt incredibly, intolerably light to him. Everything was off his shoulders. - At what hour do you have coming in written down, sir? cried the lieutenant, more and more unknown by what offended - You are invited at nine, but now it is already twelve! - I was handed it just a quarter of an hour ago, Raskolnikov replied loudly and over his shoulder, suddenly and unexpectedly irritated even, and finding some pleasure in this. - That's enough that I came sick with fever. - Do not shout!
