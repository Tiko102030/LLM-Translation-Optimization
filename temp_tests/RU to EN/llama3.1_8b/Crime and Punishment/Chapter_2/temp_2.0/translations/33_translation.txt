There they are, the writers! - and he threw a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. — There was an incident yesterday too in the tavern: had lunch, but refused to pay; 'I'll describe you all', as if to say, 'in satire because of that'. On the steamer last week, too, it's not the same - an honorable official's family and his wife and daughter, he abused with filthy words. Just yesterday in the confectionery they were asked to leave. Behold what kind of people they are - writers, litterateurs, students, heralds... damn them! You'd better run away! I'll be coming to you myself soon, then beware! Did you hear? Luiza Ivanovna, with a mocking courtesy, had begun bobbing up and down on all sides, but while bobbing near the door, bumped her rear end into one prominent officer standing there with an open fresh face and excellent thick blonde whiskers.
