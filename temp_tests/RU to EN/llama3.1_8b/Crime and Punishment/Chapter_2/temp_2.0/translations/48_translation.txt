And already, by now everyone looks in this way when meeting, glancing around, as if it only concerned him. "Perhaps I'm imagining things," he thought. Finally it came to him that maybe it would be better to go somewhere else to the Neva? There are fewer people there, and one wouldn't be noticed so easily, and besides, it's more convenient anyway – and most importantly, get further away from here! And he was suddenly surprised: how did he spend whole thirty minutes wandering around in a state of sadness and worry, and in perilous places, without having thought of that himself before? And therefore only lost thirty whole minutes on such foolish business, because it had already been decided, as if resolved, like this many times before in his sleep!
