What's this? He quickly started taking them out and throwing them on the table. Having taken everything, even turning pockets inside out to make sure there was nothing left, he transferred this pile to a corner. There, in the very corner, at the bottom, in one place the peeling wallpaper was torn from the wall: immediately he began to shove everything into this hole under a piece of paper: "it's gone! Everything is gone and the wallet too!" - he thought joyfully, standing up and staring blankly at the corner, where the hole had spread even more. Suddenly he shuddered with horror: "My God," he whispered in desperation, "what's happened to me? Is it hidden here? Is that how they hide things?"

Actually, he didn't count on objects; he thought there would only be money, so he hadn't prepared a place for them beforehand - "but now, now what am I happy about?" he thought. "Is this really how they hide things?"
