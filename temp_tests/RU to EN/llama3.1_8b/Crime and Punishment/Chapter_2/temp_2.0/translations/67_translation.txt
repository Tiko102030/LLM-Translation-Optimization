It seemed to him as if he had somehow cut himself off from everything and everyone with a pair of scissors at this very moment. He came home already by evening, so it took him just six hours. Where he went back and how he got there, he did not remember anything. After undressing, shivering all over like a whipped horse, he lay on the divan, put his greatcoat on, and immediately fell asleep...

He woke up to dark twilight from an awful cry. Good Lord, what kind of cry was this? Such unnatural sounds, such a wail, scream, scrape, tears, beatings, and cursing - he had never heard or seen anything like it before, and couldn't even imagine it. He leaped out of bed in terror and sat up on his bed, frozen and suffering each moment. But the fights, screams, and curses only grew louder and louder. And then, to his greatest amazement, he suddenly caught the sound of his mistress's voice.
