No one noticed him. In the second room sat some scribes dressed rather better than he was, but all of them looked quite peculiar. He approached one of them. – What do you want? He showed a summons from an office. – You're a student? asked the other, looking at the summons. – Yes, an ex-student. The scribe glanced at him without any curiosity. This man was some sort of especially tousled person with a fixed idea in his gaze.
