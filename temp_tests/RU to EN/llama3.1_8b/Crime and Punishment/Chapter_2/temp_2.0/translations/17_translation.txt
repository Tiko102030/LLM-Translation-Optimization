"If they ask, I might tell them," he thought, approaching the office. The office was a quarter of an arshin away from him. It had just moved into a new apartment, in a new house, to the fourth floor. On his old apartment, he used to glance at it once, but that was very long ago. As he walked through the gates, he saw a staircase to the right, down which came a man with a book in hand: "a doorman, so this must be the office"; and he began climbing upstairs at random. He didn't want to ask anyone about anything.
