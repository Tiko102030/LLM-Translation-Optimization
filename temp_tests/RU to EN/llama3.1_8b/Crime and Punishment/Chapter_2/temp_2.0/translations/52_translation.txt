He's here now, perhaps lying with the house construction and has already lain so much more. But even if we found it: who would think about me? It's all over! There are no clues!" - and he laughed. Yes, he remembered later that he had laughed nervously, quietly, barely audible, long laughter, and he was laughing all the time as he passed through the square. But when he stepped onto K-th street where two days ago he met that girl, his laughter suddenly stopped. Other thoughts sprang into his head instead. It occurred to him that it would be absolutely terrible to pass by that bench now where he sat and thought after the girl left, and it will also be heavy to meet again that scoundrel who he then gave two kopecks: "Damn him!" 

He walked looking around distractedly and hatefully. All his thoughts were now swirling around one particular key point, and he felt himself that this was indeed such a main point and that it existed and that he was alone with it precisely now, right now, and for the first time in two months. "And damn this all!"
