"Third hour already!" He sat down on the sofa, and then suddenly remembered everything! In an instant, he remembered everything! At first, he thought he had lost his mind. A terrifying cold gripped him; but the cold was also from the fever that had been going on in him for a long time now in his sleep. But now, a shiver struck him so hard that his teeth almost fell out and everything inside him began to tremble. He opened the door and started listening: in the house, everything had fallen asleep completely. With amazement, he looked at himself and all around the room and couldn't understand: how could he have come in yesterday, not locking the doors on a hook, but instead throwing himself onto the sofa, not even undressing, let alone taking off his hat – it slid down and lay right there on the floor next to the pillow. "If someone had walked in, what would they think? That I'm drunk, but..." He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly started examining himself from head to toe, all over his clothes: were there any traces?"
