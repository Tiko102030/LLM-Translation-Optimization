Then he tried to get up from the spot, wanted to run away, but always someone held him back with force, and again he succumbed to powerlessness and bewilderment. Finally, he came completely back to himself. It happened in the morning at 10 o'clock. At that hour of the morning on clear days, the sun was passing over his right wall in a long strip and illuminating the corner next to the door. By his bedside stood Nastasya and another person, who looked at him with great curiosity and was completely unknown to him. This was a young man in a kaftan, bearded, and he resembled an artisan from the workshop. The owner of the apartment stuck her head out from the half-opened door. Raskolnikov raised himself up. - Who is this, Nastasya? - he asked, pointing to the young man.
