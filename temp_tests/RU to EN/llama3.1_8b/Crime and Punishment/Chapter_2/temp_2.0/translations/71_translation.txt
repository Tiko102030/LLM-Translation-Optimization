He became extremely uncomfortable from this contemplation, even frightened. — Nastasya, what's wrong that you're staying silent? — he whispered finally in a weak voice. — It's blood, — she replied at last, quietly as if speaking to herself. — Blood! What blood?! — he mumbled, paling and stepping back towards the wall. Nastasya continued to silently stare at him. — Nobody beat their mistress, — she said again in a strict and resolute voice. He gazed at her, hardly breathing.
