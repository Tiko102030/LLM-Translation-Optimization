"Shreds of torn lace will in no way excite suspicion; that's so, I think, it's like that," he repeated, standing in the room, and with intense attention began to search again all around, on the floor and everywhere, whether he had forgotten anything else? The certainty that everything, even memory, even simple thinking, leaves him was starting to torment him intolerably. "What if it's just about to start, what if this is it, what if it's a sentencing?" He looked around, truly, pieces of fringe that he cut off from his pants were lying on the floor in the middle of the room, so that anyone could see them! "What is happening with me," he shouted again as if lost. Then a strange idea came to him: that maybe all his clothes are bloody, and there might be many spots, but he doesn't notice them because his thinking has weakened, become dispersed... his mind is obscured... Suddenly he remembered that there was blood on the wallet too. "Ha! So then in the pocket there must also be blood, because I stuffed my wet wallet into my pocket at the time!" He quickly turned the pocket inside out and – and yes, there were spots on the lining of the pocket! "So therefore, I still haven't lost my mind entirely, there's thinking and memory, since I realized it myself and came to that conclusion! I thought with triumph, deeply and joyfully breathing in his entire chest – just fleeting weakness caused by fever, a brief delirium for one minute," and he ripped out the lining from the left pocket of his pants.
