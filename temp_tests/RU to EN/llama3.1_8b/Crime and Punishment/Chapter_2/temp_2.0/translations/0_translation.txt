He lay there for a very long time. It sometimes seemed as though he was awakening, and in those moments he noticed that it had been nighttime for a while, but the thought of getting up did not occur to him. Finally, he realized that it was already daylight-like bright. He lay on the sofa on his back, still disoriented from recent forgetfulness. The terrible and desperate screams from the street came suddenly into focus, which he usually heard every night under his window at 3 am. They were what woke him now. "Ah! Now those drunk people are coming out of the bars, I thought to myself - three o'clock, and suddenly leaped up as if someone had yanked him off the sofa. - How is that? Three already?" He sat on the sofa, and then remembered everything!
