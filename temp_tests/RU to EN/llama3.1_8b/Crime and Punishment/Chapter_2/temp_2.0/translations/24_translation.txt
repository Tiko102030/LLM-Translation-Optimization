"What money? Which money?— thought Raskolnikov, but... well, it must be something else!" And he shuddered with joy. He felt suddenly incredibly, unbearably light. Everything had fallen off his shoulders. — In what hour are you to come, sir? the poruchik cried out more and more offended by the unknown—You're writing for nine, but now it's already midnight!—I was given it a quarter of an hour ago, Raskolnikov replied loudly, over his shoulder, having suddenly and unexpectedly gotten angry himself and even finding some satisfaction in it. — And that's enough, considering I came sick with a fever.—Do not shout!
