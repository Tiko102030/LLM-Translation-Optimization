The scribe smiled as he looked at them. The hot major was apparently perplexed. — It's none of your business! he shouted finally somehow unnaturally loudly, — but you'd do well to submit a review that they're demanding from you. Show him, Alexander Grigorievich. Complaints against you! You aren't paying the money! See how neatly the hawk flew off! But Raskolnikov was already no longer listening and greedily seized the paper, eagerly seeking a speedy solution. He read it once, twice, and didn't understand.
