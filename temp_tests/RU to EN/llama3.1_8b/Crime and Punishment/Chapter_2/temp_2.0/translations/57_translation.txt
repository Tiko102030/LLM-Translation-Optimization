"And do you know what? You're dazed!" noted Razumikhin, observing him closely. "No, I'm not dazed," Rascolnikov stood up from the sofa, rising to face Razumikhin as he walked towards him, not even thinking that he was about to confront this person face to face. But in a moment, on the basis of experience, he understood what least made anyone want to meet someone in their entire life right now, at all costs. All his bile rose up. He nearly choked with anger at himself for having just set foot across Razumikhin's threshold. "Farewell!" he said suddenly and walked towards the door.
