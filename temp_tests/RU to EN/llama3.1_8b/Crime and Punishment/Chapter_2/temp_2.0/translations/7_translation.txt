There were no matches even. No, it's better to go out somewhere and throw everything away. Yes! Better to throw it all away! — he repeated, sitting down on the couch again, — right now, this very minute, not lingering!... But instead of that his head fell onto the pillow again; a maddening shiver overtook him once more; he put his cloak back on himself once more. And for several hours yet, it kept making a tantalizing impression upon him, bursting into gusts: "If only I were to go somewhere now, not putting it off, and throw everything away, so that from my eyes once and for all, quickly, as quickly as possible!" He jerked at the couch several times, wanted to get up, but he couldn't already.
