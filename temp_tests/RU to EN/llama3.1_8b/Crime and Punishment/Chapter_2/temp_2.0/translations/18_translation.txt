Because of this a terrible stuffiness was felt. Sweepers rose and descended, both male and female visitors - with notebooks under their arms - were walking by, people from the district - as guests. The door to the main office was also wide open. He entered and stopped in the hallway. Everyone stood there waiting for some men here too. It was a very terrible stuffiness and, besides that, a nose-burning hit of fresh still unweathered paint on rotten linseed oil on recently repainted rooms was unbearable. Having waited a little, he thought to move forward again, into the next room. All the rooms were tiny and low. The terrible impatience drew him further and further. No one noticed him.
