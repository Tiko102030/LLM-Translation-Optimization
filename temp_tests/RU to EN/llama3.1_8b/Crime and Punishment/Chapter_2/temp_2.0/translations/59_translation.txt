Leave me alone! — Just hold on a minute, sweep-brush! Completely madman! As for me, you can do as you like. You see: I have no lessons anyway, and to boot, I don't care; but there is in Tolkuchy book-dealer Hervymov, and that's an altogether unique lesson for me. I wouldn’t now exchange him even for five merchant’s worth of lessons. He publishes little editions and natural-science pamphlets; they're a natural hit – the titles are what they are! You always said I was dumb, really, there's dumber than me! And in his genre, too, it turns out he’s good; himself doesn't feel anything, but naturally I encourage him.
