Nastasia, therefore, was able to take nothing in and thank God! Then he unfolded the summons with trepidation and began to read; for a long time he read it and only then understood. This was an ordinary summons from the neighborhood to appear today, at half past ten, to the office of the local police inspector. "What is this? I have no dealings with the police on my own behalf! And why just today? - He thought in unbearable confusion. - God, for heaven's sake!"
