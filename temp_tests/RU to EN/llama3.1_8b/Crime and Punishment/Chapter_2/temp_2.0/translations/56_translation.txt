He was taken aback by this sudden appearance of his visitor. "What's the matter with you?" he shouted, inspecting his companion from head to foot; then he paused and whistled. "Good heavens! It must be quite awful for you!" He added, looking at Razumikhin's rags, "You've really surpassed our own brother here!" He invited him to sit down, thinking that perhaps he was just tired. And when Razumikhin flopped down onto the tattered Turkish divan, which was even worse than his own, Razumikhin suddenly realized that his visitor was ill. "Are you seriously ill?" he asked, looking at Razumikhin with genuine concern. He felt his pulse, and Razumikhin jerked away his hand.
