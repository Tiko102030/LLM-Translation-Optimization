Poverty is not a vice, friend, well yes anyway! It's known that the gunpowder couldn't bear an insult. You've probably offended him in something, and you yourself didn't hold back, — continued Nikodim Fyodorovich, addressing Raskolnikov with courtesy — but this is useless: he's the most non-obliterator in the world, I'll tell you, a person, but gunpowder, gunpowder! He ignited, boiled, burned out - and that's it! And everything passed off without incident! And as a result only gold from his heart! They even nicknamed him "lieutenant-gunpowder"...

— What a regiment was there! — exclaimed Illya Petrovich, very pleased that he had been so agreeably teased, but still stirring. Raskolnikov suddenly wanted to say something extremely pleasant to all of them. — Forgive me, Captain, — he began to be quite free and easy, addressing Nikodim Fyodorovich suddenly — take an interest in my position... I'm even willing to ask for their apologies if they were slighted on my part.
