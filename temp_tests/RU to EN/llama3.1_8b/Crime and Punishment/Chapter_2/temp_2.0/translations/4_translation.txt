- Don't they hide it in plain sight then? My mind has really deserted me! In his exhaustion he collapsed onto the sofa, and instantly the unbearable chill again shook him. Automatically, he dragged out from under the stool beside him, a winter coat of his student days, warm but almost in tatters by now, covered himself with it, and at once sleep and delirium overtook him once more. He dozed off. No more than five minutes had passed when he leapt up again, and instantly, in a state of frenzy, again dashed to his clothes. 'How could I have fallen asleep just then when nothing has been done? Yes indeed, yes indeed: the noose has still not been removed from under my armpit! Forgotten, so utterly forgotten! That is an incriminating evidence!' He pulled off the noose and quickly began to tear it into pieces, stuffing them underneath his pillow in the linen. 'Shreds of torn lining won't raise any suspicions at all; it seems like that, seems just that way' - he repeated, standing amidst the room, and with agonized attention again started to search around everywhere, on the floor and all over the place, if he had forgotten anything else?
