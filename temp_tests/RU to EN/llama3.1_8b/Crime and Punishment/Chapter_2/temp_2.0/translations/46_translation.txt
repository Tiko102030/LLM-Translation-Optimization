Nothing and no one; no one was peeking in. Not even Natalya touched anything. But, God! How could he leave all these things here yesterday? He darted into a corner, thrust his hand under the wallpaper, and began pulling out things to stuff them into pockets. A total of eight items turned up: two small boxes with earrings or something similar – he looked very closely; then four small silk cases. One chain was simply wrapped in newspaper. There seemed to be something else in newspaper too... an order?

He placed everything in different pockets, in his coat and the remaining pocket of his trousers, trying to make it unnoticeable. He even took a wallet with him. Then he left the room again, this time leaving it wide open altogether.
