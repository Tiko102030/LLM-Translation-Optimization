And you, that's-her-and-this, suddenly yelled at the top of his lungs (the sorrowful lady had already left), what happened to you there last night? Again a shame, you cause a commotion all over the street, again a fight and drunkenness. You dream of getting disciplined! Because I told you ten times before, because I warned you, that in November I won't let you out! And you, again and again, you're this one! A piece of paper even fell from Raskolnikov's hand, and he looked wildly at the lush lady, who was being treated with such impertinence; but soon, however, he realized what it was all about, and right away the whole story began to please him very much. He listened with pleasure, so that it seemed like you wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh... All his nerves were jumping just like that. — Ilyich Petrovich!
