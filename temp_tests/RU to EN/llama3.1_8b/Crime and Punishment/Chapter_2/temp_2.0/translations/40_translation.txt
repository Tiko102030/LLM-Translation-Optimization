The summons was so forceful that he had already stood up to comply. "Not think for a moment?" passed through his head. "No, I'll throw the whole thing over my shoulder without thinking about it." But suddenly he stopped as if rooted in place: Nikodim Fomich spoke with ardor to Il'ya Petrovich, and his words reached him:

— It cannot be that both of them will be set free! First of all, everything contradicts; judge for yourself: why would they invite the caretaker over if this were their business? To inform on themselves, perhaps? Or out of cunning? No, it would already be too clever! And finally, the student Pestrovyk was seen near both entrance doors by two caretakers and a woman of the people at exactly that moment when he entered: he walked with three friends and parted ways with them right at the entrance gates and asked about living arrangements from the caretakers while his friends were still there. So would someone asking about living arrangements be doing so with such intentions?
