And he was opening a window onto the street and peering through it like a little piglet; and this is shameful. And how can one open a window on the street like a little piglet? And this is shameful, too. Fie upon him! And Karl behind him was yanking him by the tailcoat from the window; and there, in truth, Mr Captain, he ripped his white gloves. And then he started screaming that they were going to fine him fifteen real dollars for that misdemeanor. And I, Mr Captain, myself paid him five real dollars as a fine. And this is an impolite guest, Mr Captain, who causes any kind of trouble!
