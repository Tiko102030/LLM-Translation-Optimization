The house was the ark of Noah - noted the clerk, who listened from his seat.  The case is clear, the case is clear! exclaimed Nikodim Fomich warmly.  No, the case is very unclear - confirmed Ilya Petrovich. 

Raskolnikov raised his hat and went to the door but did not reach it...

When he came to, he saw that he was sitting in a chair, that he was being supported by someone on the right, that there was another person standing on the left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nikodim Fomich was standing in front of him looking closely at him; he stood up from the chair. 

What's wrong with you? asked Nikodim Fomich fairly sharply. They sign their names so carelessly - they can barely even hold the pen - noted the clerk, sitting down on his seat and taking up papers again.

How long have you been sick? shouted Ilya Petrovich from his place and also rummaging through the papers.
