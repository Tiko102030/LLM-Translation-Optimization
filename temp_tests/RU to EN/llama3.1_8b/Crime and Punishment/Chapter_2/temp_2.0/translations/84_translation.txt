I will bring it. Raskolnikov looked at everything with deep amazement and dull meaningless fear. He made up his mind to remain silent and wait: what would happen next? "It seems I'm not mad, - he thought, - it really...".

In two minutes Nastasya came back with soup and announced that now tea would also be served. To the soup appeared two spoons, two plates, and all the utensils: saltshaker, peppermill, mustard for beef, and other things which had not been seen in such an order for a long time already. The tablecloth was clean. — It's good, Nastasya, that Praskovya Pavlovna sent over two bottles of beer. We'll drink, then. — Oh you, clever fellow! — Nastasya muttered to herself and went to fulfill the order.
