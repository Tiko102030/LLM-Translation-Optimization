The clerk was smiling at them. The hot lieutenant seemed visibly perplexed. — This is none of your business! he finally shouted somehow unnaturally loudly, — but please do submit the complaint that they require from you. Show it to him, Alexander Grigorievich. Complaints against you! You're not paying money! And look at how well this fine falcon has flown! But Raskolnikov was no longer listening and greedily grabbed for the paper, hastily searching for a solution. He read it once, twice, and didn't understand.
