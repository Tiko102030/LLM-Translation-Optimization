No one noticed him. In the second room, clerks were sitting and writing, dressed a little better than he was, but looking like some strange people altogether. He approached one of them. — What do you want? He showed a summons from the office. — Are you a student? asked that one, glancing at the summons. — Yes, an ex-student. The clerk looked him over, however without any curiosity at all. It was some particularly disheveled person with a fixed idea in his gaze.
