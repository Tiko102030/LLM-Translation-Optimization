He looked at her barely breathing. "I heard it myself... I didn't sleep... I was sitting," he stammered again. "I listened for a long time... The assistant warden came... Everyone rushed to the stairs from all apartments..." — No one came. And there's blood crying out inside you. This is when she has no way out and will start to roast in her liver, then it starts to appear... You'll be someone, right? He didn't answer. Nastasya stood over him, staring at him intensely and not moving away. "Give me a drink... Nastasya." She went down and returned with water in a white clay mug two minutes later; but he already didn't remember what happened next. He only remembered taking one gulp of cold water and spilling some from the mug onto his chest.
