It seemed to him as if he had somehow cut himself off from everything and everyone with scissors in this moment. He arrived back home already by evening, so it was just six hours that passed. Where and how he went back, he couldn't remember anything. Undressing and shivering all over like a horse that's been beaten, he lay on the divan, put on his greatcoat and immediately fell asleep... He woke up in full darkness to an awful scream. Good God, what was this scream! Such unnatural sounds, such howling, wailing, screeching, sobbing, cursing and yelling he had never heard or seen before. He couldn't even imagine such savagery, such frenzy for himself. In horror, he sat up on his bed and froze every moment, suffering. But the fights, screams and curses grew louder and louder. And now, to his greatest astonishment, he suddenly caught the voice of his landlady.
