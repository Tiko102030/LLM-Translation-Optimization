The house is the Ark of Noah - observed the clerk, listening from his place. - The matter is clear, the matter is clear! - heatedly repeated Nikodim Fomich. - No, it's a very unclear matter, - confirmed Ilya Petrovich. Raskolnikov raised his hat and went towards the doors, but he didn't reach them...

When he came to, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that someone was supporting him from the right, another person stood to his left with a yellow cup filled with yellow water, and Nikodim Fomich was standing in front of him and intently looking at him; he got up from the chair. - What is this, are you ill? - rather sharply asked Nikodim Fomich. - They signed as if they were barely moving their pens, - observed the clerk, sitting down on his place again and resuming work with papers. - And how long have you been ill? - shouted Ilya Petrovich from his place, also rummaging through papers.
