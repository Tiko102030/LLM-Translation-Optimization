— Don't bother, he said, I came... that is: I have no lessons... I wanted to... well, I don't need any lessons at all.

Do you know what? You're rambling! observed Razumikhin, who was watching him closely. No, I'm not rambling... — Raskolnikov stood up from the sofa. As he got up to Razumikhin, he didn't think about the fact that he would have to face him face-to-face. Now, in an instant, he realized on his own experience that at this moment, he was least disposed to meet anyone face-to-face with anyone in the entire world. All his bile rose up in him. He nearly choked from anger towards himself, just after stepping over Razumikhin's threshold. — Goodbye! he said suddenly and went to the door.
