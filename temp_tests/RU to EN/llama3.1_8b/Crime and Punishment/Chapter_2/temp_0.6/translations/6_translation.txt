He thought to himself with triumph, deeply and joyfully breathing in his whole chest: "Just a weak, feverish hallucination for a minute." And he ripped out the entire lining from the left pocket of his pants. At this moment, a sunbeam illuminated his left boot: on the sock that was sticking out of the boot, as if signs had appeared. He kicked off his boot: "really signs! The whole tip of the sock is soaked in blood"; probably he carelessly stepped into that puddle then... "But what to do with this now? Where to get rid of this sock, the fringe, the pocket?"
