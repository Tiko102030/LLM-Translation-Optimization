— And you, such and such, he suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the mourning lady had already gone out), what happened to you last night? Ah? Again a scandal, producing a drunken brawl on the entire street. Again fighting and intoxication. You dream of being put in a straitjacket! For I've already told you, for I've warned you ten times that I won't let you go at eleven! And yet again, and yet again, such and such are you, like this! A piece of paper fell out of Raskolnikov's hands, and he looked wildly at the plump lady who was being so impudently treated; but soon, however, he realized what it was all about, and immediately this whole story began to please him very much. He listened with pleasure, even that he wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh... All his nerves were jumping like that. — Ilya Petrovich!
