The call was so strong that he had already stood up to answer it. "Should I think for a moment?" flashed through his head. "No, better not to think and off with it!" But suddenly he stopped as if rooted to the spot: Nikodim Fomich spoke warmly to Ilya Petrovich, and his words reached him:

— It cannot be that both will be released! First of all, everything contradicts; judge for yourself: why would they call a janitor if this were their business? To inform on themselves, perhaps? Or is it just a trick? No, it was already too cunning! And finally, the student Pestyakov was seen by both janitors and the shopkeeper at the very moment when he entered; he walked with three friends and parted from them at the gate and asked about living arrangements at the janitors', still in front of his friends. Would he ask about living arrangements if he had such intentions?
