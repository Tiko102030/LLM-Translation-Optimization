He's already been asleep for three hours! He sat down on the couch – and suddenly it all came back to him. At first he thought he'd gone crazy. A terrible chill gripped him; but the chill was also from a fever that had started in his sleep long ago. Now, suddenly, such a shiver hit him that his teeth almost fell out and everything inside him seemed to be turning over. He opened the door and began listening: the whole house was completely asleep. With amazement he looked around himself and everything in the room and didn't understand how it was possible that yesterday, entering, he hadn't locked the doors on a hook and fallen onto the couch, not only without undressing but even still wearing his hat – it had slipped off and was lying right there on the floor near the pillow. "If someone had come in, what would they have thought? That I'm drunk, but..." He rushed to the window. The light was sufficient, and he quickly began looking himself over from head to toe, all of his clothes: were there any traces?
