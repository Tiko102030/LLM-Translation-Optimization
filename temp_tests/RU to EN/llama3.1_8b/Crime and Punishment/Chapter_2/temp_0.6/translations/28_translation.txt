The scribe looked at him with a condescending smile of pity and triumph, like a novice who had just been bombarded: "How do you feel now?" But what was he to care about the borrowed letter, the pursuit! Was this worthy of any anxiety, in turn, or even attention! He stood, read, listened, replied, even asked questions himself, but all mechanically. The triumph of self-preservation, rescue from the crushing danger – that filled him with a sense of being at this moment, without forethought, without analysis, without future speculations and guesses, without doubts and questions. This was an instant of complete, direct, pure animal joy. But in this very moment something like thunder and lightning struck in the office. The lieutenant, still shaken by the impertinence, all aglow and, clearly, wanting to support the injured ambition, attacked the unfortunate "puffy lady" with all his might, who had been looking at him since he entered with a silly smile. —And you, such-a-such and so-what, he suddenly shouted from the heart (the tragic lady had already left), what happened to you last night?
