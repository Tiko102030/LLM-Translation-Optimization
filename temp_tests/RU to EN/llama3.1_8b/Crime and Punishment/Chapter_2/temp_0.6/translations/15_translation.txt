He thought to himself: "If I put it on, the tracks will be erased by the dust even more." But as soon as he put it on, he immediately pulled it off with disgust and horror. He pulled it off, but realizing that there was no other option, he took it and put it back on - and laughed again. "It's all relative, everything is just a form," he thought for a moment, but his body trembled - after all, I did put it on! After all, I ended up putting it on! His laughter was quickly replaced by despair. "No, it's beyond my power..." he thought to himself. His legs were shaking. "From fear", he muttered to himself. His head was spinning and aching from the heat.
