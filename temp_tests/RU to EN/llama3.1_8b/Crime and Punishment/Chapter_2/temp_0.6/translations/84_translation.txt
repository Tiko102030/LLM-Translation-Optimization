I will bring it. Raskolnikov looked at everything with deep amazement and a dull meaningless fear. He decided to remain silent and wait: what would happen next? "It seems I'm not in delirium," he thought, "it seems this is really...".

After two minutes Nastasya returned with soup and announced that now tea would be served too. Two spoons, two plates and the entire set of tableware appeared: a saltshaker, pepper shaker, mustard for beef, and other things which had not been seen in such an order for a long time before. The tablecloth was clean. "Not bad, Nastasya, if Praskovya Pavlovna would send two bottles of beer," I said. "We'll drink to that." "You're the one who's full of ideas!" Nastasya muttered and went to carry out my order.
