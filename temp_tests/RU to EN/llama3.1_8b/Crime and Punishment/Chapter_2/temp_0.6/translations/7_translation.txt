Better to get rid of it! — he repeated, sitting down on the couch again — and now, this very minute, without delay... But instead of that his head leaned back onto the pillow; he felt an unbearable chill again; he pulled his greatcoat over himself again. For a long time, for several hours, it continued to haunt him in fits, that "if only I could go somewhere right now and get rid of everything once and for all, so as to be free from it quickly, quickly..." He made attempts to get up from the couch several times, wanted to stand up but couldn't. It was a strong knock on the door that finally woke him up. — Open up, are you alive or not? And he's just sleeping! — Nastasya shouted, knocking her fist against the door — whole days, like a dog, sleeping!
