And he was opening the window onto the canal and peering out of it like a little pig squealing; and this is shameful. And how can one peer out of the window into the street like a little pig squealing; and this is shameful. Fie upon you! And Karl was tugging him from behind by his frock from the window, and then, I swear to God, captain, he ripped off his waistcoat. And then he cried that he had to pay fifteen whole francs in fines, and I myself, captain, paid five francs for him to have his waistcoat repaired. And this is an unworthy guest, captain, and causing all sorts of commotion! I say, I'll have a big satirical article about you in all the papers because I can write whatever I like about you. — Writers? — Yes, captain, and what an unworthy guest, captain, when he's in a noble house... — Ah-ah-ah!
