His whole room was so small that you could hook a fish without getting up from the bed. And indeed: there stands the janitor and Nastasya. Nastasya looked at him with some sort of strange glance. He gazed at the janitor with a provoking and desperate look. The janitor silently handed him a grey, folded in two paper note sealed with bottle wax. "A summons from the office," he said, handing over the paper. "From which office?"... "The police office, that is, they're summoning you to the office." "The police office!"
