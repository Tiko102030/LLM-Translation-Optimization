Behold them, writers! - and he cast a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. - There was an incident the other day in a tavern too: dined, but doesn't want to pay; "I'll describe you in satire for that", he says. On the steamer last week, another one, a respectable family of a high-ranking official, his wife and daughter, called them vile names. The confectionery threw out one of their own just recently. That's what writers are like, literati, students, heralds... ugh! Off with you! I'll pay you a visit myself then watch out! Did you hear? Louisa Ivanovna launched into a gracious curtsy in all directions and, curtsying, reached the doors; but she bumped her rear end on one prominent officer standing in the doorway, with an open fresh face and excellent thick blonde whiskers.
