I've read it once or twice and didn't understand it yet. — What is this? — he asked the clerk. — This is a demand for payment of debts with interest, collection. You must either pay all expenses, including fines and other things, or give a written receipt when you can pay, as well as an obligation not to leave the capital until payment and not sell or hide your property. And the creditor has the right to sell your property, and act according to the law with you. — I don't owe anyone anything! — This is none of our business. We have received a protested overdue loan of 115 rubles issued by you nine months ago to the widow Zarnitsyn, which was then transferred for payment to State Councilor Chebarov, and we invite you to respond accordingly. — Well, she's my landlady!
