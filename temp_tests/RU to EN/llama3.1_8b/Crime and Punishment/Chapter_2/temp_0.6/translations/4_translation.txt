— Are they hiding it like this? My mind is leaving me! He collapsed onto the couch in exhaustion and was immediately seized by an unbearable shiver. Mechanically, he pulled over his winter student coat, which had been lying on a chair, warm but already almost in tatters, covered himself with it, and sleep and delirium again simultaneously overwhelmed him. He lost consciousness. No more than five minutes later, he leapt up again and immediately, in a frenzy, rushed to his clothes once more. "How could I fall asleep again when nothing has been done! It's true, it's true: the noose under my arm is still not removed! I forgot, forgot about such a thing! Such evidence!" He pulled off the noose and quickly began to tear it into pieces, stuffing them under the pillow in his underwear. "Torn strips of fabric will by no means arouse suspicion; it seems so, it seems so!" he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with an attention that was painful to bear, he began to search around again, on the floor and everywhere, if he had not forgotten something else?
