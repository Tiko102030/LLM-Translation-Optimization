Then he tried to get up from his place, wanted to run away, but always someone would stop him with force, and he would fall into powerlessness and unconsciousness again. Finally, he completely regained consciousness. This happened in the morning, at ten o'clock. At that hour of the morning, on clear days, the sun was always passing along a long strip across his right wall and illuminating the corner near the door. By his bedside stood Nastasya and another man, very curiously looking at him and completely unknown to him. This was a young man in a caftan, with a beard, and looked like an artisan from afar. The landlady's head was peeking out from half-opened door. Raskolnikov sat up. — Who is this, Nastasya? he asked, pointing at the young man.
