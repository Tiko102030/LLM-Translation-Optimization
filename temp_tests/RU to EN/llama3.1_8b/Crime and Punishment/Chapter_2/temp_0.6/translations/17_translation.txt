If they ask me, I might tell them so, he thought as he approached the office. The office was a quarter of a verst away from him. It had just moved to a new apartment, in a new house, on the fourth floor. He used to glance at their old apartment long ago. As he entered through the gate, he saw a staircase to his right, down which a man with a book came: "it's a janitor, so that means it's the office", and he began to climb up randomly. He didn't want to ask anyone anything. "I'll go in, get on my knees and tell everything..." he thought as he entered the fourth floor. The staircase was narrow, steep and full of trash. All kitchens from all apartments on all four floors opened onto this staircase and stood there almost all day. That's why it was terribly stuffy.
