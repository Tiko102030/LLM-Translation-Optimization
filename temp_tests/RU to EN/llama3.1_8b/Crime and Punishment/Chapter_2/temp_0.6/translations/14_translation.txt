It was an ordinary summons from the district to appear today at half past ten in the office of the district supervisor. "When has this ever happened? I don't have any business with the police myself! And why exactly today?" he thought in distressful bewilderment. "Lord, let it end soon!" He had thrown himself on his knees to pray, but even laughed at himself - not at the prayer, but at himself. He hastily began to dress. "I'll ruin everything anyway! Put on a sock!"
