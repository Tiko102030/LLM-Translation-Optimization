Nothing and no one; nobody had looked in. Not even Nastasya had touched it. But, Lord! How could he have left all those things here yesterday? He dashed into the corner, stuck his hand under the wallpaper and began to pull out the things and stuff them into his pockets. There turned out to be eight items in total: two small boxes with earrings or something similar - he looked at them carefully; then four small sapphire cases. One chain was simply wrapped in newspaper. Something else was also wrapped in newspaper, it seemed like an order... He put everything in different pockets and in the coat and in the remaining right pocket of his pants, trying to make it less noticeable. He even took the wallet along with the things. Then he got out of the room, this time leaving it completely open.
