What is this? In a flash he jumped to take them out and throw them onto the table. Having taken everything, even rummaging through pockets, to make sure that nothing was left behind, he carried all of this pile over to the corner. There, in the very corner, at the bottom, in one place the torn-off wallpaper from the wall had been: immediately he began to stuff everything into this hole under the paper: "it's gone! Everything is out of sight and even the wallet!" - he thought happily, standing up and staring blankly into the corner, at the further widened hole. Suddenly he shuddered with horror: "Good God, I whispered in despair, - what has happened to me? Is it really hidden? Do they hide things like this?" 

Indeed, he hadn't counted on things; he thought there would only be money, so he didn't prepare a place beforehand - "but now, now what am I happy about? - he thought. - Do they hide things like this?"
