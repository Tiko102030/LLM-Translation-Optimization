Who knows, maybe it doesn't come out any better but worse... Take it or leave it? Raskolnikov took the German sheets silently, took three rubles and, not saying a word, left. Razumikhin looked at him in surprise as he went out. But after he had already reached the first line, Raskolnikov turned around suddenly, climbed back up to Razumikhin again, put the German papers and three rubles on the table, and without saying another word, left once more. "You've got a brain fever!" Razumikhin finally exploded in anger. "What kind of comedy are you putting on? You even threw me off balance... What made you come here after all?"
