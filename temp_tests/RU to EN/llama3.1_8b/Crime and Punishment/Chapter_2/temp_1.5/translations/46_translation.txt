Nothing and no one; no one was taking a peek. Not even Nastasya came close. But good God! How could he have left all these things here yesterday in this hole? He darted to the corner, thrust his hand under the wallpaper and began pulling out objects and stuffing them into pockets. There were eight pieces altogether: two small boxes containing earrings or something of that kind - he didn't take a close look; then four small sapphire covers. A chain was simply wrapped in newspaper. Some other thing was also in newspaper, probably an order...

He placed everything in various pockets, in the overcoat and in the remaining right pocket of the pants, trying to make it non-descript. The wallet was taken along with the objects as well. Then he got out of the room, this time even leaving it wide open.
