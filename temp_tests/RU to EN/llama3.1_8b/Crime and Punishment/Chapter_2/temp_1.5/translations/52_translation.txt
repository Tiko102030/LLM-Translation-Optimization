But when he stepped onto K Boulevard, where two days ago he met this girl, his laughter suddenly stopped. Other thoughts rushed into his head. It seemed to him that it was now incredibly repulsive to pass by that bench, on which he sat after the girl left and thought, and it would also be terribly hard to run into that fat man again, whom he gave two roubles: “Damn him!”
