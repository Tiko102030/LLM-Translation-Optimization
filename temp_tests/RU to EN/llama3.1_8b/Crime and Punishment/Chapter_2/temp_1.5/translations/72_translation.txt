He looked at her, barely breathing. — I heard it myself... I didn't sleep... I was sitting... he said more softly still. — I listened for a long time... The assistant supervisor came... Everyone rushed to the staircase from all the apartments...

— No one came. And your blood is screaming inside you. That's when she has no way out and starts cooking with liver... You start imagining such things... Yes, maybe? He didn't answer. Nastasya was standing over him, staring at him intently and not leaving. — Give me a drink... Nastasya. She went down and returned with water in a white clay cup after two minutes; but he already didn't remember what happened next. He only remembered taking one gulp of cold water and spilling it on his chest from the cup.
