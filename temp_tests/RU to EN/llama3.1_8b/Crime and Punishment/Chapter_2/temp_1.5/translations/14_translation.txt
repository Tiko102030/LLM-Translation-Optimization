Nastasya was in no position to observe anything; thank goodness! Then he nervously unfolded the summons and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood. It was just a common summons from the ward to appear on this very day, at half past ten, at the office of the ward supervisor. "When is that ever going to happen? I don't have any dealings with the police! And why now today? — He thought in tormenting perplexity. — God, come quickly for my sake!" He had thrown himself onto his knees to pray, but even he laughed at himself, not over the prayer itself but over how ridiculous it was. He hastily began dressing. "I'll perish anyway; it's all the same! Put on your sock!"
