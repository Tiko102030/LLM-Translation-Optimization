Nobody noticed him. In the second room, clerks were sitting and writing who were dressed only slightly better, looked like some strange kind of people. He turned to one of them. — What do you want? He showed him a summons from the office. — You're a student? — he asked, looking at the summons. — Yes, former student. The clerk glanced at him, however without any curiosity. It was some especially disheveled person with an unchanging idea in his gaze.
