Screws of torn fringed tape would in no case provoke any suspicion; that's what he kept repeating to himself while standing in the room and intently, almost painfully searching his surroundings, the floor, and everywhere, if he had forgotten anything else? The certainty that everything – even memory, even simple consideration - was leaving him began to torment him relentlessly. "Is it really starting, is this judgment arriving?" he cried out again, pointing. Indeed, scraps of fringed trim, which he had cut off from his pants, lay on the floor right there in the room so anyone could see them! "What's happening to me!" he shouted again as if lost. Then a strange thought suddenly came to him: what if all his clothes are bloody and there are many spots, but he just can't see them because his thinking is weak and divided... His mind has been clouded. Suddenly he remembered that the purse had blood on it too. "Ah, of course! So in the pocket too there must be a lot of blood, because I still wet the purse at that time and then stuffed it into my pants pocket!" He quickly turned over the pocket and - indeed - on the lining of the pocket there are stains, spots! "So apparently reason hasn't left completely either; apparently consideration and memory are intact too since I've caught myself and come to my senses!" he thought triumphantly, exhaling a deep and joyful breath.
