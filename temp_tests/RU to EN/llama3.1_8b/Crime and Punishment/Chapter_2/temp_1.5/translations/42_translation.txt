The house is the ark of Noah, - observed the scribe who was seated in his place. - Clear as daylight! Clear as daylight! repeated Nicholas Fyodorovich heatedly. - No, very unclear indeed, - emphasized Illia Petrovich. Raskolnikov raised his hat and went towards the door, but he didn't reach the door...

When he regained consciousness, he saw that he was sitting in a chair, that he was being supported from the right by some person, that another person stood to his left with a yellow cup filled with yellow water, and Nicholas Fyodorovich was standing before him and intently looking at him; he got up from the chair. - What is this, are you sick? - fairly roughly asked Nicholas Fyodorovich. - They signed in such a way that they hardly managed to draw it out with a pen, - noticed the scribe, sitting down again and resuming his papers. - And how long have you been ill? - shouted Illia Petrovich from his place, and also sorting through his papers.
