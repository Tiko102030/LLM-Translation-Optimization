"Money? What money?" Raskolnikov thought, "but... it must be some other thing!" And he shuddered with joy. It suddenly became incredibly easy for him. Everything had fallen from his shoulders. "And at what hour are you supposed to come," a port officer shouted more and more aggressively not knowing what was offending him - they had written to him at nine, but now it's already the twelfth hour! "I got it just a quarter of an hour ago," Raskolnikov loudly answered from over his shoulder, suddenly and unexpectedly for himself irritated and even finding some pleasure in this. "That is enough that I came as a sick man with a fever." "Please do not shout!"
