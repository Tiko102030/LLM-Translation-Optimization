There weren't even any matches. No, it's better to get up somewhere and throw everything out. Yes! Better to throw it all out! - he repeated, getting back onto the divan again — and right now, this minute, no delay!.. But instead of that his head fell back against the pillow; an unbearable chill set in once more; he pulled his greatcoat on himself again. And for a long time, several hours, it kept flashing before him in fits that 'now, without putting it off any longer, to get up somewhere and throw everything out so that it would finally disappear from my eyes as quickly as possible'. He made several attempts to get up from the divan, wanted to stand up but couldn't anymore. A strong knock on the door eventually woke him up. - Open it, are you alive or dead? And all day he just lies there like a dog sleeping! - Nastasya shouted, banging her fist on the door, whole daytime days as usual she sleeps like a log!
