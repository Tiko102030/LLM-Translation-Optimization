He thought to himself suddenly: 'If I put it on, the footprints will just get even more covered in dust and disappear.' But he had only just put it on when he hastily pulled it off with revulsion and horror. He did pull it off but then realizing there was nothing else to wear, he picked it up again and started laughing once more. 'Everything is conditional, everything relative, all of this are only forms, he thought briefly, on the tip of a fleeting thought, but shivering in every fibre of his body – I did put it on after all! I've come down to that!' However, the laughter was quickly replaced by despair. 'No, not within my powers...', came to mind. His legs were trembling. He mumbled to himself: 'From fear.' His head was spinning and aching from heat.
