It seemed to him as if he had cut himself off with scissors from all and everything in this moment. He arrived home already in the evening, therefore he spent only six hours. Where and how he returned back, he didn't remember anything. Undressing and shaking like a saddled horse, he lay down on the couch, put his greatcoat on and instantly fell asleep...

He woke up in complete twilight from an awful scream. God, what kind of scream is that! Such unnatural sounds, such a hiss, roar, shriek, sobs, beating and insults, he had never heard or seen before, and he couldn't even imagine something like this brutality, frenzy. He was filled with horror and sat up on his bed, freezing and tortured every moment. But the fights, roars, and insults only grew louder and stronger. And suddenly, to his greatest astonishment, he caught a glimpse of the voice of his landlady.
