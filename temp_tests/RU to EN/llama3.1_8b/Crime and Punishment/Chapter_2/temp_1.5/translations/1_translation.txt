Third hour already!" He sat on the sofa, and suddenly everything came back to him! All of a sudden, in one moment, everything came back to him! In the first instant, he thought he was going crazy. A terrible chill had gripped him; but it was also from the fever that had been coming on for hours while he slept. But then there came such a shiver that his teeth almost fell out and everything inside of him just froze up. He opened the door and began to listen: in the house everyone was fast asleep. Stunned, he looked all around himself in the room and couldn't understand how it was possible that he had come in yesterday without locking the doors with a hook and thrown himself onto the sofa not only unbuttoned but even wearing a hat – it had slipped off and lay right there on the floor next to the pillow. "If someone came in, what would they think? That I'm drunk, but..." He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly looked over everything, from head to toe, all of his clothes: were there any signs?
