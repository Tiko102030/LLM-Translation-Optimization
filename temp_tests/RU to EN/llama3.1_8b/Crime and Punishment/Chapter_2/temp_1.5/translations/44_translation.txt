Where to, if I may ask? On the street. Briefly and clearly. Raskolnikov replied abruptly, curtly, very pale like a handkerchief and not lowering his dark inflamed eyes before Ilia Petrovich's gaze. He was barely standing on his legs, and you... — noticed Nikodim Fomich. Nothing! — Ilya Petrovich said somehow especially. Nikodim Fomich wanted to add something else, but, glancing at the scribe who also very attentively looked at him, fell silent. Everyone suddenly fell silent. It was strange.
