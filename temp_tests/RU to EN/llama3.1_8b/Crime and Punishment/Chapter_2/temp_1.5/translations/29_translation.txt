And you, what's the story here? He suddenly yelled out at the top of his lungs (the mourning lady had already left) - did something happen to you last night? Again, a scandal, causing a ruckus all over the street. Once more, a fight and drunkenness. You dream of being taken in! Because I've warned you ten times now that I wouldn't let you go out on the eleventh; but you again, always so like this! The piece of paper had slipped from Raskolnikov's hands, and he looked wildly at the sumptuously dressed lady who was being treated with such disrespect; but soon he grasped what it was all about, and straight away this whole story started to seem very amusing to him. He listened with great enjoyment, even laughing with such gusto that it seemed as if he couldn't stop. All his nerves were literally jumping. — Ilyich Petrovich!
