Look at them, scribblers! - and he cast a scornful glance at Raskolnikov. - There was a story the day before yesterday too at the tavern: ate lunch, but doesn't want to pay; "I'll write about it in satire, that's what I mean", supposedly. On the steamer the other week as well, a respectable official family with their wife and daughter were insulted vilely by this one. Yesterday from the confectionery, they kicked out some clerk over something. So these are the kind of people we have: writers, literati, students, announcers... tchah! Go get lost yourself! I'll just come visit you myself - then you watch out! Did you hear?
