If they ask, I might say so,” he thought as he approached the office house. The office was a quarter of a verst from him. It had just moved to a new apartment, in a new building, on the fourth floor. He had been to the old apartment once long ago, but very briefly. Upon entering through the gates, he saw a staircase to his right where a man with a book came down: “janitor, so there must be the office;” and he began to go up on the fourth floor at random. He did not want to ask anyone about anything. “I will enter, kneel down, and tell everything...” he thought as he entered the fourth floor. The staircase was narrow, steep, and all in filth. All kitchens of all apartments in all four floors opened onto this staircase and stood like that almost all day long. Therefore, there was a terrible stench.
