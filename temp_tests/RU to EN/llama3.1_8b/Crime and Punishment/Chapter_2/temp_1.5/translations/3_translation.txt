What is this? He immediately threw himself at it, to extract and throw it onto the table. After selecting everything, even turning the pockets inside out to make sure that nothing was left behind, he carried all this mess over to the corner. There, in the very corner, down below, in one place the peeling off of the wall paper had been torn: immediately he began to stuff it all into this hole under the paper: "entered! All gone and the wallet too!" - he thought happily, standing up and stupidly staring at the corner with an even wider gaping hole. Suddenly he froze from fear: "God forbid," he whispered in despair, "what is happening to me? Is this hidden? Are they hiding it like this?" Indeed, he hadn't counted on anything; he thought there would only be money, so he hadn't prepared a place for it in advance - "but now, what am I happy about?" he thought. "Are they hiding things like this?"
