The summons was so strong that he had already stood up to go, to fulfill it. "Shall I think about it for just a moment?" a thought went through his head. "No, let's not even think about it and be rid of it altogether!" But suddenly he stopped as if rooted to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was talking with ardor to Ilya Petrovich, and his words reached him:

"It cannot possibly happen that they will both be released! First of all, everything contradicts; let us judge: why would they invite a janitor if this is their affair? To inform on themselves, perhaps? Or as a trick? No, it would be too cunning! And finally, student Pestyakova was seen by the two janitors and a woman near the entrance at exactly the same time when he entered; he walked with three friends and parted from them just at the entrance and inquired about their living arrangements while still with his friends. Would someone inquire about their living arrangements if he went with such an intention?"
