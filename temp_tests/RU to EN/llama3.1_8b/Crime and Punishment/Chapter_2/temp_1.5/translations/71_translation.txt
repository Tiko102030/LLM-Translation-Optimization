It became extremely unpleasant and even frightening for him as a result of this examination.  "Nastasya, why are you so silent?" he finally spoke weakly, his voice barely above a whisper.  "It's blood," she replied softly, as if speaking to herself.  "Blood!... What blood?..." He murmured, paling and retreating from her towards the wall. Nastasya continued to silently stare at him.  "No one has ever beaten their mistress," she said again in a strict and resolute voice.
