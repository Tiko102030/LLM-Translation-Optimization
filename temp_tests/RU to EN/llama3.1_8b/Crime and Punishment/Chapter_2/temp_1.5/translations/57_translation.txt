— No need to, he said, I came... you see: I have no lessons whatever... I wanted to... anyway, I don't need any lessons at all.

— Do you know what? You're daydreaming! noted Razumikhin, observing him closely. — No, I'm not daydreaming... — Raskolnikov stood up from the couch. As he rose to Razumikhin, he didn't think about the fact that he would have to face someone with his head held high. Now, in one instant, having experienced it for himself, he realized that at this moment, nobody in the world would be less pleased than he to face anyone face-to-face. His anger rose up and almost choked him with resentment towards himself for just crossing Razumikhin's threshold. — Farewell! — he said suddenly and walked towards the door.
