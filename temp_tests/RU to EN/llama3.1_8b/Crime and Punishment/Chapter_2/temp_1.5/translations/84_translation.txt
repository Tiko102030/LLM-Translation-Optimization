I will bring it. Raskolnikov looked at everything with a deep astonishment and a dull meaningless fear. He had decided to be silent and wait: what would happen next? "It seems I am not in delirium," he thought, "it seems it's really...". 

Two minutes later Nastasya returned with soup and announced that now tea will also be served. Two spoons, two plates, and the entire set arrived: saltshaker, pepper shaker, mustard for beef and other things that had not been there in such an order for a long time. The tablecloth was clean. — "Not bad, Nastasya, if Praskovya Pavlovna sends two bottles of beer, we will drink to it." — "You go ahead," said Nastasya under her breath and went to carry out the command.
