He asked, pointing to the boy. "Ah, so he's awake!" she said. "We're awake," replied the foreman. Guessing that they were both awake, the housewife immediately hid behind the door, peeping out. She had always been shy and with difficulty carried on conversations and explanations; she was forty years old, fat and plump, black-eyed and black-haired, gentle from her thickness and slothfulness, and even quite clumsy herself. Excessively shy by necessity. "You... who are you?" he continued to question, addressing the foreman himself. But at that moment, the door creaked open again, and, leaning slightly to one side because of his height, Dunyasha's Razumikhin entered.
