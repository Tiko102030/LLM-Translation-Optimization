It seemed to him as if he had somehow cut himself off from everyone and everything with scissors in this moment. He came back to his senses by evening, so it took him only six hours. Where and how he made the journey back, he did not remember anything about that. Undressed and shaking all over like a beaten horse, he lay on the divan, put on his greatcoat and immediately fell asleep...

He woke up in the full darkness of night from an awful scream. God, what was this cry! He had never heard or seen such unnatural sounds, such a wail, howl, screeching, sobbing, beating and cursing before. He couldn't even imagine such savagery, such frenzy to himself. In horror he sat up on his bed, freezing every moment and suffering. But the fights, cries and curses only grew stronger and stronger. And suddenly, in the greatest amazement, he distinctly heard the voice of his landlady.
