— And you, such a one and such a one, he suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the mourning lady had already gone out), what happened to you there last night? Ah? Again shame, you cause a commotion on the whole street. Again fights and drunkenness. You dream of being restrained! For I've told you ten times already, for I've warned you already that I won't let you out at eleven! And again, again, such a one as you are! Even the paper fell from Raskolnikov's hands, and he looked wildly at the elegant lady who was being so shamelessly treated; but soon, however, he understood what it was all about, and immediately this whole story began to please him very much. He listened with pleasure, so much so that he wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh... All his nerves were jumping like that. — Ilya Petrovich!
