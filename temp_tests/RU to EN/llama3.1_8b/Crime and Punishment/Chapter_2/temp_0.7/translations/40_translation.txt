The call was so strong that he had already stood up to do it. "Shouldn't I think for a minute?" flashed through his head. "No, better not to think and get rid of it!" But suddenly he stopped as if rooted to the spot: Nikodim Fomich spoke with fervor to Il'ia Petrovich, and his words reached him:

— It's impossible, both will be released! First of all, everything contradicts; judge for yourself: why would they invite a janitor, if this was their affair? To denounce themselves, what?! Or out of cunning?! No, it would have been too cunning! And finally, the student Pestyakov was seen by both janitors and the housewife right at the gates in that very moment when he entered: he walked with three friends and parted from them at the gates and asked about living arrangements from the janitors while his friends were still there. Well, would they ask about living arrangements if they had such an intention?
