He didn't even have a match. No, it would be better to get out somewhere and get rid of everything. Yes! Get rid of everything! — he repeated, sitting down on the couch again, — right now, this minute, no delay!.. But instead his head bent over onto the pillow once more; another wave of unbearable chills swept over him; he pulled the greatcoat around himself once more. And for a long time, several hours, it kept happening to him in fits that "if only I could get up right now and go somewhere and get rid of everything so as to be rid of it all at last, quickly, quickly!" He tried several times to spring off the couch, wanted to stand up but couldn't. A strong knock on the door finally woke him up. — Open up, are you alive or not? And he's dozing away again! — Nastasya shouted, knocking on the door with her fist, — daytime days like a dog, dozing away for whole days!
