I read it once, another time and didn't understand. - What is this? - he asked the scribe. - This is a demand for payment of debts by you due to loan notes, collection. You must either pay all costs, including interest and other charges, or give a written response when you can pay, and at the same time an obligation not to leave the capital until payment and not sell or conceal your property. And the creditor has the right to sell your property, and act towards you according to the law. - Ah, I... I don't owe anyone! - This is none of our business. And we have received a past-due and lawfully protested loan note for 115 rubles, issued by you nine months ago to the widow Zarnitsyna, a collegiate assessor, who then passed it on to be paid to Counsellor Chibarov, and we invite you therefore to give a response. - But she's my landlady!
