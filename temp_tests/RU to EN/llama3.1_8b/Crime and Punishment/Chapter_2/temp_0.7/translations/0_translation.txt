He lay there for a very long time. Sometimes he seemed to wake up, and in those moments he noticed that it was already late at night, but getting up didn't come into his head. Finally, he realized that it was light like daytime. He was lying on the couch upside down, still dazed from the recent forgetfulness. The terrible, desperate screams from the street were heard sharply by him, which, however, he listened to every night under his window at three in the morning. It was they who woke him up now. "Ah! And here the drunken people come out of the drinking dens," he thought - "third hour" - and suddenly jumped up as if someone had pulled him off the couch. - What? Already the third already!" He sat on the couch, - and then everything came back to him!
