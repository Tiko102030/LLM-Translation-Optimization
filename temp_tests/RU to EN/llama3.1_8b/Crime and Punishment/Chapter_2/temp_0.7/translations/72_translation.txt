He looked at her, barely breathing. — I myself heard... I did not sleep... I sat, — he spoke even more timidly. — I listened for a long time... The assistant warden came in... Everyone rushed to the stairs from all apartments...

— Nobody came. And there's blood crying out inside you. It happens when she has no way out and will start to ripen with her liver, then it starts to appear... You'll be something, maybe? He didn't answer. Nastasya stood over him, looking at him intently and not leaving. — Give me a drink... Nastasyushka. She went down and came back up with water in a white clay cup two minutes later; but he already didn't remember what happened next. He only remembered taking one gulp of cold water and spilling some from the cup onto his chest.
