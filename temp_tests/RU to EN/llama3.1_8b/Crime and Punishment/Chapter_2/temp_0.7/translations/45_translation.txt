It was strange. — Ah, well, all right, — concluded Ilya Petrovich, — we are not keeping you. Raskolnikov left. He could still hear the lively conversation that began as he exited, with Nikodim Fomich's questioning voice standing out most... On the street, he came to himself completely. "A search, a search, it's a search now! — he repeated to himself in a hurry; — bandits! They suspect!" His old fear suddenly gripped him all over, from head to toe. II

"What if there was a search? What if they catch me right at their place?" But here is his room. Nothing and no one; no one has looked in.
