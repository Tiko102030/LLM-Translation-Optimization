"There they are, scribblers! - and he cast a scornful glance at Raskolnikov. - There was a story about him in the tavern three days ago too: he ate and refused to pay; 'I'll write you up in satire for that', he said. On the steamer as well, last week, a dignified family of a high-ranking official, his wife and daughter, he insulted with vile words. We just threw one out from the confectionery recently because of him. Look at them, these scribblers, writers, students, heralds... tch! Off you go! I'll come to visit you myself... then watch out! Did you hear? Louise Ivanovna launched into a half-hearted politeness and, bending down in all directions, managed to reach the doors; but she bumped her backside on one of the prominent officers standing in the doorway, with his face fresh and open and magnificent thick yellow sideburns."
