And he was opening the window onto the gutter and peering out of it like a little pig squealing; and this is shameful. And how can one peek out of the window into the street like a little pig squealing; and this is shameful. Fie upon him, fie upon him, fie! And Karl behind him tugged at his coat from the window, and then, I swear to you, sir captain, he ripped off my tie. And then he shouted that he had been fined fifteen whole marks for it, and I myself, sir captain, paid five whole marks for his tie. And this is a lowly guest, sir captain, and causing trouble everywhere! I'm saying so, sir captain, you'll get a big satire from me, because I can write about you in all the newspapers. — From writers, then? — Yes, sir captain, and what kind of a lowly guest are you, sir captain, when in a noble house... — Ah-ah-ah!
