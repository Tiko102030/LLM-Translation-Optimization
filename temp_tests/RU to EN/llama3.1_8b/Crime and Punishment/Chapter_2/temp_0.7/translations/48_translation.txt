But how won't the cases sink, and float away? And of course they will. Everyone will see it. As it is already, everyone looks around when meeting, glances, as if it's all about him. "Why not, or maybe I'm just imagining things," he thought. Finally, an idea came to his head that perhaps it would be better to go somewhere to the Neva? There are fewer people there, and one is less noticeable, and at any rate it's more convenient, and most importantly - further away from these places. And he was surprised: how could he have wandered for a whole half hour in despair and anxiety, in dangerous places, and not think of that earlier! And only because he had already decided on such an absurd thing while asleep, in a dream! He became extremely absent-minded and forgetful and knew this.
