It had become very unpleasant and even frightening for him from this consideration. — Nastasya, why don't you say anything? — he finally whispered in a weak voice. — This is blood, — she answered at last, quietly as if to herself speaking. — Blood!... What blood?!... — he muttered, paling and retreating towards the wall. Nastasya continued silently looking at him. — Nobody beats up the mistress, — she said again in a strict and resolute voice. He looked at her barely breathing.
