The air was stiflingly hot. Sweepers with notebooks under their arms, officials and various people of both sexes - visitors - were going up and down. The door to the very office was also wide open. He entered and stopped in the entrance hall. Everyone else stood there waiting for some men. Here too the heat was extraordinary and besides that, a fresh paint smell from newly painted rooms was hitting his nose to nausea. After waiting a bit, he thought to move a little further forward, into the next room. All the rooms were tiny and low-ceilinged. A terrible impatience kept pushing him further and further. No one noticed him.
