Nothing and no one; nobody had been looking in. Not even Nastasya had touched anything. But, Lord! How could he have left all these things here yesterday? He rushed into the corner, stuck his hand under the wallpaper and started pulling out things and stuffing them into pockets. There were eight items in total: two small boxes with earrings or something like that – he didn't look closely; then four small sapphire-covered cases. One chain was simply wrapped in newspaper. Something else was also wrapped in newspaper, it seemed to be an order... 

He put everything in different pockets, in the coat and in the remaining right pocket of his pants, trying to make it as unnoticeable as possible. He even took a wallet with him along with the things. Then he left the room, this time leaving it completely open.
