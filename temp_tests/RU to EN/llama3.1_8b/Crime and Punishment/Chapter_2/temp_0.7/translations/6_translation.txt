He thought to himself with triumph, deeply and joyfully taking a breath with his whole chest, "Just a fleeting weakness, delirium for a minute", and he tore out the entire lining from the left pocket of his pants. At this moment, a sunbeam illuminated his left boot: on the tip of the sock that was sticking out of the boot, it seemed as if signs appeared. He removed his boot: "really signs! The whole tip of the sock is soaked in blood"; probably he carelessly stepped into that puddle then... "But what to do with this now? Where to get rid of this sock, the braid, the pocket?"

He gathered everything together in his hand and stood in the middle of the room. "Into the fireplace? But first, they will start rummaging through it there. Burn it? And how can you burn it? There are no matches either."
