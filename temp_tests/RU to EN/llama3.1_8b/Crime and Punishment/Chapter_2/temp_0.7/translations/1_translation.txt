"He's been here for three hours!" He sat on the sofa, and suddenly remembered everything! In one instant he recalled everything! At first he thought he was losing his mind. A terrible chill gripped him; but the chill was also from the fever that had already started in his sleep a long time ago. Suddenly such a shiver hit him that his teeth almost fell out, and it all just crawled through his body. He opened the door and began to listen: everything in the house was completely asleep. With amazement he looked around at himself and everything around him in the room, not understanding: how could he have done this yesterday, entering, not locking the doors on a latch and throwing himself onto the sofa, not only undressing, but even wearing a hat: it fell off and lay right there on the floor near the pillow. "If someone had come in, what would they think? That I'm drunk, but..." He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began to look over himself, from head to toe, his whole outfit: were there any signs?"
