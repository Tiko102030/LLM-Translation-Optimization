Then he tried to get up from the place, wanted to run away, but always someone would stop him by force, and he would fall into powerlessness and forgetfulness again. Finally, he came fully back to himself. This happened in the morning at ten o'clock. At this hour of the morning, on clear days, the sun always passed through a long strip along his right wall and lit up the corner near the door. By his bedside stood Nastasya and another person who was very curiously examining him and completely unknown to him. This was a young man in a caftan with a beard, and from appearance he looked like an artisan. The landlady was looking out from half-opened door. Raskolnikov raised himself up. "Who is this, Nastasya?" he asked, pointing at the young man.
