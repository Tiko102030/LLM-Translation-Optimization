"Oh, blast it all! — he thought suddenly in an unrelenting fit of spite. — Well, it's started, so let's get on with it, I don't care about her or this new life! How foolish, Lord have mercy! And how many times I've lied and sycophanted today! How disgusting I was fawning over that scoundrel Ilya Petrovich yesterday! But what's the point of even thinking about all this? I'm not bothered by any of them, nor by my own fawning and flattery. Not at all, not at all..."

He suddenly stopped; a completely unexpected and extremely simple question had suddenly struck him dumb and filled him with bitter amazement:

"If it was indeed done consciously, not carelessly, if you really did have a definite and firm goal, then how on earth haven't you even looked in your wallet yet and don't know what you got, for which you've endured all the suffering and deliberately set out on such a vile, foul, low deed?"
