I will bring it. Raskolnikov looked at everything with deep amazement and a dull meaningless fear. He decided to remain silent and wait: what would happen next? "It seems I'm not in delirium," he thought, "it seems this is really..."

After two minutes Nastasya turned back with soup and announced that now tea would also be served. To the soup came two spoons, two plates, and the entire tableware: saltshaker, pepper mill, mustard for beef and other things that had not been seen in such order for a long time before. The tablecloth was clean. — "Not bad, Nastasya, if Praskovya Pavlovna would commandeer two bottles of beer, we'll drink it." — "Oh no you won't," the easterner said and muttered to herself, going to carry out the order.
