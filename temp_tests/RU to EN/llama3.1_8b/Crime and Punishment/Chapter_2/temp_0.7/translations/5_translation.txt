"Pieces of torn lace will not arouse any suspicions at all; it's like that, it's like that!" he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with a tense attention began to look around again, on the floor and everywhere, had he forgotten anything else? The certainty that everything, even memory, even simple consideration left him, began to torment him intolerably. "Is it really starting, is this really the execution coming?" He cried out again, lost as if. Indeed, the frayed braid he cut from his pants was lying on the floor, in the middle of the room, so that anyone could see it at first glance! "What's happening to me!" he shouted again, utterly bewildered. Then a strange idea came to him: what if all his clothes are covered with blood, what if there are many stains, but he just can't see them, can't notice them because his thinking is weak, fragmented... his mind is clouded... Suddenly he remembered that the wallet was also bloody. "Ha! So it means there must be blood in the pocket too, because I put a wet wallet in there at the time!" He immediately turned out the pocket and - yes, there were indeed stains on the lining of the pocket. "So it means I haven't completely lost my mind, so there is still reason and memory if I've even remembered myself and figured it out! - he thought with triumph, deeply and joyfully breathing in his whole chest - just a weak attack of fever, delirium for one minute," and he tore off the entire lining from the left pocket of his pants.
