Was he really hiding it? The truth has left me! In exhaustion, he sat down on the sofa, and instantly an unbearable shiver shook him again. Mechanically, he pulled out from under the stool the student's winter coat that was lying there, warm but already almost in tatters, covered himself with it, and sleep and delirium caught him once more. He lost consciousness. Not more than a minute later, he jumped up again and at once, in a frenzy, rushed to his clothes again. "How could I fall asleep again when nothing has been done! It's so, it's so: the noose is still under my armpit! I forgot, forgot such a thing! Such evidence!" He pulled off the noose and quickly began to tear it into pieces, shoving them under the pillow in his bed. "The pieces of torn cloth will not arouse any suspicion at all; it seems that way, it seems that way," he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with a strained attention began to look around again, on the floor and everywhere, had he forgotten something else?
