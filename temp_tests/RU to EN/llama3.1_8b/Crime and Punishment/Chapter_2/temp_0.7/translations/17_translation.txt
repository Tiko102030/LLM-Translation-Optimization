If asked, I might say," he thought, approaching the office. The office was a quarter of a verst from him. It had just moved to a new apartment, in a new house, on the fourth floor. He used to pass by his old apartment occasionally, but very long ago. Entering through the gates, he saw a staircase to the right, down which a man with a book came: "a janitor, so it means; that's where the office is," and he began to go up on the fourth floor at random. He did not want to ask anyone anything. "I'll enter, fall on my knees and tell everything..." he thought, entering the fourth floor. The staircase was narrow, steep and full of filth. All kitchens from all apartments in all four floors opened onto this staircase and stood there almost all day. That's why there was a terrible stench.
