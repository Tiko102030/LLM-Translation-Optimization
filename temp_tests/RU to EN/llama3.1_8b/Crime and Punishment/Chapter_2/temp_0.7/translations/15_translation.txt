He thought to himself suddenly: "I'll put it on! It will only get dirty even more, and the tracks will disappear." But just as he was about to put it on, he instantly pulled it off with a shudder of disgust and horror. He pulled it off, but then realizing there wasn't another one, he picked it up again and put it on – and laughed again. "Everything is conditional, everything is relative, all this are only forms," he thought briefly, just a corner of his mind, while himself shaking with the whole body, "look, I've put it on! Look, I ended up putting it on!" The laughter was immediately replaced by despair. "No, not within my power..." – he thought to himself. His legs were trembling. "From fear," he muttered to himself. His head was spinning and aching from the heat.
