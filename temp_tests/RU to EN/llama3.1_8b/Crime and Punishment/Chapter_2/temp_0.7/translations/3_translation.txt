What is this? He immediately jumped up and began to take everything out and throw it onto the table. Having taken all of it, even turning over the pockets to make sure nothing was left behind, he carried the whole pile to the corner. There, in the very corner, at the bottom, in one place, the faded wallpaper that had peeled off from the wall: immediately he started shoving everything into this hole, under a sheet of paper: "It's all gone! All is clear and even the wallet too!" - he thought joyfully, standing up and blankly staring into the corner at the increasingly gaping hole. Suddenly he jerked with horror: "Good God," he whispered in despair, "what am I doing? Is it really hidden? Do they hide things like this?"
