The house is Noah's Ark – noted the clerk, who had been listening from his seat. "It's quite clear, it's quite clear!" Nikodim Fomich exclaimed warmly. "No, it's all very unclear," Ilya Petrovich contradicted him. Raskolnikov raised his hat and started walking towards the doors, but he didn't make it... When he came to, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, being supported by someone to his right, while another person stood to his left with a yellow cup filled with yellow water, and Nikodim Fomich was standing in front of him staring intently at him; he got up from the chair. "What's wrong, are you ill?" Nikodim Fomich asked rather sharply. "They didn't even sign properly – they barely moved the pen," noted the clerk, sitting down and starting to work with papers again. "And how long have you been ill?" Ilya Petrovich yelled from his seat, also rummaging through papers.
