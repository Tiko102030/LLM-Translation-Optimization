What have you got, book or what? — Book, yes, here's yours. — Give it to me. Well, Razumikhin, get up. I'll hold you; tap on him Rascolnikov, take the pen, because, brother, money is now dearer than honey. — Don't bother, said Raskolnikov, moving the pen away. — What's this? — No need to sign. — Fie upon it, devil take it, how can we do without a signature? — No need... for the money!
