Nastasya couldn't have seen anything from afar, thank God!" Then he nervously unfolded the summons and began to read; he read for a long time before finally understanding. This was an ordinary summons from the quarter to appear today at half past ten in the office of the quarter's overseer. "When has this ever happened? I don't have anything to do with the police myself! And why now, today? - he thought in tormenting perplexity. - Lord, please hurry it up!" He had thrown himself on his knees to pray, but even laughed at himself - not over the prayer, but over himself. He hurriedly began to get dressed. "I'll be ruined anyway! I'll just put on my socks!"
