"And at what time are you coming, sir," the officer shouted, growing increasingly and more and more offended by something unknown - "you were told to come at nine o'clock, and now it's already twelve!" "They brought it to me just a quarter of an hour ago," Rascolnikov replied loudly, looking over his shoulder, suddenly and unexpectedly irritated and even finding some pleasure in this.
