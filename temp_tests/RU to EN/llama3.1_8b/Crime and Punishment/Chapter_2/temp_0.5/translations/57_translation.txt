"Do you know what? You're babbling!" observed Razumikhin, who had been watching him closely. "No, I'm not babbling," - Raskolnikov stood up from the divan. As he rose to meet Razumikhin, he didn't think about the fact that he would have to face him, as it were, face to face with himself. Now, in a single moment, he realized, on his own experience, that at this very moment he was least disposed to confront anyone in the entire world face to face. All his bitterness rose up in him. He almost choked with rage against himself for having just crossed Razumikhin's threshold. - "Goodbye!" he said suddenly and went towards the door."
