"Oh, for goodness' sake! — he thought suddenly in a fit of unquenchable spite. — Well, it's started, so let it start! To hell with her and the new life! How idiotic is this... And how much I've been pouring out and flattering today! How disgusting I was crawling and flirting yesterday with that scoundrel Ilya Petrovich! But what does it matter? It doesn't matter to me at all, nor what I was crawling and flirting for. Not at all! Not at all!

He suddenly stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question struck him like a thunderbolt and bitterly amazed him:

"If indeed everything was done consciously, not carelessly, if you really had a definite and firm goal, then how did you manage to look into the wallet even now and don't know what you've got, because of which you're suffering so much and went deliberately to such a vile, disgusting, low thing?"
