What is this? He quickly threw everything out and onto the table. Having picked up all of it, even rummaging through pockets to make sure nothing was left behind, he transferred the whole pile to the corner. There, in the very corner, at the bottom, in one place, the old wallpaper had been torn away from the wall: immediately he began shoving everything into this hole under a sheet of paper: "in! All out of sight and the wallet too!" - he thought joyfully, standing up and staring blankly into the corner, where the hole was now even larger. Suddenly he jumped with horror: "Good God, - he whispered in despair, - what's happened to me? Is it really hidden? Do they hide things like this?"
