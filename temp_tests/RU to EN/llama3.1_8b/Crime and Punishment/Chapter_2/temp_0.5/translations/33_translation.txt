There they are, writers! - and he cast a scornful glance at Raskolnikov. - There was a story about him in the tavern just three days ago: he ate his fill but refused to pay; "I'll write you into satire for that," he said. On the steamer too, last week, a respectable family of a high-ranking official, his wife and daughter, were insulted with vile words. They kicked one out of a confectionery recently. That's what writers are like, literati, students, heralds... pshaw! You go ahead! I'll come to you myself... then watch out! Did you hear? Louise Ivanovna set off with a benevolent smile to curtsy all around and, curtsying, made her way to the door; but in the doorway she stumbled on her backside into one of the prominent officers, with an open fresh face and magnificent thick fair mustaches.
