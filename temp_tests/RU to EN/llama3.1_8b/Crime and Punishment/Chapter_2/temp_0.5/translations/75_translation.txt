He pointed to the boy and asked, "Ah, he's awake!" She said, "We're awake," replied the foreman. Guessing that he was awake, the house owner, who had been peeking through the door, immediately shut it and hid herself. She was always shy and endured conversations and explanations with difficulty; she was forty years old, fat and plump, black-eyed and black-haired, kind due to her obesity and laziness; and even quite unattractive herself. Excessively shy beyond necessity. — You... who are you? — he continued questioning, addressing the foreman himself. But at this moment the door creaked open again, and slightly leaning forward because he was tall, Razumikhin entered.
