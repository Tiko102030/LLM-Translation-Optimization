He looked at her, barely breathing. — I myself heard... I didn't sleep... I sat, he said in a quieter tone. — I listened for a long time... The assistant warden came by... Everyone gathered on the staircase from all the apartments...

— No one came. And there's blood crying out inside you. It's when she has no way out and will start to burn with her liver, then it starts to seem like... You'll be someone, or something? He didn't respond. Nastasya stood over him, staring intently at him and not leaving. — Give me a drink... Nastasyushka. She went downstairs and came back two minutes later with water in a white clay cup; but he already didn't remember what happened next. He only remembered taking one gulp of cold water and spilling it from the cup onto his chest.
