Nothing and no one; no one had been snooping around. Not even Nastasya had touched anything. But, Lord! How could he have left all these things here yesterday? He rushed to the corner, shoved his hand under the wallpaper and started pulling out things and loading them into his pockets. There were eight items in total: two small boxes with earrings or something similar – he didn't take a good look; then four small leather cases. A chain was simply wrapped in newspaper. Something else was wrapped in newspaper too, maybe an order... He put everything into different pockets, into the coat and into the remaining right pocket of his pants, trying to make it less noticeable. He even took the wallet along with the things. Then he left the room, this time leaving it completely open.
