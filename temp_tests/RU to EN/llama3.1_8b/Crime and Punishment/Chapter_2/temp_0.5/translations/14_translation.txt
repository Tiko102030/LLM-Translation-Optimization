Nastasya couldn't have noticed anything anyway, thank God! Then he nervously unfolded the summons and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood. It was an ordinary summons from the neighborhood to appear today at half past ten at the office of the quarter's overseer. "When could this be? I don't have any dealings with the police myself! And why just today?" he thought in anguished bewilderment. "God, let it be over quickly!" He had thrown himself on his knees to pray, but even laughed himself - not at the prayer, but at himself. He hastily began to get dressed. "I'll lose everything anyway! Put on a sock!"
