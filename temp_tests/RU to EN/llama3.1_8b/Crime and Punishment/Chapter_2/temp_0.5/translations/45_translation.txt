It was strange. — Well, good then, concluded Ilya Petrovich, we're not detaining you. Raskolnikov left. He could still hear the lively conversation starting outside, in which Nikodim Fyodorovich's interrogative voice stood out most... On the street he came to himself completely. "Search, search, it's a search now! - he repeated to himself, hurrying; - robbers! They suspect!" A familiar fear again gripped him from head to toe. II

"What if there was really a search? What if I just happen to catch them at their own place?"
