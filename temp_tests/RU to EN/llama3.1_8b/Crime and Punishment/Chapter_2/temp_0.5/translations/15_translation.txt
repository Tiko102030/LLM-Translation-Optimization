He thought to himself suddenly: "Put it on! It will get even dirtier and the marks will disappear." But as soon as he put it on, he immediately took it off with disgust and horror. He took it off, but realizing there was no other option, he picked it up and put it back on again - and laughed again. "It's all conditional, everything is relative, everything is just forms," he thought briefly, only a corner of his mind, while trembling all over his body - after all, he had put it on! After all, that was the result! His laughter was immediately replaced by despair. "No, not within my power..." he thought to himself. His legs were shaking. "From fear," he muttered to himself. His head spun and hurt from the heat.
