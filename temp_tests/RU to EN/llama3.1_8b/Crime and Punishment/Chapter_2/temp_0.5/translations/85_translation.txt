Meanwhile Razumikhin sat down next to him on the couch, clumsily like a bear, wrapped his left hand around his head, despite the fact that he himself could have gotten up, and with his right hand brought a spoonful of soup to his mouth, blowing on it several times beforehand so as not to burn himself. But the soup was only just warm. Raskolnikov swallowed one spoonful greedily, then another, a third. But after bringing several more spoons, Razumikhin suddenly stopped and announced that as for further action, it would be necessary to consult with Zosima. Nastasya entered, carrying two bottles of beer. "Do you want tea? — I want it. — Hurry up with the tea, Nastasya, because as for tea, apparently we can do without a doctor."
