It seemed to him that he had somehow cut himself off from everyone and everything with scissors in this moment. He arrived home already by evening, so it took him only six hours. Where and how he went back, he didn't remember anything. After undressing and trembling all over like a horse that's been whipped, he lay down on the sofa, put on his greatcoat and immediately fell asleep... He woke up in the full darkness from an awful scream. God, what was that scream! Such unnatural sounds, such howling, wailing, screeching, sobbing, beating and cursing he had never heard or seen before. He couldn't even imagine such savagery, such frenzy to himself. In horror, he sat up on his bed, freezing in terror every moment. But the fights, screams and curses only grew louder and louder. And then, to his greatest amazement, he suddenly caught a glimpse of his landlady's voice.
