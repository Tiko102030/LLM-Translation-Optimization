If they ask me, I might tell them, he thought as he approached the office. The office was a quarter of a verst from him. It had just moved to a new apartment, in a new house, on the fourth floor. He had been there briefly once before, but very long ago. As he entered through the gates, he saw a staircase to his right, down which a man with a book in his hands came out: "a janitor, so it's probably here", and he started going up randomly. He didn't want to ask anyone anything about anything. "I'll go in, get on my knees, and tell everything...", he thought as he entered the fourth floor. The staircase was narrow, steep, and covered with filth. All the kitchens of all apartments on all four floors opened onto this staircase and stood like that almost all day. That's why it smelled awful.
