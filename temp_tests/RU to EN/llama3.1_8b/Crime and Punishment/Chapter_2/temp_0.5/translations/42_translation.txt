The house is Noah's Ark - observed the clerk, who was listening from his seat. - Clearly, clearly! - Nikodim Fomich repeated heatedly. - No, it's very unclear - Ilya Petrovich confirmed. Raskolnikov raised his hat and went towards the doors, but did not reach them...

When he awoke, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, being supported by someone to his right, with another person standing to his left holding a yellow cup filled with yellow water, and Nikodim Fomich standing before him staring at him intensely; he got up from the chair. - What is this, are you ill? - Nikodim Fomich asked rather sharply. - They signed as if they were barely moving their pens - the clerk noted, sitting down in his seat again and resuming work on his papers. - And how long have you been ill? - Ilya Petrovich shouted from his seat, also rummaging through his papers.
