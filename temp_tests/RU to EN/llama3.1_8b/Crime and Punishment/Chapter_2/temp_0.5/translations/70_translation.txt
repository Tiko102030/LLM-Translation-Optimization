Looking at him carefully and noticing that he wasn't sleeping, she put a candle on the table and began to lay out what had been brought: bread, salt, a plate, a spoon. — You probably didn't eat from yesterday. You've been walking around all day, and you're getting feverish now. — Nastasya... for what did they beat up the mistress? She looked at him intently. — Who beat up the mistress? — Just now... half an hour ago, Ilya Petrovich, the assistant warden, on the stairs... Why did he hit her like that and why did he come? Nastasya silently and frowningly examined him and looked for a long time. He felt very uncomfortable from this examination, even frightened.
