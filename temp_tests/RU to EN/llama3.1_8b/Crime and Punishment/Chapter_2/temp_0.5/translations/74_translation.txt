Then he tried to get up from the place, wanted to run away, but always someone would stop him by force, and he would fall into weakness and unconsciousness again. Finally, he came completely to himself. This happened in the morning at ten o'clock. At that hour of the morning, on clear days, the sun always passed through a long strip along his right wall and lit up the corner near the door. By his bedside stood Nastasya and another person who was very curiously looking at him and completely unknown to him. This was a young man in a kaftan with a beard, and he looked like an artisan from afar. The landlady peered out of the half-open door. Raskolnikov raised himself up. - Who is this, Nastasya? - he asked, pointing to the young man.
