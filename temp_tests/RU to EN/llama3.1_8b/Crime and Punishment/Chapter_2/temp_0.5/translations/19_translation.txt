No one noticed him. In the second room sat and wrote some clerks, dressed slightly better than he was, looking like some strange kind of people. He approached one of them. — What's it to you? He showed a summons from the office. — Are you a student? — asked that one, glancing at the summons. — Yes, former student. The clerk looked him over, however without any curiosity. This was some especially disheveled person with an immobile idea in his gaze.
