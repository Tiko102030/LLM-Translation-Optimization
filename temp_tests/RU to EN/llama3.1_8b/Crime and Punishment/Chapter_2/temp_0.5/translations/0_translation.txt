He lay there for a very long time. It sometimes happened that he seemed to wake up and in those moments noticed that it was already late at night, but getting up did not come to his mind. Finally, he realized that it was already light as during the day. He lay on the sofa upside down, still stunned from recent forgetfulness. The terrible, desperate screams from the street reached him sharply, which, however, he listened to every night under his window at 3 am. They were what woke him up now. "Ah! And here they're already coming out of the drinking dens drunk," he thought - "third hour" - and suddenly jumped up as if someone had pulled him off the sofa. - What! Already three hours!" He sat on the couch, and then everything came back to him!
