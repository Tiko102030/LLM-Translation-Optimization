— And you, such-and-such and so-and-so, he suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the mournful lady had already left), what happened to you last night? Ah? Again a scandal, producing a debauchery on the whole street. Again a fight and drunkenness. You dream of being put in a straitjacket! For I've told you ten times already, I've warned you, that I won't let you out at eleven! And you again, again, such-and-such and so-and-so! Even a piece of paper fell from Raskolnikov's hands, and he looked wildly at the lush lady, who was being treated with such disrespect; but soon, however, he realized what was going on, and this whole story began to please him very much. He listened with pleasure, even so that he wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh... All his nerves were jumping like that. — Ilya Petrovich!
