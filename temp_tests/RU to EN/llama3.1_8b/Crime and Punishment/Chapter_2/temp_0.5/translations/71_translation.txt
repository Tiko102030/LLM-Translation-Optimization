It became extremely unpleasant for him from this consideration, even frightening. — Nastasya, why are you silent? — he finally said weakly in a low voice. — It's blood, — she replied finally, quietly as if speaking to herself. — Blood! What blood?! — he muttered, paling and retreating towards the wall. Nastasya continued to silently look at him. — No one beats the mistress, — she said again in a strict and resolute voice. He looked at her, hardly breathing.
