He thought triumphantly, deeply and joyfully taking a breath with his whole chest, - just a weak feverish delirium for a minute', - and he pulled out the entire lining from the left pocket of his pants. At this moment, a sunbeam lit up his left boot: on the toe that was sticking out of the boot, as if signs appeared. He kicked off the boot: 'really signs! The whole tip of the toe is soaked in blood'; probably he stepped into that puddle carelessly then... 'But what to do with this now? Where to put this sock, balm, pocket?'
