The third hour already! He sat on the couch, and then suddenly remembered everything in one moment! At first he thought he was going out of his mind. A terrifying chill gripped him; but the chill was also from the fever that had started with him during sleep a long time ago. Now it struck such a shiver that his teeth almost jumped out and everything inside him felt numb. He opened the door and began to listen: in the house everyone was completely asleep. With amazement he looked around at himself and all around the room and didn't understand: how could he have yesterday, entering, not locked the doors on a hook and thrown himself onto the couch, not only not undressing but even wearing a hat: it had slipped off and lay right there on the floor next to the pillow. "If someone came in, what would they think? That I'm drunk, but..." He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began to look over himself from head to toe, all his clothes: are there any signs?
