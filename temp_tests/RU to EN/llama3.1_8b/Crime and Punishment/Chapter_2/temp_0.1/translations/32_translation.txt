— Mr. Illya Petrovich! — the clerk said again with significance. The lieutenant quickly glanced at him; the clerk slightly nodded his head.

...So here it is, most esteemed Lavisia Ivanovna, my last word, and this will be for the last time, — continued the lieutenant. — If there's even one more scandal in your noble house, I'll make you a cudgeon, as they say in high style.
