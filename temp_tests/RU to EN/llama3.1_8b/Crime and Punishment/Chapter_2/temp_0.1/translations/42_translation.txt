The house is Noah's Ark, the clerk observed from his seat. - It's clear, it's clear! - Nikodim Fomich repeated heatedly. - No, it's very unclear, - Ilya Petrovich confirmed. Raskolnikov raised his hat and walked towards the doors, but he didn't reach them...

When he came to, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, being supported by someone on his right, another person standing on his left with a yellow cup filled with yellow water, and Nikodim Fomich standing in front of him looking at him intently; he stood up from the chair. - What's this, are you ill? - Nikodim Fomich asked quite sharply. - They signed it just as they barely moved their pen, - the clerk noted, sitting down on his seat and resuming work with papers. - And how long have you been ill? - Ilya Petrovich shouted from his place, also rummaging through papers.
