He thought to himself suddenly: "I'll put on my socks! They will get even more dusty and the tracks will disappear." But as soon as he put them on, he immediately took them off with disgust and horror. He took them off, but realizing there were no others, he picked them up again and put them back on – and laughed again. "Everything is conditional, everything is relative, it's all just forms," he thought briefly, with only a corner of his mind, while trembling all over his body – after all, he had put them on! After all, that was the end result! His laughter was immediately replaced by despair. "No, not within my power..." he thought to himself. His legs were shaking. "From fear," he muttered to himself. His head was spinning and aching from the heat.
