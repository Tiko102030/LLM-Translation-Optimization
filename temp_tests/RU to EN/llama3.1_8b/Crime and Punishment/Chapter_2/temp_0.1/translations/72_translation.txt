He looked at her barely breathing. — I heard it myself... I didn't sleep... I sat, he said more timidly. — I listened for a long time... The assistant warden came by... Everyone gathered on the stairs from all apartments...

— No one came. And there's blood crying in you. It happens when she has no way out and will start to be cooked with the liver, then it starts to appear... You'll become something, right? He didn't answer. Nastasya stood over him, looking at him intently and not leaving. — Give me a drink... Nastasya. She went down and came back up two minutes later with water in a white clay cup; but he already didn't remember what happened next. He only remembered taking one sip of cold water and spilling it from the cup on his chest.
