His entire room was so small that you could hook a hook without getting up from bed. And indeed: the janitor and Nastasya are standing there. Nastasya somehow strangely glanced at him over her shoulder. He looked at the janitor with an impudent and desperate expression. The janitor silently handed him a gray, folded in half piece of paper sealed with bottle wax. — It's a summons from the office, he said, handing over the paper. — From which office?... — To the police, that is, they're calling to the office. We know what office it is. — To the police!
