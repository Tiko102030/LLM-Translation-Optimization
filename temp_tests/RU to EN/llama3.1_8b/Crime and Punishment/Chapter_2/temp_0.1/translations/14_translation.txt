It was an ordinary summons from the district to appear today at half past ten at the office of the district supervisor. "When could this be? I don't have any dealings with the police myself! And why just today?" he thought in tormenting bewilderment. "God, please hurry!" He had thrown himself on his knees to pray, but even he laughed at himself - not at the prayer, but at himself. He hastily began to get dressed. "I'll be lost anyway! Put on a sock!"
