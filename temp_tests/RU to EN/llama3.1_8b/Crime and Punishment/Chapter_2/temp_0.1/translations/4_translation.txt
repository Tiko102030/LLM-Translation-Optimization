— Do they hide it like that? Truly, reason has left me! In exhaustion he sat down on the sofa, and at once an unbearable chill shook him again. Mechanically he pulled over to himself the winter coat lying nearby on a chair, which had been his student's coat, warm but already almost in tatters, covered himself with it, and sleep and delirium seized him once more. He dozed off. Not more than five minutes later he leapt up again and at once, in a frenzy, rushed to his clothes once more. "How could I fall asleep again when nothing has been done! It's true, it's true: the noose is still under my arm! I forgot, forgot such a thing! Such evidence!" He pulled off the noose and quickly began to tear it into pieces, stuffing them under the pillow in his clothes. "Torn strips of fabric will not arouse any suspicion; it seems so, it seems so!" he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with an attention that was almost painful began to search around again, on the floor and everywhere, if he hadn't forgotten anything else?
