"Rips of torn hem will not arouse any suspicions; it seems so, it seems so!" he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with a painful attention began to search around again, on the floor and everywhere, if he had forgotten something else? The certainty that everything, even memory, even simple reasoning leaves him, was starting to torment him unbearably. "What, is it really starting, is this really the execution coming?" He exclaimed, pointing at the oblongs of braid that he cut off from his pants, which were lying on the floor in the middle of the room, so that anyone could see them! "What's going on with me!" he cried out again like a lost person. Then a strange idea came to him: maybe all his clothes are bloody, maybe there are many stains, but he just doesn't see them, doesn't notice them because his reasoning has weakened, is fragmented... his mind is clouded... Suddenly he remembered that the wallet was also bloody. "Ah! So it means that there must be blood in the pocket too, because I put a wet wallet in the pocket then!" He turned out the pocket at once, and - yes, there are stains on the lining of the pocket! "So it means I haven't lost my mind completely, so there is still reasoning and memory, since I remembered myself and figured it out!" he thought with triumph, taking a deep breath all over his chest, — just a weak moment of feverishness, a delirium for a minute," and he ripped off the entire lining from the left pocket of his pants.
