"Oh, what the devil is all this! — he thought suddenly in a fit of boundless malice. — Well, it's started now, so let it start, to hell with her and her new life! How silly, Lord, how silly... And how much I've been flattering and fawning on them today! How disgusting I was and played up to that scoundrel Ilya Petrovich yesterday! But what's the point of all this? I don't care about any of them or what I did. Not at all! Not at all...

He suddenly stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question knocked him off his feet and bitterly surprised him:

"If indeed everything was done consciously, not stupidly, if you really had a definite and firm goal, then how on earth have you not even glanced into your wallet and don't know what you got, because of which you've taken all the torments and deliberately went to such a vile, disgusting, low deed?"
