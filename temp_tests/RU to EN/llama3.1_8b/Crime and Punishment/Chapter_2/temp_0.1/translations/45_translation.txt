It was strange. — Well, all right, concluded Ilya Petrovich, we're not detaining you. Raskolnikov left. He could still hear the lively conversation that began as he exited, in which Nikodim Fyodorovich's questioning voice stood out most... On the street, he came to himself completely. "Search, search, now it's a search! — he repeated to himself, hurrying along; — bandits! they suspect!" A fresh wave of fear enveloped him from head to toe once again. II

"What if there was a search? What if I just happen to catch them at home?" But here is his room. Nothing and no one; no one had looked in.
