— And you, such-and-such and so-and-so, he suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the mournful lady had already left), what happened to you last night? Ah? Again a scandal, producing a commotion on the whole street. Again fights and drunkenness. You dream of being put in a straitjacket! For I've already told you, for I've already warned you ten times that I won't let you out at eleven! And again, again, such-and-such is so-and-so! The paper fell from Razumikhin's hands and he looked wildly at the plump lady who was being treated with such disrespect; but soon, however, he realized what was going on, and this whole story began to please him very much. He listened with pleasure, even wanting to laugh, laugh, laugh... All his nerves were jumping like that. — Ilya Petrovich!
