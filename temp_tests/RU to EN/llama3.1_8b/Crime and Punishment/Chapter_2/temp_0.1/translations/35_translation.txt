Poverty is not a vice, my friend, but indeed what can you do! It's well known that he couldn't bear an insult. You probably took offense at him and didn't hold back yourself, continued Nikodim Fomich, addressing Raskolnikov with courtesy - but it's pointless: the most non-blasphemous, I'll tell you, person, but powder, powder! He blew up, boiled over, burned out - and that's it! And everything passed! And as a result, only gold in his heart! They even nicknamed him "lieutenant-powder"...

— And what an army was there! — exclaimed Ilia Petrovich, very pleased that they had tickled him so pleasantly, but still half-awake. Raskolnikov suddenly wanted to say something unusually pleasant to all of them. — Forgive me, Captain, he began quite freely, addressing Nikodim Fomich suddenly - take an interest in my situation... I'm even ready to ask them for forgiveness if I made any mistakes on my part.
