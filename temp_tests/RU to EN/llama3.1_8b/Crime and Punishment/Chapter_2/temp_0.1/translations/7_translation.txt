There weren't even any matches. No, it's better to go somewhere and throw everything away. Yes! Better to get rid of it all! — he repeated, sitting down on the couch again, — right now, this very minute, no delay!.. But instead of that his head leaned back onto the pillow once more; an unbearable chill gripped him once more; he wrapped himself in his greatcoat once more. And for a long time, several hours, it still seemed to him in fits and starts that "if only I could go somewhere right now, without putting it off, and throw everything away so as to get rid of it all, quickly, quickly!" He tried to get up from the couch several times, wanted to stand up, but he couldn't. A strong knock at the door finally woke him up. — Open up, are you alive or not? And what's this, sleeping like a dog! — Nastasya shouted, knocking on the door with her fist, — whole daytime days, like a beast, sleeping all day!
