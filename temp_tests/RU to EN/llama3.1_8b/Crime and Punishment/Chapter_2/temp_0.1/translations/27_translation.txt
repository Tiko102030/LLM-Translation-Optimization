I've read it once or twice and didn't understand it. — What is this? — he asked the clerk. — This is a demand for repayment of the loan with interest, collection. You must either pay all expenses, including penalties and others, or give a written response when you can pay, and at the same time an obligation not to leave the capital until payment and not to sell or hide your property. And the creditor is free to sell your property and act according to the law with you. — I... owe no one anything! — This is none of our business. We have received a collection notice for a delayed and legally protested loan in the amount of 115 rubles, issued by you nine months ago to the widow, Collegiate Assessor Zarnitsyna, which has since been transferred to payment to Counsellor of State Chubarov, and we invite you therefore to respond. — But she's my landlady!
