But how will the cases not sink, but float? And of course it's like that. Everyone will see. And anyway, they're already all looking at him like this when meeting, glancing around as if their only concern is him. "Why so, or maybe I'm just imagining," he thought. Finally, an idea came to his head: why not go somewhere along the Neva? There are fewer people there, it's less noticeable, and in any case more convenient, and most importantly - further from these places. And he was surprised suddenly: how did he spend whole half-hours wandering in sorrow and anxiety, and in dangerous places, but this he couldn't have thought of before! And that's why he only wasted whole half-hours on a senseless business, which had already been decided upon in his sleep, in delirium. He became extremely absent-minded and forgetful and knew it.
