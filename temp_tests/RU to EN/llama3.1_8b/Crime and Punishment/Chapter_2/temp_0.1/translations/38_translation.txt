And where did these feelings come from? On the other hand, now, if suddenly the room were filled not with quarterlies, but his closest friends, then even then, it seems, he would have had no human word for them, so suddenly empty was his heart. A dismal feeling of painful and endless isolation and alienation suddenly became conscious to his soul. Not the baseness of his heart's outpourings before Ilya Petrovich, not the poruchik's triumph over him turned his heart around so suddenly. Oh, what a matter now for his own baseness, for all these ambitions, poruchiks, Germans, demands, offices and so on! If he were even sentenced to be burned at this moment, then he would not have stirred, and it is unlikely that he would have listened to the sentence carefully. Something was happening with him that was completely unfamiliar, new, sudden, and never before experienced. Not that he understood, but he clearly felt, with all the force of feeling, that not only could he no longer address these people in the quarter's office with his sensitive expansiveness as before, but even anything at all; it would be pointless for him to turn to them, even if they were all his own brothers and sisters, and not quarterlies, and never in any case; he had never experienced such a strange and terrifying feeling before. And what is most tormenting - this was more of an experience than a consciousness, more of a sensation than a concept; a direct and most tormenting sensation of all the sensations he had experienced in life so far. The clerk began to dictate to him the usual form of a response in such cases, that is, I cannot pay, I promise to do it sometime, I will not leave the city, I will not sell or give away my property and so on.
