The signal was so strong that he had already risen from his seat to respond. "Should I at least take a minute to think about it?" thought he, but then quickly added: "No need to think about it, let's just get rid of him!" But suddenly he stopped as if rooted to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was speaking with passion to Ilya Petrovich, and his words reached him:

— It can't be that they will both be released! First, everything contradicts; consider this: why would they summon a janitor if it were their business? To inform on themselves, or for cunning? No, it would be too cunning even! And finally, student Pestryakov was seen by the two janitors and the housewife right next to the entrance at the exact moment when he entered; he had been walking with three friends and parted ways with them right next to the entrance, and asked about their living situation while still in front of his friends. So would he be asking about their living situation if he was going for that intention?
