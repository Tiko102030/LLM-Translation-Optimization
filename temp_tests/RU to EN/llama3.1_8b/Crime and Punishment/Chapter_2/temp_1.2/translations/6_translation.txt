He thought triumphantly, deeply and joyfully breathing in all his chest, "Just a feverish weakness, a momentary delirium." And he ripped out the entire lining from the left pocket of his pants. At that moment, a sunbeam lit up his left boot: on the toe sticking out of the boot, it seemed like signs appeared. He kicked off the boot: "Really signs! The whole tip of the toe is soaked in blood"; probably he carelessly stepped into that puddle then... "But what to do with this now? Where to put this sock, rag, pocket?" He gathered everything in his hand and stood in the room. "Into the fireplace? But they will start rummaging there first. Burn it? And how to burn it? There aren't even any matches."
