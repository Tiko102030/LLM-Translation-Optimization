"Money? What money?" thought Raskolnikov, "but... it must be that!" And he shuddered with joy. Suddenly everything became incredibly and unbearably easy for him. All burdens were lifted off his shoulders. - And what time is written on the note, kind sir? cried the lieutenant, growing more and more outraged by the unknown, - they wrote to you at nine, but now it's already twelve! - They brought me just a quarter of an hour ago, Raskolnikov loudly replied from over his shoulder, also suddenly and unexpectedly angry and even taking some pleasure in this. - And that is enough, since I came ill with a fever. - Do not shout!
