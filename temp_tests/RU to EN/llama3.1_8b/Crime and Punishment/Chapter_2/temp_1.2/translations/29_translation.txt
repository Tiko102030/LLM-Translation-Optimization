— And you, so and so, cried he all of a sudden with his voice hoarse from shouting (the mournful lady had already left), did something happen to you last night? And again you make a scandal, causing a commotion on the whole street. Again you create a brawl and drunk behavior. You dream about restraint! I've warned you ten times already, I've told you beforehand that I won't let you out at eleven. But again, always so and so! A piece of paper slipped from Raskolnikov's hands as he looked wildly at the luxuriant lady who was being so impudently treated; but soon he realized what was happening and then this whole story started to please him very much indeed. He listened with enjoyment, even wanting to laugh, laugh, laugh... All his nerves were jumping like that. — Illya Petrovich!
