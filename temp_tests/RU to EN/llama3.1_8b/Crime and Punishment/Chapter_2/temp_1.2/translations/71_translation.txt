It became very unpleasant for him from this consideration, even frightening. — Nastasya, why are you silent? — he finally whispered in a weak voice. — This is blood, — she replied at last, quietly and as if to herself. — Blood! What blood? ... — he muttered, paling and edging away from the wall. Nastasya continued to silently look at him. — No one has beaten the mistress, — she said again in a strict and resolute voice. He looked at her, barely breathing.
