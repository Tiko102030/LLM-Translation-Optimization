The house is Noah's Ark – observed the clerk, listening from his place – clear as day, clear as day! exclaimed Nikodim Fomich heatedly – No, it's very unclear – corrected Ilya Petrovich. Raskolnikov raised his hat and went towards the door, but did not reach the door... 

When he came to, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that someone was supporting him from the right, another person standing on the left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and Nikodim Fomich stood before him looking at him intently; he got up from the chair. – What is this? Are you ill? asked Nikodim Fomich quite roughly. – They signed in such a way that they barely moved the pen, remarked the clerk, taking his seat again and going back to the papers – How long have you been ill? shouted Ilya Petrovich from his place and also busy with the papers.
