Meanwhile Razumikhin moved closer to him on a divan, clumsily, like a bear, he enveloped his head with his left hand, although he himself could get up, and with his right brought a spoon of soup to his mouth, blowing on it several times so that he wouldn't burn. But the soup was only just warm. Rascolnikov greedily swallowed one spoonful, then another, third. But having put out a few more spoons, Razumikhin suddenly stopped and announced that as for further action it would be necessary to consult with Zosima. Nastasya came in carrying two bottles of beer.
