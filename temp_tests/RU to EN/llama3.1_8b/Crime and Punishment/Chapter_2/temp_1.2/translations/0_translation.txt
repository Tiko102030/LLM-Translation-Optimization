He had lain there for a long time. Sometimes it seemed to him that he was waking up and in those moments he noticed that it was already late at night, but the thought of getting up didn't come into his head. Finally, he noticed that it was light during the day. He lay on the couch upside down, still dazed from recent forgetfulness. The terrifying, desperate cries from the street reached him sharply, which, however, he had listened to every night under his window at three o'clock. It was these screams that awakened him now. "Ah! they're already coming out of the bars drunk," he thought - "it's third hour" - and suddenly jumped up as if someone had pulled him off the couch from above. "- What! Third hour already!" He sat down on the couch, and at that moment it all came back to him!
