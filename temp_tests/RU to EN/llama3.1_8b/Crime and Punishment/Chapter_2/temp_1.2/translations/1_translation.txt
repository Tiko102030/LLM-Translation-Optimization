The third time already an hour! He sat on the sofa, and all of a sudden he remembered everything! In one moment he remembered everything! At first he thought he was going mad. A terrible chill enveloped him; but it was also from the fever that had started with him in his sleep long ago. Now, suddenly, such chills struck him that his teeth almost jumped out and everything was so icy inside of him. He opened the door and began to listen: in the house everyone is completely asleep. With amazement he glanced around at himself and everything around in the room and didn't understand: how could it be that yesterday, entering, he hadn't locked the doors on the hook and rushed onto the sofa not only without undressing, but even in his hat: it had fallen off and was lying right there near the pillow. "If someone came in, what would they think? That I'm drunk, but..." He threw himself at the windowpane. The light was quite good, and he quickly began to look over himself, from head to foot, everything: are there any signs?
