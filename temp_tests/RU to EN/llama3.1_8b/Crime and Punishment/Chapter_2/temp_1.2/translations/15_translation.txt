He thought to himself: "I'll put on socks! And they'll just get dirtier and the tracks will be erased!" But as soon as he put them on, he immediately took them off with a shudder of disgust. He took them off, but then realized there were none left, so he picked them up again and put them back on – and burst out laughing once more. "It's all conditional, everything is relative, it's just forms...," he thought briefly, a tiny corner of his mind, while trembling all over – after all, I did put them on! I ended up doing it!" His laughter soon turned to despair. "No, not within my powers...", he thought. His legs were shaking. He muttered to himself: "From fear". His head was spinning and throbbing from the heat.
