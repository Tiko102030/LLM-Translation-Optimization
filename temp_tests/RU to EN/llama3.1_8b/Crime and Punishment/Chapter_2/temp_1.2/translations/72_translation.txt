He looked at her, barely breathing. — I myself heard... I didn't sleep... I was sitting, he said even more softly. — I listened for a long time... The assistant warden came... Everyone rushed to the staircase from all the apartments...

— No one came. But blood in you is screaming. It's when she has no way out and will start to cook in the liver, then it will start to seem... You'll become something, will you? He didn't answer. Nastasya stood over him, looking at him intently and not leaving. — Give me a drink... Nastas'ka. She went down and came back with water in a white clay cup after two minutes; but he already didn't remember what happened next. He only remembered taking one swallow of cold water and spilling it from the cup onto his chest.
