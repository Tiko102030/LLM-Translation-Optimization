The clerk looked at him with a condescending smile of sorrow and triumph at the same time, as if at a novice who was just beginning to be bombarded: "So you're feeling quite miserable now, are you?" But what was his concern for the temporary loan or the summons? Was it worth causing himself any worry or even attention? He stood there, read, listened, responded, and even asked questions, all mechanically. The triumph of self-preservation, rescue from the oppressive danger – that filled him at this moment without consideration, analysis, or future guessing games and riddles, without doubts or questions. This was a moment of pure, immediate, animal joy. But in that very moment a thunderclap and lightning struck the office.
