Nothing and no one; no one was snooping around. Not even Nastasya touched anything. But good Lord! How could he leave all these things in this hole the other day? He rushed to the corner, stuck his hand under the wallpaper and started pulling out things and loading them into pockets. There were a total of eight items: two small boxes with earrings or something similar – he didn't take a close look; then four small leather cases. A chain was simply wrapped in newspaper. Something else was also wrapped in newspaper, it seemed like an order...

He placed everything in different pockets, in his coat and in the remaining right pocket of his pants, trying to make it unnoticeable. He took the wallet along with the things too. Then he left the room this time even leaving the door wide open.
