No one noticed him. In the second room sat and wrote some clerks dressed a bit better than he was, but looking like some strange kind of people. He approached one of them. — What do you want? He showed him a summons from the office. — Are you a student? — asked that one, glancing at the summons. — Yes, a former student. The clerk looked at him, however without any curiosity. This was some especially rumpled person with an unchanging idea in his gaze.
