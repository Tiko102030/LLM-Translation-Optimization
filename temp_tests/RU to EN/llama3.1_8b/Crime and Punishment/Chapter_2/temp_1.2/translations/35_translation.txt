Poverty is not a vice, comrade, well done! I mean, gunpowder cannot bear an insult. You must have taken offense at him and couldn't hold back either - said Nikodim Fomich, addressing Raskolnikov with friendly familiarity - but it's in vain: that's the most enviable person, I'll tell you, but gunpowder! He ignited, boiled over, burned out - and nothing is left! And it all passed! And as a result only one gold of heart! They even nicknamed him "lieutenant-powder"... 

— What another company was! - exclaimed Ilya Petrovich, very pleased that they had tickled him so nicely, but still trying to rouse Raskolnikov. Suddenly Raskolnikov wanted to say something uncommonly pleasant to them all. — Oh forgive me, Captain, he began with great looseness, addressing Nikodim Fomich suddenly - penetrate and my situation... I am even ready to ask for their apologies if I was at fault on my part.
