What is this? He quickly jumped up and began to pull out and throw them onto the table. Having taken everything, even rummaging through the pockets, to make sure if anything was left over, he transferred all this pile to a corner. There, in the very corner, at the bottom, in one place, the worn-out wallpaper had been torn away from the wall: immediately he began to stuff everything into that hole under the paper: "it's gone! Everything is out of sight and even the wallet!" - he thought with joy, standing up and blankly looking into the corner, into an even larger gaping hole. Suddenly he shuddered in horror: "Good God, I whispered to myself in despair, Is this really hidden? Do they hide things like this?" Indeed, he didn't count on things; he thought there would only be money, so he hadn't prepared a place for it beforehand - Now what am I happy about now? I don't think things are hidden like this.
