Then he tried to get up from the place, wanted to run away, but someone always held him back with force and he would fall into powerlessness and unconsciousness again. Finally he came completely to his senses. This happened in the morning at ten o'clock. At this hour of the morning on clear days, the sun always passed by a long strip across his right wall and lit up the corner next to the door. There was Nastasya standing beside his bed, and another person looking at him very curiously, completely unknown to him. This was a young man in a kaftan, with a beard, resembling an artisan. The house owner was peeking from half-opened door. Raskolnikov sat up. "Who is that, Nastasya?" he asked, pointing to the boy.
