There they are, scribblers! He cast a scornful glance at Raskolnikov — there was a story about that in the tavern too three days ago: he had eaten and didn't want to pay; "I'll describe you in my satire for it", he supposedly said. The same on the steamboat last week, where an esteemed family of a councilor's wife and daughter were called scoundrels by him with vile words. A candy store employee was thrown out a few days ago due to his conduct. Those are the kind of scribblers, writers, students, heralds...tut! And you get away! I myself will drop in on you later... then be careful! Did you hear? Louise Ivanovna, with her usual amicability, went down on one knee all around and, going down, reached the door; but she bumped into it backside first onto a prominent officer, with an open fresh face and excellent thick fluffy mustache.
