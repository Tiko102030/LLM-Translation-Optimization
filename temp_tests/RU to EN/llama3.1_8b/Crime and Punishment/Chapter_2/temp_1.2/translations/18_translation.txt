The air was incredibly stuffy. The caretakers with books under their arms, respectable and various people of both sexes - visitors, were climbing up and down. The door to the office was also wide open. He entered and stopped in the entrance hall. Everyone stood there waiting for some men. Here too the smell was unbearable and, in addition, a strong fresh paint odor on dirty oil from newly painted rooms hit his nose like nausea. After a while, he thought that it was time to move forward again, into the next room. All the rooms were tiny and low-ceilinged. A terrible impatience pushed him further and further. Nobody noticed him.
