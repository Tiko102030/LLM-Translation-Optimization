He was not entirely unaware during his illness; this was a delirious state with hallucinations and partial consciousness. He remembered many things later on. It seemed to him that many people were gathering around him, trying to take him away somewhere, arguing about him heatedly and quarreling among themselves. Sometimes he felt as if he was alone in the room, everyone had left, and they all feared him; only occasionally someone would open the door slightly to glance at him, threatening him, whispering something to each other, laughing and teasing him. Nastasya often stood by his side; he could distinguish another person as well, very familiar-looking, but he couldn't figure out who exactly it was, and he longed for that person deeply, even crying about it. Sometimes it seemed to him that a month had passed since he lay there; at other times it was as if the same day was passing in one moment.
