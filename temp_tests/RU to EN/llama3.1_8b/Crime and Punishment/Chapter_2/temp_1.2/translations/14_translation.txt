Then he nervously unfolded the summons and began to read; he read on for a long time before finally understanding. It was an ordinary summons from the neighborhood to appear today at half past ten at the office of the neighborhood supervisor. "When did this ever happen? I have no dealings whatsoever with the police myself! And why precisely today? — he thought in perplexed bewilderment. "Lord, please let it be over soon!" He jumped down onto his knees to pray, but even himself laughed at the idea; not because of prayer itself, but because of himself.
