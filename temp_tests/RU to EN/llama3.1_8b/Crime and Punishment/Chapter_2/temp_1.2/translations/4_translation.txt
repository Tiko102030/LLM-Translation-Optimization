-- Was it possible to hide it like that? My reason really has left me! He sank onto the couch in exhaustion and was instantly overcome with a severe shiver. He mechanically pulled under him from a chair beside his student winter coat, warm but now almost in tatters, threw it over himself, and once again both sleep and delirium simultaneously seized him. He dozed off. No more than five minutes later he jumped up again and immediately, in a state of frenzy, rushed back to his clothes. "How could I fall asleep again when nothing has been done! Yes, that's right, yes, that's right: the noose under my arm still hasn't been removed! I forgot, completely forgot about such things! Such a clue!" He pulled off the noose and quickly began to tear it into pieces, stuffing them under the pillow in his bedding. "The pieces of torn lace will not arouse any suspicion at all; it seems so, it seems so!" -- he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with a tense attention began again to search around everywhere on the floor and elsewhere if he had forgotten anything else?
