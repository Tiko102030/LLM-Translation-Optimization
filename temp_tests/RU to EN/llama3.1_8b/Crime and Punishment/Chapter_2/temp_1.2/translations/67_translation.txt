It seemed to him as though he had somehow cut himself off from everything and everyone with scissors in this very moment. He came back to himself already by evening, so that only six hours had passed. Where and how he walked back, he did not remember anything. Having undressed and trembling all over like a horse whipped into a frenzy, he lay down on the sofa, put on his greatcoat, and immediately fell asleep...

He woke up in full darkness to a terrible scream. God, what kind of scream is this! Such unnatural sounds, such yelling, screaming, scraping, crying, beating, and curses he had never heard or seen before, and could not even imagine to himself. In horror, he sat up on his bed and froze every moment, suffering terribly. But the fights, screams, and curses were getting stronger and stronger. And suddenly, to his greatest amazement, he suddenly caught a glimpse of the voice of his landlady.
