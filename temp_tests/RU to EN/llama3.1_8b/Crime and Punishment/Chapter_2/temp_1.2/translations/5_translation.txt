"Shreds of torn fabric won't raise any suspicions; it looks like that, yes, it does!" he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, his attention strained to the point of pain as he began searching around once more, on the floor and everywhere else, had he forgotten something else? The certainty that everything was leaving him, even his memory, even simple reasoning, began to torture him unbearably. "What if it's already begun? What if this is the execution arriving?" He shouted again, "Really, really!" Indeed, the pieces of tassel he had cut off from his pants were lying on the floor in the middle of the room so that anyone could see them first! "What's gotten into me?" he shouted again, lost. At that moment a strange thought came to him: maybe everything about his clothes is stained with blood, maybe there are many spots, but he doesn't notice them because his thinking has weakened, broken apart... his mind obscured... Suddenly he remembered that the wallet had also been bloody. "Hmph! So it must be on the money too, because I put my still-wet wallet into the pocket then!" He rummaged through his pocket in an instant and – sure enough – there were traces, spots on the lining of the pocket! "So it stands to reason that I didn't leave completely behind reason itself; so there's thinking and memory, since he himself realized and remembered it! - thought he with triumph, deeply and joyfully taking a breath with his entire chest. It was just some fleeting weakness due to fever, momentary madness," and he tore out the lining of the left pocket from his pants.
