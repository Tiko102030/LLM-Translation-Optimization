Leave me alone! — Ah, hold on for a minute, bookbinder! Completely insane! Well, as far as I'm concerned, you can do what you want. Look: I have no lessons and it's not like I care, but I do have Hervemov, the bookseller from Tolkuchy, which is, in its own way, a lesson in itself. I wouldn't trade him for five merchants' lessons now. He prints such publications and publishes natural science booklets – and they sell like hotcakes! The titles alone are worth something! You always claimed I was stupid; Lord have mercy, brother, there are even stupider people than me! Now he's also heading in the right direction; not a clue about what he does, but of course, I'm encouraging him.
