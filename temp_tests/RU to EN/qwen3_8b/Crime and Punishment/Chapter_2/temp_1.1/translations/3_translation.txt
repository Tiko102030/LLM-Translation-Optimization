What is this? He immediately rushed to take them out and throw them on the table. Having selected everything, even turning out his pockets to make sure nothing else remained, he carried all this pile to the corner. There, in the very corner, down below, in one place, the wall paper that had peeled away from the wall was torn: at once he began stuffing everything into this hole, under the paper: "It's in! Everything out of sight and the wallet too!"—he joyfully thought, standing up and staring blankly at the corner, the hole now even more protruding. Suddenly he shuddered all over with fear: "My God," he whispered in despair, "what is happening to me? Is this hidden? Do they hide like that?" Indeed, he had not expected anything else; he thought there would be only money, and therefore had not prepared a hiding place in advance—"But now, now what am I happy about?" he thought. "Do they hide like that?"
