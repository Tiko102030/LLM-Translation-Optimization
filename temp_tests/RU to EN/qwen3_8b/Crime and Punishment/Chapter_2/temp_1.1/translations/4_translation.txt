— Is that how they hide? My mind is leaving me!— He collapsed into the sofa in exhaustion, and immediately the unbearable shiver shook him again. Mechanically, he pulled the lying nearby, on the chair, his former student's winter coat, warm but already almost in rags, and covered himself with it. Sleep and delirium once again enveloped him. He fell asleep. No more than five minutes later, he jumped up again and immediately, in a frenzy, ran again to his dress. "How could I have fallen asleep again, when nothing has been done! That's true, that's true: I still haven't removed the loop under my armpit! I forgot, I forgot about such a thing! Such a clue!" He pulled the loop and quickly began tearing it into pieces, stuffing them under the pillow in the linen. "Pieces of torn flannel will certainly not raise suspicion; it seems so, it seems so!" – he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with painful intensity began to scan around, on the floor and everywhere, whether he had forgotten anything else?
