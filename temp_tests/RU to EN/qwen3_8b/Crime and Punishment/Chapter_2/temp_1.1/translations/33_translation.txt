There they are, the authors!—and he cast a disdainful glance at Raskolnikov. Three days ago, there was an incident in the tavern as well: he dined and refused to pay; "I shall describe you in satire for that," he said. On the steamer too, another incident, last week, a respected family of a State councillor, his wife and daughter, were insulted with the worst language. Yesterday, they threw one out of the confectionery in a scuffle. That's what they're like, authors, literati, students, agitators... tsk! Go on, you! I'll come myself to you... then beware! Did you hear? Louisa Ivanovna, with affected cordiality, began to bend and scrape to everyone, and while bowing, she finally reached the door; but at the door, she bumped into a notable officer, with a fresh, open face and with excellent thick golden whiskers.
