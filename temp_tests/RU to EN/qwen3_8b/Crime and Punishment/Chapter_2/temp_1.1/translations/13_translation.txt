Even though he could hardly think clearly at that moment, he felt that people would treat him differently when they came to take him. "But... the police?" — Would you like some tea? Want something? I'll bring it; there's still... — No... I'll go: I'll go right now," he muttered, standing up. "Go ahead, and you won't get off the stairs?" — I'll go... — As you like. She went after the janitor. Immediately he ran to the light to inspect the sock and hem: "There are stains, but they aren't very noticeable; everything has gotten dirty, worn out, and already faded. Whoever doesn't know beforehand won't notice anything. Nastasya, therefore, couldn't have noticed them from a distance, thank goodness!" Then, with trembling, he carefully unsealed the summons and began reading; he read for a long time and finally understood.
