"No, the matter is very unclear," remarked Ilia Petrovich. Raskolnikov lifted his hat and went towards the door, but he did not reach it... When he came to, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that someone was supporting him on the right, that another person was standing on the left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nickodim Fomich was standing before him, looking at him intently; he rose from the chair. "What is this, are you ill?" Nickodim Fomich rather sharply asked. "They barely signed with a pen, as if barely tracing with a pen," remarked the scribe, sitting down at his place and resuming his work with the papers. "How long have you been ill?" shouted Ilia Petrovich from his place, also going through the papers.
