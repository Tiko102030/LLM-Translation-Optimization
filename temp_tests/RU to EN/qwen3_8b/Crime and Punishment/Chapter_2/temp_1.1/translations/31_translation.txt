He opened the window on the ditch and stood in the window like a little pig, squealing; and this is a disgrace. How can one stand in the window to the street, like a little pig, squealing; and this is a disgrace. Fuy-fuy-fuy! And Carly pulled him by the frock from the window and here, truly, sir captain, he tore his shirt. And then he cried out that he had to pay fifteen cents in fines. And I myself, sir captain, paid him five cents for the fine. And this is an ungentlemanly guest, sir captain, and one who causes every scandal! I, being a speaker, will write a big satire on you, because I can write everything about you in all newspapers. — From the writers, meaning? — Yes, sir captain, and what kind of an ungentlemanly guest, sir captain, when in a noble house… — Hmph-hmph-hmph!
