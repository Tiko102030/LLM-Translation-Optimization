No one noticed him. In the second room, some scribes were sitting and writing, dressed a bit better than him, they looked like an odd bunch of people. He turned to one of them. "What do you want?" He showed a summons from the office. "Are you a student?" asked the man, looking at the summons. "Yes, a former student." The scribe looked at him, however without any curiosity. This was a particularly disheveled person with an unmoving idea in his gaze.
