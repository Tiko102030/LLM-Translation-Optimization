There was an awful stuffiness. Janitors went up and down with books under their arms, haggard and assorted people of both sexes—visitors. The door to the main office was also left ajar. He entered and stopped in the hallway. Everyone was standing there waiting for some men. There was an extraordinary stuffiness here as well, and in addition, the fresh, still drying paint on the rancid oil made his nose ache to the point of nausea. After waiting for a while, he decided to move forward into the next room. All the rooms were tiny and low. A terrifying impatience pulled him further and further. No one noticed him.
