— Poverty is not a sin, friend, well, what can you do! It's known that gunpowder couldn't bear an insult. You, perhaps, offended him in some way and couldn't help it—continued Nikodim Fomich, politely addressing Rasputin— but you're wasting your time: the most noble person, I can tell you, but gunpowder, gunpowder! He got angry, boiled over, burned out—and that's it! Everything passed! And in the end, only a golden heart! They even called him in the regiment: "sergeant-gunnery"…

— What regiment could it be! — exclaimed Ilyá Petrovich, very pleased that he had been so nicely teased, but still irritable. Suddenly, Rasputin felt like saying something unusually pleasant to them all. — Good Lord, captain, — he began, very carelessly addressing Nikodim Fomich suddenly — consider my situation… I'm even ready to ask them for forgiveness if I erred on my part.
