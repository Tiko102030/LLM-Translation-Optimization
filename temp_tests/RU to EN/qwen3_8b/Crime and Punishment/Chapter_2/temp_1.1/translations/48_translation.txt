What if the cases don't sink but float? Of course, that's the way it is. Everyone will see it. And already now everyone looks at each other, meeting, glancing, as if it's all about them. "Why should it be like that, or perhaps it's just how I imagine it," he thought. Finally, it occurred to him that perhaps it would be better to go somewhere on the Neva. There are fewer people, it's less noticeable, and in any case more convenient, and above all, farther from here. He suddenly realized: how could he have walked for half an hour in gloom and anxiety, in dangerous places, and yet couldn't imagine it before! And precisely because he had already decided in a dream, in a fit of delirium, to do this whole half hour of reckless business! He became extremely absent-minded and forgetful, and he knew it.
