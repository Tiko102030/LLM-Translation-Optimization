He felt as if he had cut himself off from everyone and everything with scissors at that moment. He came to himself by evening, meaning he had walked for only six hours. He didn't remember where and how he had gone back. Undressing and shaking all over like a frightened horse, he lay on the sofa, pulled on his overcoat, and immediately fell asleep...

He awoke in the depths of dusk from a terrifying scream. God, what a scream! Such unnatural sounds, such howling, screaming, screeching, tears, brawling, and swearing he had never heard or seen before. He couldn't even imagine such cruelty, such frenzy. In terror, he raised himself up and sat on his bed, trembling and suffering every moment. But the fights, screams, and curses became stronger and stronger. And here, to his greatest astonishment, he suddenly recognized his mistress's voice.
