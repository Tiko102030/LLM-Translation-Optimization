It was strange. — Well, well, that's good, — concluded Ilya Petrovich, — we won't detain you. Raskolnikov left. He still managed to hear the lively conversation that began as he exited, in which Nikodim Fomich's questioning voice was most prominent... On the street, he completely came to himself. "Search, search, a search now! — he repeated to himself, hurrying to get there; — bandits! they suspect!" The old fear once again engulfed him from head to toe. II
