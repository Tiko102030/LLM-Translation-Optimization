Nastasya, I suppose, couldn't have noticed anything from afar, praise be! Then, trembling, he opened the summons and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood. It was an ordinary summons from the district to appear today at half-past ten in the district supervisor's office. "When has this ever happened? I have no business with the police on my own! Why today?—he thought in torment. Lord, hurry up!" He was about to kneel down to pray, but even he laughed to himself—not at the prayer, but at himself. He hurried to dress. "I'll be lost anyway, I'll be lost! Put on a sock!"
