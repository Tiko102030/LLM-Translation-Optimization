Then he tried to get up, wanted to run away, but someone always stopped him by force, and he again fell into helplessness and unconsciousness. Finally, he completely came to himself. This happened in the morning, at ten o'clock. At this hour of the morning, on clear days, the sun always cast a long ribbon of light along his right wall, illuminating the corner by the door. By his bed stood Nastasya and another person, who was very curiously observing him and completely unknown to him. This was a young man in a kaftan, with a beard, and from appearance looked like a member of an artel. From the half-open door peeked out the housekeeper. Raskolnikov raised himself. "Who is this, Nastasya?" he asked, pointing at the young man.
