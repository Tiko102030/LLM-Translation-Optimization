— Yes, Mr. Captain, and what a base guest this is, Mr. Captain, when in a noble house…  
— Ну-ну-ну! Enough! I've already told you, told you, I've told you…  
— Ilyia Petrovich! — the clerk spoke again significantly. The lieutenant quickly glanced at him; the clerk slightly nodded his head. — …So here's my final tale, esteemed Laviza Ivanovna, and this is for the last time, — continued the lieutenant. — If there's another scandal in your noble house, I'll flog you myself, as they say in high style. Did you hear? So, a writer, a scribe, took five coins in the "noble house" for a fold? There they are, the scribes!
