"Pieces of torn linen will never raise suspicion; it seems so, it seems so!" He kept repeating, standing in the middle of the room, and with painful intensity began to scan around again, on the floor and everywhere, not having forgotten something else? The certainty that everything, even memory, even the simplest thought had left him began to mercilessly torment him. "What, is it starting now, is this punishment about to begin? There, there, that's exactly it!" Indeed, the scraps of tassels he had cut from his pants were lying on the floor, in the middle of the room, to be seen by the first person! "What is happening to me!" — he cried out again, as if lost. At this moment, a strange thought came to him: perhaps all his clothes were in blood, perhaps there were many stains, but he just didn't see them, didn't notice, because his thinking had weakened, shattered… his mind was clouded… Suddenly he remembered that there was blood on his wallet. "Bah! So, that means there should also be blood in the pocket, because I still put the wet wallet into the pocket!" Immediately he turned the pocket inside out, and — indeed — there were traces, stains on the pocket's lining! "So, he hasn't lost all his wits yet, so there is thought and memory, since he managed to realize and guess by himself! — he thought triumphantly, taking a deep and joyful breath from his entire chest, — it's just the weakness of a fever, a momentary delirium," — and he pulled out all the lining from the left pocket of his pants.
