Three hours have already passed! He sat on the couch—and suddenly everything came back to him! In the first moment, he thought he was going mad. A terrible cold gripped him; but the cold was also from the fever that had already begun with him in his sleep. Now, however, a sudden chill struck him so violently that his teeth seemed ready to jump out, and everything inside him trembled. He opened the door and began listening: the whole house was completely asleep. He looked at himself in astonishment and everything around him in the room, not understanding: how could he, yesterday, entering, not lock the doors with the latch and fling himself onto the couch, not only without undressing, but even with his hat on? It had slipped off and immediately lay on the floor near the pillow. "What would someone think if they came in? That I'm drunk, but..." He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began examining himself, from head to toe, his entire attire: are there any traces?
