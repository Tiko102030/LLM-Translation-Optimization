He felt very unpleasant from this gaze, even terrifying. "Nastasya, why are you staying silent?" he finally whispered timidly. "This is blood," she finally answered, quietly, as if speaking to herself. "Blood!... Which blood?..." he muttered, growing pale and moving away from the wall.
