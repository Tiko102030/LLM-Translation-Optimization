The clerk looked at them with a smile. The young ensign was clearly puzzled. "It's not your business, you know!" he finally shouted, somehow unnaturally loudly, "Now, you may as well submit the review they require from you. Show him, Alexander Grigoryevich. Complaints about you! Don't pay any money! What a sharp-eyed hawk you are!" But Raskolnikov was no longer listening and greedily grabbed the paper, seeking to quickly find the answer. He read it once, twice, and still didn't understand.
