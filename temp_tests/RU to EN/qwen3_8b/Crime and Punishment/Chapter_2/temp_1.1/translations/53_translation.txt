"By God, that's it!—he suddenly thought, in a fit of unrelenting wrath. Well, it's begun, so it's begun, by God with her and the new life! How could this be, Lord, how foolish!... And how much I lied and disgraced myself today! How miserably I bowed and flattered lately with the worst Ilyich Petrovich! And as for that, nonsense! I don't care about them all, nor about the fact that I bowed and flattered! It's completely different! It's completely different!..."

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected, and extremely simple question at once confused him and greatly astonished him:

"If indeed all this was done intentionally, not by accident, if you indeed had a definite and firm goal, then how come you haven't even looked into your wallet yet and don't know what you've gotten, for what you've suffered and for such a vile, dirty, low act you've gone on purpose?"
