But just as he had put it on, he immediately pulled it off with revulsion and horror. He pulled it off, but realizing that there was no other, he took it and put it on again — and laughed again. "Everything is conditional, everything is relative, everything is just forms," — he thought briefly, with just a fleeting thought, while his whole body was trembling, — why, he did put it on! Why, he ended up by putting it on!" The laughter, however, immediately turned into despair. "No, it's not within my power… — he thought. His legs were trembling. "From fear," — he muttered to himself. His head was spinning and aching from the heat.
