But instead, his head again leaned on the pillow; again the unbearable chill seized him; again he pulled on his overcoat. And for a long time, several hours, he still had impulses, wanting to go somewhere and throw everything out, so that it would be out of sight, as soon as possible, as soon as possible! He tried to get up from the couch several times, wanted to stand up, but already couldn't. Finally, he was awakened by a strong knock on the door. "Open up, alive or not? And he's all asleep!" Nastasya shouted, pounding her fist on the door, "sleeping all day long, like a dog, sleeping!"
