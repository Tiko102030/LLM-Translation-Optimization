He looked at her, barely breathing. — I heard it myself... I didn't sleep... I was sitting, — he spoke even more hesitantly. — I listened for a long time... The assistant of the warden came... Everyone ran down the stairs, from all the apartments... — No one came. But blood in you is screaming. This is when she has no way out and starts to burn in the liver, then visions start... Will you eat at all? He didn't answer. Nastasya stood over him, looked at him intently and didn't leave. — Give me something to drink... Nastasya. She went downstairs and came back in two minutes with water in a white clay cup; but he already didn't remember what happened next. He remembered only taking one sip of cold water and spilling it from the cup onto his chest.
