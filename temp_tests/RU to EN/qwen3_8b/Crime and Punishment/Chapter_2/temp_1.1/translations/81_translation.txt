What have you got, a book, eh? — A book, here you are. — Give it here. Well, Rodia, get up. I'll hold you; give him a slap, Nikolai, take the pen, for brother, money is now more than honey. — No need, — said Nikolai, pushing the pen away. — Why not? — I won't sign. — Fiddlesticks, how can we do without a signature? — No need… money… — This is not money!
