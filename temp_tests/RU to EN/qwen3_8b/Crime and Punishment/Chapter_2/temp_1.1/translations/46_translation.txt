Nothing and no one; no one had looked in. Not even Nastasya touched them. But, good Lord! How could he have left all these things in this hole back then? He rushed to the corner, stuck his hand under the wallpaper, and started pulling out items, loading them into his pockets. There were a total of eight items: two small boxes with earrings or something similar—he hadn't looked closely; then four small sapphire cases. One chain was simply wrapped in newspaper. Something else in newspaper, seems like an order… 

He placed everything in different pockets, in the coat and in the remaining right pocket of his pants, trying to make it as inconspicuous as possible. He also took the wallet along with the items. Then he left the room, this time even leaving it completely open.
