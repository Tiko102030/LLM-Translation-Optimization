— You, such-and-such and so-and-so!— he suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the funeral lady had already left).— What happened during the past night, you? Again disgrace, causing a row on the whole street. Again a fight and drunkenness. You're dreaming of quiet! I've already told you, I've already warned you ten times that I won't let you come on the eleventh! And you again, again, you such-and-such, you so-and-so! Even the paper dropped from Raskolnikov's hands, and he wildly looked at the wealthy lady who was being so rudely treated; but soon, however, he understood what was happening, and immediately this whole story began to please him very much. He listened with enjoyment, so much so that he wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh… All his nerves were twitching.— Ilya Petrovich!
