"Put on the sock! — he suddenly thought, — it would get even more dusty, and the traces would disappear." But just as he put it on, he immediately pulled it off with disgust and horror. He pulled it off, but realizing there was no other option, he took it again and put it on — and laughed again. "Everything is conditional, everything is relative, all of this is just forms," — he thought briefly, with just a flicker of thought, his whole body trembling, — for here he put it on! For he ended up by putting it on!" Smiling, however, immediately turned into despair. "No, it's beyond my strength…" — he thought. His legs were trembling. "From fear," — he muttered to himself. His head was spinning and aching from the fever.
