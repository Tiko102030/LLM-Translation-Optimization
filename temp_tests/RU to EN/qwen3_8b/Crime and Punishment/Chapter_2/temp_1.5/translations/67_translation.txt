It seemed to him as if he had cut himself off with scissors from everyone and everything at that moment. He had returned to himself by evening, which meant he had been walking for only six hours. He did not remember where or how he had gone back. Undressed and trembling all over like a chased horse, he lay on the sofa, pulled on his overcoat, and immediately fell asleep...

He awoke in the depths of dusk from a terrifying scream. God, what kind of cry is this! Such unnatural sounds, such howling, wailing, screeching, tears, brawling, and curses he had never heard or seen before. He could not even imagine such cruelty, such frenzy. In terror, he raised himself up and sat on his bed, pausing and suffering each moment. But the fights, screams, and curses grew stronger and stronger. And here, to his greatest astonishment, he suddenly recognized his mistress's voice.
