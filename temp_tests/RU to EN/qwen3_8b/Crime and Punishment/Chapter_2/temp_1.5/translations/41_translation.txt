Well, why would they start questioning about residency if they came with such an intention? And Koch, he sat for half an hour downstairs by the silverware keeper before going up to the old woman at exactly seven fifty-five. Now, think it over...

—"But allow me, how did such a contradiction arise: they themselves claim they knocked and that the door was locked, yet three minutes later, when they came with the janitor, it turned out the door was unlocked?" — The trick is that the murderer had to be sitting there and locked himself in; and he would have been caught there for sure if only Koch hadn't been foolish and gone to check on the janitor himself. And he precisely took that opportunity to descend the stairs and slip past them somehow. Koch crosses himself with both hands: "If I had stayed there, he would have jumped out and killed me with an axe." A Russian liturgy wants to be served, hehe... — But no one saw the murderer? — Where would you see him? The house is Noah's Ark, — noted the clerk, who was listening from his place.
