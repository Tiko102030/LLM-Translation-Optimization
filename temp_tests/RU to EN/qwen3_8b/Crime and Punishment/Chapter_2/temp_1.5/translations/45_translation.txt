It was strange. "Well, well, all right," concluded Ilya Petrovich, "we won't detain you." Raskolnikov left. He still could overhear the lively conversation that had begun upon his exit, in which Nikodim Fomich's questioning voice stood out most clearly... On the street he fully came to himself. "A search, a search, a search is coming!" he repeated to himself, hurrying to reach his destination; "bandits! They suspect!" The previous fear gripped him from head to toe. II

"What if there already was a search? What if I encounter them exactly here?"
