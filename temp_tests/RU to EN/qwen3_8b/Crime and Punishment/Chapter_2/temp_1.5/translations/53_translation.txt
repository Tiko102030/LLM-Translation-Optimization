"By hell, that's it! — he suddenly thought, seized by an unremitting rage. Well, it's begun, and so it's begun; let hell take her and her new life! How foolish, Lord!.. How much I have lied and corrupted today! How vilely I bowed and danced with the most loathsome Ilya Petrovich! In any case, nonsense even that! I care nothing for them all, nor for the fact that I bowed and danced! Entirely different! Entirely different!.."

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question at once confused him and greatly astonished him:

"If indeed all this was done deliberately, not by accident, if you indeed had a definite and firm goal, then how is it that you haven't even looked into your wallet yet and don't know what you've received, for what torment you've endured and deliberately gone through such a vile, nasty, low act?"
