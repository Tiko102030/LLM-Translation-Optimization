— No need to, — he said, — I came… here's the thing: I have no lessons… I wanted to… in any case, I don't need lessons at all…

— Do you know what? You're losing your mind! — remarked Razumikhin, watching him closely. — No, I'm not… — Raskolnikov stood up from the sofa. As he got up to face Razumikhin, he hadn't considered that he would have to meet someone face to face. Now, in an instant, he realized, through experience, that at this moment he was least inclined to meet anyone face to face in the whole world. All his bile rose within him. He almost choked on his own fury at himself for having crossed Razumikhin's threshold. — Goodbye! — he said abruptly and walked towards the door.
