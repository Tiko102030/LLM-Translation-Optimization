Nothing and nobody; nobody looked in. Even Nastasya didn't touch it. But, Lord! How could he leave all these things in this hole back then? He rushed to the corner, stuck his hand under the wall coverings, and began pulling out the items and stuffing them into his pockets. In total, there were eight items: two small boxes with earrings or something like that—he didn't look closely; then four small sapphire cases. One chain was simply wrapped in newspaper paper. Something else in newspaper paper, it seems an order… He placed everything in different pockets, in the coat and in the remaining right pocket of the pants, trying to make it inconspicuous. The wallet he also took along with the items. Then he exited the room, this time even leaving it completely open.
