Then he tried to get up, wanted to run away, but always someone stopped him by force, and he again fell into helplessness and confusion. Finally, he completely came to himself. This happened in the morning, at ten o'clock. At this hour in the morning, on clear days, the sun always passed along his right wall in a long strip, illuminating the corner near the door. Standing by his bed was Nastasya and another person, who was looking at him very curiously and was completely unfamiliar to him. This was a young man in a kaftan, with a beard, and from appearance looked like an artisan. The mistress was peeking out from the half-open door. Raskolnikov raised himself. — Who is this, Nastasya? — he asked, pointing at the young man.
