The clerk smiled at them. The fiery ensign seemed clearly flustered. "— This isn't your affair, you know! — he shouted at last in an unnaturally loud voice, — but here, go ahead and submit the report they demand from you. Show him, Alexander Grigoryevich. Complaints against you! Don't pay any money! What a bold eagle you've flown!" But Raskolnikov had already stopped listening and greedily grabbed the paper, seeking quickly to unravel the mystery. He read it once, twice, and didn't understand.
