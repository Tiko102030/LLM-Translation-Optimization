But instead of that, his head again leaned back on the pillow; again an unbearable chill froze him; again he pulled on his overcoat. And for a long time, several hours, he still kept having fits of seeing that "now, without delay, to go somewhere and throw everything away, so it's out of sight, as soon as possible, as soon as possible!" He several times tried to get up from the sofa, wanted to stand, but already could not. Finally, he was fully awakened by a strong knock on the door. "Open up, are you alive or not? And all the time he sleeps!" cried Nastasya, knocking with her fist on the door, "whole days, daylight hours, he sleeps like a dog!"
