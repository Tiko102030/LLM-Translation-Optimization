I've forgotten this very apartment; in fact, I never remembered it because I didn't know it. As for the previous apartment, I remember only that it was at Five Corners, Harlamov's house. I sought that Harlamov house over and over, but it turned out it wasn't Harlamov's at all, but Bukha's—sometimes you do mix up sounds! So I got angry, stormed off, and the next day went to the address office. Imagine my surprise: they found me there in two minutes. You are registered there.
