Realizing that he had woken up, the homeowner, who had been peeking through the door, immediately closed it and hid. She had always been shy and took with reluctance conversations and explanations; she was forty years old, and she was fat and chubby, with black eyebrows and black eyes, kind from her corpulence and laziness; and she was even very oily in her own way. Shy beyond necessity. — "Who are you?" — he continued interrogating, addressing the foreman directly. But at that moment the door opened wide again, and, slightly leaning forward, because he was tall, Razumikhin entered.
