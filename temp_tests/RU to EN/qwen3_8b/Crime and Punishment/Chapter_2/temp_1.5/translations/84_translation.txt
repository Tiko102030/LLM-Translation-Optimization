Two minutes later, Nastasya returned with soup and announced that tea would come soon. With the soup came two spoons, two plates, and the entire set of tableware: salt, pepper, horseradish for beef, and other things that had long since ceased to be used in such order. The tablecloth was clean. "It wouldn't be bad, Nastasya, if Praskovya Pavlovna sent two beer bottles," he said. "We'll have a drink, you know." — "Well, you bigfoot!" — muttered Nastasya and went to carry out the order.
