Nastasya, so to speak, could not possibly have noticed anything from a distance, thank God! Then, with trembling, he unsealed the notice and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood. It was an ordinary notice from the district to appear today at half past ten in the office of the district supervisor. "When did this ever happen? I have no business with the police on my own!" And why precisely today? — he thought in agony of confusion. — Lord, just hurry up! He was about to kneel down to pray, but even he laughed — not at the prayer, but at himself. He quickly started getting dressed. "I'll be lost either way! Put on the sock!"
