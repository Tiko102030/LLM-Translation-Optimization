Read once, again, and didn't understand. — What is this? — he asked the scribe. — This is a demand for payment from your loan document, a collection. You must either pay all costs, interest, and other fees, or give a written response stating when you can pay, along with an obligation not to leave the capital until payment is made and not to sell or hide your property. The lender is free to sell your property and act according to the law against you. — Well, I... owe no one! — This is not our concern. However, we have received a legally protested and overdue loan note for one hundred fifteen rubles, issued to you, the widow Zarnitsyna, nine months ago, and transferred for collection to the town counselor Chebarov, and we are hereby summoning you to respond. — But she is my housekeeper!
