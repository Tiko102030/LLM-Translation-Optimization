— Hurry up and make tea, Nastasya, because, it seems, you can manage the tea without a faculty. But here comes the beer! — he moved to his chair, brought the soup and meat closer, and started eating with such an appetite as if he hadn't eaten in three days. — Bro Rodya, I eat this way every day here, — he muttered, as his full mouth of meat allowed, — and it's all Pashenka, your housekeeper, entertaining me wholeheartedly. I, of course, don't insist, but I don't protest either. Here's Nastasya with the tea. She's quick! Nastasya, do you want some beer? — By the mischief! — And a little tea?
