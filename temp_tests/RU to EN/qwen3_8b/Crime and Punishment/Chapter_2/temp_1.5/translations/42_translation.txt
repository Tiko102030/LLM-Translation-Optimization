The house — Noah's Ark," remarked the clerk, who was listening from his place. "The matter is clear, the matter is clear!" eagerly repeated Nikodim Fomich. "No, the matter is very unclear," added Ilya Petrovich. Raskolnikov lifted his hat and went toward the door, but he did not reach the door... 

When he came to himself, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that someone was supporting him from the right, that another person was standing on the left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nikodim Fomich was standing before him, looking intently at him; he rose from the chair. "What is this, are you ill?" Nikodim Fomich asked rather sharply. "They signed as if barely touching with a pen," remarked the clerk, sitting down at his place and taking up the papers again. "How long have you been ill?" Ilya Petrovich shouted from his place, also rummaging through the papers.
