There they are, the authors!—and he cast a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. —Three days ago, there was an incident in a tavern: he dined, but refused to pay; "I, as if to say, will describe you in satire for that." On a steamer, too, another incident occurred last week: a respected family of a State Councillor, his wife and daughter, were insulted with the most contemptuous words. From a confectionery, yesterday, they kicked one out for pushing. These are the sort of writers, literary men, students, public speakers... Tsk! Go ahead, you! I'll come myself to you... then watch out! Did you hear? Louisa Ivanovna, with insincerely cordial politeness, began to bow and curtsy in all directions, and, bowing and curtsying, she waddled up to the door; but she ran right into a prominent officer's backside, with a fresh, open face and excellent thick, blonde sideburns.
