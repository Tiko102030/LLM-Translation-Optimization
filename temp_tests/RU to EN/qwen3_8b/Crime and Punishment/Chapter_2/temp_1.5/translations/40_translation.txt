The call was so strong that he already rose from his seat to execute it. "Should I think it over for a minute?" — the thought passed through his mind. "No, better not to think at all and just throw it to the wind!" But suddenly he stopped as if rooted to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was fervently speaking to Ilyy Petrovich, and the words reached him: "It can't be, both will be set free! First of all, everything contradicts; judge for yourselves: why call for a housekeeper if it's their own matter? To denounce themselves? Or for cunning? No, that would be too cunning! And finally, Pestryakov, the student, was seen by both the housekeepers and the townswoman right at the moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted from them right at the gates, and was inquiring about residence with the housekeepers, still with his friends. Well, would he start asking about residence if he came with such an intent?"
