Though he could hardly fully comprehend at that moment, he felt that people would not treat him properly when they came to take him. "But... the police?" — Would you like some tea? If you want, I'll bring it; there's still... — No... I'll go: I'll go right now," he muttered, getting to his feet. "Go, and you won't even get down the stairs?" — I'll go... — As you like. She went after the janitor. Immediately, he ran to the light to examine the sock and tinsel: "There are stains, but not entirely noticeable; everything has gotten dirty, worn out, and has already faded. Whoever doesn't know beforehand won't notice anything. Therefore, Nastasya couldn't have noticed anything from afar, praise be!" Then, trembling, he unsealed the notice and began reading; he read for a long time and finally understood.
