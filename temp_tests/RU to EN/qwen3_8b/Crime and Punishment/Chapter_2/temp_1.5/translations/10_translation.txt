His entire room was of such a size that one could take off a hook without getting up from the bed. So it is: the doorman and Nastasya are standing. Nastasya glanced at him strangely. He looked at the doorman with a defiant and desperate look. He silently handed him a grey, folded-in-half paper sealed with bottle seal wax. — A summons from the office, — he said, handing over the paper. — From which office? — They call the police, so they go to the office. It's known which office. — To the police!...
