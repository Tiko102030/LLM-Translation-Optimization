"By God, that's it!—he suddenly thought, seized by an unrelenting fit of wrath. Well, it's begun, so it's begun, by hell with her and the new life! How could this be, Lord, how foolish!... How much I lied and disgraced myself today! How despicably I fawned and flattered the worst Ilyich Petrovich! Well, nonsense and that too! I don't care about them all, and about the fact that I fawned and flattered! It's entirely different! It's entirely different!"

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question at once confused him and greatly astonished him:

"If indeed all this was done deliberately, not foolishly, if you indeed had a definite and firm goal, then how come you haven't even looked into your wallet yet and don't know what you've gotten, for which you've endured all these torments and deliberately went on such a low, dirty, despicable deed?"
