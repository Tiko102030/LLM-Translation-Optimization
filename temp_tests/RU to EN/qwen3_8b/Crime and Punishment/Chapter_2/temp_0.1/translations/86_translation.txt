But here comes the beer! — he moved to his chair, brought the soup and beef toward him, and started eating with such an appetite as if he hadn't eaten for three days. — I, brother Rodya, here I eat every day now, — he muttered, as much as his mouth full of beef allowed, — and this is all Pashenka, your housekeeper, who is genuinely honoring me. I, of course, don't insist, but I don't protest either. Here's Nastasya with the tea. What a quick one! Nastenka, do you want some beer? — To the devil with him! — A cup of tea?
