— Yes, Mr. Captain, and what a base guest, Mr. Captain, when in a noble house…  
— Nun-nun-nun! Enough! I've already told you, told you, I've told you…  
— Ilya Petrovich! — the clerk spoke again significantly. The ensign quickly looked at him; the clerk slightly nodded his head. — …Here you go, esteemed Laviza Ivanovna, my final word, and this is for the last time, — the ensign continued. — If there's another scandal in your noble house, even just once more, I'll take you to the cugundar, as it's said in high style. Hear that? So the literateur, the writer, took five kopecks in "the noble house" for a fold? There they are, the writers!
