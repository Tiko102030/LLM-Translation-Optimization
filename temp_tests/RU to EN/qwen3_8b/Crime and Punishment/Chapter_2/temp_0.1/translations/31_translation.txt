And he opened the window into the ditch and stood in the window, like a little pig, screaming; and this is a disgrace. And how can one scream into the street, like a little pig, screaming; and this is a disgrace. Fui-fui-fui! And Karl pulled him by the coat from the window and there, indeed, sir captain, his rock was broken. And then he shouted that he had to pay fifteen shillings as a fine. And I myself, sir captain, paid five shillings as a fine. And this is an unworthy guest, sir captain, and one who makes a scandal! I, saying, will be a big satirist against you, because I can write about you in all the newspapers. — From the writers, meaning? — Yes, sir captain, and what an unworthy guest, sir captain, when in a noble house…
