Nothing and no one; no one had looked in. Not even Nastasya had touched anything. But, my God! How could he have left all these things in this hole? He rushed to the corner, stuck his hand under the wallpaper, and started pulling out the items, loading them into his pockets. In total, there were eight items: two small boxes with earrings or something similar—he hadn't looked closely; then four small saffron-colored cases. One chain was simply wrapped in newspaper. Something else was also wrapped in newspaper, apparently an order...

He placed everything into different pockets, the coat, and the remaining right pocket of his pants, trying to make it as inconspicuous as possible. He also took the wallet along with the items. Then he left the room, this time even leaving it completely open.
