Third hour already! He sat on the couch—and suddenly everything came back to him! In an instant, he remembered everything! At first, he thought he was going mad. A terrible cold gripped him; but the cold was also from the fever, which had already begun with him in his sleep. Now, however, a sudden chill struck him so strongly that his teeth almost jumped out, and everything inside him trembled. He opened the door and began to listen: everyone in the house was completely asleep. He looked at himself in astonishment and everything around him in the room, not understanding how he could have entered yesterday, not locked the doors with the hook, and fallen onto the couch without even undressing, let alone with his hat on—it had slipped down and immediately lay on the floor near the pillow. "What would someone think if they came in? That I'm drunk, but…" He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began to examine himself, from head to toe, his entire outfit: were there any traces?
