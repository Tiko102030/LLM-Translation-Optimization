— No need, — he said, — I came… here's the thing: I have no lessons… I wanted to… in any case, I don't need lessons at all… 

— Do you know what? You're going mad! — remarked Razumikhin, who had been watching him intently. — No, I'm not raving… — Raskolnikov got up from the sofa. As he rose to face Razumikhin, he didn't consider that he would have to meet him face to face. Now, in an instant, he realized, through experience, that he was least inclined, at this moment, to face anyone in the world. All his bitterness rose within him. He almost choked on his own fury at himself for having crossed Razumikhin's threshold. — Goodbye! — he suddenly said and went toward the door.
