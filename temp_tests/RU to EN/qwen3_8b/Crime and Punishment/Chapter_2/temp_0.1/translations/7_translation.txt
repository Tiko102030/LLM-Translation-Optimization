Not even a match. No, better to go somewhere and throw everything away. Yes! Better to throw it all away!—he kept repeating, sitting down again on the couch—and right now, this very minute, without delay!... But instead of that, his head once again leaned on the pillow; once again an unbearable chill seized him; once again he pulled on his overcoat. For a long time, several hours, he still kept imagining, in fits, that "now, without delay, go somewhere and throw everything away, so that it's out of sight, as soon as possible, as soon as possible!" He tried to get up from the couch several times, wanted to rise, but already couldn't. Finally, he was awakened by a strong knock on the door. "Open up, alive or not? And all the time he's snoring!"—Nastasya shouted, pounding her fist on the door— "sleeping all day long, like a dog!"
