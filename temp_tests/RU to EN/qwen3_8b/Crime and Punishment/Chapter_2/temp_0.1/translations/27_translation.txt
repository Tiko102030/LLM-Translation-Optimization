Read once, twice, and still didn't understand. — What is this? — he asked the scribe. — This is a demand for payment under the loan note, a collection. You must either pay all expenses, interest, and other charges, or provide a written response stating when you can pay, along with an obligation not to leave the capital until payment is made and not to sell or hide your property. The creditor is free to sell your property and act according to the law. — Yes, I… owe no one! — This is not our affair. However, a protested and legally protested loan note for one hundred fifteen rubles, issued to the widow, Collegiate Assessor Zarnitsyna, nine months ago, which has now passed to the town councillor Chebarov for collection, and therefore we are inviting you to respond. — Yes, she is my housekeeper!
