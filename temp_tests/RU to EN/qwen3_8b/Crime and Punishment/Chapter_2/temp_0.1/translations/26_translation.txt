The clerk looked at them with a smile. The hot-headed captain seemed visibly puzzled. "This isn't your affair!" he shouted at last, somewhat unnaturally loudly, "and here, you may as well submit the review they require from you. Show him, Alexander Grigoryevich. Complaints about you! Don't pay money! What a bold eagle you've flown!" But Raskolnikov was already not listening and greedily grabbed the paper, seeking to quickly find the solution. He read it once, twice, and didn't understand.
