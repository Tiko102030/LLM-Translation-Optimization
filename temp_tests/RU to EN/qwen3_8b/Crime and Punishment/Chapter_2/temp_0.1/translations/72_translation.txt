He looked at her, barely breathing. — I myself heard… I didn't sleep… I was sitting, — he spoke even more timidly. — I listened for a long time… The assistant of the overseer came… Everyone ran down the stairs, from all the apartments… — No one came. But your blood is crying out. This is when she has no way out and starts to roast her liver, then hallucinations begin… Will you eat anything, at least? He didn't answer. Nastasya stood over him, stared at him intently and didn't leave. — Give me something to drink… Nastasyushka. She went downstairs and came back two minutes later with water in a white clay cup; but he already didn't remember what happened next. He remembered only how he took a sip of cold water and spilled it from the cup onto his chest.
