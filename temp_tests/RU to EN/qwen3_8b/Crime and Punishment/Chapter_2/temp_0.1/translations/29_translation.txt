— You, such a one and such another!— he suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the widow had already left),— what happened last night? a? Again, disgrace, you're causing a commotion throughout the street. Again, a fight and drunkenness. You're dreaming of peace! I've already told you, I've already warned you ten times that I won't let you go at eleven! And you again, again, you such a one and such another! Even the paper fell from Raskolnikov's hands, and he stared wildly at the plump lady, who was so rudely handled; but soon, however, he realized what was going on, and immediately this whole story began to please him very much. He listened with pleasure, so much that one wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh… All his nerves were jumping.— Ilya Petrovich!
