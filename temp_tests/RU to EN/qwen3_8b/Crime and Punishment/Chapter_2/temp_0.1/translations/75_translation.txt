Realizing he had awakened, the woman, who had been peeking through the door, immediately closed it and hid. She had always been shy and endured conversations and explanations with reluctance; she was forty years old, plump and fat, with black eyebrows and black eyes, kind because of her plumpness and laziness; she was even very smooth in her manner. She was shy beyond necessity. — "You... who are you?" he continued questioning, addressing the foreman directly. But at that moment the door opened wide, and, leaning slightly because he was tall, Razumikhin entered.
