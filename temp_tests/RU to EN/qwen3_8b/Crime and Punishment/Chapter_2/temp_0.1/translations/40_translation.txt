The call was so strong that he already rose from his place to act. "Should I think for a moment?"—ran through his mind. "No, better not to think at all and just get going!" But suddenly he stopped as if nailed to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was speaking passionately to Ilyia Petrovich, and the words reached him: "It's impossible to free both! First of all, everything contradicts; judge for yourselves: why call a servant if it's their affair? To report on themselves? Or for cunning? No, that would be too cunning! And finally, the student Pestryakov was seen by both the servants and the citizen right at the moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted from them right at the gates, asking about residence with the servants even in the presence of his friends. Well, would he start asking about residence if he came with such an intention?"
