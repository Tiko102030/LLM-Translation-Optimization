It was strange. — Well, well, that's good, — concluded Ilya Petrovich, — we won't detain you. Raskolnikov left. He could still hear a lively conversation beginning outside his room, in which Nikodim Fomich's questioning voice was most prominent... On the street, he completely came to himself. "Search, search, a search is coming! — he repeated to himself, hurrying to get there; — bandits! They suspect!" The previous fear once again enveloped him from head to toe. II

"But what if there was already a search? What if I just happen to catch them at home?"

But here is his room. Nothing and no one; no one looked in.
