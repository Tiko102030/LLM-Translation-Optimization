"If they ask, maybe I will say," he thought as he approached the office. The office was a quarter of a verst away. She had just moved to a new apartment, a new building, to the fourth floor. On the previous apartment, he had been briefly before, but a long time ago. Entering under the gates, he saw a staircase to the right, down which a man with a book in his hand was descending: "a janitor, that means; that means the office is here," and he started going up randomly. He didn't want to ask anyone anything. "I'll go in, kneel down, and tell everything..." he thought as he entered the fourth floor. The staircase was narrow, steep, and entirely in filth. All the kitchens of all the apartments in all four floors opened onto this staircase and stood like that almost all day. Because of that, there was a terrible stuffiness.
