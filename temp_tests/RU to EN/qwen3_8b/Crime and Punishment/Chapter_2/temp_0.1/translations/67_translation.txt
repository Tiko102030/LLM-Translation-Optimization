It seemed to him that he had cut himself off from everyone and everything as if with scissors at that moment. He came to himself by evening, which meant he had walked only six hours. He did not remember where or how he had walked back. Undressing and trembling all over like a driven horse, he lay on the sofa, pulled on his overcoat, and immediately fell asleep...

He awoke in the full dusk from a terrible scream. God, what a scream! Such unnatural sounds, such howling, wailing, screeching, tears, brawling, and swearing he had never heard or seen before. He could not even imagine such cruelty, such frenzy. In fear, he raised himself up and sat on his bed, trembling and suffering every moment. But the fights, screams, and swearing became stronger and stronger. And here, to his greatest surprise, he suddenly recognized his mistress's voice.
