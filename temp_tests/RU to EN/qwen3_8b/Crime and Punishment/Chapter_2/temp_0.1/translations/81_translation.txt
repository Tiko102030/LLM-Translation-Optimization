What have you got there, a book, by the way? — A book, you see, here you are. — Give it here. Well, Rodia, get up. I'll hold you; give him Raskolnikov, take the pen, for goodness' sake, brother, money is now more precious than syrup. — No need, — said Raskolnikov, pushing away the pen. — Why not? — I won't sign. — Blazes, how can we do without a signature? — No need… money… — This is not needed, the money!
