There they are, the writers!—and he cast a disdainful glance at Raskolnikov. —Another incident happened in the tavern three days ago: he had lunch but refused to pay; "I'll describe you in satire," he said. On the ship, too, another incident occurred last week: a respected family of a state councillor, his wife and daughter, were insulted with the most vulgar words. Just yesterday, from the confectionery, I was thrown out for some reason. That's how they are, the writers, intellectuals, students, agitators… damn it! Go ahead, you! I'll come myself… then be careful! Did you hear? Louise Ivanovna, with a falsely polite manner, began to bow in all directions, and while bowing, she managed to reach the doors; but she bumped into a prominent officer at the door, with a fresh, open face and magnificent thick blond curls.
