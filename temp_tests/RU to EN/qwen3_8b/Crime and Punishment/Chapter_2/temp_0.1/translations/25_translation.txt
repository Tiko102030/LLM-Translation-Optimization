— Don't shout! — I am not shouting, but speaking very calmly, and it is you who are shouting at me; I am a student, and I will not allow myself to shout. The assistant was so enraged that for the first moment he couldn't utter a word, and only some droplets were spilling from his mouth. He jumped up from his seat. — Be quiet, you are in the presence. Don't be a rude person, sir! — And you are also in the presence, — Raskolnikov shouted, — besides shouting, you are smoking a cigarette, so you are spoiling us all. — After saying this, Raskolnikov felt an indescribable pleasure. The clerk looked at them with a smile.
