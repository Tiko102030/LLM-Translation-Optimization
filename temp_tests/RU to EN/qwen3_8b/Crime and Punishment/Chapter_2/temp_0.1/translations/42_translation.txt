"No, the matter is very unclear," added Ilya Petrovich. Raskolnikov raised his hat and went to the doors, but he did not reach the doors... When he awoke, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, supported on the right by a person, on the left stood another person with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nicodim Fomich stood before him, staring intently at him; he stood up from the chair. "What is this, are you ill?" rather sharply asked Nicodim Fomich. "They signed so faintly, barely with a pen," noted the scribe, sitting down at his place and resuming his work on the papers. "How long have you been ill?" shouted Ilya Petrovich from his place, also sorting through the papers.
