He looked at her, hardly breathing. — I heard it myself... I didn't sleep... I was sitting, — he muttered even more hesitantly. — I listened for a long time... The assistant overseer came... Everyone ran to the stairs, from all the apartments... — Nobody came. But your blood is screaming. It's when she has no way out and her organs start to roast, then it starts to hallucinate... Will you at least eat? He didn't answer. Nastasia kept standing over him, intensely staring at him and didn't leave. — Give me something to drink... Nastasia. She went downstairs and came back two minutes later with water in a white clay cup; but he already didn't remember what came afterward. He remembered only how he took one sip of cold water and spilled it from the cup onto his chest.
