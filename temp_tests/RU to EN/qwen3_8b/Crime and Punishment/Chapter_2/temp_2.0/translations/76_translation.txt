But at that moment the door flew open once more, and, slightly bending down because he was tall, Razumichin entered. — What a nautical cabin! — he cried upon entering, — always banging my forehead against it; it's called a apartment, too! And you, brother, have awakened? Just heard from Pashenkin. — I've just awakened, — said Nastasya. — We've just awakened, — the artel'shik again nodded with a smirk. — And who are you yourselves, pray? — Razumichin suddenly addressed him. — Let me tell you, you see, I am Vrazumikhin; not Razumichin, as everyone calls me, but Vrazumikhin, a student, son of a nobleman, and he is my friend. Well, and you, pray, who are you?
