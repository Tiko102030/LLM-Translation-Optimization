Nastasya, so it follows, couldn't notice anything from afar, praise be! Then, trembling, he unwrapped the summons and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood. It was an ordinary summons from the district to appear today at half past ten in the district supervisor's office. "When have I ever done that? I have no dealings with the police on my own! And why precisely today?" — he thought in painful bewilderment. "Lord, just quickly!" He was about to kneel to pray, but even he laughed — not at the prayer, but at himself. He hurriedly began to dress. "So be it, I'll go regardless! Put on a sock!"
