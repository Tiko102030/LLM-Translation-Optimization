Here's the beer! — he moved to his chair, brought the soup and beef closer, and started eating with such an appetite as if he hadn't touched food in three days. — I, brother Rodia, have lunch like this every day here, — he muttered, as his beef-filled mouth allowed, — and that's all Pashenka, your mistress, who's enthusiastically treating me with honor. Of course, I don't insist, nor do I protest. Here's Nastasya with the tea. What nimbleness! Nastya, do you want some beer? — And to the prankster! — How about a bit of tea?
