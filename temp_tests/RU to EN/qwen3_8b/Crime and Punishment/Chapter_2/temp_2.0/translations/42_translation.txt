Raskolnikov lifted his hat and went toward the doors, but he didn't reach them... When he awoke, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, supported to his right by someone, that to his left stood another person with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nicholay Fomichev stood before him, intently staring at him; he stood up from the chair. "— What's this, are you ill?" he rather sharply asked Nicholay Fomichev. "— They barely even signed, barely even traced with a pen," noted the clerk, sitting down at his place and resuming his paperwork. "— Have you been ill long?" shouted Ilya Petrovich from his place, also sorting through papers.
