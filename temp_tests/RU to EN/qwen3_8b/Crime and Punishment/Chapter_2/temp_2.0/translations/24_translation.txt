"Money? What money? — Raskolnikov thought, — but... obviously, it's not that!" And he trembled with joy. Suddenly, it became unbearably, impossibly easy for him. Everything fell from his shoulders. — And what time do you have to come, my lord? — shouted the ensign, more and more irritated without knowing why — You are told to come at nine, but now it's already eleven! — They brought it to me just a quarter of an hour ago, — answered Raskolnikov loudly, over his shoulder, also suddenly and unexpectedly for himself becoming enraged, even finding some pleasure in it. — And it's quite enough that I came feverish and ill. — Don't shout!
