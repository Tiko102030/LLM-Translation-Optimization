Though he could barely fully comprehend at that moment, he felt that he would not be treated properly when they came to take him. "But… the police?" — Would you like some tea? Well, whatever; I'll bring it; there's some left… — No… I'll go: I'll go now," he muttered, rising to his feet. "Go ahead, and you won't be able to descend the stairs?" — I'll go… — As you wish. She went after the janitor. He immediately rushed to the light to examine the sock and tucks: "There are stains, but they are not entirely noticeable; everything is dirty, worn out, and has already faded. Whoever doesn't know in advance won't notice anything. Nastasya, therefore, could not have noticed anything from afar, thanks be!" Then, with trembling, he opened the summons and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood.
