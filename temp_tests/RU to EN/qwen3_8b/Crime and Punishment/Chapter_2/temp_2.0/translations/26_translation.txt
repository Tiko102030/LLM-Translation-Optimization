The clerk smiled at them. The hot young ensign seemed flustered. "It's not your business, you know!" he finally shouted unnaturally loudly, "but you may as well submit the report they demand from you. Show him, Alexander Gregoryevich. Complaints about you! No money to be paid! What a bold hawk you are!" But Raskolnikov no longer listened and greedily grabbed the paper, seeking quick answers. He read it once, twice, and still didn't understand.
