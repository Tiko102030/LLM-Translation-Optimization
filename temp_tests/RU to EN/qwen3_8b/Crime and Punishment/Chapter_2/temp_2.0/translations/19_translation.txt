No one noticed him. In the second room, some scribes were sitting and writing, dressed a bit better than him, and they all looked like an oddly strange crowd. He approached one of them. — What do you want? He showed him a summons from the office. — Are you a student? — asked the other, looking at the summons. — Yes, a former student. The scribe looked him over, though without any curiosity. He was some particularly disheveled person with an unmoving idea in his gaze.
