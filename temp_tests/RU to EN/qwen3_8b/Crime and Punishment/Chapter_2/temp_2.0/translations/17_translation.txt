"If they ask, I might say," he thought, approaching the office. The office was a quarter of a versta away. She had just moved to a new apartment, a new building, to the fourth floor. He had been there briefly on the previous apartment, but a long time ago. After entering under the gates, he saw a narrow, steep staircase to the right, down which a man was descending with a book in his hand: "That's the janitor; so the office must be here," and he began climbing up at random. He didn't want to ask anyone anything. "I'll go in, kneel down, and confess everything..." he thought, entering the fourth floor. The staircase was narrow, steep, and completely filled with filth. All the kitchens of the apartments in all four floors opened onto this staircase and remained that way almost all day. That's why it was horribly stuffy.
