At that moment, a beam of sunlight lit his left boot: on the toe, which protruded from the boot as if signs had appeared. He kicked off the boot: "Indeed, signs! The entire toe sole is soaked in blood"; he must have stepped carelessly into that puddle then... "But now, what to do with this? Where to put this sock, the wrinkle, the pocket?" He gathered all this in his hand and stood in the middle of the room. "Into the stove? But they would first start digging in the stove. To burn it? And with what to burn it? There are even no matches."
