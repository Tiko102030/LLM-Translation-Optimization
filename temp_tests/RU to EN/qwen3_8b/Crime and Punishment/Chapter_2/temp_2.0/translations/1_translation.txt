"It's already three o'clock!" He sat on the sofa — and all at once, he remembered everything! All at once, he suddenly remembered everything! At first, he thought he was losing his mind. A terrible cold surrounded him; but the cold was also from a fever that had long begun with him in his dream. Now, suddenly, a chill hit him so violently that his teeth would have jumped out, and everything inside him was churning. He opened the door and began listening: everyone in the house was entirely asleep. With disbelief, he looked at himself and everything around him in the room, not understanding how he could have, upon entering yesterday, failed to lock the doors with a hook and flung himself onto the couch, not only not undressing but still in his hat: it had slipped down and immediately lay on the floor near the pillow. "If someone had come in, what would they have thought? That I am drunk, but..." He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he hurried to examine himself, everything from his feet to his head, his entire outfit: were there any traces?"
