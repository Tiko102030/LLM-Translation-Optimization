Not even a match. No, better to go somewhere and throw everything out. Yes! Better to throw it all out!" he kept repeating, sitting again on the sofa, "and right now, this very minute, without delay!.." But instead, his head again inclined onto the pillow; again the unbearable chills seized him; again he pulled the greatcoat over himself. And for a long time, several hours, he still kept feeling impulses to go somewhere and throw everything out, so that it would be out of sight, as quickly as possible, as quickly as possible! He several times tried to get up from the sofa, wanted to stand up, but already couldn't. Finally, he was awakened by a loud knock on the door. — Open up, is he alive or not? And all the time he's been sleeping! — cried Nastasya, knocking with her fist on the door, — whole days, entire days, like a dog, he's been sleeping!
