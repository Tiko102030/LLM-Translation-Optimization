There was a terrible stuffiness. Sweepers with books under their arms ascended and descended, along with a mixed crowd of people of both sexes—visitors. The door leading into the main office was also wide open. He entered and stopped in the hallway. There, everyone was standing and waiting for some men. There was also an extraordinary stuffiness here, and additionally, the scent of fresh, still undried paint on stale varnish provoked a nauseating smell. After waiting for a while, he decided to move forward into the next room. All the rooms were tiny and low. A terrifying impatience was pulling him further and further. No one noticed him.
