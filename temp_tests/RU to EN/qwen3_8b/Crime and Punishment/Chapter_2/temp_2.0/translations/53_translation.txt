“By thunder, that's it! — he suddenly thought, overcome by relentless wrath. — Well, it has begun, and so be it! Let hell take the new life! How foolish, Lord!… How many lies I spread and misrepresented today! How vilely I fawned and played my part with the worst Ilyi Petrovich! But, in any case, that's all folly! I don’t care about them all and about the fact that I fawned and played my part! Not at all! Not at all!…” Suddenly he stopped; a new, utterly unexpected, and extremely simple question at once confused him and greatly amazed him: “If everything was truly done with intention, not by foolishness, if you actually had a definite and firm aim, then how come you have not even glanced into your wallet so far and don’t know what you have received, from what you took all these pains, and why you deliberately went upon such a low, dirty, and despicable undertaking?”
