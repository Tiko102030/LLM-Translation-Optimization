Realizing that he had awoken, the landlady, who had been peeking through the door, quickly closed it and hid. She had always been shy and endured conversations and explanations with reluctance; she was forty, plump and corpulent, dark-browed and dark-eyed, good-natured from her plumpness and laziness; and she was even extremely smug. And shamefaced beyond necessity. — Who are you? — he continued interrogating, addressing the laborer directly. But at that moment the door burst open again, and, slightly bending down because he was tall, Razumikhin entered.
