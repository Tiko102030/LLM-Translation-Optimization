What have you got, a book, you know?—A book, you know.—Pass it here. Well, Rodia, come up. I'll hold you; give him a good slap with the quill, take the pen, brother, money is now more than honey for us.—No need, said Raskolnikov, pushing the pen away.—Why not?—I won't sign.—Blazhennyy, how can we proceed without a signature?—No need… money…—We don't need the money!
