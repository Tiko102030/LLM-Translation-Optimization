This very apartment, the current one, I've forgotten; in fact, I never remembered it because I didn't know it. As for the previous apartment—I only remember that it was at Pяти Углов, Харламова дом. I was searching for the Харламов home, but it turned out that it wasn't actually the Харламов home at all, but Буха—how sometimes you get the sounds mixed up! Well, I got angry, and without delay I went to the address office the next day, and guess what—there, they found me in two minutes. You are registered there.
