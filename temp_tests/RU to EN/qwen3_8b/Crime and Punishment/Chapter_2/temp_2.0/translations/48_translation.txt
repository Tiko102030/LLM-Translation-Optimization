What if the cases didn't sink but floated? Yes, that's certainly the case. Everyone will see it. In any case, people are already looking at each other, scrutinizing one another as if their sole concern was him. "Why not, or perhaps it's just me," he thought. Finally, it occurred to him that perhaps it would be better to go somewhere on the Neva. There are fewer people, it's less noticeable, and at any rate more convenient, above all, it's farther away from here. He suddenly realized how could it be that he had wandered for half an hour in melancholy and anxiety, in dangerous places, and had never imagined this before! And precisely because he had spent half an hour on this reckless endeavor, it had already been decided once in a dream, in a fit of madness! He had become extremely distracted and forgetful, and he knew it.
