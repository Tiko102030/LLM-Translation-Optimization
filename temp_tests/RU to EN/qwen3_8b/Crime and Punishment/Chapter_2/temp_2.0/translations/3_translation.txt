What is this? He immediately seized them, removing and throwing them onto the table. Choosing everything, even turning inside his pockets to make sure nothing remained, he moved the whole pile to the corner. There, at the very corner, beneath, in one spot, were torn-off, discarded wall coverings: immediately he began to jam everything into that hole under the paper: "It's in! All gone and the wallet too!" — joyfully he thought, leaping up and staring stupidly at the corner, at the more gaping hole. Suddenly, he jerked in horror: "Good Lord, — he muttered in despair, — what's happening to me? Is it hidden? Do they hide like that?" In fact, he hadn't expected any belongings; he thought that money alone was hidden, thus not having prepared a place beforehand, — "but now, now why am I happy? — he thought. — Do they hide like that?"
