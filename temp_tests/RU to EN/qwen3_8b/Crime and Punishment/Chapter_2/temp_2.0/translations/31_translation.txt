And he opened the window and stood by it, like a little pig squealing; and that's disgraceful. And how can one squeal like a little pig by the window to the street; and that's disgraceful. Ugh-ugh-ugh! And Carl dragged him by the coat from the window, and really, sir captain, his noble rock was broken. And then he shouted that he had to pay a fine of fifteen crowns. And I myself, sir captain, gave him five crowns as a fine. And this is a lowly guest, sir captain, and a troublemaker! I, saying so, will be a big satire writer, for I can write everything about you in all the newspapers. — From authors, that is? — Yes, sir captain, and what kind of lowly guest, sir captain, is it when in a noble home… — Well, well, well!
