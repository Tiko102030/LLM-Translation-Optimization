"The torn pieces of linen will never raise suspicion; it seems so, it seems so!" he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with an almost torturous intensity, began searching around again, on the floor and everywhere, ensuring he had not forgotten anything. The certainty that everything remained, even memory, even the simplest reasoning, started to torture him mercilessly. "What, is it really beginning, is the execution upon me? Look, look, so it is!" Indeed, the frayed fringe, which he had cut from his trousers, lay scattered on the floor in the middle of the room, waiting to be seen by the first passerby! "What is happening to me!" he exclaimed again, as if lost. Suddenly an odd thought came to him: perhaps his entire attire was stained with blood, perhaps there were many spots, yet he simply could not see them, did not notice them, since his reasoning had grown feeble, fragmented… his mind was darkened… Suddenly he remembered that there was blood on his money clip. "Bah! So it means there should also be blood in the pocket, since I had just put the damp money clip into my pocket!" In an instant, he turned his pocket inside out, and—there it was—on the inside of the pocket there were traces, stains! "Therefore, I have not completely lost my wits, there is still reasoning and memory, since I have noticed it myself and figured it out!" he thought to himself triumphantly, drawing a deep and joyful breath from his entire chest, "it is only the weakness of the fever, a moment of delirium!" And he tore out the entire lining from the left pocket of his trousers.
