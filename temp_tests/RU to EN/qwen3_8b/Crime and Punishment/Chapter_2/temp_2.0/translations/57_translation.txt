— No need, — he said, — I came… that is, I have no lessons… I was about to… in any case, I don't need any lessons at all…

— Do you know what? You're going mad! — remarked Razumikhin, who was watching him intently. — No, I'm not… — Raskolnikov stood up from the couch. As he rose to approach Razumikhin, he didn't realize that he would have to confront him face to face. Now, in an instant, he realized, through experience, that he was least inclined, at this moment, to face anyone in the whole world. All his bile rose within him. He almost choked on his own wrath for having crossed Razumikhin's threshold. — Goodbye! — he said suddenly and walked towards the door.
