— "Mrs. Louise, you'd sit," he said to the abruptly dressed blood-red lady, who stood still, as if unable to sit down herself, although the chair was right there. "Ich danke," [17 - Thank you (German) — Ed.] she said, and gently descended onto the chair with a silk-like rustle. Her light blue dress, adorned with white lace, spread around the chair like a balloon, filling almost half the room with its fragrance. Yet the lady, obviously feeling self-conscious about both her presence filling the room and the fragrance wafting off her, though she smiled timidly and proudly in an impudent manner, clearly with a visible sense of nervousness. The mournful lady finally finished and began to rise, when suddenly, with quite a racket, rather proudly and particularly swinging her shoulders with each step, an officer entered, flung his cockaded cap onto the table, and sat in the chair. The fluffy lady hopped up upon spotting him, and commenced curtsying with a kind of special, rapturous enthusiasm, yet the officer paid her no attention at all, and she could no longer even dare to sit down in his presence.
