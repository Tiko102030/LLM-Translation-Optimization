Read once, twice, and didn't understand. — What is this? — he asked the scribe. — This is a claim for money from you through the loan document, a garnishment. You must either pay all costs, penalties, and other charges, or provide a written response stating when you can pay, and at the same time commit not to leave the capital until payment is made and not to sell or hide your property. The lender is allowed to sell your property and deal with you according to the law. — Indeed, I... owe no one! — This is not our affair. However, we have received a notice for a overdue and legally protested loan document for 150 rubles issued to you for the widow Zarnitsina, who was a collegiate assistant, nine months ago, and now passed for payment to the town councillor Chubarov. Therefore, we invite you to provide a written response. — But she's my housekeeper!
