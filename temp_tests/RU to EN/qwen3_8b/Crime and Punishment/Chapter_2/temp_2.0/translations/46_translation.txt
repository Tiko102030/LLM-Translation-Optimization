Nothing and nobody; no one came in. Even Nastasya did not touch. But, goodness me! How could he leave all these things in this hole? He rushed to the corner, pushed his hand under the wallpaper, and began pulling out the items, loading them into his pockets. There were eight items: two small boxes with earrings or something like that—he hadn't looked carefully; then four small sapphire cases. One necklace was simply wrapped in newspaper. Something else in newspaper, seems like an order… He put everything into different pockets, in the coat and in the remaining right pocket of the pants, trying to make it inconspicuous. The wallet also took along with the items. Then he left the room, this time even leaving it completely open.
