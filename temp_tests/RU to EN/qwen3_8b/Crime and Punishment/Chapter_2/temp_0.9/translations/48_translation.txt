And what if the cases don't sink but float? Indeed, that's the case. Everyone will see. And already, people look at each other, glancing as if their whole concern is him. "Why should it be so, or perhaps it's just me imagining," he thought. Finally, it occurred to him that perhaps it would be better to go somewhere on the Neva. There are fewer people, it's less noticeable, and at least more convenient; above all, it's farther from these places. He suddenly realized: how could he have wandered for half an hour in despair and anxiety, in dangerous places, and yet couldn't imagine this before! And precisely because of that, he spent half an hour on a reckless endeavor, which had already been decided once in a dream, in a delirium! He became extremely absent-minded and forgetful, and he knew it.
