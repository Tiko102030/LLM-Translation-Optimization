He looked at her, barely breathing. — I heard it myself… I didn't sleep… I sat there, — he spoke even more timidly. — I listened for a long time… The overseer's assistant came… Everyone ran down the stairs, from all the apartments… — No one came. But your blood is screaming. That's when she has no way out and starts roasting her liver, and then hallucinations begin… Will you eat at all? He didn't answer. Nastasia stood over him, stared at him intently, and didn't leave. — Give me something to drink… Nastasia. She went downstairs and came back in two minutes with water in a white earthenware cup; but he already didn't remember what came next. He remembered only how he took a sip of cold water and spilled it from the cup onto his chest.
