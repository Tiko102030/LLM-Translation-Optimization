There they are, the scribblers!—and he cast a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. Three days ago, there was an incident in the tavern: he had lunch, but refused to pay; "I shall describe you in satire," he said. On the ship, too, another incident occurred last week: a respectable family of a state councillor, his wife and daughter, were insulted with the most vile words. From the confectionery, they expelled one of them in a hurry. That's how they are, the scribblers, writers, students, mouthpieces… Tchugh! Go away! I'll come myself to see you—then beware! Did you hear? Luiza Ivanovna, with an affectedly polite manner, started to sit down everywhere, and while sitting down, she finally reached the doors; but she bumped into an officer's back at the door, a man with an open, fresh face and excellent thick golden ringlets.
