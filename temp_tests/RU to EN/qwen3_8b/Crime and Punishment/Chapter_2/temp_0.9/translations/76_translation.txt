But at that moment the door opened wide again, and, slightly bending down because he was tall, Razumikhin entered. "What a sea cabin!" he shouted as he entered, "I always bump my forehead; it's also called an apartment! And you, brother, have you come to your senses? I just heard from Pashenka." "Just came to my senses," said Nastasya. "Just came to their senses," nodded the artel' shik again with a smile. "And who are you, I ask you?" asked Razumikhin, suddenly addressing him. "Well, I am Vrazumikhin, as you can see; not Razumikhin, as everyone calls me, but Vrazumikhin, a student, son of a nobleman, and he is my friend. Well, and who are you?"
