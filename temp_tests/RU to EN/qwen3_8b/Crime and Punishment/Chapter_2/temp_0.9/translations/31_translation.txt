And he opened the window and stood by the window, like a little pig, squealing; and this is a disgrace. And how can one be in the window on the street, like a little pig, squealing; and this is a disgrace. Fuh-fuh-fuh! And Karl pulled him by the coat from the window, and here, it's true, Mr. Captain, he broke his rock. And then he shouted that he had to pay fifteen shillings as a fine. And I myself, Mr. Captain, paid five shillings to him as a fine. And this is an ungentlemanly guest, Mr. Captain, and makes every scandal! I, speaking, will be a big satirist, because I can write about you in all the newspapers. — From the writers, you mean? — Yes, Mr. Captain, and what kind of an ungentlemanly guest, Mr. Captain, when in a noble house… — Nun-nun-nun!
