The call was so strong that he was already standing up to carry it out. "Should I think about it for a minute?"—ran through his mind. "No, better not to think at all and just get rid of it!" But suddenly he froze like a statue: Nikodim Fomich was speaking passionately to Ilyia Petrovich, and the words reached him: "It can't be, both will be freed! First of all, everything contradicts; judge: why call a watchman if it's their business? To report on themselves? Or for cunning? No, that would be too cunning! And finally, the student Pestryakov was seen near the gates by both watchmen and the merchant's wife at the exact moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted from them near the gates and inquired about the residents of the watchmen, still with his friends. Well, he would start inquiring about the residents if he came with such an intent?"
