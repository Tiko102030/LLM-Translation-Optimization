The steno-typist looked at them with a smile. The brave ensign seemed clearly perplexed. "This is not your affair!" he shouted finally in an unnaturally loud voice, "but you may as well submit the review they require from you. Show him, Alexander Grigoryevich. Complaints about you! Don't pay money! What a bold eagle you've flown!" But Raskolnikov was already not listening and greedily grabbed the paper, seeking to quickly find the solution. He read it once, twice, and still did not understand.
