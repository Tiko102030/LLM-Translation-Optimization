Nothing and nobody; no one had looked in. Even Nastasya hadn't touched anything. But, Lord! How could he have left all these things in this hole before? He darted to the corner, stuck his hand under the wallpaper, and started pulling out items, filling his pockets with them. There were eight items in total: two small boxes with earrings or something like that—he didn't look closely; then four small saffian leather cases. One chain was simply wrapped in newspaper. Something else in newspaper, seems like an order… He put everything into different pockets, in the coat and the remaining right pocket of the pants, trying to make it inconspicuous. He also took the wallet along with the items. Then he left the room, this time even leaving it completely open.
