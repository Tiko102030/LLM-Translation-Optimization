It seemed to him as if he had cut himself off from everything and everyone at that very moment. He had returned to himself by evening, which meant he had walked for only six hours. He didn’t remember where or how he had gone back. Undressing and trembling all over like a driven horse, he lay on the sofa, pulled on his coat, and immediately fell asleep...

He awoke in pitch darkness from a terrible scream. God, what was that scream! Such unnatural sounds, howling, wailing, screeching, tears, abuse, and curses—he had never before heard or seen anything like it. He couldn’t even imagine such cruelty, such frenzy. In terror, he raised himself up and sat on his bed, every moment freezing and suffering. But the fighting, the screams, and the curses grew stronger and stronger. And here, to his greatest amazement, he suddenly recognized his mistress's voice.
