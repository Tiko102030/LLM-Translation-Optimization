Realizing he had woken up, the housekeeper, who had been peeking through the door, immediately closed it and hid. She had always been shy and bore conversations and explanations with reluctance; she was about forty, plump and corpulent, with dark eyebrows and dark eyes, kind from her plumpness and laziness; and she was very unctuous in her manner. She was shy beyond necessity. —Who are you?" —he continued questioning, addressing the collective member directly. But at this moment the door flew open wide, and, leaning slightly forward because he was tall, Razumikhin entered.
