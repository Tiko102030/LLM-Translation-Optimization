When he awoke, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that someone was supporting him on the right, that another person was standing on the left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nikodim Fomich was standing before him, looking intently at him; he rose from the chair. "What is this, are you ill?" rather sharply asked Nikodim Fomich. "They signed so feebly, with such a feeble stroke of the pen," remarked the clerk, sitting down in his place and resuming his work on the papers. "How long have you been ill?" shouted Ilyia Petrovich from his place, also sorting through the papers.
