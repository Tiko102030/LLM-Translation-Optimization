Although he could hardly make much sense of the situation at that moment, he felt that people would not treat him properly when they came to take him. "But… the police?" — Would you like some tea? If you want, I can bring some; there's still some left… — No… I'll go: I'll go now," he muttered, standing up. "Go, and you won't get down from the stairs?" — I'll go… — As you like. She went after the janitor. He immediately ran to the light to examine the sock and the fringe: "There are stains, but not very noticeable; everything got dirty, wore out, and has already faded. Who doesn't know in advance won't notice anything. Nastasya, therefore, couldn't have noticed anything from a distance, praise be!" Then, trembling, he opened the summons and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood.
