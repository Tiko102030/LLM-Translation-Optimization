No one noticed him. In the second room, some scribes sat and wrote, dressed only a little better than him, and from the look of them, they were quite a strange sort of people. He turned to one of them. "What's up?" He showed a summons from the office. "Are you a student?" the man asked, looking at the summons. "Yes, a former student." The scribe glanced at him, nevertheless without any curiosity. He was some particularly disheveled person with a fixed idea in his gaze.
