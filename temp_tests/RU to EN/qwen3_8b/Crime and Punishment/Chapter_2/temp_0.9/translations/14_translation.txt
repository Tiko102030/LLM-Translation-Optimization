Nastasya, in other words, couldn't have noticed anything from a distance, thank goodness! Then, trembling, he unsealed the notice and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood. It was an ordinary notice from the district to appear today, at half past ten, in the office of the district overseer. "When did this ever happen? I have no dealings with the police on my own!" And why today? — he thought in agonizing confusion. — Lord, just hurry up! He was about to kneel down to pray, but even he laughed — not at the prayer, but at himself. He hurried to dress. "I'll be lost either way! Put on a sock!"
