Money? What money? — Raskolnikov thought, — but... surely it's not that! And he trembled with joy. It suddenly became to him extremely, unimaginably easy. Everything fell from his shoulders. — And in which hour are you to come, dear sir? — shouted the officer, increasingly unsure of what to be offended by — you are written to come at nine, but now it's already twelve o'clock! — They brought me just a quarter of an hour ago, — Raskolnikov answered loudly, from behind his shoulder, also suddenly and unexpectedly for himself becoming angry and even finding some pleasure in it. — That's enough, that I came here sick with fever. — Do not shout!
