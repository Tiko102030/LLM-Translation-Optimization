What is this? He dashed to remove them and throw them onto the table. After selecting everything, even turning inside out his pockets to make sure nothing was left, he moved all this pile to the corner. There, in the very corner, at the bottom, in one place, the wallpaper that had peeled away from the wall was torn: immediately he began stuffing everything into this hole, under the paper: "It's in! Everything out of sight and the wallet too!"—he thought joyfully, standing up and staring blankly at the corner, at the hole that had grown even larger. Suddenly he trembled all over with fear: "My God," he whispered in despair, "what is happening to me? Is this hidden? Is this how one hides?" Indeed, he had not expected any items; he thought there would be only money, and therefore had not prepared a place in advance—"But now, now what am I happy about?" he thought. "Is this how one hides?"
