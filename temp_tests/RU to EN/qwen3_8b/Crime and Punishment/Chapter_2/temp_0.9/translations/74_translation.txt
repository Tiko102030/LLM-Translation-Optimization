Then he tried to move away, wanted to run, but someone always stopped him by force, and he would again fall into helplessness and confusion. Finally, he completely came to himself. This happened in the morning, at ten o'clock. At this hour of the morning, on clear days, the sun always swept across his right wall in a long stripe, illuminating the corner by the door. By his bed stood Nastasya and another person, who was staring at him with great curiosity and completely unknown to him. He was a young man in a kaftan, with a beard, and from appearance, he resembled an artelshik. The mistress peered out from the half-open door. Raskolnikov raised himself. — Who is this, Nastasya? — he asked, pointing at the young man.
