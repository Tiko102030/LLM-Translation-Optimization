— And you, so peculiar and such, — he suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the widow had already left), — what happened last night? Ah? Again disgrace, making a commotion all over the street. Again a fight and drunkenness. You dream of being calm! I've already told you, I've warned you ten times that I won't let you down in the eleventh! And you again, again, so peculiar you are! Even a paper fell from Raskolnikov's hands, and he wildly looked at the affluent lady who was so rudely handled; but soon, however, he realized what was going on, and immediately this entire story began to very much please him. He listened with enjoyment, so much that he wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh... All his nerves were jumping. — Ilyich Petrovich!
