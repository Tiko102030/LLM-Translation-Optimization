Not even a match. No, better to go somewhere and throw everything away. Yes! Better to throw it all away! — he kept repeating, sitting down again on the sofa — and right now, at this very moment, without delay!.. But instead of that, his head again leaned on the pillow; again an unbearable chill gripped him; again he pulled his overcoat over himself. And for a long time, several hours, he still kept imagining, in fits, that "now, without delay, he should go somewhere and throw everything away, so that it's finally out of sight, as soon as possible, as soon as possible!" He tried to get up from the sofa several times, wanted to stand up, but already couldn't. He was finally woken up by a strong knock on the door. — Open up, alive or not? And all the time he's sleeping! — Nastasya shouted, pounding her fist on the door — sleeping all day, like a dog, all day long!
