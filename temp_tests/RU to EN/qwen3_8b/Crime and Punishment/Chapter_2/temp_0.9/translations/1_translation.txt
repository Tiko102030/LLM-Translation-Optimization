"Three hours already!" He sat on the couch—and then suddenly remembered everything! All at once, in an instant, he remembered everything! At first, he thought he was going mad. A terrible cold gripped him; but this cold was also from the fever that had already begun with him in his sleep. Now, however, a sudden shiver struck him so strongly that his teeth almost came out, and everything inside him trembled. He opened the door and began to listen: everyone in the house was completely asleep. He looked at himself in astonishment and everything around him in the room and didn't understand: how could he, yesterday, having entered, not lock the doors with a hook and flung himself onto the couch, not only without undressing but even with his hat on? It had slipped down and lay on the floor, near the pillow. "What would someone have thought if they had come in? That I was drunk, but..." He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began to examine himself, everything, from his feet to his head, his entire attire: were there any traces?
