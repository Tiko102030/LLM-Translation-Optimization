"By God, that's it!—he thought suddenly, seized by an unrelenting fit of wrath. Well, here we go, so be it—let hell take the new life! How foolish, Lord!... How much I lied and disgraced myself today! How vile I fawned and flattered the worst Ilyich Petrovich! In short, nonsense even this! I care nothing for them all, and for the fact that I fawned and flattered! It's not that at all! It's not that at all!..." 

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected, and extremely simple question at once confused him and greatly astonished him: "If indeed all this was done intentionally, not foolishly, if you indeed had a definite and firm goal, then how come you haven't even looked into your wallet yet and don't know what you've received, for which you've endured all these torments and intentionally went through such a base, dirty, low act?"
