The call was so strong that he already rose from his place to act. "Should I think for a moment?"—ran through his mind. "No, better not to think at all and just get going!" But suddenly he stopped as if rooted to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was speaking passionately to Ilya Petrovich, and the words reached him: "It's impossible to free both! First of all, everything contradicts; judge for yourselves: why call a janitor if it's their affair? To report on themselves? Or for cunning? No, that would be too cunning! And finally, the student Pestryakov was seen by both the janitors and the merchant's wife exactly at the moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted from them right at the gates, asking about residence with the janitors even in the presence of his friends. Well, would he start asking about residence if he had such an intention?"
