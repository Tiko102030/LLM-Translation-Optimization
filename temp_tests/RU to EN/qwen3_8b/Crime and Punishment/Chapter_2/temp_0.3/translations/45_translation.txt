It was strange. — Well, well, that's good, — concluded Ilia Petrovich, — we won't detain you. Raskolnikov left. He could still hear a lively conversation beginning as he exited, in which Nikodim Fomich's questioning voice was most clearly heard… On the street, he completely came to himself. "A search, a search, a search is coming! — he repeated to himself, hurrying to get there; — bandits! They suspect!" The previous fear once again gripped him from feet to head. II

"But what if there was already a search? What if I just happen to catch them at home?"
