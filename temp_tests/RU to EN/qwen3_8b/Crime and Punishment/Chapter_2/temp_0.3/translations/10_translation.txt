His entire room was so large that you could take off a hook without getting out of bed. So it is: the janitor and Nastasya are standing. Nastasya looked at him in some strange way. He looked at the janitor with a defiant and desperate expression. The janitor silently handed him a gray, folded-in-half paper sealed with bottle-seal wax. "A summons from the office," he said, handing over the paper. "From which office?" "They call to the police, meaning to the office. It's known which office." "To the police!"
