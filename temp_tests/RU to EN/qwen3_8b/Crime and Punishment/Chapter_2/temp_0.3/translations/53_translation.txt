"By God, that's it!—he suddenly thought to himself in a fit of unrelenting wrath. Well, it's begun, so be it, let her go to hell and the new life! How could this be, Lord, how foolish!... And how much I lied and debased myself today! How despicable I was, bowing and flattering the most wretched Ilya Petrovich! Well, nonsense even that! I care nothing for them all, nor for the fact that I bowed and flattered! Not at all! Not at all!..."

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question at once confused him and greatly astonished him:

"If indeed all this was done deliberately, not foolishly, if you indeed had a definite and firm goal, then how is it that you have not even looked into your wallet yet and don't know what you've received, for which you've endured all this suffering and deliberately embarked on such a vile, dirty, lowly deed?"
