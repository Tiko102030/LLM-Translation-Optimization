Nothing and no one; no one had peeked. Not even Nastasya had touched anything. But, my goodness! How could he have left all these things in this hole? He rushed to the corner, stuck his hand under the wallpaper, and started pulling out items, loading them into his pockets. In total, there were eight items: two small boxes with earrings or something similar—he hadn't looked closely; then four small sapphire cases. One chain was simply wrapped in newspaper. Another item was also wrapped in newspaper, seemingly an order...

He placed everything into different pockets, the coat, and the remaining right pocket of his pants, trying to make it as inconspicuous as possible. He also took the wallet along with the items. Then he left the room, this time even leaving it completely open.
