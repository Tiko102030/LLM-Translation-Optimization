No one noticed him. In the second room, some scribes were sitting and writing, dressed only a bit better than him, appearing to be some strange sort of people. He approached one of them. — What do you want? He showed a summons from the office. — Are you a student? — asked the scribe, looking at the summons. — Yes, a former student. The scribe looked at him, albeit without any curiosity. He was some particularly disheveled person with a fixed idea in his gaze.
