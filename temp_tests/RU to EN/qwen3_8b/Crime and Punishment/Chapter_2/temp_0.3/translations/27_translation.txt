Read once, twice, and still didn't understand. — What is this? — he asked the scribe. — This is a demand for payment from a loan note, a collection. You must either pay all costs, penalties, and other charges, or give a written response stating when you can pay, along with an obligation not to leave the capital city until payment is made and not to sell or hide your property. The lender is free to sell your property and act according to the law against you. — Yes, I... owe no one! — This is not our affair. However, we have received a overdue and legally protested loan note for 150 rubles, issued to you to the widow Zarnitsyna, nine months ago, which was transferred to the town councilor Chebarov by the widow Zarnitsyna. Therefore, we are inviting you to respond. — But she is my housekeeper!
