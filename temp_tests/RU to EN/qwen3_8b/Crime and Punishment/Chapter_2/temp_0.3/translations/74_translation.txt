Then he tried to get up, wanted to run away, but someone always stopped him by force, and he would again fall into helplessness and confusion. Finally, he completely came to himself. This happened in the morning at ten o'clock. At this hour in clear days, the sun always cast a long strip of light along his right wall, illuminating the corner by the door. Standing by his bed was Nastasya, and another person, who was examining him with great curiosity and was completely unknown to him. He was a young man in a kaftan, with a beard, and from his appearance, he resembled an artelshik. The mistress peeked through the half-open door. Raskolnikov raised himself slightly. "Who is that, Nastasya?" he asked, pointing at the young man.
