There they are, the writers!—and he cast a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. Three days ago, there was also an incident at the tavern: he ate lunch but refused to pay; "I, you know, will describe you in satire for that." On the steamship, there was another incident, last week, a respectable family of a state councillor, his wife and daughter, called them the worst names. Yesterday, from the confectionery, they expelled one person from the crowd. Here they are, writers, literati, students, heralds… pah! Go ahead, you! I'll come myself… then beware! Did you hear? Louise Ivanovna, with an ingratiating politeness, began to bow in all directions and, bowing, managed to reach the doors; but at the doors she collided with a prominent officer from behind, with an open, fresh face and with excellent thick golden sideburns.
