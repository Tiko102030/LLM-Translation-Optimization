The clerk smiled at them. The hot-headed lieutenant was clearly puzzled. "This isn't your business!" he shouted finally in an unnaturally loud voice, "and here, you may as well submit the review they require from you. Show him, Alexander Gritsyevich. Complaints about you! Don't pay money! What a clear eagle you've flown!" But Raskolnikov was already not listening and greedily grabbed the paper, seeking to quickly find the solution. He read it once, twice, and didn't understand.
