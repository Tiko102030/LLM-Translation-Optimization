And he opened the window, and stood in the window like a little pig squealing; and that's a disgrace. And how can one squeal in the window to the street like a little pig; and that's a disgrace. Fuf-fuf-fuf! And Karl pulled his coat from the window, and there, truly, sir captain, he tore a rock from him. And then he cried out that he must pay fifteen shillings, man, the fine. And I myself, sir captain, paid him five shillings. And this is an unworthy guest, sir captain, and a scandal-maker! I, saying, will write a big satire, because I can write about you in all the newspapers. — From writers, then? — Yes, sir captain, and what an unworthy guest, sir captain, when in a noble house…
