Well, will he inquire about residence if he came with such intent? And Koch, that one, before going to the old woman, sat for half an hour at the silverware and exactly at seven forty-eight went upstairs to the old woman. Now figure it out…

— But allow me, how did they end up with such a contradiction: they themselves claim they knocked and the door was locked, but three minutes later, when they came with the janitor, it turns out the door was unlocked? — The thing is: the murderer was definitely there sitting and locked himself with the lock; and he would definitely have been caught there if it weren't for Koch's foolishness, not going himself to the janitor. And he exactly during this interval managed to descend the stairs and slip past them somehow. Koch crosses himself with both hands: "If I had stayed there, he would have jumped out and killed me with an axe." A Russian prayer service wants to serve, he-he… — No one saw the murderer? — Where would you see him? The house is Noah's ark, noted the clerk, listening from his place.
