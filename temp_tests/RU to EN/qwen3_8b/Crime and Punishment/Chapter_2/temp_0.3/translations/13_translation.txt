Although he could hardly think clearly at that moment, he felt that people would not treat him properly when they came to take him. "But... the police?" — Would you like some tea? Want something? I'll bring it; there's still... — No... I'll go: I'll go right now," he muttered, standing up. "Go, and you won't even descend the stairs?" — I'll go... — As you like. She went after the janitor. Immediately he ran to the light to examine the sock and fringe: "There are stains, but not very noticeable; everything is dirty, worn out, and has already faded. Whoever doesn't know beforehand won't notice anything. Nastasya, therefore, couldn't have noticed anything from a distance, thank God!" Then he carefully unsealed the notice and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood.
