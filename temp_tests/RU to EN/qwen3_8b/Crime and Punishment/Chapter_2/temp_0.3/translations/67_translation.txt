It seemed to him that he had somehow cut himself off from everyone and everything at that moment with scissors. He had returned to himself by evening, meaning he had been walking for only six hours. He had no recollection of where or how he had walked back. Undressing and trembling all over, like a frightened horse, he lay down on the sofa, pulled on his overcoat, and immediately fell asleep...

He awoke in deep dusk from a terrible scream. Goodness, what was that scream! Such unnatural sounds, such howling, screaming, screeching, weeping, abuse, and curses—he had never before heard or seen anything like it. He couldn't even imagine such cruelty, such frenzy. In terror, he raised himself up and sat on his bed, trembling and suffering every moment. But the fights, screams, and curses grew stronger and stronger. And here, to his greatest surprise, he suddenly heard his mistress's voice.
