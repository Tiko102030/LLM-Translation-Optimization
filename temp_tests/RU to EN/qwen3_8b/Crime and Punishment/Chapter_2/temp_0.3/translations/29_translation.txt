— You, so flirty and all that!— he suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the widow had already left),— what happened last night? Ah? Another disgrace, another rowdy scene on the whole street. Another fight and drunkenness. You dream of calm! I've already told you, I've already warned you ten times that I won't let you go on the eleventh! And you, again, again, you, so flirty and all that! Even the paper fell from Raskolnikov's hands, and he wildly looked at the plump lady who was so rudely handled; but soon, however, he realized what was going on, and immediately this whole story began to please him very much. He listened with pleasure, so much so that he wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh... All his nerves were twitching.— Ilya Petrovich!
