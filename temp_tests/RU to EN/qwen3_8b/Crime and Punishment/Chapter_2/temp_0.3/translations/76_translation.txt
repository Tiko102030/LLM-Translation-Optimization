But at that moment the door opened wide again, and, leaning slightly because he was tall, Razumikhin entered. "What a sea cabin!" he shouted as he entered, "I always bump my forehead; it's called a room too! And you, brother, have you come to your senses? I just heard from Pashenka." "I've just come to my senses," said Nastasya. "We've just come to our senses," the foreman nodded again with a smile. "And you, sir, what are you?" asked Razumikhin, suddenly addressing him. "I am Vrazumikhin, as you can see; not Razumikhin, as everyone calls me, but Vrazumikhin, a student, a nobleman's son, and he is my friend."
