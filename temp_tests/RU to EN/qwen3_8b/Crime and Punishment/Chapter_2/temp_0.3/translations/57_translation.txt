— No, said he, — I came… here's the thing: I have no lessons… I wanted to… in any case, I don't need lessons at all…

— Do you know what? You're talking nonsense! — remarked Razumikhin, observing him intently. — No, I'm not talking nonsense… — Raskolnikov stood up from the sofa. As he rose to approach Razumikhin, he didn't think about the fact that he would have to face him directly. Now, in an instant, he realized, through experience, that he was least inclined, at this moment, to face anyone in the whole world. All his bile rose within him. He was almost overcome by self-loathing, just for having crossed Razumikhin's threshold. — Goodbye! — he said suddenly and went to the door.
