"No, the matter is very unclear," added Il'yas. Raskolnikov raised his hat and went toward the door, but he did not reach the door... When he awoke, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that a person was supporting him on his right, that another person was standing on his left with a yellow glass cup filled with yellow water, and that Nikonov was standing before him, staring intently at him; he rose from the chair. "What is this, are you ill?" asked Nikonov rather sharply. "They signed barely with a pen, as if they had scarcely touched the ink," noted the note-taker, sitting down in his place and resuming his work with the papers. "How long have you been ill?" shouted Il'yas from his place, also rummaging through the papers.
