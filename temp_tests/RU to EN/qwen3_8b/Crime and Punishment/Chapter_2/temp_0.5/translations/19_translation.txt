No one noticed him. In the second room, some scribes were sitting and writing, dressed somewhat better than him, appearing to be a strange sort of people. He approached one of them. "What do you want?" He showed the summons from the office. "Are you a student?" asked the scribe, looking at the summons. "Yes, a former student." The scribe looked at him, albeit without any curiosity. He was some particularly disheveled person with a fixed idea in his gaze.
