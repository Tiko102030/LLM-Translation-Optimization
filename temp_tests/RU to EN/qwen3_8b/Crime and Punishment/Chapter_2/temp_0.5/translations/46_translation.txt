Nothing and no one; no one had peeked. Not even Nastasya had touched anything. But, my goodness! How could he have left all these things in this hole? He ran to the corner, stuck his hand under the wallpaper, and started pulling out items, loading them into his pockets. In total, there were eight items: two small boxes with earrings or something similar—he hadn't looked closely; then four small sapphire cases. One chain was simply wrapped in newspaper paper. Another item was also in newspaper paper, it seemed like an order...

He put everything into different pockets, the coat, and the remaining right pants pocket, trying to make it inconspicuous. He also took the wallet along with the items. Then he left the room, this time even leaving the door completely open.
