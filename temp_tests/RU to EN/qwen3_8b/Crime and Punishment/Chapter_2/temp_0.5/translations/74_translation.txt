Then he tried to get up, wanted to run away, but someone always stopped him by force, and he would again fall into helplessness and confusion. Finally, he completely regained consciousness. This happened in the morning at ten o'clock. At this hour in clear days, the sun always streamed across his right wall in a long stripe, illuminating the corner by the door. Standing by his bed was Nastasia and another person, who was keenly observing him and completely unfamiliar to him. He was a young man in a kaftan with a beard, and from his appearance, he resembled an artel member. The landlady peeked through the half-open door. Raskolnikov raised himself slightly. — Who is this, Nastasia? — he asked, pointing at the young man.
