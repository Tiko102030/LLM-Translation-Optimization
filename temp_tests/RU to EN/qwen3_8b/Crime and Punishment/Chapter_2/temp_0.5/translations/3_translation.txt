What is this? He immediately started taking them out and throwing them onto the table. Having selected everything, even turning out his pockets to make sure nothing was left, he moved all this pile to the corner. There, in the very corner, at the bottom, in one place, the wallpaper that had peeled away from the wall was torn: at once he began stuffing everything into this hole, under the paper: "It's in! Everything out of sight and the wallet too!" — happily thinking, standing up and staring blankly at the corner, at the even bigger hole. Suddenly he shuddered with fear: "My God," he whispered in despair, "what's happening to me? Is this hidden? Is this how they hide it?" Indeed, he hadn't expected anything but money; he thought only money would be there, so he hadn't prepared a place in advance, — "But now, now what am I happy about?" — he thought. — Is this how they hide it?"
