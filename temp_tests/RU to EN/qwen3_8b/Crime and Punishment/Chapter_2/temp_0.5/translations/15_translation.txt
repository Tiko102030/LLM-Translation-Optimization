"Put on the sock!" — it suddenly occurred to him, — it will get even more dusty, and the traces will disappear." But just as he put it on, he immediately pulled it off with revulsion and terror. He pulled it off, but realizing there was no other, he took it and put it on again — and laughed again. "All of this is conditional, all is relative, all of this are mere forms," — he thought briefly, with just a flicker of thought, while his whole body was trembling, — for here he put it on! For he ended up by putting it on!" Laughter, however, immediately turned into despair. "No, it’s not within my strength…" — it occurred to him. His legs were trembling. "From fear," — he muttered to himself. His head was spinning and aching from the heat.
