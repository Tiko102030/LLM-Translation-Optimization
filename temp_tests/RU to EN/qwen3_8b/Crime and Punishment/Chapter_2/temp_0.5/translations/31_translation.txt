And he opened the window, and climbed in like a little pig squealing; that's a disgrace. And how can one squeal like a little pig in the window to the street; that's a disgrace. Fuh-fuh-fuh! And Carl pulled him back by the coat from the window, and indeed, sir captain, he broke his noble reputation. And then he shouted that he was owed fifteen coins as a fine. And I myself, sir captain, paid five coins as a fine. And this is an unworthy guest, sir captain, and a man who makes a scandal! I, saying so, will write a big satire about you, because I can compose everything about you in all the newspapers. — From the writers, you mean? — Yes, sir captain, and what an unworthy guest, sir captain, when in a noble house…
