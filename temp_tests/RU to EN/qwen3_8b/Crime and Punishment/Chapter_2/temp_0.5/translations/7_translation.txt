There are not even any matches. No, better to go somewhere and throw everything out. Yes! Better to throw out! — he repeated, sitting down again on the sofa — and now, at this very moment, without delay!... But instead, his head again leaned on the pillow; again the unbearable chill seized him; again he pulled on his overcoat. And for a long time, several hours, it still seemed to him in fits that "now, without delay, go somewhere and throw everything out, so that it's out of sight, as soon as possible, as soon as possible!" He several times tried to get up from the sofa, wanted to stand up, but already could not. Finally, he was awakened by a strong knock on the door. — Come on, are you alive or not? And all the time he sleeps like a dog! — Nastasya shouted, pounding her fist on the door — sleeping all day, like a dog!
