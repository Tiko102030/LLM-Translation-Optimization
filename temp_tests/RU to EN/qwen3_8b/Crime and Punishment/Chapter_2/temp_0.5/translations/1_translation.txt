Third hour already! He sat on the sofa—and suddenly everything came back to him! In an instant, he remembered everything! At first, he thought he was going mad. A terrible cold gripped him; but the cold was also from the fever, which had already begun with him in his sleep. Now, however, a sudden chill struck him so strongly that his teeth almost came out, and everything inside him was trembling. He opened the door and began to listen: the whole house was completely asleep. He looked at himself in astonishment and everything around him in the room, not understanding how he could have entered yesterday, not locked the doors with the hook, and collapsed on the sofa, not only without undressing but even with his hat on; it had slipped down and lay on the floor near the pillow. "What would someone think if they came in? That I'm drunk, but..." He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began to examine himself, from head to toe, all his clothes—were there any traces?
