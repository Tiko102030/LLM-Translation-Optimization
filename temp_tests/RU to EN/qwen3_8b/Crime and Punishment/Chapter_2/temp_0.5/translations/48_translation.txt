And what if the cases don't sink but float? Yes, of course. Everyone will see it. And in fact, they already look at each other like that, meeting and glancing as if only interested in each other. "Why not, or perhaps it's just me," he thought. Finally, it occurred to him that perhaps it would be better to go somewhere on the Neva. There are fewer people, it's less noticeable, and at any rate more convenient, and above all, farther from here. He suddenly realized: how could he have wandered for half an hour in melancholy and anxiety, in dangerous places, and not thought of this before! And precisely because he had spent the whole half hour on this reckless endeavor, it had already been decided in a dream or delirium! He became extremely distracted and forgetful, and he knew it.
