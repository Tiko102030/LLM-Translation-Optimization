It was strange. — Well, well, that's good, — concluded Ilya Petrovich, — we won't detain you. Raskolnikov left. He could still hear a lively conversation beginning as he exited, in which Nikodim Fomich's questioning voice was most clearly heard… On the street, he completely came to himself. "Search, search, a search is coming! — he repeated to himself, hurrying to get there; — bandits! They suspect me!" The old fear seized him all over, from his feet to his head. II

"What if there was already a search? What if I just happen to catch them here?"

But here was his room. Nothing and no one; no one had peeked.
