He found this observation very unpleasant, even frightening. — Nastasya, why are you silent? — he finally whispered timidly. — This is blood, — she finally answered, quietly, as if speaking to herself. — Blood!... Which blood?... — he muttered, paling and moving away from the wall. Nastasya continued to look at him silently. — No one beat the mistress, — she spoke again in a strict and resolute voice. He looked at her, barely breathing.
