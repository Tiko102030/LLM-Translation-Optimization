There was a terrible stuffiness because of that. Up and down, janitors with books under their arms came and went, along with a mixed crowd of both sexes—visitors. The door to the very office was also left ajar. He entered and stopped in the hallway. Here, everyone stood and waited for some men. Here, too, there was extraordinary stuffiness, and in addition, the fresh, still drying paint on the old, rancid oil of the newly painted rooms made the nose feel as if it would vomit. After waiting a bit, he decided to move forward a bit into the next room. All the rooms were tiny and low. Terrible impatience was pulling him further and further. Nobody noticed him.
