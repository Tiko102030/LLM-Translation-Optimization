There they are, the scribes!—and he cast a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. Three days ago, there was an incident in the tavern: he had lunch, but refused to pay; "I, you see, will describe you in satire for that." On the ship, too, another incident occurred last week: a respected family of a state councillor, his wife and daughter, were insulted with the most contemptible words. Recently, from the confectionery, he was thrown out for some reason. So these are the scribes, the writers, the students, the heralds… damn! Go away! I'll come myself to see you… then be careful! Did you hear? Luiza Ivanovna, with a servile politeness, began to sit down on all sides, and while sitting down, she managed to reach the doors; but she bumped into an officer at the door, a man with an open, fresh face and excellent thick golden mustaches.
