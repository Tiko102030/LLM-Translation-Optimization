He asked, pointing at the boy. "Oh, you're awake!" she said. "They've woken up," the foreman replied. Realizing he had woken up, the mistress, who had been peeking through the door, quickly closed it and hid. She had always been shy and reluctantly bore conversations and explanations; she was forty years old, plump and fat, with black eyebrows and black eyes, kind from her plumpness and laziness; and even very smooth in her own way. She was shy beyond necessity. "You... who are you?" he continued questioning, addressing the foreman directly. But at that moment the door opened wide, and, slightly bending down because he was tall, Razumikhin entered.
