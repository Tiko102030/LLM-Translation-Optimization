The call was so strong that he had already risen from his place to carry it out. "Should I think about it for a moment?"—whispered his thoughts. "No, better not to think at all and just get going!" But suddenly he stopped as if rooted to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was speaking passionately to Ilyia Petrovich, and the words reached him: "It's impossible to free both! First of all, everything contradicts; judge for yourselves: why call a steward if it's their own business? To accuse themselves? Or for cunning? No, that would be too clever! And finally, Pestryakov the student was seen at the very gates by both the stewards and the merchant woman at the exact moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted from them at the gates, asking about lodging with the stewards while still with his friends. Well, would he start asking about lodging if he had such an intent?"
