His entire room was so small that one could take off a hook without getting out of bed. So it is: the butler and Nastasya are standing there. Nastasya looked at him in a strange way. He met the butler's gaze with a defiant and desperate look. The butler silently handed him a folded, gray paper sealed with a bottle seal. — A summons from the office, — he said, handing over the paper. — From which office? — They call the police, meaning the office. It's well known which office. — To the police!
