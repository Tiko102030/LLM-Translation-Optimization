The clerk smiled at them. The hot-headed captain seemed visibly confused. "This isn't your affair!" he shouted finally in an unnaturally loud voice, "so you may as well submit the review they require from you. Show him, Alexander Grigoryevich. Complaints about you! Don't pay money! What a bold falcon you've flown!" But Raskolnikov was already not listening and greedily grabbed the paper, seeking to quickly find the answer. He read it once, again, and didn't understand.
