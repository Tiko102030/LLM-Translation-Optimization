Nastasya, so to speak, could not notice anything from afar, praise be! Then, trembling with fear, he opened the summons and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood. It was an ordinary summons from the district to appear today, at half past ten, at the district supervisor's office. "When did this ever happen? I have no business with the police on my own! And why exactly today?" — he thought in tormenting confusion. "Lord, hurry up!" He was about to kneel down to pray, but even he laughed — not at the prayer, but at himself. He hurried to dress. "I'll be lost either way! Put on a sock!"
