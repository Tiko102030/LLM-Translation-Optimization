It seemed to him that he had somehow cut himself off from everything and everyone at that moment with scissors. He arrived at himself by evening, which meant he had been walking for about six hours. He didn't remember where or how he had returned. Undressing and trembling all over like a driven horse, he lay on the sofa, pulled on his coat, and immediately fell asleep...

He awoke in the full dusk to a terrible scream. God, what was that cry! Such unnatural sounds, howls, screams, screeches, tears, curses, and violence—he had never before heard or seen such cruelty and madness. He couldn't even imagine such a horror, such frenzy. In terror, he raised himself up and sat on his bed, each moment freezing and suffering. But the fighting, the screams, and the curses grew stronger and stronger. And suddenly, to his greatest astonishment, he clearly heard his mistress's voice.
