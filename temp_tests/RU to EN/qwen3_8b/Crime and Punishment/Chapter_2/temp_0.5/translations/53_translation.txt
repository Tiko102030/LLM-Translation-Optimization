"By God, that's it! — he suddenly thought, seized by unrelenting fury. — Well, it's begun, so let it begin! Let hell take the new life! How foolish, Lord, how foolish!... How much I lied and disgraced myself today! How vilely I bowed and fawned on the most despicable Ilyi Petrovich! Well, in any case, that's nonsense too! I don't care about them all, nor about the fact that I bowed and fawned! It's completely the opposite! It's entirely different!..."

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question at once confused him and bitterly surprised him:

"If indeed all this was done intentionally, not foolishly, if you really had a definite and firm goal, then how come you haven't even glanced into your wallet up to now and don't know what you've received, from which all these torments came, and why you deliberately went on such a low, disgusting, and base course?"
