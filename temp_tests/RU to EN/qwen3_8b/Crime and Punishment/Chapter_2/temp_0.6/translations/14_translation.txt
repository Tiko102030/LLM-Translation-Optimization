Nastasya, therefore, could not have noticed anything from afar, thank God! Then, with trembling, he opened the notice and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood. It was an ordinary summons from the quarter to appear today at half past ten in the office of the quarter's overseer. "When has this ever happened before? I have no business with the police on my own! And why exactly today?" he thought in agonizing confusion. "Lord, just hurry up!" He was about to kneel down to pray, but even he laughed to himself—not at the prayer, but at himself. He hurriedly began to dress. "I'll be lost either way, regardless! Put on a sock!"
