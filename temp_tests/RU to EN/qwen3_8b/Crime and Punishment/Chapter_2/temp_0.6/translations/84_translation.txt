Two minutes later, Nastasya returned with soup and announced that tea would come soon. With the soup came two spoons, two plates, and the entire set of utensils: salt, pepper, horseradish for beef, and other items that hadn't been arranged in such order for a long time. The tablecloth was clean. "It would be nice, Nastasya, if Praskovya Pavlovna sent two beer bottles," he said. "We'll have a drink." — "Well, you tall fellow!" — Nastasya muttered and went to carry out the order.
