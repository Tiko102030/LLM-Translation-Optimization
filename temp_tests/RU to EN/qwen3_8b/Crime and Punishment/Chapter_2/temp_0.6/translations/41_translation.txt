— But allow me, how did such a contradiction arise? They themselves claim they knocked and the door was locked, yet within three minutes, when they came with the janitor, it turned out the door was unlocked? — The trick is that the murderer was necessarily sitting there and locked himself in; he would surely have been caught there had it not been for Koх's carelessness, had he not gone himself to fetch the janitor. And he precisely during that interval managed to descend the stairs and slip past them somehow. Koх crosses himself with both hands: "If I had stayed there, he would have jumped out and killed me with an axe." A Russian prayer service wants to be held, heh-heh... — But no one saw the murderer? — Where could you see him? The house is Noah's ark, — remarked the clerk, listening from his place.
