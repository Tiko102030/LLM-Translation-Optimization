It was strange. — Well, well, all right, all right, — concluded Ilyá Petrovich, — we won't detain you. Raskolnikov left. He could still overhear a lively conversation that had begun upon his exit, in which Nikodim Fomich's questioning voice was most prominent... On the street, he completely came to himself. "Search, search, a search is coming! — he repeated to himself, hurrying to get there; — bandits! They suspect me!" The fear from before seized him all over, from his feet to his head. II

"What if there was already a search? What if I run into them right here?"

But here was his room. Nothing and no one; no one looked in.
