Better to throw it all away! — he kept repeating, sitting down again on the couch, — and right now, this very minute, without delay!.. But instead, his head again leaned on the pillow; again the unbearable chill seized him; again he pulled on his overcoat. And for a long time, several hours, he still had flashes of the urge that 'now, without delay, go somewhere and throw everything away, so that it's out of sight, as soon as possible, as soon as possible!' He tried to get up from the couch several times, wanted to stand up, but was already unable to. The final loud knock on the door woke him up. — Open up, alive or not? And he's all the time sleeping! — cried Nastasya, pounding her fist on the door, — all day long, like a dog, he's sleeping!
