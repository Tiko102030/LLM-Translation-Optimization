Read it once, twice, and still didn't understand. — What is this? — he asked the clerk. — This is a demand for payment under the loan note, a recovery claim. You must either pay all costs, penalties, and other expenses, or give a written statement when you can pay, along with an undertaking not to leave the capital until payment is made and not to sell or hide your property. The lender is entitled to sell your property and act according to the law against you. — I don't owe anyone! — That's not our affair. However, a past due and legally protested loan note for 150 rubles has arrived at our office, issued by you to the widow, collegiate assessor Zarntsyayeva, nine months ago, and now transferred by the widow Zarntsyayeva to the town councillor Chebarov. Therefore, we are inviting you to provide a statement. — But she is my housekeeper!
