Then he tried to get up, wanted to run, but someone always stopped him by force, and he would again fall into helplessness and confusion. Finally, he completely came to himself. This happened in the morning at ten o'clock. At this hour in clear days, the sun always traveled in a long strip across his right wall, illuminating the corner by the door. Standing by his bed was Nastasya and another person, who was observing him with great curiosity and was completely unknown to him. He was a young man in a kaftan, with a beard, and at first glance resembled an artel member. The housekeeper peeked through the half-open door. Raskolnikov raised himself slightly. — Who is this, Nastasya? — he asked, pointing at the man.
