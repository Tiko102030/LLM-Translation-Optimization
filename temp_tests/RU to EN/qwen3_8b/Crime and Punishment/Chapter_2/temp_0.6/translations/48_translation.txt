And what if the cases don't sink but float? Of course, everyone will see. They already look at each other like that, glancing as if their only concern is each other. "Why not, or perhaps it's just me," he thought. Finally, it occurred to him that perhaps it would be better to go somewhere on the Neva. There are fewer people, it's less noticeable, and at least more convenient, and above all, farther from here. He suddenly realized how could he have wandered for half an hour in gloom and anxiety, in dangerous places, and never thought of this before! And precisely because of that, he spent half an hour on a reckless endeavor, which had already been decided in a dream or delirium! He became extremely distracted and forgetful, and he knew it.
