— You, so pretty and such, — he suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the widow had already left), — what happened last night? a? Another disgrace, making a row all over the street again. Another fight and drunkenness. You're dreaming of peace! I've already told you, I've already warned you ten times that I won't let you go out on the eleventh! And you again, again, you, so pretty and such! Even Raskolnikov's paper slipped from his hands, and he stared wildly at the plump lady who had been so rudely handled; but soon, however, he realized what was going on, and immediately this whole affair began to please him very much. He listened with enjoyment, so much so that he longed to laugh, laugh, laugh… All his nerves were twitching. — Ilya Petrovich!
