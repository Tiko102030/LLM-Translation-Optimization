There they are, the writers!—and he cast a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. Also, three days ago, there was an incident at the tavern: he had lunch, but didn't want to pay; "I, you see, will describe you in satire for that." On the steamship, too, another incident, last week, a respectable family of a state councillor, his wife and daughter, called them the foulest names. Yesterday, from the confectionery, they threw out one person. Here they are, the writers, litterateurs, students, heralds… damn it! You go on! I'll come myself to you… then beware! Did you hear? Louise Ivanovna, with servile politeness, began to bow to everyone and, bowing, reached the doors; but at the doors she collided with a prominent officer, with an open, fresh face and with excellent thick golden whiskers.
