Although he could hardly think clearly at that moment, he felt that people would not treat him kindly when they came to take him. "But... the police?" — Would you like some tea? Whatever you like? I'll bring it; there's still... — No... I'll go: I'll go right now," he muttered, standing up. "Go, and you won't even descend the stairs?" — I'll go... As you like. She went after the janitor. Immediately, he ran to the light to examine the sock and the tassels: "There are stains, but not very noticeable; everything is dirty, worn out, and has already faded. Whoever doesn't know beforehand won't notice anything. Nastasya, therefore, couldn't have noticed anything from a distance, thank goodness!" Then, trembling, he opened the summons and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood.
