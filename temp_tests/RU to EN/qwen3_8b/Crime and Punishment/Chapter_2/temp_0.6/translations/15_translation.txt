Put on the sock! – it suddenly occurred to him – it would get even more embedded in the dust, and the traces would disappear. But just as he put it on, he immediately pulled it off with disgust and horror. He pulled it off, but realizing there was no other, he took it and put it on again – and laughed again. "Everything is conditional, everything is relative, all of this is just forms," – he thought briefly, with just a corner of his mind, while trembling all over, – for here he put it on! For he ended up by putting it on! The laughter, however, immediately turned into despair. "No, it's not within my power…" – it occurred to him. His legs were trembling. "From fear," – he muttered to himself. His head was spinning and aching from the heat.
