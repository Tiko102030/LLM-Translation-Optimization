"By God, that's it!—he suddenly thought, seized by an unrelenting fit of wrath. Well, it's begun, so be it—let hell take the new life! How foolish, Lord!... How much I lied and disgraced myself today! How despicably I fawned and flattered the worst Ilyá Petrovich! Well, that's nonsense too! I don't care about them all, nor about the fact that I fawned and flattered! It's entirely different! It's entirely different!..." 

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected, and extremely simple question at once confused him and greatly surprised him: 

"If indeed all this was done intentionally, not foolishly, if you indeed had a definite and firm goal, then how come you haven't even looked into your wallet yet and don't know what you've received, for which you've endured all these torments and deliberately went through such a vile, dirty, low act?"
