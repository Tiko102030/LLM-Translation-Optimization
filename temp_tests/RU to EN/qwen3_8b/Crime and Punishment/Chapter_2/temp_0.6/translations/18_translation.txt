Therefore, there was a terrible stuffiness. Up and down, the janitors with books under their arms went up and down, strutting and various people of both sexes—visitors. The door into the main office was also left open. He entered and stopped in the hallway. Here, everyone stood and waited for some men. Here, too, there was an extraordinary stuffiness, and additionally, it hit the nose to the point of nausea with the fresh, still drying paint on the rancid oil of the newly painted rooms. After waiting a bit, he decided to move further forward into the next room. All the rooms were tiny and low. Terrible impatience was pulling him further and further. Nobody noticed him.
