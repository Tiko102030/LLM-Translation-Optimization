Raskolnikov raised his hat and went toward the doors, but he did not reach the doors... When he awoke, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that a person was supporting him on the right, that another person stood on the left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nikodim Fomich stood before him, staring intently at him; he stood up from the chair. "What is this, are you ill?" rather sharply asked Nikodim Fomich. "They signed so faintly, barely with a pen," noted the scribe, sitting down in his place and resuming his work with the papers. "How long have you been ill?" shouted Ilyia Petrovich from his place, also sorting through the papers.
