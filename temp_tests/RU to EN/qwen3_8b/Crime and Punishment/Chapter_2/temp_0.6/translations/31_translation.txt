And he opened the window, and stood in the window like a little pig squealing; that's a disgrace. How can one squeal in the window like a little pig; that's a disgrace. Ugh-ugh-ugh! And Karl was dragging him by the coat from the window, and indeed, sir captain, he had a fine of fifteen cents to pay. And he cried out that he had to pay fifteen cents. And I myself, sir captain, paid five cents to him for the fine. And this is an unworthy guest, sir captain, and a man who makes a scandal! I, saying so, will be a big satirical writer, because I can write about you in all the newspapers. — From writers, you mean? — Yes, sir captain, and what an unworthy guest, sir captain, when in a noble house...
