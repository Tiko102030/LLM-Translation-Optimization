What is this? He rushed to take them out and throw them on the table. Having selected everything, even turning out the pockets to make sure there wasn't anything left, he moved all this pile to the corner. There, in the very corner, at the bottom, in one place, the peeling wallpaper was torn: immediately he began to stuff everything into this hole, under the paper: "It's in! Everything out of sight and the wallet too!" — he happily thought, standing up and staring blankly at the corner, the hole now even more prominent. Suddenly, he shuddered all over with fear: "My God," — he whispered in despair, — "what's happening to me? Is this hidden? Is this how one hides?" Indeed, he hadn't expected anything but money; that's why he hadn't prepared a place in advance — "But now, now what am I happy about?" — he thought. — "Is this how one hides?"
