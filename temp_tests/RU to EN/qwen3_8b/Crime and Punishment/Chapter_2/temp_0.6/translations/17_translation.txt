"If they ask, maybe I'll say," he thought, approaching the office. The office was a quarter of a verst away. She had just moved to a new apartment, a new building, on the fourth floor. He had once briefly visited her old apartment, but a long time ago. Entering the gates, he saw a staircase to the right, down which a man was coming with a book in his hand: "a janitor, then; that means the office is here," and he started going up randomly. He didn't want to ask anyone anything. "I'll go in, kneel down, and tell everything..." he thought, entering the fourth floor. The staircase was narrow, steep, and full of filth. All the kitchens from every apartment in all four floors opened onto this staircase and remained open almost all day, causing oppressive stuffiness.
