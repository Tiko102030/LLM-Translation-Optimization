No one noticed him. In the second room, some scribes were sitting and writing, dressed somewhat more neatly than him, but they appeared to be a strange sort of people. He turned to one of them. "What do you want?" He showed him a summons from the office. "Are you a student?" the scribe asked, looking at the summons. "Yes, a former student." The scribe looked at him, albeit without any curiosity. He was a particularly disheveled person with a fixed idea in his gaze.
