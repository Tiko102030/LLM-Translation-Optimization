Nothing and no one; nobody had peeked. Even Nastasya did not touch. But, Lord! How could he leave all these things in this hole? He rushed to the corner, pushed his hand under the wallpaper, and began pulling out the items, loading them into his pockets. There were a total of eight items: two small boxes with earrings or something like that—he didn't look carefully; then four small sapphire cases. One chain was simply wrapped in newspaper. Something else in newspaper, seems like an order… He put everything into different pockets, in the coat and in the remaining right pocket of the pants, trying to make it less noticeable. He also took the wallet along with the items. Then he left the room, this time even leaving it completely open.
