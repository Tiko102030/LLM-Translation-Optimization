It seemed to him that he had somehow cut himself off from everyone and everything at that moment, as if with scissors. He had come to himself by evening, which meant he had walked for only six hours. He didn't remember where or how he had gone back. Undressing and shaking all over like a driven horse, he lay on the sofa, pulled on his overcoat, and fell asleep immediately...

He awoke in the full dusk from a terrible scream. God, what a scream! Such unnatural sounds, such howling, wailing, screeching, tears, brawling, and swearing he had never heard or seen before. He could not imagine such cruelty, such madness. In fear, he raised himself and sat on his bed, trembling and suffering every moment. But the brawls, screams, and curses became stronger and stronger. And here, to his greatest astonishment, he suddenly heard his mistress's voice.
