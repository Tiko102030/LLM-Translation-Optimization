The impulse was so strong that he had already risen from his seat to carry it out. "Should I think about it for a moment?"—passed through his mind. "No, better not to think at all and just get it over with!" But suddenly he froze in place: Nikodim Fomich was speaking passionately to Ilyia Petrovich, and the words reached him: "It's impossible to free both! First of all, everything contradicts; judge for yourselves: why call a servant if it were their own business? To report themselves? Or for cunning? No, that would be too clever! And finally, the student Pestryakov was seen near the gates by both the servants and the townsman at the very moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted from them at the gates, asking about the servants' residence while still with his friends. Well, would he start asking about the residence if he had such an intention?"
