The clerk looked at them with a smile. The hot-tempered captain seemed clearly perplexed. "This isn't your business!" he shouted finally in an unnaturally loud voice, "but you may as well submit the report they require from you. Show him, Alexander Grigoryevich. Complaints about you! Don't pay money! What a bold hawk you've flown!" But Raskolnikov had already stopped listening and greedily grabbed the paper, seeking to find the answers quickly. He read it once, twice, and didn't understand.
