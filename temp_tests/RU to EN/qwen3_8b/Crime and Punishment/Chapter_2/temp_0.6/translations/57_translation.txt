— No need, — he said, — I came… here's the thing: I have no lessons… I wanted to… in any case, I don't need lessons at all…

— Do you know what? You're talking nonsense! — remarked Razumikhin, who had been watching him intently. — No, I'm not talking nonsense… — Raskolnikov stood up from the sofa. As he rose to face Razumikhin, he didn't think about the fact that he was supposed to meet him face to face. Now, in an instant, he realized, through experience, that he was least inclined, at this moment, to confront anyone in the whole world. All his bitterness rose within him. He was almost overcome by wrath at himself for having crossed Razumikhin's threshold. — Goodbye! — he suddenly said and went to the door.
