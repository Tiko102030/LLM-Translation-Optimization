— Yes, Mr. Captain, and what a base guest this is, Mr. Captain, when in a noble house…
— Nun-nun-nun! Enough! I've already told you, told you, I've told you…
— Ilyá Petrovich! — the clerk spoke again significantly. The ensign quickly glanced at him; the clerk slightly nodded his head. — …Here is your tale, esteemed Laviza Ivanovna, my last story, and this is for the last time — the ensign continued. — If there is another scandal in your noble house, I'll take you to the Tsugundér, as is said in high style. Did you hear? So a writer, a story-teller, took five coins for a cloak in the noble house? There they are, the story-tellers!
