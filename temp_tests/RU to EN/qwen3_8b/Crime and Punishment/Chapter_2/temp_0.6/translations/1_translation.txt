"Three hours already!" He sat on the couch—and suddenly everything came back to him! In an instant, he remembered everything! At first, he thought he was going mad. A terrible cold gripped him; but the cold was also from the fever that had already begun with him in his sleep. Now, however, a sudden chill struck him so violently that his teeth almost came out, and everything within him was shaking. He opened the door and began to listen: everyone in the house was completely asleep. He looked at himself and everything around him in the room with astonishment and did not understand: how could he, upon entering yesterday, not lock the doors with the hook and jump onto the couch, not only not undressing, but even in his hat? It had slid down and immediately lay on the floor near the pillow. "If someone had come in, what would they think? That I'm drunk, but…" He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began to examine himself, from head to toe, his entire attire: were there any traces?
