He looked at her, barely breathing. — I heard it myself... I didn't sleep... I was sitting, — he spoke even more timidly. — I listened for a long time... The assistant of the overseer came... Everyone rushed to the stairs, from all the apartments... — No one came. But your blood is screaming. It's when she has no way out and her liver starts to cook, then hallucinations begin... Will you eat at all? He didn't answer. Nastasya stood over him, stared at him intently and didn't leave. — Give me something to drink... Nastasyushka. She went downstairs and came back in two minutes with water in a white clay cup; but he no longer remembered what happened next. He only remembered how he took a sip of cold water and spilled it from the cup onto his chest.
