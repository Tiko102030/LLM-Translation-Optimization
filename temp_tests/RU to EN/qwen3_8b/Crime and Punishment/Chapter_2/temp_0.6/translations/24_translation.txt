Money? Which money? — thought Raskolnikov, — but... surely it's not that! — He shivered with joy. It suddenly became to him incredibly easy. Everything fell from his shoulders. — And what time is written for you to come, my lord? — shouted the poruchik, increasingly and more unknownly offended — you are told to come at nine, but now it's already twelve! — I was brought only a quarter of an hour ago, — Raskolnikov answered loudly and over his shoulder, also suddenly and unexpectedly for himself getting angry and even finding some pleasure in it. — It's enough that I came here as a feverish patient. — Do not shout!
