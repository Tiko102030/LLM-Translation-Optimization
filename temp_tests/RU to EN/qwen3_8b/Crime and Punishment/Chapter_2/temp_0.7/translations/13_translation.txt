Though he could scarcely comprehend anything at that moment, he felt that people would not treat him properly when they came to take him. "But... the police?" — Would you like some tea? Want anything? I'll bring it; there's still... — No... I'll go: I'll go right now," he muttered, getting to his feet. "Go, and you won't even get down from the stairs?" — I'll go... — As you like. She went after the janitor. Immediately he ran to the light to examine the sock and tassel: "There are stains, but not entirely noticeable; everything has gotten dirty, worn out, and has already faded. Whoever doesn't know beforehand won't notice anything. Thank goodness, Nastasya couldn't have noticed anything from a distance!" Then, trembling with fear, he unsealed the notice and began reading; he read for a long time and finally understood.
