His entire room was of such a size that one could take off a hook without getting out of bed. So it is: the janitor and Nastasya are standing. Nastasya looked at him in some strange way. He looked at the janitor with a defiant and desperate expression. He silently handed him a gray, folded-in-half paper sealed with a bottle-seal. "A summons from the office," he said, handing over the paper. "From which office?..." "They call to the police, so they go to the office. It's known which office." "To the police!..."
