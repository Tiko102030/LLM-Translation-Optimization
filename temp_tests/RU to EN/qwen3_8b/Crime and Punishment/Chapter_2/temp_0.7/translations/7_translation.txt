Not even a match. No, better to go somewhere and throw everything away. Yes! Better to throw it all away!" he repeated, sitting again on the sofa, "and right now, this very minute, without delay!..." But instead, his head once again sank back onto the pillow; once again an unbearable chill seized him; once again he pulled on his overcoat. For a long time, several hours, he still felt impulses to go somewhere and throw everything away, to get rid of it once and for all, as soon as possible, as soon as possible! He tried to get up from the sofa several times, wanted to stand, but could no longer. Finally, a strong knock on the door woke him up. "Open the door, are you alive or not? And all the time he's sleeping!" Nastasya shouted, pounding her fist on the door, "all day long, like a dog, he's sleeping!"
