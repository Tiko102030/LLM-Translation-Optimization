— No, said he, — I came… here's the thing: I have no lessons at all… I was going to… in any case, I don't need lessons at all… — Do you know what? You're talking nonsense! — remarked Razumikhin, observing him closely. — No, I'm not talking nonsense… — Raskolnikov rose from the sofa. As he got up to approach Razumikhin, he didn't think about the fact that he was supposed to meet face to face with him. Now, in an instant, he realized, through experience, that at this moment he was least inclined to meet anyone face to face in the whole world. All his bitterness rose within him. He was almost choked by hatred for himself, just after stepping over Razumikhin's threshold. — Goodbye! — he suddenly said and went to the door.
