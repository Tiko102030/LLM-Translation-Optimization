"If they ask, I might say," — he thought as he approached the office. The office was a quarter of a verst away from him. She had just moved to a new apartment, a new building, to the fourth floor. He had been in her old apartment briefly once, but that was very long ago. Entering under the gates, he saw a staircase to the right, down which a man with a book in his hand was going: "A janitor, that means; that means the office is here," and he started going up randomly. He didn't want to ask anyone anything. "I'll go in, kneel down, and tell everything..." — he thought as he entered the fourth floor. The staircase was narrow, steep, and entirely in filth. All the kitchens of the apartments in all four floors opened onto this staircase and remained that way almost all day. That's why there was a terrible stuffiness.
