There they are, the authors!—and he cast a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. Three days ago, there was an incident in the tavern: he had a meal, but refused to pay; "I'll describe you in satire," he said. On the steamship, too, there was another incident, last week: a respected family of a state councillor, his wife and daughter, were insulted with the most vulgar words. Just yesterday, they threw out one of the customers from the confectionery. Here they are, the authors, the literati, the students, the agitators… Tsk! Go away! I'll come myself… then beware! Did you hear? Luiza Ivanovna, with an affected politeness, began prancing around, trying to sit down on all sides, and, as she sat down, she waddled up to the doors; but she bumped into an officer at the door, a man with an open, fresh face and superb, thick flaxen whiskers.
