What's this? He dashed to throw them all out onto the table. Having selected everything, even rummaging through his pockets to make sure nothing remained, he carried all this pile to the corner. There, in the very corner, at the bottom, in one place, the wallpaper that had peeled away from the wall was torn; at once he began stuffing everything into this hole, under the paper: "It's all in! Everything out of sight and the wallet too!" — he thought joyfully, standing up and staring blankly at the corner, at the even more gaping hole. Suddenly he shuddered with fear: "My God," he whispered in despair, "what is happening to me? Is this hidden? Is this how you hide things?" Indeed, he hadn't expected to find anything; he thought there would be only money, and thus hadn't prepared a place in advance — "But now, now what am I happy about?" — he thought. — "Is this how you hide things?"
