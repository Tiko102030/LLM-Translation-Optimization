When he awoke, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that someone was supporting him on his right, that another person was standing on his left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nikodim Fomich was standing before him, staring intently at him; he rose from the chair. "What's this, are you ill?" Nikodim Fomich rather sharply asked. "They signed barely with a pen, as if they had scarcely touched the paper," noted the scribe, sitting down in his place and resuming his work with the documents. "How long have you been ill?" cried Ilyá Petrovich from his place, also rummaging through the papers.
