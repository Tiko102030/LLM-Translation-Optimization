— To the police!... Why?... — And I don't know why. They require it, and go. — He looked at him attentively, surveyed the surroundings, and turned to leave. — Not at all sick? — remarked Nastasya, not taking her eyes off him. The servant also turned his head for a moment. — Since yesterday, it's been hot, — she added. He didn't answer and held the paper in his hands, not unsealing it.
