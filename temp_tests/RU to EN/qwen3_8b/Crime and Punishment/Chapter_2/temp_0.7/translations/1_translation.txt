Third hour already! He sat on the couch — and then he suddenly remembered everything! All of a sudden, in an instant, he remembered everything! In the first moment, he thought he was going mad. A terrible cold wrapped around him; but the cold was also from the fever that had already begun with him in his sleep. Now, suddenly, such a chill struck him that his teeth almost came out, and everything inside him was spinning. He opened the door and started listening: everything in the house was completely asleep. He looked at himself and everything around him in the room with astonishment and did not understand: how could he, entering yesterday, not lock the doors on the hook and throw himself on the couch, not only without undressing, but even in a hat: it had slipped and immediately lay on the floor, near the pillow. "If someone had come in, what would they have thought? That I’m drunk, but…" He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began to examine himself, from head to toe, his entire outfit: are there any traces?
