There was a terrible stuffiness because of that. Up and down, the janitors with books under their arms ascended and descended, sturdy and varied people of both sexes—visitors. The door to the main office was also left ajar. He entered and stopped in the hallway. Here, everyone stood and waited for some men. Here too, the stuffiness was extraordinary, and additionally, the nose was hit to the point of nausea by the fresh, still drying paint on the rancid oil of the newly painted rooms. After waiting a bit, he decided to move forward into the next room. All the rooms were tiny and low. Terrible impatience pulled him further and further. No one noticed him.
