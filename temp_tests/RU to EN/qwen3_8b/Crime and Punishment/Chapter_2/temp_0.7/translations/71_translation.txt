It became very unpleasant for him from this observation, even terrifying. — Nastasia, why are you keeping silent? — he finally whispered weakly. — This is blood, — she finally answered, quietly and as if speaking to herself. — Blood!... Which blood?... — he muttered, paling and moving away from the wall. Nastasia continued to look at him silently. — No one beat the mistress, — she said again in a strict and resolute voice. He looked at her, barely breathing.
