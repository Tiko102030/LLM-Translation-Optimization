Realizing he had awakened, the housekeeper, who had been peeking through the door, immediately closed it and hid. She had always been shy and reluctantly bore conversations and explanations; she was about forty, plump and corpulent, black-browed and black-eyed, kind from her plumpness and laziness; and even in her appearance, she was very smarmy. She was shamefaced beyond necessity. — "Who are you?" he continued interrogating, addressing the collective worker directly. At that moment, the door flew open again, and, leaning slightly because he was tall, Razumikhin entered.
