The light was sufficient, and he quickly began inspecting himself, from head to toe, his entire outfit: were there any traces? But that wasn't possible: shivering from a chill, he started removing everything from his body and inspecting the surroundings again. He turned everything inside out, down to the last thread and shred, and, not trusting himself, repeated the inspection three times. But there was nothing, it seemed, no traces; only on the spot where his pants had been cut off and hung by frayed tassels, there remained thick traces of congealed blood on the tassels. He grabbed a large folding knife and cut off the tassels. It seemed there was nothing else. Suddenly he remembered that the wallet and items he had taken from the old woman's chest were still lying in his pockets! He hadn't even thought to take them out and hide them yet! He hadn't even thought of them now, while inspecting his dress! What is this?
