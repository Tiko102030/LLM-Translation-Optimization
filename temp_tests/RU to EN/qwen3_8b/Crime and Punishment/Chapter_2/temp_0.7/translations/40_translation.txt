The impulse was so strong that he had already risen from his place to carry it out. "Should I think about it for a moment?" — ran through his mind. "No, better not to think at all and just take it and run!" But suddenly he froze as if nailed to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was speaking passionately to Il'yas Petrovich, and the words reached him: "It can't be, both will be freed! First of all, everything contradicts; judge: why call a footman if it's their business? To report on themselves, perhaps? Or for cunning? No, that would be too cunning! And finally, the student Pestryakov was seen near the gates by both footmen and a townswoman at the very moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted from them near the gates, inquiring about lodging with the footmen, still with his friends. Well, he would inquire about lodging if he had such an intention?"
