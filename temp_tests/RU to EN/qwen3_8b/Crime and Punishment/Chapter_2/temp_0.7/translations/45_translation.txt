It was strange. — Well, well, that's good, — concluded Ilia Petrovich, — we won't detain you. Raskolnikov left. He could still hear a lively conversation beginning outside his room, in which Nikodim Fomich's questioning voice was most clearly heard… On the street, he completely came to himself. "A search, a search, a search is coming! — he kept repeating to himself, hurrying to get away; — bandits! They suspect me!" The previous fear seized him all over again, from his feet to his head. II

"But what if there was already a search? What if I just happen to run into them here?"
But here was his room. Nothing and no one; no one had come in.
