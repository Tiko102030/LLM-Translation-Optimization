But who knows, perhaps it's not getting better, but worse… Will you take it or not? Raskolnikov silently took the German notes, took three rubles, and without saying a word, left. Razumikhin looked after him in surprise. But having reached the first line, Raskolnikov suddenly turned back, climbed up again to Razumikhin, placed the German notes and three rubles on the table, and again said nothing, and went out. — You've got white fever, that's it! — roared the finally enraged Razumikhin. — What kind of comedy are you playing! Even I was confused… Why did you come back after that, damn it?
