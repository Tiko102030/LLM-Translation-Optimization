"By God, that's it!—he thought suddenly, seized by an unrelenting fit of wrath. Well, it's begun, and so be it! Let her damned be and her new life! How foolish, Lord, how foolish!... How many lies and degradations I've told and committed today! How despicable I was, bowing and flattering lately with the most vile Ilyich Petrovich! Well, nonsense and that too! I don't care about them all, and not at all about the fact that I bowed and flattered! Entirely different! Entirely different!..."

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected, and extremely simple question at once confused him and greatly astonished him:

"If indeed all this was done deliberately, not foolishly, if you indeed had a definite and firm goal, then how come you haven't even looked into your wallet yet and don't know what you've gotten, because of which you endured all these torments and willingly went into such a vile, dirty, low deed?"
