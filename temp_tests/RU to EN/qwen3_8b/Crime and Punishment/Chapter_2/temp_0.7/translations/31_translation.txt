And he opened the window on the canal and stood by the window, squealing like a little pig; and this is a disgrace. And how could one stand by the window on the street, squealing like a little pig; and this is a disgrace. Fui-fui-fui! And Karl dragged him by the coat from the window, and indeed, sir captain, his rock was torn. And then he screamed that he had to pay fifteen shillings as a fine. And I myself, sir captain, paid five shillings to him for the rock. And this is an unworthy guest, sir captain, and one who causes every scandal! I, saying, will be a big satirist, because I can write about you in all the newspapers. — From writers, then? — Yes, sir captain, and what kind of an unworthy guest, sir captain, when in a noble house…
