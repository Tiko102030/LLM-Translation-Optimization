It seemed to him that he had somehow cut himself off from everything and everyone at that moment with scissors. He arrived at himself by evening, meaning he had walked for only six hours. He didn't remember where or how he had returned. Undressing and trembling all over like a driven horse, he lay on the sofa, pulled on his overcoat, and immediately fell asleep...

He awoke in the full darkness of twilight to an awful scream. God, what was that scream! He had never before heard such unnatural sounds, such howling, screaming, screeching, weeping, cursing, and blasphemy. He couldn't even imagine such cruelty, such frenzy. In terror, he raised himself up and sat on his bed, each moment trembling and suffering. But the fighting, the screams, and the curses grew stronger and stronger. And suddenly, to his greatest astonishment, he clearly heard the voice of his mistress.
