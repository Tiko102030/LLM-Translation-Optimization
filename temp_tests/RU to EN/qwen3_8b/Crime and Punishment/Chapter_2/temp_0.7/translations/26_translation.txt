The clerk looked at them with a smile. The hot-headed captain was evidently confused. "This isn't your business!" he shouted finally in an unnaturally loud voice, "but you may as well submit the report they require from you. Show him, Alexander Grigoryevich. Complaints about you! Don't pay money! What a bold eagle you've flown!" But Raskolnikov no longer listened and greedily grabbed the paper, searching for the solution as quickly as possible. He read it once, twice, and didn't understand.
