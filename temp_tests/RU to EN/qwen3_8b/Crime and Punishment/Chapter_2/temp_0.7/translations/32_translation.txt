— Yes, Mr. Captain, and what a dishonorable guest, Mr. Captain, when in a noble house…

— Ну-ну-ну! Enough! I've already told you, I've told you, I've told you…

— Colonel Ilyich! — the clerk spoke again significantly. The ensign quickly glanced at him; the clerk slightly nodded his head. — …So here's my final tale, esteemed Lady Laviza Ivanovna, and this is for the last time, — continued the ensign. — If another scandal occurs in your noble house, I'll punish you the most severely, as is said in high style. Did you hear? So a writer, a composer, took five kopecks in "The Noble House" for a fur? There they are, the composers!
