—"You, such a peculiar and unconventional person," he suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the widow had already left), "what happened last night? Again, disgrace, making a rowdy scene on the whole street. Again, a fight and drunkenness. You're dreaming of peace! I've already told you, I've warned you ten times that I won't let you in on the eleventh! And you, again, again, you, such a peculiar and unconventional person! Even the paper fell from Raskolnikov's hands, and he wildly stared at the plump lady who was so impertinently teasing her; but soon, however, he realized what was going on, and immediately this entire incident began to please him very much. He listened with enjoyment, so much so that he wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh... All his nerves were jumping. — Ilya Petrovich!"
