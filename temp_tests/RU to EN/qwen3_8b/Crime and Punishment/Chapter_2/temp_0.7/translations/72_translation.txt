He looked at her, barely breathing. — I heard it myself... I didn't sleep... I was sitting, — he spoke even more hesitantly. — I listened for a long time... The assistant overseer came... Everyone rushed to the stairs, from all the apartments... — No one came. But your blood is screaming. It's when she has no way out and starts to roast her liver, then hallucinations begin... Will you eat at all? He didn't answer. Nastasia stood over him, stared intently at him and didn't leave. — Give me something to drink... Nastasyushka. She went downstairs and came back two minutes later with water in a white clay cup; but he already didn't remember what came next. He only remembered how he took a sip of cold water and spilled it from the cup onto his chest.
