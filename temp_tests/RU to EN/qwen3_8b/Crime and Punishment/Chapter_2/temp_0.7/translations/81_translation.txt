What have you got there, a book, eh? — A book, you see. — Give it here. Well, Rodia, get up. I'll hold you; give him Raskolnikov, take the pen, brother, money is now sweeter than molasses. — No need, — said Raskolnikov, pushing away the pen. — Why not? — I won't sign. — Blazes, how can we do without a signature? — No need… money… — This is not about money!
