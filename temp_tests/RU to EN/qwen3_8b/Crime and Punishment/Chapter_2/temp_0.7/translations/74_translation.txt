Then he tried to get up, wanted to run, but someone always stopped him by force, and he again fell into helplessness and confusion. Finally, he completely came to himself. This happened in the morning, at ten o'clock. At this hour of the morning, on clear days, the sun always passed across his right wall in a long strip, illuminating the corner by the door. Standing by his bed was Nastasia and another person, who was very curiously observing him and completely unknown to him. This was a young man in a kaftan, with a beard, and appeared to be an artel'nik. The landlady peered through the half-open door. Raskolnikov raised himself. — Who is this, Nastasia? — he asked, pointing at the man.
