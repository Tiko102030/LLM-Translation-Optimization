— You, such and such a person, — he suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the mourning lady had already left), — what happened last night, eh? Another disgrace, making a rowdy scene throughout the street again. Another fight and drunkenness. You're dreaming of peace! I've already told you, I've already warned you ten times that I won't let you in on the 11th! And you're doing it again, again, you such and such a person! Even a paper fell from Raskolnikov's hands, and he stared wildly at the plump lady, who was so impertinently treated; but soon, nevertheless, he realized what was going on, and immediately the whole story began to appeal to him very much. He listened with pleasure, so much so that he longed to laugh, to laugh, to laugh… His nerves were jumping. — Ilya Petrovich!
