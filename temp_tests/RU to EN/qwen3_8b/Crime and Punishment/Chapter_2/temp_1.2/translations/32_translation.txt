— Yes, Captain, and what an unhonorable guest, Captain, when in a noble house…

— Nun-nun-nun! Enough! I've already told you, told you, I've told you…

— Colonel Ilyich! — the scribe spoke again significantly. The ensign quickly glanced at him; the scribe slightly nodded his head. — …Here you go, esteemed Laviza Ivanovna, my final tale, and this is for the last time, — continued the ensign. — If another scandal occurs in your noble house, I'll personally take you to the highest style, as it's said in high style. Did you hear? A writer, a scribe, took five kopecks in the noble house for a fold? There they are, the writers!
