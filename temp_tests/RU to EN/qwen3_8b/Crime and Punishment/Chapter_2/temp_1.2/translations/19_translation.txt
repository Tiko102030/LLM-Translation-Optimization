No one noticed him. In the second room, some scribes sat and wrote, dressed only a little better than him, appearing to be an oddly peculiar sort of people. He addressed one of them. "What do you want?" He showed a summons from the office. "Are you a student?" asked the other, looking at the summons. "Yes, a former student." The scribe glanced at him, albeit without any curiosity. He was a particularly unkempt man with a fixed idea in his gaze.
