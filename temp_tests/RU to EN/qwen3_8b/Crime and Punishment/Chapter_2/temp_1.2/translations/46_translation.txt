Nothing and no one; nobody had peeked. Even Nastasya did not touch. But, Lord! How could he leave all these things in this hole? He rushed to the corner, pushed his hand under the wallpaper, and started pulling out items, loading them into his pockets. There were eight items: two small boxes with earrings or something like that—he didn't look carefully; then four small sapphire cases. One chain was simply wrapped in newspaper. Something else in newspaper, seems an order… He put everything into different pockets, in the coat and in the remaining right pocket of pants, trying to make it inconspicuous. The wallet also took along with the items. Then he left the room, this time even leaving it completely open.
