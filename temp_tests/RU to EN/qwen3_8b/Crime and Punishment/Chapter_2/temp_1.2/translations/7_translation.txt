There's not even a match. No, better to go somewhere and throw everything out. Yes! Better to throw it all out! — he repeated, sitting again on the sofa — and right now, this very minute, without delay!... But instead, his head again leaned on the pillow; again, an unbearable chill seized him; again, he pulled on his overcoat. For a long time, several hours, he still felt a sudden urge, as if thinking, "Now, without delay, go somewhere and throw everything out, so it's out of sight, as soon as possible, as soon as possible!" He tried to get up from the sofa several times, wanted to stand up, but couldn't anymore. Finally, he was awakened by a strong knock on the door. "Open the door, alive or not? And he's sleeping all the time!" Nastasya shouted, pounding her fist on the door, "All day, all day, like a dog, he's sleeping!"
