It seemed to him that he had somehow cut himself off with scissors from everyone and everything at that moment. He had come back to himself by evening, meaning he had walked for only six hours. He didn't remember where or how he had walked back. Undressing and trembling all over, like a terrified horse, he lay on the couch, pulled on his overcoat, and immediately fell asleep...

He awoke in pitch darkness from a terrible scream. God, what a scream! He had never before heard or seen such unnatural sounds, such howling, wailing, screeching, tears, fighting, and swearing. He could not even imagine such cruelty, such frenzy. In fear, he raised himself up and sat on his bed, each moment trembling and suffering. But the brawls, screams, and swearing grew louder and louder. And there, to his greatest astonishment, he suddenly recognized his mistress's voice.
