— No, said he, — I came… here's the thing: I have no lessons… I wanted to… in any case, I completely don't need lessons… — Do you know what? You're talking nonsense! — remarked Razumikhin, who had been observing him closely. — No, I'm not raving… — Raskolnikov rose from the sofa. As he got up to face Razumikhin, he didn't think about the fact that he was supposed to meet him face to face. Now, in an instant, he realized, through experience, that he was least inclined, at this moment, to confront anyone in the whole world. All his bile rose within him. He almost drowned in self-loathing for having just crossed Razumikhin's threshold. — Goodbye! — he said suddenly and walked toward the door.
