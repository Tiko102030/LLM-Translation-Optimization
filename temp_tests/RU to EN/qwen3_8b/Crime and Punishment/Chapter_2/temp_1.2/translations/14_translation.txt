Nastasya, so to speak, couldn't have noticed anything from afar, praise be! Then, trembling with fear, he opened the notice and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood. It was an ordinary notice from the district to appear today, at half past ten, in the district overseer's office. "When has this ever happened? I have no business with the police on my own! And why exactly today?" he thought to himself in agonizing bewilderment. "Lord, just hurry up!" He was about to kneel down to pray, but even he himself laughed—not over the prayer, but over himself. He quickly started to dress. "I'll be lost anyway, no matter what! Put on a sock!"
