"No, the matter is very unclear," said Ilyia Petrovich. Raskolnikov raised his hat and went towards the door, but he did not reach the door... 

When he awoke, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that someone was supporting him on the right, that on the left stood another person with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nickodim Fomich was standing before him, staring intently at him; he got up from the chair. "What is this, are you ill?" Nickodim Fomich asked rather sharply. "They scarcely wrote their signatures with a pen," noted the note-taker, sitting down at his place and resuming his work with the papers. "How long have you been ill?" Ilyia Petrovich shouted from his place, also rummaging through the papers.
