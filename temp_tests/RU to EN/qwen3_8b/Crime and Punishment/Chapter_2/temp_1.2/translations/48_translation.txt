And what if the cases don't sink, but float? Yes, and of course that's how it is. Everyone will see. And already, they look at each other, glancing, as if their entire concern is for him. "Why not so, or perhaps it's just me?" he thought. Finally, it occurred to him that perhaps it would be better to go somewhere on the Neva? There are fewer people, it's less noticeable, and in any case more convenient, above all, farther from these places. And he suddenly realized: how could he have walked for half an hour in melancholy and anxiety, and in dangerous places, and this he couldn't imagine before! And that's why he spent half an hour on a reckless undertaking, since it was already decided once in a dream, in a delirium! He became extremely distracted and forgetful and knew this.
