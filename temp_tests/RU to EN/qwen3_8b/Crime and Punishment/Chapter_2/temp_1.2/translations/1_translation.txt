"Three hours already!" He sat on the couch — and then he suddenly remembered everything! In the first moment, he thought he was going mad. A terrible cold wrapped around him; but the cold was also from the fever, which had already begun with him in his sleep. Now, suddenly, such a chill struck that his teeth almost came out, and everything inside him was twitching. He opened the door and began listening: everyone in the house was completely asleep. He looked at himself and everything around him in the room with amazement and didn't understand: how could he, upon entering yesterday, not lock the doors on the hook and throw himself onto the couch, not only not undressing, but even wearing a hat: it had slipped and immediately lay on the floor, near the pillow. "If someone had come in, what would they think? That I'm drunk, but..." He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began examining himself, from head to toe, all his clothes: were there any signs?
