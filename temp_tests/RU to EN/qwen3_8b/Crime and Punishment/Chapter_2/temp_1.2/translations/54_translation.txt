Not at all!...

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question immediately threw him off and greatly surprised him:

"If indeed all this was done on purpose, not foolishly, if you indeed had a definite and firm goal, then how come you haven't even looked into the wallet yet and don't know what you've gotten, why you've endured all this suffering and consciously went through such a mean, nasty, low act? You were even about to throw it all— the wallet, together with all the things you hadn't even seen yet—into the water. What's going on?"

Yes, that's exactly it; that's all true. He had known that all along, and it wasn't a new question for him at all; and when it was decided during the night to throw it all away, it was decided without any hesitation or objections, as if that was the only way it could be, as if it was impossible otherwise... Yes, he knew all this and remembered it all; it was almost as if this had been decided just yesterday, at the very moment when he had been sitting over the chest and pulling the cases out of it... But that's exactly what happened! "It's because I'm very sick," he decided grimly at last, "I've tortured myself and torn myself apart, and I don't even know what I'm doing... And yesterday, and the day before, and all this time I've been torturing myself... I'll recover and... stop torturing myself... But what if I never recover at all? Lord! How tired I am of all this!..." He kept walking without stopping. He desperately wanted to somehow get rid of these feelings, but he didn't know what to do or what to do next. One new, insurmountable sensation was taking hold of him more and more, almost with every minute: it was an infinite, almost physical repulsion toward everything he encountered and everything around him, persistent, spiteful, hateful. Everything around him was repulsive to him—their faces, their gait, their movements. He just wanted to spit on someone, maybe even bite them if they spoke to him...

He suddenly stopped when he reached the Embankment of the Small Neva, on the Vyborg Island, near the bridge.
