"By the devil, that's it! — he thought suddenly, seized by an unrelenting wrath. — Well, it's begun, so it's begun, may the devil take the new life! How could this be, Lord, how foolish!... How much I lied and slandered today! How contemptible I was, fawning and flattery over the most despicable Ilyich Petrovich! And nonsense, even that! I care nothing for them all, nor for the fact that I fawned and flattered! Entirely different! Entirely different!..."

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question at once bewildered him and bitterly surprised him:

"If indeed all this was done consciously, not foolishly, if you truly had a definite and firm goal, then how come you have not even glanced into your wallet up to now and do not know what you have received, for what you have undergone all these torments, and for what you intentionally went through such a despicable, nasty, lowly deed?"
