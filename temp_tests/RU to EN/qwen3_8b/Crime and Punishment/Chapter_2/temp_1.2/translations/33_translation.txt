There they are, the writers! — and he cast a disdainful glance at Raskolnikov. — Three days ago, in the tavern, there was another incident: he dined, but refused to pay; "I'll describe you in satire," he said. On the steamship, another incident occurred last week: a respected family of a state councillor, his wife and daughter, were insulted with the most contemptible words. Yesterday, from the confectionery, I was thrown out for some reason. That's how they are, the writers, the literary figures, the scholars, the public speakers… tut! Go away! I'll come myself… then be careful! Did you hear? Luiza Ivanovna, with affected politeness, began to sit down on all sides, and as she sat down, she managed to reach the door; but she bumped into an officer's back in the doorway, a man with an open, fresh face and excellent, thick, golden whiskers.
