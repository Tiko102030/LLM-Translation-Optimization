It was strange. — Well, then, good, so— concluded Ilya Petrovich— we won't detain you. Raskolnikov left. He still managed to overhear how, upon his exit, a lively conversation began, in which the interrogative voice of Nikodim Fomich stood out most clearly… On the street he completely came to himself. "Search, search, a search now!— he repeated to himself, hurrying to get there;— bandits! They suspect!" The previous fear again seized him from head to toe. II

"What if there was already a search? What if I just happen to catch them at home?"
