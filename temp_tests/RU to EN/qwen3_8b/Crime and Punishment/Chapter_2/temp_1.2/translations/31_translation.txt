And he opened the window, and stood in the window, squealing like a little pig; that's a disgrace. How can one squeal like a little pig in the street, and that's a disgrace. Phooey! And Karl pulled his coat from behind the window, and indeed, sir captain, he tore his coat. And then he cried out that he must pay a fine of fifteen crown pieces. And I myself, sir captain, paid five crown pieces to him. And this is an ungentlemanly guest, sir captain, and he makes a lot of fuss! I, speaking, will be a great satyr about you, because I can write about you in all the newspapers. — From the writers, then? — Yes, sir captain, and what an ungentlemanly guest, sir captain, when in a noble house… — Well, well, well!
