Read once, twice, and still didn't understand. — What is this? — asked he the clerk. — This is a claim for the loan document, recovery. You must either pay all costs, penalties, and other charges, or give a written response stating when you can pay, along with an undertaking not to leave the capital until payment is made and not to sell or hide your property. The creditor is at liberty to sell your property and act according to the law. — Yes, I... owe nobody! — That's not our affair. However, we have received a loan document worth 150 rubles, issued to the widow Zarnitsyna, collegiate assessor, nine months ago, which has now passed to the town councillor Chebarov for recovery; therefore, we are inviting you to respond. — But she's my housekeeper!
