He asked, pointing at the boy. "Well, look! He's come to!" she said. "They woke up," replied the artelshchik. Realizing that he had woken up, the mistress, who had been peeking through the door, immediately closed them and hid. She had always been shy and reluctantly endured conversations and explanations; she was forty years old, fat and corpulent, dark-browed and dark-eyed, kind from her fatness and laziness; and even very oily in her appearance. She was shy beyond necessity. "Who are you?" he continued to question, addressing the artelshchik directly. But at this moment the door opened wide again, and, leaning slightly because he was tall, Razumikhin entered.
