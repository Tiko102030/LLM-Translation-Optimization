— But allow me, how could there be such a contradiction: they themselves claim they knocked and the door was locked, but three minutes later, when they came with the groundskeeper, it turned out the door was unlocked? — The trick is that the killer must have been sitting there and locked himself in; he would certainly have been caught there if it weren't for Koch taking a chance and going himself to the groundskeeper. And he exactly managed to go down the stairs during that time and sneak past them somehow. Koch crosses himself with both hands: "If I had stayed there, he would have jumped out and killed me with an axe." A Russian prayer service wants to be held, heh-heh! — But no one saw the killer? — Where would you see him? The house is like Noah's ark, — noted the clerk, who was listening from his place.
