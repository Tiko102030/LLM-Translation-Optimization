—"Where to, may I ask?" — "Along the street." — "Briefly and clearly." Raskolnikov answered sharply, abruptly, entirely pale as a handkerchief, without lowering his black, inflamed eyes before Ilya Petrovich's gaze. — "He's barely on his feet, but you…" — Nikodim Fomich was about to remark. — "Nothing!" — Ilya Petrovich said in a particularly manner. Nikodim Fomich had wanted to add something else, but upon looking at the secretary, who was also intently staring at him, he fell silent. Everyone suddenly fell silent. It was strange.
