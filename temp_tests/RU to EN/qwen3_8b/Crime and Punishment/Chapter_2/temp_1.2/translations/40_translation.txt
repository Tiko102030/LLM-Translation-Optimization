The call was so strong that he already rose from his place to carry it out. "Should I think for a minute?"—passed through his mind. "No, better not think at all and just throw it to the shoulders!" But suddenly he stopped as if nailed to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was speaking passionately to Ilyia Petrovich, and the words reached him: "It's impossible, both of them can't be freed! First of all, everything contradicts; judge: why call a servant if this is their business? To report on themselves, or for cunning? No, that would be too cunning! And finally, Pestryakov, the student, was seen near the gates by both the servants and the merchant's wife at the exact moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted from them near the gates, inquiring about the residents with his friends. Well, will he inquire about the residents if he came with such an intention?"
