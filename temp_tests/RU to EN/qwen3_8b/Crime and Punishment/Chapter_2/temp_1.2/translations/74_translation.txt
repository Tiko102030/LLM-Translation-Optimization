Then he tried to get up, wanted to run away, but someone always stopped him by force, and he would again fall into helplessness and confusion. Finally, he completely regained consciousness. This happened in the morning, at ten o'clock. At this time of morning, on clear days, the sun always passed along his right wall, illuminating the corner near the door. By his bed stood Nastasya and another person, who was looking at him with great curiosity and was completely unknown to him. This was a young man in a caftan, with a beard, and from appearances resembled an artelshik. The mistress peered out from the half-open door. Raskolnikov raised himself. — Who is that, Nastasya? — he asked, pointing at the man.
