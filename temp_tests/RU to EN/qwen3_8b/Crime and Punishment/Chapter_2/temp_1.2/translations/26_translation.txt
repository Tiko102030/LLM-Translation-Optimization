The clerk looked at them with a smile. The fiery captain seemed visibly perplexed. "This isn't your business!" he finally shouted unnaturally loudly, "Now, you may as well submit the report they require from you. Show him, Aleksey Grigoryevich. Complaints about you! Don't pay any money! What a bold eagle you've flown!" But Raskolnikov was no longer listening and greedily grabbed the paper, seeking to quickly unravel the mystery. He read it once, twice, and still didn't understand.
