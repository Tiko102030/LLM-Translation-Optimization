His entire room was so large that one could take off a hook without leaving the bed. So it is: the janitor and Nastasya are standing. Nastasya looked at him in some strange way. He looked at the janitor with a defiant and desperate expression. He silently handed him a gray, folded-in-half paper sealed with bottle gum. — A summons from the office, — he said, handing over the paper. — From which office? — They call it the police office. It's known which office. — To the police!
