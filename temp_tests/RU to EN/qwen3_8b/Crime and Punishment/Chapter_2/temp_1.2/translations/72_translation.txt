He looked at her, barely breathing. — I heard it myself… I didn't sleep… I was sitting, — he said even more hesitantly. — I listened for a long time… The assistant of the overseer came… Everyone ran down the stairs, from all the apartments… — No one came. But your blood is crying out. That's when she has no way out and starts to burn from the inside, and then hallucinations begin… Are you going to eat at all? He didn't answer. Nastasya stood over him, looked at him intently, and didn't leave. — Give me something to drink… Nastasyushka. She went downstairs and came back minutes later with water in a white clay cup; but he already didn't remember what happened next. He only remembered how he took a sip of cold water and spilled it from the cup onto his chest.
