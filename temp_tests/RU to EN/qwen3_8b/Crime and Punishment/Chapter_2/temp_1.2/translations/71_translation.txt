It became very unpleasant for him from this observation, even frightening. — Nastasia, why are you staying silent? — he finally managed to say timidly in a weak voice. — This is blood, — she finally answered, quietly and as if speaking to herself. — Blood!... What blood?... — he muttered, growing paler and moving away from the wall. Nastasia continued to look at him silently. — No one beat the mistress, — she said again in a strict and resolute voice. He looked at her, barely breathing.
