Leave me alone! — Wait a minute, chimney sweep! Completely crazy! As for me, it's up to you. You see, I have no lessons, and I don't care; there is a bookseller Heruvimov on Tolkuchoy, which is in itself a lesson. I won't exchange him for five merchant lessons. He produces such publications and issues natural science books — how they sell! The titles alone are worth something! You've always said I'm stupid; by God, brother, there are stupider than me! Now he's also getting into direction; he feels nothing at all, but of course, I encourage him.
