Having guessed that he had come to, the landlady, peering in from the door, immediately shut it and hid. She was always shy and could hardly bear conversations and explanations; she was about forty years old, fat and plump, dark-browed and black-eyed, kind because of her corpulence and laziness; and even very pretty in appearance. Shy beyond necessity. “You… who are you?” he continued to interrogate, turning to the foreman himself. But at that moment the door opened wide again, and Razumikhin entered, bending slightly because he was tall.
