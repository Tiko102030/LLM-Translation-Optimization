 — But isn't she my employer? — Well, so what if she's the employer? The clerk looked at him with a condescending smile of сожаления, and at the same time some triumph, as at a newbie who is just beginning to be обстрелян: "What, how do you feel now?" But what, what did he care now about the loan letter, about collection! Was it worth any anxiety now, in turn, even some attention! He stood, read, listened, answered, even asked himself, but all this mechanically. The triumph of self-preservation, escape from pressing danger — that's what filled his whole being at that moment, without foresight, without analysis, without future guessing and solving, without doubts and questions. It was a minute of complete, непосредственной, чисто животной joy. But at that very moment something like thunder and lightning occurred in the office. The lieutenant, still shaken by the disrespect, all flushed and obviously wanting to support his injured ambition, pounced on the unfortunate "pompous lady," who had been looking at him since he entered with the silliest smile. — And you, you this and that and the other, — he shouted suddenly at the top of his lungs (the mourning lady has already left), — what happened to you last night?
