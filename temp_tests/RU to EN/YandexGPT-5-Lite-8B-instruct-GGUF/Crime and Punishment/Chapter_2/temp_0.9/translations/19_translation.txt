 No one noticed him. In the second room some scribes were sitting and writing, dressed only a little better than him, they looked like an odd sort of people. He turned to one of them. "What do you want?" He showed the summons from the office. "Are you a student?" the man asked, glancing at the summons. "Yes, a former student." The scribe looked him over, however without any curiosity. It was some particularly disheveled person with a fixed idea in his gaze.
