 Leave me alone! — Wait a minute, chimneysweep! You're crazy! Do with me as you please. You see, I have no lessons and don't care, but there's a bookseller named Cheruvimov at Tolkuchy Market, that's a lesson in its own way. I wouldn't trade him for five merchant's lessons now. He makes such little publications and issues natural science booklets — and how they sell! Just the titles are worth something! You always said I was stupid; by God, brother, there are people stupider than me! Now he has ventured into direction too; he doesn't feel anything himself, but of course I encourage him.
