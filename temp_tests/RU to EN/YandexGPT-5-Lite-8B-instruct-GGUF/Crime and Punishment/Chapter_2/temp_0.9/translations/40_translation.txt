 The impulse was so strong that he got up to act on it. “Shouldn’t I think about it for at least a minute?” passed through his head. “No, it’s better not to think and get it over with!” But suddenly he stopped as if nailed to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was talking passionately to Ilya Petrovich, and some words reached him:

— It can't be, both will be released! Firstly, everything contradicts; judge for yourself: why would they call a janitor if it were their business? To inform on themselves, perhaps? Or is it cunning? No, that would be too clever! And finally, the janitors and a tradeswoman saw student Pestryakov at the very gates the moment he was entering: he was walking with three friends and parted with them at the very gate, and asked about living quarters from the janitors, still in front of his friends. Well, would someone like that ask about living quarters if they were going there with such an intention?
