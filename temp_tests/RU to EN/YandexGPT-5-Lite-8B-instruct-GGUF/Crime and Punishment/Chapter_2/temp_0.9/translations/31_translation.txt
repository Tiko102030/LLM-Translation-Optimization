 And he opened the window onto the canal and stuck his head out like a little pig squealing; and that's shameful. And how can one stick out of the window onto the street, like a little pig squealing; and that's shameful. Fie-fie-fie! And Karl was pulling him by the tailcoat from the window behind him, and it is true, Mr. Captain, his coat got torn. And then he shouted that he had to pay a fine of fifteen silver coins. And I myself, Mr. Captain, paid him five silver coins for his coat. And this is an ignoble guest, Mr. Captain, who makes all the scandals! I, I say, will gather up great satire on you, because I can write about you in all the newspapers. — From writers, then? — Yes, Mr. Captain, and what kind of a noble guest is this, Mr. Captain, when he's in a noble house…
