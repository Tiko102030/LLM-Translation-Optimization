 There was a terrible stuffiness because of that. Sweepers with books under their arms went up and down, porters and various people of both sexes — visitors. The door to the office itself was also wide open. He entered and stopped in the hallway. Some men were standing and waiting here too. There was also extreme stuffiness here, and besides, the fresh, not yet set paint on the rotten linseed oil in the newly painted rooms hit the nose to the point of nausea. After waiting a while, he decided to move even further into the next room. All the rooms were tiny and low-ceilinged. A terrible impatience pulled him further and further. Nobody noticed him.
