 Nothing and nobody; no one looked in. Not even Nastassia touched anything. But, oh my God! How could he have left all these things in that hole the other day? He rushed to a corner, reached under the wallpaper and began to pull out items and stuff them into his pockets. There were eight of them: two small boxes with earrings or something like that — he didn't take a good look; then four small morocco cases. The chain was simply wrapped in newspaper. Something else in newspaper paper, an order, it seems...
He put everything in different pockets, in his coat and in the remaining right pocket of his pants, trying to make it less noticeable. He also took the wallet along with the things. Then he left the room, this time even leaving it wide open.
