In two minutes, Nastasya returned with the soup and announced that tea would follow shortly. Two spoons, two plates, and all the cutlery appeared for the soup: salt cellar, pepper shaker, mustard for beef, and other things that had not been seen in such order for a long time before. The tablecloth was clean. "It wouldn't be bad, Nastasya, if Praskovya Pavlovna would send over two bottles of beer. We can drink some." "Oh, you sly one!" murmured Nastasya and went to carry out the order.
