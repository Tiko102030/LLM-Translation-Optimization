 It seemed to him that at that moment he had cut himself off from everyone and everything as if with scissors. He came back to his place only by evening, therefore he had been out for about six hours. He did not remember anything about where and how he was going back. After undressing and shaking like a galloped horse, he lay down on the sofa, pulled his overcoat over himself and immediately fell asleep...

He woke up in complete twilight from a terrible scream. God, what a scream that was! He had never heard or seen such unnatural sounds, such howling, wailing, grinding, crying, beatings and cursing.
