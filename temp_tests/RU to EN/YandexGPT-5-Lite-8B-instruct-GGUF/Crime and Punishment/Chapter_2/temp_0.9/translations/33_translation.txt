 Look at them, the writers! — and he darted a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. — The other day in a tavern there was also a story: had lunch, but does not want to pay; “I, they say, will describe you in satire for that”. On the steamer too, another one, last week, the respectable family of a statsky sovetnik, the wife and daughter, were called names in the vilest words. The other day they kicked someone out of a pastry shop. That's what writers, literati, students, heralds... ugh! And you went off! I'll come to see you myself… then beware! Heard that? Louise Ivanovna hurriedly started curtsying in all directions and, while curtsying, backed up to the door; but in the doorway she bumped into an officer with an open, fresh face and excellent thick blond sideburns.
