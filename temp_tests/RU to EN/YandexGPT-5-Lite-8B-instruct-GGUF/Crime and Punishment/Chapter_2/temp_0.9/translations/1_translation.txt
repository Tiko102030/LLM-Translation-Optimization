 “It’s the third hour already!” He sat down on the sofa — and then he remembered everything! Suddenly, in an instant, he remembered everything! For a moment he thought he would go mad. A terrible chill seized him; but the cold was also from the fever that had long begun with him in his sleep. Now suddenly such a chills struck him that his teeth nearly jumped out and everything inside him shook. He opened the door and began to listen: the whole house was completely asleep. With amazement he looked around at himself and everything around in the room, and did not understand: how could he yesterday, having entered, not lock the doors with a hook and throw himself on the sofa, not only without undressing, but even in his hat: it rolled down and lay there right on the floor, near the pillow. “If anyone had come in, what would he have thought? That I’m drunk, but…” He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began to look at himself, all over, from head to toe, his entire clothes: were there any traces?”
