 But no, he hears too clearly!.. But, therefore, they will come to him now too, if so, “because... surely, all this is the same... because of yesterday... Lord!” He wanted to lock himself in with a hook, but his hand wouldn't rise... and it’s useless! Fear surrounded his soul like ice, tortured him, numbed him... But finally, after about ten minutes, all this uproar began to gradually subside. The landlady was moaning and groaning, Ilya Petrovich was still threatening and swearing... But finally, it seems, he too fell silent; you can no longer hear him; “could he have left! Lord!” Yes, the landlady is leaving now too, still groaning and crying...and her door slammed shut... Now the crowd is dispersing from the stairs to their apartments — they gasp, argue, call out to each other, raising their speech to shouting and lowering it to a whisper. There must have been many of them; almost the whole house had come running. “But God, is all this really possible! And why, why did he come here!”
Raskolnikov fell powerlessly onto the sofa, but could no longer close his eyes; he lay there for half an hour in such suffering, in such unbearable feelings of boundless horror, which he had never experienced before. Suddenly, bright light illuminated his room: Nastasya entered with a candle and a plate of soup. After looking at him attentively and seeing that he was awake, she put the candle on the table and started to arrange what she had brought: bread, salt, a plate, a spoon.
