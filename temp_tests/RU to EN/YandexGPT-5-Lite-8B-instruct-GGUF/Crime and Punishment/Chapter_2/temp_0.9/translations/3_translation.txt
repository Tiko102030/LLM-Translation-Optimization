 Well, what's this? He rushed to take them all out and throw them onto the table. Having chosen everything, even turning his pockets inside out to make sure there was nothing left, he carried the whole pile to the corner. There, in the very corner, down below, the wallpaper had been torn away from the wall in one place: immediately he began shoving everything into this hole under the paper: “It went in! Everything is out of sight, and so is my wallet!” — he thought joyfully, standing up and staring blankly into the corner at the hole that was sticking out even more. Suddenly he вздрогнул with horror: “Oh my God,” he whispered in despair, “what's happening to me? Was this hidden here? Is this how you hide things?”

Indeed, he hadn't expected any items; he thought there would be only money, which is why he hadn't prepared a place beforehand — “but what am I happy about now? — he thought. — Is this really how you hide things?”
