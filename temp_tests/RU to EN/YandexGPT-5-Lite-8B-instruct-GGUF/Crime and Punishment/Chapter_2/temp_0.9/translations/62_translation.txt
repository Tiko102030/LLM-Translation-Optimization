Raskolnikov silently took the German sheets of the article, took three rubles and left without saying a word. Razumikhin looked at him in surprise. But having reached the first line, Raskolnikov suddenly returned, went up to Razumikhin again and, putting both the German sheets and three rubles on the table, left again without saying a word. "You must have delirium tremens or something!" roared Razumikhin, finally furious. "Why are you playing this comedy! You even confused me... Why did you come then, damn it?"
