And he managed to go down the stairs and sneak past them somehow during that interval. Koh crosses himself with both hands: ‘If I had stayed there, he would have jumped out and killed me with an axe.’ He wants to serve a Russian prayer service, heh-heh!..” “And no one saw the murderer?” “Where could you see him?
