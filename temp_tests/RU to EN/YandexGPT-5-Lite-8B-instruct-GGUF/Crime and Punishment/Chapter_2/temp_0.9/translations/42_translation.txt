 "The house is like Noah's Ark," remarked the clerk, listening from his place. "It's obvious, it's obvious!" Raskolnikov repeated hotly. "No, it's very unclear," emphasized Ilya Petrovich. Raskolnikov picked up his hat and went to the door, but he didn't reach the door...

When he came to, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, supported by some man on the right, another man with a yellow glass filled with yellow water stood on his left, and Nikodim Fomich was standing in front of him and staring at him; he got up from the chair. "What's going on, are you sick?" asked Nikodim Fomich rather sharply. "They could barely write even when they signed their names," the clerk noted, sitting back down in his place and resuming work on the papers. "And how long have you been ill?" shouted Ilya Petrovich from his place, also going through the papers.
