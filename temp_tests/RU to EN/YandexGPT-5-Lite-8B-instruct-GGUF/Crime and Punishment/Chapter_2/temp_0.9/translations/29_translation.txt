 — And you, you hussy and that sort, he suddenly shouted at the top of his voice (the mourning lady had already left), — what happened to you last night? Eh? Shame again, making a racket in the street. Fighting and drinking again. You dream about being put in a straitjacket! After all, I told you, I warned you ten times that on the eleventh I wouldn't let it slide! And you again, again, you hussy! Even Raskolnikov's paper fell out of his hands, and he stared wildly at the stout lady, who was being so unceremoniously dealt with; but soon, however, he realized what was going on, and immediately this whole story began to please him very much. He listened with pleasure, so much so that he wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh... All his nerves were jumping. — Ilya Petrovich!
