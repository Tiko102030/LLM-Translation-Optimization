 “No need,” he said, “I came… here’s what: I don’t have any lessons... I was about to... впрочем, me совсем не надо уроков...”
“But you know what? You're delirious!” — Razumikhin noted, observing him intently. “No, I’m not delusional…” Raskolnikov got up from the sofa. Rising towards Razumikhin, he had not thought that he would have to meet face to face with him. Now, in an instant, he realized, having learned through experience, that at that moment he was least disposed to come face to face with anyone in the whole world. All his bile rose up in him. He almost choked with rage against himself as soon as he crossed Razumikhin's threshold. “Goodbye!” — he said suddenly and went to the door.
