 "Pieces of torn canvas will not arouse suspicion in any case; it seems so, it seems so!" — he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with painfully intense attention began to look again around, on the floor and everywhere, whether he had forgotten anything else? The certainty that everything, even memory, even simple reasoning was failing him, began to torment him unbearably. "What, is it already beginning, is this punishment coming now? There, there, sure enough!" Indeed, scraps of fringe, which he had cut from his pants, were lying on the floor, in the middle of the room, for anyone to see first! "But what's happening to me!" — he cried again like a lost soul. Then a strange thought came to him: that perhaps all his clothes were covered in blood, that perhaps there were many stains, but that he just didn’t see them, didn’t notice them, because his reasoning had weakened, fragmented...his mind was darkened... Suddenly he remembered that there was also blood on the wallet. "Bah! So, then there must be blood in my pocket too, because I put a wet wallet into my pocket back then!" He instantly turned out his pocket, and sure enough — there were traces, stains on the lining of the pocket! "So it seems I haven’t completely lost my mind yet, so there is still some reasoning and memory, since I noticed it myself and guessed it!" — he thought triumphantly, breathing deeply and joyfully with his whole chest, "it's just a feebleness of feverishness, delirium for a minute" — and he tore out the entire lining from the left pocket of his pants.
