 It seemed to him that at that moment he had cut himself off from everyone and everything as if with scissors. He came to himself only in the evening, so it must have been about six hours. He didn't remember anything about how and where he walked back. After undressing and shaking like a hunted horse, he lay down on the sofa, pulled his overcoat over himself and immediately fell asleep...

He woke up in complete twilight from an awful scream. God, what a scream! He had never heard or seen such unnatural sounds, such howling, wailing, grinding, crying, fighting and swearing. He couldn't even imagine such brutality, such frenzy. In horror, he sat up on his bed, freezing and tormenting himself every moment. But the fights, screams and curses grew stronger and stronger.
