 Louise Ivanovna began curtseying in all directions with hurried любезностью and, as she curtsied, backed up to the door; but at the door she bumped into a distinguished officer from behind, with an open fresh face and excellent thick blond sideburns. It was Nikodim Fomich himself, the police inspector. Louise Ivanovna поспешила to curtsy almost to the floor and flew out of the office in quick small steps, bouncing. "Thunder again, thunder and lightning again, tornado, hurricane!" — addressed Nikodim Fomich to Ilya Petrovich with любезность and friendliness, — "again they stirred up my heart, again I boiled over! Heard it from the stairs." "Yes what!" — said Ilya Petrovich with noble negligence (and even not "shto" but somehow: "Da-a shta-a!"), moving to another table with some papers and картинно shrugging his shoulders with each step, where he stepped, there went the shoulder; — here, please see: Mr. writer, that is, a student, former that is, does not pay money, gave bills, does not clear out the apartment, continuous complaints are received about them, and you entered into a claim because I lit a cigarette in front of them! They themselves п-п-подличают, but here, please look at them: this is their most attractive appearance now! "Poverty is not a vice, friend, well, whatever!"
