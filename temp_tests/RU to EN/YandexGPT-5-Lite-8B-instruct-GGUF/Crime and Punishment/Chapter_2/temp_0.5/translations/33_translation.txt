 There they are, the writers! — and he shot a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. — The other day in the tavern there was also a story: had lunch, but doesn't want to pay; "I, say, will describe you in satire for that." On the ship too, another one, last week, the respectable family of a statsky советник, his wife and daughter, called them the vilest words. The other day they were kicked out of the confectionery by pushing someone. That's what they are, writers, literati, students, heralds... ugh! And you went there! I'll come to see you myself… then look out! Have you heard? Louise Ivanovna rushed to curtsy on all sides with hurried любезностью and, curtsying, backed up to the door; but in the doorway she bumped into an officer with a prominent open fresh face and excellent thick blond sideburns from behind.
