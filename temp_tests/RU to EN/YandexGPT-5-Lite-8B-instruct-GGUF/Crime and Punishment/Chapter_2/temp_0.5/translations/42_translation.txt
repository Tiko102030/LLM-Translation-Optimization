 "The house is like Noah's ark," remarked the clerk, listening from his place. "It's clear, it's clear!" Raskolnikov repeated fervently. "No, it's very unclear," affirmed Ilya Petrovich. Raskolnikov picked up his hat and went to the door, but he didn't reach the door...

When he came to his senses, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that someone was supporting him from the right, that another person with a yellow glass filled with yellow water was standing to his left, and that Nikodim Fomich was standing in front of him and staring at him; he got up from the chair. "What's going on, are you sick?" asked Nikodim Fomich rather harshly. "They could barely write with a pen even when they signed their names," the clerk noted, sitting down in his place and starting to deal with the papers again. "And how long have you been ill?" shouted Ilya Petrovich from his place, also sorting through the papers.
