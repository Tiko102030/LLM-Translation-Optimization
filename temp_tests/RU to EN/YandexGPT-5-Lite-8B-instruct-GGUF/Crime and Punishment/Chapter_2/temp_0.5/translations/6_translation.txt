 — he thought triumphantly, taking a deep and joyful breath with his whole chest — “just delirious weakness, a momentary hallucination” — and he tore out the entire lining from the left pocket of his pants. At that moment, a ray of sunlight illuminated his left boot: signs seemed to appear on the toe that peeked out from the boot. He threw off the boot: “there really are signs! The whole tip of the sock is soaked in blood”; he must have stepped into that puddle carelessly then… “But what am I supposed to do with this now? Where should I put this sock, fringe, pocket?”
He gathered all this in his hand and stood in the middle of the room. “In the stove? But they will start rummaging through the stove first. Burn it? And how can I burn it? There aren’t even any matches.”
