 But no, he hears too clearly!.. But, therefore, they will come to him now, if so, "because... surely, all this is the same... because of yesterday... Lord!" He wanted to lock himself in with a hook, but his hand did not rise... and it was useless! Fear, like ice, enveloped his soul, tormented him, chilled him... But finally, after about ten minutes, all this din began to gradually subside. The landlady moaned and groaned, Ilya Petrovich still threatened and swore... But finally, it seems, he too fell silent; now you can't hear him anymore; "did he really leave! Lord!" Yes, here the landlady is leaving, still moaning and crying... and her door slammed shut... Here the crowd disperses from the stairs to their apartments — they gasp, argue, call out to each other, now raising their speech to a shout, then lowering it to a whisper. There must have been many of them; almost the whole house had come running. "But God, is all this really possible! And why, why did he come here!"
Raskolnikov fell powerlessly on the sofa, but could no longer close his eyes; he lay there for half an hour in such suffering, in such unbearable feelings of boundless horror, which he had never experienced before. Suddenly, bright light озарил his room: Nastasya entered with a candle and a plate of soup. After looking at him carefully and seeing that he was awake, she put the candle on the table and began to lay out what she had brought: bread, salt, a plate, a spoon.
