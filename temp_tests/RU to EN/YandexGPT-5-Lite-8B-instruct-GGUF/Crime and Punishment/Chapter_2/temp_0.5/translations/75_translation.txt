Having guessed that he had come to, the landlady, peeking from the door, immediately closed it and hid. She was always shy and found it difficult to carry on conversations and explanations; she was about forty years old, and she was plump and fat, dark-browed and black-eyed, good-natured from her corpulence and sloth; and she was even very pretty in appearance. Shy beyond necessity. “You… who are you?” he continued to question, turning to the foreman himself. But at that moment the door opened wide again, and, bending down a little because he was tall, Razumikhin entered.
