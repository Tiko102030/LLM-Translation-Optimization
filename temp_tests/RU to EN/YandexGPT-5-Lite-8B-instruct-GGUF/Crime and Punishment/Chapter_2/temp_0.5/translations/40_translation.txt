 The call was so strong that he had already got up from his seat to carry it out. “Shouldn’t I at least think about it for a minute?” — flashed through his head. — “No, it’s better not to think and get it over with!” But suddenly he stopped as if struck in his tracks: Nikodim Fomich was talking heatedly to Ilya Petrovich, and words reached him:

— It can't be, both will be released! Firstly, everything contradicts; judge for yourself: why would they call the caretaker if it were their business? To inform on themselves, perhaps? Or for cunning? No, that would be too crafty! And finally, student Pestryakov was seen near the gate by both the janitors and a commoner at the very moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted with them at the very gate and asked about living quarters from the janitors, even in front of his friends. Well, would someone like that ask about accommodation if they were going there with such an intention?
