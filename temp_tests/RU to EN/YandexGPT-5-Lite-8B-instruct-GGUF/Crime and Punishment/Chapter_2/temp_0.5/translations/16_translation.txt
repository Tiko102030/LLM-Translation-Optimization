 His head was dizzy and aching from the heat. "This is a trick! They want to lure me in with a trick and then knock me off everything," he continued to himself as he went out onto the stairs. "The bad thing is that I'm almost delirious... I might blurt out some stupidity..."

On the stairs, he remembered that he was leaving all his things like that, in the wallpaper hole — "and perhaps they'll search here without him," he recalled and stopped. But such despair and such, if one can say, cynicism of doom suddenly seized him that he waved his hand and went on. "If only it would be quick!.."
