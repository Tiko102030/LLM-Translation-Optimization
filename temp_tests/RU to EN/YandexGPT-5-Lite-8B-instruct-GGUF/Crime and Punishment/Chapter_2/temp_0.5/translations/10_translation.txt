 His entire room was of such a size that one could remove the hook without getting out of bed. Sure enough: the janitor and Nastasya are standing there. Nastasya looked at him in some strange way. He glanced at the janitor with a defiant and desperate look. The latter silently handed him a gray piece of paper folded in half, sealed with bottle sealing wax. “Summons from the office,” he said, handing over the paper. “From what office?..” “They want you to go to the police station, to the office.” Everyone knows which office it is. “To the police!..”
