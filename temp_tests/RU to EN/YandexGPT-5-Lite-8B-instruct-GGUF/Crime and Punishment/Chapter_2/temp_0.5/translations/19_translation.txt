 No one noticed him. In the second room some scribes were sitting and writing, dressed a little better than him, they all looked like some strange people. He turned to one of them. "What do you want?" He showed the summons from the office. "Are you a student?" the man asked, glancing at the summons. "Yes, a former student." The scribe glanced at him, however, without any curiosity. It was some particularly disheveled person with a fixed idea in his gaze.
