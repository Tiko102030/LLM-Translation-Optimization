In two minutes, Nastasya returned with soup and announced that tea would be served soon. Two spoons, two plates, and the whole set appeared for the soup: saltcellar, pepper shaker, mustard for beef, and other things that had not been in such order for a long time before. The tablecloth was clean. “It wouldn’t be bad, Nastasiushka, if Praskovya Pavlovna would send two bottles of beer. We’ll drink it.” “Oh, you nosy one!” mumbled Nastasya and went to carry out the order.
