 The clerk looked at them with a smile. The hot lieutenant was apparently puzzled. "This is none of your business!" he shouted finally, somewhat unnaturally loudly, "but please submit the review they demand from you. Show him, Alexander Grigorievich. Complaints about you! You don't pay any money! Look at this bright falcon flying out!" But Raskolnikov was no longer listening and greedily grabbed the paper, searching for a quick answer. Read it once, read it again, and didn't understand.
