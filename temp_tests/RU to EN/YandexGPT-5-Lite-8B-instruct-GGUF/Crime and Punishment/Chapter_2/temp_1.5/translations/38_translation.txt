 And where did these feelings come from? On the contrary, now, if suddenly the room were filled not with orderlies, but with his foremost friends, then even then, it seems, he would not have a single human word for them, their hearts had been so suddenly empty. A gloomy feeling of painful, endless loneliness and alienation suddenly consciously revealed itself to his soul. Not his meanness of heartfelt outpourings in front of Ilya Petrovich, not the meanness and Poroukov's triumph over him suddenly turned his heart like that. Oh, what does he care now about his own meanness, all these ambitions, lieutenants, Germans, reprimands, offices, etc., etc.! If they had even sentenced him to be burned at this very moment, he would not have moved, probably he would hardly listen to the sentence attentively. Something completely unfamiliar, new, sudden and never experienced before was happening to him. Not that he understood, but he clearly felt, with all the power of sensation, that not only with sensitive expansivenesses, as recently, but even with anything else it was no longer possible for him to turn to these people, in the quarter office, and if these were all his own brothers and sisters, and not quarter lieutenants, then even then he would have absolutely no reason to turn to them and even in any case of life; he had never experienced such a strange and terrible sensation before. And what is most excruciating — it was more of a feeling than a consciousness, than a concept; an immediate feeling, the most painful feeling out of all the feelings he has experienced in his life so far. The clerk began to dictate to him the form of an ordinary response in such cases, that is, I cannot pay, I promise then-то (sometime), I will not leave the city, I will not sell or give away my property, etc.
