Guessing that he had come to his senses, the landlady, who was peeping through the door, immediately shut it and hid. She had always been shy and found it hard to tolerate conversations and explanations; she was about forty years old, and she was stout and plump, with dark eyebrows and dark eyes, kind from her corpulence and sloth; and she was very good-looking in appearance. Shy beyond necessity. “You... who are you?” he continued to interrogate, turning to the foreman himself. But at that moment the door opened wide again, and Razumikhin entered, bending down a little because he was tall.
