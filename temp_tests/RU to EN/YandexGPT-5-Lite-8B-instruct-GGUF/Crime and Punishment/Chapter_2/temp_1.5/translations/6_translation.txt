At that moment, a ray of sun illuminated his left boot: signs seemed to appear on the toe that peeked out from the boot. He kicked off his shoe: “there really are signs! The whole tip of the sock is soaked in blood;” he must have stepped into that puddle carelessly then... “But what am I supposed to do with this now? Where to put this sock, fringe, pocket?”
He gathered all this in his hand and stood in the middle of the room. “In the stove? But they’ll start rummaging through the stove first. Burn it? And how to burn it? There are not even any matches.”
