Raskolnikov silently took the German sheets of the article, accepted three rubles, and without saying a word, left. Razumikhin looked after him in surprise. But having reached the first line, Raskolnikov suddenly returned, went up to Razumikhin again, and without a word put both the German sheets and the three rubles back on the table, then walked out. "Do you have delirium tremens or what!" shouted the infuriated Razumikhin. "Why are you playing these tricks? You even confused me... Why did you come in after that, damn it?"
