 His entire room was of such a size that he could remove the hook without getting out of bed. Sure enough: there were the caretaker and Nastasya standing there. Nastasya gave him an odd look. He glanced at the caretaker with a defiant and desperate air. The latter silently handed him a gray, double-folded paper sealed with bottle wax. “A summons from the office,” he said, handing over the paper. “From what office?..” “They want you in the police station, in the office. You know which office it is.” “To the police!..”
