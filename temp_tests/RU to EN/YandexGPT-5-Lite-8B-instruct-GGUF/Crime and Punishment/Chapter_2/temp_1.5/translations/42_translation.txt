 "The house is like Noah's Ark," remarked the clerk, listening from his place. "It's clear, it's clear!" Nikodim Fomich repeated fervently. "No, the matter is very unclear," confirmed Ilya Petrovich. Raskolnikov picked up his hat and went to the door, but he didn't get there...

When he came to his senses, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that someone was supporting him on the right, that another person was standing on the left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nikodim Fomich was standing in front of him and staring at him intently; he got up from the chair. "What's wrong, are you sick?" asked Nikodim Fomich rather sharply. "They could barely write even when they signed their names," remarked the clerk, sitting down in his place and taking up papers again. "And how long have you been ill?" Ilya Petrovich shouted from his place, shuffling through the papers as well.
