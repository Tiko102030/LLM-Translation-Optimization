In two minutes, Nastasya returned with the soup and announced that tea would be coming soon. Two spoons, two plates, and the whole set appeared for the soup: salt shaker, pepper shaker, mustard for beef, and other things that hadn't been seen in such order for a long time before. The tablecloth was clean. "It wouldn't be bad, Nastasiushka, if Praskovya Pavlovna would send two bottles of beer. We will drink it," — "Well, you nosy one!" mumbled Nastasya and went to execute the order.
