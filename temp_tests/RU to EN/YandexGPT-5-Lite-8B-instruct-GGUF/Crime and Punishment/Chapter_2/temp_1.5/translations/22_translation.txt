 — Louise Ivanovna, you should sit down, — he said to the scarletly dressed and rather purple lady who was standing as if she didn't dare to sit down herself, although there was a chair рядом. "Ich danke," [17 - Thank you (нем.)] — she replied and quietly, with a silky noise, sank into the chair. Her light-blue dress with white lace trim spread out around the chair like a balloon, taking up almost half the room. There was a smell of perfume. But the lady was obviously робкая about taking up so much space and having such a strong perfume, although she smiled timidly and boldly at the same time, but with явным беспокойство. The траурная lady finally finished and began to stand up. Suddenly, making some noise, quite smartly and turning his shoulders in a special way with each step, an officer entered, threw his hat with a cockade on the table and sat in the armchair. The lush lady jumped up from her seat as soon as she saw him, and started curtseying with some kind of special enthusiasm; but the officer did not pay her the slightest attention, and she no longer dared to sit down in front of him.
