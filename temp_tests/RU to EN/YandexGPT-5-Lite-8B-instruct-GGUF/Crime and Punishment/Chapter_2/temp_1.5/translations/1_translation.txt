 “It's already the third hour!” He sat down on the couch — and suddenly he remembered everything! All of it came back to him in an instant! For a split second, he thought he would go crazy. A terrible cold seized him; but the chill was also from the fever that had long begun with him in his sleep. Now all of a sudden such a chills struck him that his teeth almost jumped out and everything inside him started shaking. He opened the door and began to listen: everything in the house was completely asleep. He gazed at himself and everything around in the room with astonishment and could not understand: how could he yesterday, having entered, not lock the doors with a hook and rush onto the couch, not only without undressing, but even in his hat: it rolled down and lay right there on the floor, near the pillow. “If anyone had come in, what would they have thought? That I’m drunk, but…” He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began to look at himself, all over, from head to toe, all his clothes: were there any traces?”
