 A small depression formed under the stone; immediately he began to throw everything from his pocket into it. The wallet ended up at the very top, and there was still space in the depression. Then he grabbed the stone again, перевернул it with one turn to its original side, and it fell back into its original place, perhaps a little higher than before. But he raked up some earth and pressed it down on the edges with his foot. Nothing could be noticed. Then he went out and headed towards the square. Again strong, barely tolerable joy, as before in the office, took hold of him for an instant. “The ends are hidden! And who, who might think to look under this stone? It has been there perhaps since the construction of the house and will remain so for just as long.”
