Meanwhile, Razumikhin moved over to him on the sofa, awkwardly like a bear, embraced his head with his left hand, even though he could have raised himself up, and with his right hand brought a spoonful of soup to his mouth, blowing on it several times first so that he wouldn't burn himself. But the soup was just warm. Raskolnikov greedily swallowed one spoonful, then another, a third. But after serving a few more spoons, Razumikhin suddenly paused and announced that further action should be consulted with Zosimov. Nastasya entered, carrying two bottles of beer. — "Do you want some tea?" — "I do." — "Go and get some tea quickly, Nastasya, because I think one can have tea without consulting the faculty."
