It’s better to throw it all away!” he repeated, sitting down on the sofa again — “and right now, this very minute, without delay!..” But instead his head sank onto the pillow once more; another unbearable chill struck him; he pulled the overcoat over himself again. And for a long time, several hours, it still seemed to him in fits and starts that “right now, without delaying, go somewhere and throw everything away, get it out of sight, quickly, quickly!” He made a sudden effort to get up from the sofa several times, he tried to stand up, but could not do it. A strong knock on the door finally woke him up. — “Yes, open the door, is he alive or what? And all he does is sleep! ”Nastassia shouted, pounding on the door with her fist, “he’s been sleeping all day like a dog!”
