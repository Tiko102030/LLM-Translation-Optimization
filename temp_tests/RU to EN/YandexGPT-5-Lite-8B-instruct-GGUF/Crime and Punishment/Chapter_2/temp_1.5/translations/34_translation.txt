 Louise Ivanovna with hasty любезностью began to curtsy in all directions and, curtsying, backed her way to the door; but at the door she bumped into an important officer with a fresh open face and excellent thick blond sideburns from behind. It was Nikodim Fomich himself, the district supervisor. Louise Ivanovna hurried to curtsy almost to the floor and flew out of the office in small steps, jumping. "Again thunder, again lightning and thunderstorm, tornado, hurricane!" - said Nikodim Fomich to Ilya Petrovich amiably and friendly, - they have stirred up my heart again, it has boiled over! I heard from the stairs. "Oh well!" — said Ilya Petrovich with noble negligence (and not even "shto", but somehow: "Da-a shta-a!"), moving to another table with some papers and theatrically shrugging his shoulders with each step, wherever he steps, there goes the shoulder; — here you see: Mr. writer, that is, a former student, does not pay money, has issued promissory notes, does not clear out the apartment, complaints against them come in continuously, and you took offense at me for smoking a cigarette in their presence! They themselves commit fraud, but here you can look at them: this is their most attractive appearance now! "Poverty is not a vice, buddy, well, what of it!"
