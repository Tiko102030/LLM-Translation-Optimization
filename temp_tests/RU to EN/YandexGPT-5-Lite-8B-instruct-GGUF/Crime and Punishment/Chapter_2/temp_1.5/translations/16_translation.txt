 His head was dizzy and hurting from the heat. "This is a trick! They want to lure me in with guile and suddenly knock me down on everything," he continued to himself as he went out onto the stairs. "The bad thing is that I'm almost delirious... I might tell some stupid lie..."

On the stairs, he remembered that he was leaving all his things like that, in the wallpaper hole — "and perhaps there's a search here without him on purpose," he recalled and stopped. But such despair and such, if I may say, cynicism of destruction suddenly overwhelmed him that he waved his hand and went on. "If only it would be over soon!.."
