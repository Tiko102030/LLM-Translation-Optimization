 Further, in the depth of the yard, the corner of a low, закопчённого, stone shed peeped out from behind the fence, evidently part of some workshop. There must have been some kind of establishment here, carriage or locksmith's, or something like that; everywhere, almost from the very gate, there was a lot of coal dust darkened. "That's where I would drop everything and leave!" — it suddenly occurred to him. Not noticing anyone in the yard, he stepped through the gate and immediately saw, right near the gate, a trough attached to the fence (as is often done in such houses where there are many factory workers, artel workers, carmen and so on), and above the trough, right there on the fence, was written with chalk the usual joke in such cases: "Stopping of the cart is prohibited here". Thus, it was already good that there was no suspicion that he had come in and stopped. "Here I could drop everything all at once in a heap and leave!"

Looking around once more, he had already stuck his hand into his pocket when suddenly he noticed a large rough stone near the outer wall, between the gate and the trough, where the entire distance was about an arshin wide (approximately a yard), probably weighing about a pound and a half, adjacent directly to the stone street wall. Behind this wall was the street, the pavement, you could hear passers-by going back and forth, of which there were always many; but no one could see him beyond the gate, unless someone came from the street, which, however, could very well happen, so he had to hurry. He bent down to the stone, gripped it tightly at the top with both hands, gathered all his strength and turned the stone over. Under the stone there was a small углубление; immediately he began to throw everything out of his pocket into it.
