 And he opened the window onto the канава and stood at the window, squealing like a little pig; and that's shameful. And how can one stand in the window on the street, squealing like a little pig; and that's shameful. Fie-fie-fie! And Karl was pulling him by his coat from the window from behind, and here, this is true, Mr. Captain, his suit got torn. And then he screamed that he had to pay a fifteen-target fine. And I myself, Mr. Captain, paid him five targets for his suit. And it's an ungrateful guest, Mr. Captain, who makes all the scandals! I will gather great satire about you, Mr. Captain, because I can write about you in all the newspapers. — Are you a writer, then? — Yes, Mr. Captain, and what kind of an ungrateful guest is that, Mr. Captain, when he came to a noble house…
