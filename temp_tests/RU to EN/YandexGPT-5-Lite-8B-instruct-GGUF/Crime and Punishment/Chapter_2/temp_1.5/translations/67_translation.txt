 It seemed to him that at that moment he had cut himself off from everyone and everything as if with scissors. He came to himself only in the evening, therefore, it had been about six hours. He did not remember where and how he was walking back. Having undressed and shivering like a horse that has been driven too hard, he lay down on the sofa, pulled his overcoat over himself and immediately fell asleep…

He came to full twilight from the terrible screaming. God, what a scream! He had never heard or seen such unnatural sounds, such howling, yelling, grinding, crying, beating and swearing. He could not even imagine such atrocity, such frenzy. In horror, he sat up in bed, dying and tormenting himself every instant. But the fights, screams and curses became stronger and stronger. And then, to his greatest astonishment, he suddenly heard the voice of his landlady.
