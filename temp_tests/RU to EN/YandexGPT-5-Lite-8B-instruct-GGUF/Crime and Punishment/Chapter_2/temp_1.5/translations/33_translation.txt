 There they are, the writers! — and he gave Raskolnikov a contemptuous look. — The other day in a tavern there was also a story: he had dinner, but doesn't want to pay; “I will describe you in satire for that, I say.” On the ship too, another one, last week, the respectable family of a state councilor, wife and daughter, were called by the vilest words. Recently from the confectionery they kicked someone out with pushing. That's what they are like, writers, litterateurs, students, heralds… ugh! And you went there! I myself will drop in on you... then look out! Did you hear? Louise Ivanovna began to curtsy on all sides with hasty politeness and, curtsying, backed away to the door; but at the door she ran into the back of a prominent officer with an open, fresh face and excellent thick blond sideburns.
