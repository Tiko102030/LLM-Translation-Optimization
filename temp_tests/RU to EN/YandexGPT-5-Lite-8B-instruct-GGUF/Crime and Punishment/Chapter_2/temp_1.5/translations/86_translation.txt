 — Make haste and bring some tea, Nastasya, because concerning tea, it seems, one can do without the faculty. But here's the beer too! — he moved to his chair, pushed his soup and beef towards him and began to eat with such appetite as if he hadn't eaten for three days. — I, brother Rodya, dine like this every day here now, — he muttered as much as his mouth full of beef allowed, — and all this is due to Pashenka, your хозяйюшка, she is managing, treats me with all her heart. I, разумеется, do not insist, well, I don't object either. And here comes Nastasya with the tea. What a nimble one! Nastenka, want some beer? — Oh, go to the mischievous one! — And some tea?
