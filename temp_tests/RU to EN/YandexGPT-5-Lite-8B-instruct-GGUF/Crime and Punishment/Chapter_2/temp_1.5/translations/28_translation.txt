 — But isn't she my landlady? — Well, so what if she is the landlady? The clerk looked at him with a condescending smile of сожаления, and at the same time with some triumph, as at a novice who is just beginning to be shelled: "What, how are you feeling now?" But what, what did he care about a loan letter, about collection now! Was it worth any anxiety now, in turn, even some attention! He stood, read, listened, replied, asked questions himself, but all this mechanically. The triumph of self-preservation, salvation from pressing danger — that's what filled his whole being at that moment, without foresight, without analysis, without future guesswork and solving, without doubts and without questions. It was a minute of complete, непосредственной, purely animal joy. But at that very moment something like thunder and lightning occurred in the office. The lieutenant, still shaken by the disrespectfulness, all flushed and obviously wanting to support his wounded ambition, lashed out with all his might at the unfortunate "grand dame", who had been looking at him since he entered with a silly smile. — And you, such-and-such and such-like, — he shouted suddenly at the top of his lungs (the mourning lady has already left), — what happened to you last night?
