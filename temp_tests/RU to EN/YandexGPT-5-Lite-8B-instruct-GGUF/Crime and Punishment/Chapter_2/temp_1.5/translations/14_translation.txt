 "Nastasia, therefore, could not notice anything from a distance, thank God!" Then he opened the summons with trepidation and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood. It was an ordinary summons from the quarter to appear today, at half past nine, at the office of the district constable. "But when has this ever happened? I don't have any business with the police on my own! And why именно today?" — he thought in painful bewilderment. "Lord, just hurry up!" He had almost fallen to his knees to pray, but even laughed at himself — not at the prayer, but at himself. He hastily began to get dressed. "I will go down that way, I don't care! Put on a sock!"
