 The clerk looked at them with a smile. The hot lieutenant was obviously puzzled. "This is none of your business!" he shouted finally, somewhat unnaturally loudly, "but please submit the отзывом they demand from you. Show him, Alexander Grigorievich. Complaints against you! You don't pay any money! Look how clear-eyed the falcon flew out!" But Raskolnikov no longer listened and grasped the paper greedily, looking for a quick solution. Read it once, twice, and didn't understand.
