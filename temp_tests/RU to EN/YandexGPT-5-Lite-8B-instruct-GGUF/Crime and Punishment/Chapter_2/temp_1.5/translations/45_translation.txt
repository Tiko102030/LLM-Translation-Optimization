 “That was strange.” — Well, all right then,” concluded Ilya Petrovich, “we are not keeping you any longer.” Raskolnikov left. He could still hear an animated conversation begin after he exited, in which the questioning voice of Nikodim Fomitch was the most audible... He came to his senses completely outside. “A search, a search, it’s a search right now! — he repeated to himself, hurrying to get there; — robbers! they suspect!” His previous fear gripped him again, from head to toe. II

“But what if there was already a search? What if I find them just as they are in their place?”

But here is his room. Nothing and nobody; no one has looked in.
