 There was a terrible stuffiness because of that. Street cleaners went up and down with books tucked under their arms, as well as errand boys and various people of both genders — visitors. The door to the office itself was also wide open. He entered and stood in the hallway. Some men were standing and waiting there too. There was also extreme stuffiness here, and besides, the fresh, still uncured paint on the rancid drying oil in the newly painted rooms made one sick to their stomach with its overpowering smell. After waiting for a while, he decided to move even further forward, into the next room. All the rooms were tiny and low-ceilinged. Dreadful impatience pulled him further and further ahead. No one noticed him.
