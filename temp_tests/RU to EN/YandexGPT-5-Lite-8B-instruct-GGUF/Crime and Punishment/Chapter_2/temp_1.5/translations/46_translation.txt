 Nothing and nobody; no one looked in. Not even Nastasya touched anything. But goodness! How could he have left all these things in that hole just the other day? He rushed to the corner, stuck his hand under the wallpaper and began to pull out things and load them into his pockets. There were eight of them: two small boxes with earrings or something like that - he didn't take a good look; then four small satin cases. One chain was simply wrapped in newspaper. Something else in newspaper paper, it seems like an order...
He put everything in different pockets, in the coat and in the remaining right pocket of his trousers, trying to make it less noticeable. He also took the wallet along with the things. Then he left the room, this time even leaving it wide open.
