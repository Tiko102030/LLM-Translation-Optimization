But, it follows that they will come to him now, if so, “because… right, all this is the same… because of yesterday… Lord!” He wanted to lock himself on a hook, but his hand didn't rise... and it’s useless! Fear, like ice, encircled his soul, tortured him, stiffened him... But finally, after ten minutes or so, all this hubbub began to gradually subside. The landlady moaned and groaned, Ilya Petrovich still threatened and swore… But finally, it seems, he too fell silent; now you can’t hear him either; “did he really leave! Lord!” Yes, here the landlady is also leaving, still with groans and crying… and her door slammed shut... And the crowd disperses from the stairs to their apartments — they gasp, argue, call out to each other, now raising their speech to a scream, then lowering it to a whisper. There must have been many of them; almost everyone in the house came running. “But God, is all this really possible! And why, why did he come here!”
Raskolnikov collapsed powerlessly on the sofa, but could no longer close his eyes; he lay for half an hour in such suffering, in such unbearable feelings of boundless horror, which he had never experienced before. Suddenly, bright light illuminated his room: Nastasya entered with a candle and a plate of soup. Having looked at him carefully and seeing that he was not asleep, she put the candle on the table and began to lay out what she brought: bread, salt, a plate, a spoon.
