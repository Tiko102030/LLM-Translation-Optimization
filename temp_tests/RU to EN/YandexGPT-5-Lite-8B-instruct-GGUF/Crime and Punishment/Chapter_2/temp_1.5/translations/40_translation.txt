 The urge was so strong that he had already got up to fulfill it. “Shouldn't I think about it for at least a minute?” ran through his head. “No, it’s better not to think and get it over with!” But suddenly he stopped as if thunderstruck: Nikodim Fomich was talking heatedly to Ilya Petrovich, and words reached him:
— It can't be, they will both be released! First of all, everything contradicts; judge for yourself: why would they call the caretaker if it were their business? To inform on themselves, what for? Or is it a trick? No, that would be too clever! And finally, the student Pestryakov was seen near the gates by both janitors and a philistine woman at the very moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted with them at the gate itself and asked about living quarters from the janitors, even in front of his friends. Well, would someone like that ask about living quarters if they had come with such an intention?
