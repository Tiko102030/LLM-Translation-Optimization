 “— Don't need it,” he said, “I came… here’s what: I have no lessons… I was about to… however, I don’t need lessons at all…”
“— But you know what? You’re delirious!” — remarked Razumikhin, who was watching him closely. 
“No, not бредящий...” Raskolnikov got up from the sofa. Rising towards Razumikhin, he had not thought that he should, therefore, come face to face with him. Now, in an instant, he realized, already by experience, that at this moment he was least disposed to come face to face with anyone in the whole world. All his bile rose in him. He nearly drowned in anger towards himself as soon as he crossed Razumikhin's threshold. 
“— Goodbye!” — he said suddenly and went to the door.”
