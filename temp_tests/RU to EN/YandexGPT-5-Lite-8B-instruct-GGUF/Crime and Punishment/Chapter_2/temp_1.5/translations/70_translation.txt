 Having looked at him carefully and seen that he was not sleeping, she put the candle on the table and began to arrange what she had brought: bread, salt, a plate, a spoon. “Probably didn’t eat since yesterday. He’s been wandering around all day, and has fever.” “Nastasya… why did they beat the landlady?” She looked at him intently. “Who beat the landlady?” “Right now… half an hour ago, Ilya Petrovich, the overseer's assistant, on the stairs… Why did he beat her so much? And… why did he come?” Nastasya examined him silently and frowning for a long time. He became very unpleasantly affected by this examination, even afraid.
