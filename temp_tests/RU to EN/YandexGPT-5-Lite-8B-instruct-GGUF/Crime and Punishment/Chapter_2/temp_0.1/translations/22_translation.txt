 — Louise Ivanovna, you should sit down, — he said to the smartly dressed purplish-red lady, who was still standing as if not daring to sit down herself, although there was a chair nearby. “Ich danke,” she said and quietly sank onto the chair with a silky noise. Her light blue dress with white lace trim spread out around the chair like a balloon, taking up almost half the room. A scent of perfume filled the air. But the lady was obviously робкая that she took up half the room and that her perfume was so strong, although she smiled timidly and insolently at the same time, but with явным беспокойство. The траурная lady finally finished and started to stand up. Suddenly, with some noise, quite smartly and turning his shoulders in a special way with each step, an officer entered, threw his cap with a cockade on the table and sat down in an armchair. The fluffy lady jumped up from her seat as soon as she saw him, and began to curtsy with some special enthusiasm; but the officer did not pay her the slightest attention, and she no longer dared to sit down in front of him.
