 There they are, the writers! — and he cast a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. — The other day in the tavern there was also a story: had lunch, but doesn't want to pay; "I, say, will describe you in satire for that." On the steamboat too, another one, last week, the respectable family of a statsky советник, the wife and daughter, were called the most vile names. The other day they kicked someone out of the pastry shop by pushing him. That's what they are like, writers, litterateurs, students, heralds... ugh! And you went there! I myself will come to see you... then look out! Heard that?
