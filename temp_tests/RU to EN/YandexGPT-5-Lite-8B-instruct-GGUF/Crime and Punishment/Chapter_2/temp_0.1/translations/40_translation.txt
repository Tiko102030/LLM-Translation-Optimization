 The urge was so strong that he had already gotten up from his seat to carry it out. “Shouldn’t I at least think about it for a minute?” ran through his head. “No, it’s better not to think and get it over with!” But suddenly he stopped as if struck in his tracks: Nikodim Fomich was speaking heatedly to Ilya Petrovich, and words reached him:

— It can't be, both will be released! Firstly, everything contradicts; judge for yourself: why would they call the caretaker if it were their business? To inform on themselves, perhaps? Or is it a trick? No, that would be too clever! And finally, student Pestyakov was seen at the very gate by both caretakers and a commoner woman at the very moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted with them at the very gate and asked about living quarters from the caretakers, even in front of his friends. Well, would someone like that ask about living quarters if they were going there with such an intention?
