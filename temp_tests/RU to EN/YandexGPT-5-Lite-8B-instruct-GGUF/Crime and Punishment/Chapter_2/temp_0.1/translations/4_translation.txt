 “— Is this really how one hides things? My mind is truly failing me!” In exhaustion, he sat down on the sofa, and immediately an unbearable chill shook him again. Mechanically, he pulled over the former student's winter coat that was lying nearby on a chair, warm but already almost in tatters, covered himself with it, and sleep and delirium again seized him at once. He lost consciousness. In no more than five minutes, he jumped up again and immediately, in frenzy, rushed to his clothes again. “How could I fall asleep again when nothing has been done! That's right, that's right: I still haven't taken off the петлю under my arm! I forgot, I really forgot about such a thing! Such evidence!” He pulled down the петля and quickly began to tear it into pieces, stuffing them under the pillow in the linen. “Pieces of torn canvas will not arouse suspicion in any case; it seems so, it seems so!” — he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with attention strained to the point of pain, began to look around again, on the floor and everywhere, to see if he had forgotten anything else?
