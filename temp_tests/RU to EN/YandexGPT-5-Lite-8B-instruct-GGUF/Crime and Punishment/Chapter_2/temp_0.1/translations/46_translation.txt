 Nothing and nobody; no one looked in. Not even Nastasya touched anything. But, goodness me! How could he have left all these things in that hole the other day? He rushed to a corner, reached under the wallpaper, and began pulling out items and stuffing them into his pockets. There were eight of them: two small boxes with earrings or something like that — he didn't take a good look; then four small morocco cases. One chain was simply wrapped in newspaper. Something else in newspaper paper, an order, it seems...
He put everything in different pockets, in the coat and in the remaining right pocket of his trousers, trying to make it less noticeable. He also took the wallet along with the items. Then he left the room, this time even leaving it wide open.
