 The stout lady jumped up from her seat at the sight of him and began to curtsy with some special delight; but the officer did not pay any attention to her, and she no longer dared to sit down in front of him. It was a lieutenant, an assistant district supervisor, with reddish mustaches sticking out horizontally in both directions and with extremely fine facial features that expressed nothing special, except for some impudence. He looked askance at Raskolnikov, somewhat indignantly: his costume was too bad, and despite all the humiliation, his posture still did not match his clothes; Raskolnikov, through неосторожности, looked at him too straight and long, so much so that he was even offended. 
“What do you want?” he shouted, probably surprised that such a ragamuffin does not think to shy away from his lightning glance. 
“They demanded... on the summons...” Raskolnikov answered somehow.
