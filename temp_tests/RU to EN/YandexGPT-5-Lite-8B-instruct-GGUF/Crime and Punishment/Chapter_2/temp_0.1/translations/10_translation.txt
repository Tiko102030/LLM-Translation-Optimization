 His entire room was of such a size that one could remove the hook without getting out of bed. Sure enough: there are the janitor and Nastasya. Nastasya looked at him in some strange way. He glanced at the janitor with a defiant and desperate look. The latter silently handed him a gray, double-folded piece of paper sealed with bottle wax. “Summons from the office,” he said, handing over the paper. “From what office?..” “They’re calling you to the police station, to the office. You know which office it is.” “To the police!..”
