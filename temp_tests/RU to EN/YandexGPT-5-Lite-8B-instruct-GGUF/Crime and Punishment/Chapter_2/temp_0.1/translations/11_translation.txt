They demand it, and you go. — He looked at him attentively, looked around, and turned to leave. — Haven't you fallen ill completely? — Nastassia remarked, keeping her eyes on him. The janitor also turned his head for a minute. — Feverish since yesterday, — she added. He did not answer and held the paper in his hands without opening it.
