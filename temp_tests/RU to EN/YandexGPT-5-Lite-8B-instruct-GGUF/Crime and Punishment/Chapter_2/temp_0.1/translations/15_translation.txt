But as soon as he put it on, he immediately pulled it off with disgust and horror. He pulled it off, but realizing that there was no other one, he took it and put it on again — and laughed again. "It's all conditional, it's all relative, it's all just forms," he thought fleetingly, with only the edge of his mind, while trembling all over, "after all, I did put it on! After all, I ended up by putting it on!" Laughter, however, was immediately replaced by despair. "No, this is beyond my strength..." — he thought to himself. His legs were shaking. "From fear," he muttered to himself. His head was dizzy and aching from the heat.
