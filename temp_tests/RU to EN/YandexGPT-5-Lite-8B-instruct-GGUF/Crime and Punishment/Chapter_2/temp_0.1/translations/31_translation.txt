 And he opened the window onto the canal and stuck his head out like a little pig, squealing; and that's shameful. And how can you stick your head out of the window onto the street, like a little pig, squealing; and that's shameful. Fie-fie-fie! And Karl was pulling him by the tailcoat from the window, and here, it's true, Mr. Captain, his coat got torn. And then he shouted that he had to pay a fine of fifteen целковых. And I myself, Mr. Captain, paid him five целковых for the coat. And this is an ungracious guest, Mr. Captain, and he makes all sorts of scandals! I, said, there will be a big satire about you, because I can write anything about you in all the newspapers. — From writers, then? — Yes, Mr. Captain, and what kind of ungracious guest is this, Mr. Captain, when it's a noble house...
