 It was strange. — Well, all right then, — Ilya Petrovich concluded, — we're not keeping you any longer. Raskolnikov left. He could still hear how an animated conversation began after he left, in which Nikodim Fomich’s questioning voice was the most audible... He came to his senses completely outside. “Search, search, there's a search right now! — he repeated to himself, hurrying to get there; — robbers! they suspect!” The same fear seized him again, from head to toe. II

“But what if there already was a search? What if I find them in their place right now?”

But here is his room. Nothing and no one; nobody looked in.
