 It seemed to him that at that moment he had cut himself off from everyone and everything as if with scissors. He came back to his place only in the evening, which meant he had been walking for about six hours. He did not remember anything about where and how he walked back. After undressing and shaking like a загнанная horse, he lay down on the sofa, pulled his overcoat over himself and immediately fell asleep...

He woke up in complete twilight from an awful scream. God, what a scream that was! He had never heard or seen such unnatural sounds, such howling, wailing, grinding, crying, beatings and cursing. He could not even imagine such cruelty, such frenzy. In horror, he raised himself up and sat on his bed, freezing and tormenting himself every moment. But the fights, screams and curses became stronger and stronger. And then, to his greatest изумление, he suddenly heard the voice of his landlady.
