But when he set foot on K-y Boulevard, where he had met that girl the other day, his laughter suddenly stopped. Other thoughts came into his head. It also seemed to him all of a sudden that it was terribly disgusting for him now to pass by that bench, on which he had sat and brooded after the girl left, and it would be terribly hard too to meet that mustachioed man again, to whom he gave two rouble coins then: "Damn him!"

He walked, looking around рассеянно and злобно. All his thoughts now revolved around one main point — and he himself felt that this really was the main point, and that now, precisely now, he was left one on one with this main point — and that this was even for the first time after these two months. "Damn it all!"
