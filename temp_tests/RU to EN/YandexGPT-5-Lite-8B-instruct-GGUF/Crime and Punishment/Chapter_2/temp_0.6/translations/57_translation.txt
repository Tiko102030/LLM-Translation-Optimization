“Ah, you know what? You’re delirious!” observed the attentive Razumikhin. 
“No, I'm not delusional…” Raskolnikov got up from the sofa. Rising towards Razumikhin, he had not thought that he would have to face him, face to face. Now, in an instant, he guessed, already through experience, that at this moment he was least disposed to come face to face with anyone in the whole world. All his bile rose up in him. He nearly choked with anger at himself as soon as he crossed Razumikhin's threshold. 
“Goodbye!” he said suddenly and went to the door.
