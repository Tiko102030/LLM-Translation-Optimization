 "The house is like Noah's ark," remarked the clerk, listening from his place. "It's clear, it's clear!" Raskolnikov repeated hotly. "No, it's very unclear," affirmed Ilya Petrovich. Raskolnikov picked up his hat and went to the door, but he didn't reach the door...

When he came to, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that someone supported him from the right, that another person stood to his left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nikodim Fomich was standing in front of him and staring at him; he got up from the chair. "What's this, are you sick?" asked Nikodim Fomich rather sharply. "They could hardly write even when they signed their names," remarked the clerk, sitting down in his place and picking up papers again. "And how long have you been ill?" shouted Ilya Petrovich from his place, also going through the papers.
