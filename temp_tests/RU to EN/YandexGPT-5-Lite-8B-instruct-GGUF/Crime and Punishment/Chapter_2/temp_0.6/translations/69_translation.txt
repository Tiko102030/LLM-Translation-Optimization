He wanted to lock himself in with a hook, but his hand did not rise up... and it's useless! Fear, like ice, surrounded his soul, tortured him, numbed him... But finally, after ten minutes or so, all this racket began to subside gradually. The landlady moaned and groaned, Ilya Petrovich still threatened and swore... But finally, it seems, he too fell silent; now you can't hear him anymore; "could he have left! Lord!" Yes, here the landlady is leaving, still moaning and crying... and her door slammed shut... Here the crowd disperses from the stairs to their apartments — they gasp, argue, call out to each other, now raising their speech to a shout, then lowering it to a whisper. There must have been many of them; almost the whole house had gathered. "But God, is all this really possible! And why, why did he come here!"
Raskolnikov fell powerlessly on the sofa, but could no longer close his eyes; he lay there for half an hour in such suffering, in such unbearable feeling of boundless horror, which he had never experienced before. Suddenly, bright light озарил his room: Nastasya entered with a candle and a plate of soup. Looking at him attentively and seeing that he was not sleeping, she put the candle on the table and began to lay out what she brought: bread, salt, a plate, a spoon.
