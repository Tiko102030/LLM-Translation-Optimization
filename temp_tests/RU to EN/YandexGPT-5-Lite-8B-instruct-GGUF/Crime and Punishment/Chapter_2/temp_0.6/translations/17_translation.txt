 “If asked, perhaps I will say,” he thought, approaching the office. The office was a quarter of a verst from him. It had just moved to a new address, into a new building, on the fourth floor. He had been to the previous apartment once long ago and only briefly. Entering through the gate, he saw a staircase to his right, down which a peasant was going with a book in hand: “A дворник [ дворник – caretaker], so this must be the office,” and he began to climb up at random. He didn't want to ask anyone anything. “I will go in, kneel down and tell them everything…” he thought as he entered the fourth floor. The staircase was narrow, steep, and covered in slops. All the kitchens of all the apartments on all four floors opened onto this staircase and stood there almost all day long. That's why it was terribly stuffy.
