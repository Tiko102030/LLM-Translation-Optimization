 — Make haste with the tea, Nastasya, because regarding tea, it seems one can do it without the faculty. But here's some beer too! — he moved to his chair, pushed his soup and beef towards him and began to eat with such an appetite as if he hadn't eaten for three days. — I, brother Rodya, have lunch like this every day now, — he muttered as much as his mouth full of beef would allow, — and it's all Pashenka, your landlady, who is managing things, honoring me with all her heart. Of course, I don't insist, well, I'm not protesting either. And here comes Nastasya with the tea. She's so quick! Nastenka, do you want some beer? — Oh, go to the rascal! — And some tea?
