 Nothing and nobody; no one looked in. Not even Nastasya touched anything. But, goodness me! How could he have left all these things in that hole just the other day? He rushed to a corner, reached his hand under the wallpaper and began to pull out items and load them into his pockets. There were eight of them: two small boxes with earrings or something like that — he didn't look carefully; then four small morocco cases. One chain was simply wrapped in newspaper. Something else in newspaper paper, an order, it seems...
He put everything in different pockets, in the coat and in the remaining right pocket of his pants, trying to make it less noticeable. He took the wallet too, along with the things. Then he left the room, this time even leaving it wide open.
