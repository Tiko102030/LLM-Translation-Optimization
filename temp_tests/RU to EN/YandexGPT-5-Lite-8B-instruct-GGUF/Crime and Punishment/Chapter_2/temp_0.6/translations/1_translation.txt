 “It’s the third hour already!” He sat on the sofa — and then he remembered everything! Suddenly, in an instant he remembered everything! For a moment he thought he would go crazy. A terrible chill gripped him; but it was also cold from the fever that had long begun with him in his sleep. Now suddenly such a chills struck that his teeth almost jumped out and everything inside him shook. He opened the door and began to listen: everything in the house was completely asleep. He looked at himself and everything around him in the room with astonishment and did not understand: how could he yesterday, when entering, not lock the doors on the hook and throw himself onto the sofa, not only without undressing, but even in a hat: it rolled down and lay there on the floor, near the pillow. “If someone had come in, what would he have thought? That I’m drunk, but…” He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began to look at himself, all over, from head to toe, his whole clothes: are there any traces?”
