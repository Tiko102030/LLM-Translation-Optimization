 — And you, you such and such and such,— he shouted all at once with might and main (the mourning lady had already left),— what happened to you last night? eh? Again shame, a brawl making the whole street. Fighting and drinking again. You dream of being in a straitjacket! After all, I've told you, I warned you ten times that I wouldn't put up with it the eleventh time! And you again, again, you such and such! Even Raskolnikov's paper fell out of his hands, and he stared wildly at the stately lady, who was being so unceremoniously dealt with; but soon, however, he grasped what was going on, and immediately this whole story began to please him very much. He listened with pleasure, so much so that he wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh... All his nerves were jumping. — Ilya Petrovich!
