 — I'll bring it. Raskolnikov was looking at everything with deep surprise and stupid senseless fear. He decided to remain silent and wait: what would happen next? "I don't seem to be delirious," he thought, "it seems this is really happening..."

In two minutes, Nastasya returned with the soup and announced that tea would be served soon. Two spoons, two plates, and all the cutlery appeared for the soup: saltcellar, pepper shaker, mustard for beef, and other things that had not been in such order for a long time before. The tablecloth was clean. "It wouldn't be bad, Nastasya, if Praskovya Pavlovna would send two bottles of beer. We'll have a drink." "Oh, you nosy one!" muttered Nastasya and went to carry out the order.
