 The stout lady jumped up from her seat at sight of him and began to curtsy with some special enthusiasm; but the officer did not pay her the slightest attention, and she no longer dared to sit down in front of him. It was a lieutenant, an assistant district constable, with ginger-colored mustaches sticking out horizontally in both directions and extremely fine facial features that expressed nothing special, except for some impudence.
