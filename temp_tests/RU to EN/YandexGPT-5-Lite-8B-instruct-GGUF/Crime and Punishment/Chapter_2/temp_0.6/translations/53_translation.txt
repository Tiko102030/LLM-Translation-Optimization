 “Damn it all! — he thought suddenly in a fit of inexhaustible anger. — Well, it started, and that's how it started, the devil take her and this new life! How stupid it is, Lord!.. And how much I lied and scheming today! How vilely I fawned and flirted just now with the awful Ilya Petrovich! But then, all this is nonsense! I don't care about any of them, or that I fawned and flirted! That's not it at all! That's not it at all!..”

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question knocked him off balance and surprised him bitterly:

“If indeed this whole thing was done consciously, and not stupidly, if you really had a definite and firm goal, then how is it that you haven't even looked into the wallet yet and don't know what you got, why you endured all the муки and deliberately went to such a base, vile, low deed?”
