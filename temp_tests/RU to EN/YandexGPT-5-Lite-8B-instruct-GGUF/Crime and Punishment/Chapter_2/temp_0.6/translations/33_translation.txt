 There they are, the writers! — and he threw a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. — The other day in the tavern there was also a story: had lunch, but doesn't want to pay; "I, say, will describe you in satire for that." On the boat too, another one, last week, respectable family of a stat councillor, wife and daughter, called them the vilest words. The other day they threw someone out of the pastry shop by pushing him. That's what they are like, writers, litterateurs, students, heralds...ugh! And you went your way! I will come to see you myself…then beware! Have you heard? Louise Ivanovna began to curtsy with hurried politeness in all directions and, curtsying, backed up to the door; but in the doorway she bumped into an imposing officer with a fresh open face and superbly thick blond sideburns from behind.
