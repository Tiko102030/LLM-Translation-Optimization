 There was terrible stuffiness because of it. Street cleaners went up and down with books under their arms, visitors and people of both sexes — visitors. The door to the office itself was also wide open. He entered and stopped in the hallway. Some men were standing here too and waiting. There was extreme stuffiness here too, and besides, the fresh, not yet dried paint on the rotten linseed oil of the newly painted rooms hit the nose to the point of nausea. After waiting a moment, he decided to move forward a little more, into the next room. All the rooms were tiny and low. Dreadful impatience pulled him further and further. No one noticed him.
