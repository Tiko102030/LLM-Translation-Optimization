— It can't be, both will be released! Firstly, everything contradicts; judge for yourself: why would they call the caretaker if it were their business? To inform on themselves, perhaps? Or is it a trick? No, that would be too cunning! And finally, student Pes tryakov was seen at the very gate by both caretakers and a commoner at the exact moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted with them at the very gate and asked about living quarters from the caretakers, even in front of his friends.
