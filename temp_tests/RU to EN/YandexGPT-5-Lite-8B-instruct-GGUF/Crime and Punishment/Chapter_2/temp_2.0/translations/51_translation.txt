 A small depression formed under the stone; immediately he began to throw everything from his pocket into it. The wallet came to the very top, and still there was room left in the depression. Then he again grasped the stone, перевернул it with one turn to its original side, and it fell right into its original place, perhaps a little, slightly higher. But he raked up some earth and pressed it down at the edges with his foot. Nothing could be noticed. Then he went out and headed towards the square. Again a strong, almost unbearable joy, as recently in the office, овладела him for an instant. "The ends are hidden! And who, who would think of looking under this stone? It has been there, perhaps since the construction of the house, and will lie there for just as long."
