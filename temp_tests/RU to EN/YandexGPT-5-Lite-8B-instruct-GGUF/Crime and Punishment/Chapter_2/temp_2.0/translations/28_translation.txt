The clerk looked at him with a снисходительной smile of сожаление and some triumph, like looking at a newcomer who was just beginning to be shelled: “What, how do you feel now?” But what, what did he care now about the loan letter, about collection! Was it worth any anxiety now, in turn, even some attention! He stood, read, listened, answered, even asked himself, but all this mechanically. The triumph of self-preservation, salvation from the pressing danger — that's what filled his whole being at that moment, without foresight, without analysis, without future guessing and guessing, without doubts and questions. It was a minute of complete, непосредственной, purely animal joy. But at that very moment something like thunder and lightning occurred in the office. The lieutenant, still shaken by the disrespect, flushed red and, obviously wanting to support his injured ambition, lashed out with all his might at the unfortunate "grand dame", who had been looking at him since he entered with a ridiculously stupid smile. — And you, such-and-such and so-and-so — he suddenly shouted at the top of his voice (the mourning lady had already left) — what happened to you last night?
