 — Bravo! And now, brother, are you hungry? 
— I am, — answered Raskolnikov.
— Do you have soup?
— Yes, it's from yesterday, — Nastasyya replied, having stood there all this time.
— With potatoes and rice groats?
— With potatoes and groats.
— I know it by heart. Bring the soup and make some tea too.
— I'll bring it.
