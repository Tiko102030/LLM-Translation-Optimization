 What is this? He rushed to take them out and throw them on the table in a moment. Having taken everything out, even turning his pockets inside out to make sure there was nothing left, he carried all this pile into the corner. There, in the very corner, down below, in one place the wallpaper was torn away from the wall: immediately he began to shove everything into this hole, under the paper: “It went in! Everything is out of sight, and so is the wallet!” — he thought joyfully, standing up and staring stupidly into the corner, at the hole that bulged even more. Suddenly he trembled all over with horror: “My God,” he whispered in despair, “what’s happening to me? Was this supposed to be hidden? Is this how one hides things?”

Indeed, he did not expect any objects; he thought there would only be money, which is why he had not prepared a place in advance — “But what am I happy about now? — he thought. — Is this how one hides things?”
