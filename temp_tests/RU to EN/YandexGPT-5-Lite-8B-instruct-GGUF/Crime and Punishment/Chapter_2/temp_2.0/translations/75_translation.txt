 “He asked, pointing at the guy. ‘Look at him, he came to his senses!’ she said. ‘He has come to his senses,’ the foreman replied. Guessing that he had awakened, the landlady, peering from the door, immediately closed it and hid. She had always been shy and found conversations and explanations difficult; she was about forty years old, stout and fat, with dark eyebrows and dark eyes, kind due to her plumpness and sluggishness; and she was also very comely. Shy beyond necessity. ‘Who are you…?’ he continued to question, addressing the foreman himself. But at that moment the door opened wide again, and Raskolnikov entered, bending slightly because he was tall.”
