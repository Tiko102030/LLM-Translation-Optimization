 "Nastasyia, therefore, could not make out anything from a distance, thank God!" Then he opened the summons with trepidation and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood. It was an ordinary summons from the police precinct to appear today, at half past nine, at the office of the quarter police superintendent. "But when has this ever happened? I have no dealings with the police on my own! And why just today?" — he thought in excruciating bewilderment. "Lord, hurry it up!" He would have rushed to his knees to pray, but even laughed at himself—not at the prayer, but at himself. He hastily began to get dressed. "I'll be lost either way! Put on a sock!"
