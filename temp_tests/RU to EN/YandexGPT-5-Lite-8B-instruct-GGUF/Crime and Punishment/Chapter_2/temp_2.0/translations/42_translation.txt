 "The house is like Noah's Ark," observed the clerk, listening from his place. "It's clear, it's clear!" repeated Nikodim Fomich warmly. "No, the case is very unclear," confirmed Ilya Petrovich. Raskolnikov picked up his hat and went to the door, but he didn't reach the door...

When he came to, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that someone was supporting him from the right, that another man was standing on the left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nikodim Fomich was standing in front of him and gazing intently at him; he got up from the chair. "What is this, are you sick?" asked Nikodim Fomich rather sharply. "They could barely write even when they signed their names," observed the clerk, settling back into his place and getting to work on the papers again. "And have you been ill for long?" shouted Ilya Petrovich from his place while shuffling through the paperwork.
