 There they are, the writers! — and he shot a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. — The day before yesterday there was an incident in the tavern too: he had lunch, but doesn't want to pay; "I, they say, will describe you in satire for that." On the boat also another one, last week, respectable family of a state councilor, wife and daughter, were called the most vile words. They kicked someone out of the pastry shop the other day by shoving him. That's what writers, litterateurs, students, heralds... ugh! But you went away! I'll drop in to see you myself... then watch out! Have you heard? Luiza Ivanovna with hurried courtesy began to curtsy on all sides and, curtsying, backed up to the door; but in the doorway she ran into the back of a distinguished officer, with an open, fresh face and excellent thick blond sideburns.
