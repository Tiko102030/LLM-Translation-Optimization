 The clerk looked at them with a smile. The hot lieutenant was obviously puzzled. "It's not your business!" he shouted finally in an unnaturally loud voice, "but please submit the feedback that you are being asked to provide. Show him, Alexander Grigorievich." Complaints about you! You don't pay the money! Look how the fine falcon flew out! But Raskolnikov was no longer listening and greedily grasped at the paper, searching for a clue as quickly as possible. He read it once, twice, and did not understand.
