In two minutes, Nastasya returned with soup and announced that tea would be coming soon. Two spoons, two plates, and all the cutlery appeared for the soup: salt cellar, pepper shaker, mustard for beef, and other things that had not been in such order for a long time before. The tablecloth was clean. “It wouldn’t be bad, Nastasiuschka, if Praskovya Pavlovna would send two bottles of beer. We will have a drink.” “Oh, you nosy one!” mumbled Nastasya and went to execute the order.
