 Nothing and nobody; no one looked in. Not even Nastasya touched anything. But, good lord! How could he have left all those things in that hole the other day? He dashed into a corner, reached under the wallpaper, began to pull out items and stuff them into his pockets. There were eight of them: two small boxes with earrings or something like that — he didn't take a good look; then four small morocco cases. One chain was simply wrapped in newspaper. Something else in newspaper wrapping, an order, it seems…
He put everything in different pockets, in his coat and in the remaining right pocket of his trousers, trying to make it less noticeable. He took the wallet too along with the things. Then he left the room, this time even leaving it wide open.
