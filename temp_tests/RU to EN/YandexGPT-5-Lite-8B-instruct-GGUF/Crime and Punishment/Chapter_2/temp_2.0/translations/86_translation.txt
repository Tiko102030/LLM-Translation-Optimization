 — Make tea quickly, Nastasya, because it seems that one can drink tea without the faculty. But here's some beer! — he moved to his chair, pushed his soup and beef closer to him and began to eat with such an appetite as if he had not eaten for three days. — I, brother Rodya, dine like this every day now,» he muttered as much as his mouth full of beef allowed, «and all this is the work of Pashenka, your mistress, she is managing everything, treating me with all her heart. I certainly don't insist, well, I don't object either. And here comes Nastasya with tea. What a nimble one! Nastenka, do you want some beer? — Oh, go to the rascal! —And some tea?»
