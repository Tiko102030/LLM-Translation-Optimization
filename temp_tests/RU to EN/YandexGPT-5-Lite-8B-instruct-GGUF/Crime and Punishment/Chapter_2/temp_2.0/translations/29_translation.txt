 — And you, you damn rascal, — he suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the funeral lady had already left), — what happened to you last night? Eh? Again shame, a brawl on the whole street. Again fighting and drinking. You're dreaming of being placed in a straitjacket! I already told you, I warned you ten times that I wouldn't put up with it the eleventh time! And you again, again, you damn rascal! Even the paper dropped from Raskolnikov's hands, and he looked wildly at the curvaceous lady who was being so unceremoniously scolded; but soon, however, realized what was going on, and immediately this whole story started to please him a lot. He listened with pleasure, so much that he wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh… All his nerves were jumping. — Ilya Petrovich!
