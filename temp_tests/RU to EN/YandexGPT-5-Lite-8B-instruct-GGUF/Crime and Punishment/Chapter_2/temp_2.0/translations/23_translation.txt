 The stout lady jumped up from her seat upon seeing him and began to curtsy with some special delight; but the officer did not pay her the slightest attention, and she no longer dared to sit down in front of him. This was a lieutenant, an assistant police inspector, with reddish mustaches sticking out horizontally in both directions and extremely fine facial features that did not express anything special except for some impudence.
