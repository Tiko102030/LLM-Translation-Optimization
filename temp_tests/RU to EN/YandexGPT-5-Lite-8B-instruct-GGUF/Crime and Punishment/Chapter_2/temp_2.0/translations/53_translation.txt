 “Oh, hell with it all! — he thought suddenly in a fit of irrepressible anger. — Well, it started, so be it, hell with her and this new life! How foolish is that, Lord!.. And how much I lied and schemed today! How vilely I fawned and flirted just now with the most dreadful Ilya Petrovich! But then, nonsense all that too! I don't care about any of them or the fact that I fawned and flirted! Not at all! Not at all!..”

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question knocked him off balance and amazed him bitterly:

“If indeed this whole thing was really done consciously and not foolishly, if you действительно did have a specific and firm goal, then how is it that until now you haven’t even looked into the wallet and don't know what has fallen to you, why you endured all the муки, and deliberately went for such an vile, гадкий, low deed?”
