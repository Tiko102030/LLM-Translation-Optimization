 — Poverty is not a vice, my friend, but what of it! It's well known, Powder couldn't bear the insult. You must have been offended by him somehow and could not restrain yourself, — Nikodim Fomich continued, addressing Raskolnikov affably, — but you were wrong to do that: he's a most benevolent person, I'll tell you, but Powder, Powder! He flared up, boiled over, burned out — and that's it! And everything passed! And as a result, only the gold of his heart remains! They even called him "Lieutenant Powder" in the regiment...
