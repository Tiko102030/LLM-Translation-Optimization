 — But who locked themselves with the hook? — objected Nastasya, — look, he started to lock it! Will they take him away himself? Open up, you fool, wake up! “What do they want? Why a janitor? Everything is known. To resist or to open? Let them perish...”

He got up, leaned forward and took off the hook. His entire room was so small that he could remove the hook without getting out of bed.
