 “It's already the third hour!” He sat down on the sofa — and then he remembered everything! Suddenly, in an instant he remembered everything! At first он thought he would go mad. A terrible cold seized him; but it was also from a fever that had started long ago with him in his sleep. Now all of a sudden such a chill struck him that his teeth almost jumped out and everything inside him went wild. He opened the door and began to listen: everything in the house was completely asleep. He looked at himself and everything around in the room with amazement and did not understand: how could he have entered yesterday without locking the doors on the hook and throwing himself onto the sofa, not only undressed, but even wearing a hat: it had rolled down and lay there on the floor, near the pillow. “If someone were to come in, what would he think? That I'm drunk, but…” He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began to look at himself, all over, from head to toe, all his clothes: are there any traces?”
