 “That's not it at all!..”

Suddenly he stopped; a new, совершенно неожиданный and extremely simple question knocked him off balance and amazed him bitterly:

“If indeed all this was done consciously and not stupidly, if you really had a specific and firm goal, then how is it that you have not even looked into the wallet yet and do not know what you got, why you took all the suffering, and deliberately went to such a vile, nasty, low deed? Why, you wanted to throw it into the water right now, the wallet, along with all the things you haven't seen either... How is that possible?”

Yes, it is so; it’s all like that. However, he knew this before, and this is not a new question for him at all; and when it was decided to throw it into the water at night, it was decided without any hesitation or objection, as if that's how it should be, as if there could be no other way… Yes, he knew all this and remembered everything; yes, perhaps this had already been decided yesterday, at the very moment when he was sitting over the chest and dragging cases out of it... And so it is!.. “This is because I am very sick,” he finally decided sullenly, “I have tormented and tortured myself, and do not know what I am doing… Both yesterday and the day before yesterday, and all this time I have been tormenting myself… I will recover and... will not torture myself… But what if I don't recover at all? Lord! How tired I am of all this!..” He walked without stopping. He desperately wanted to somehow distract himself, but he didn’t know what to do or what to undertake. A new, irresistible feeling took possession of him more and more almost every minute: it was some kind of endless, almost physical disgust for everything that met and surrounded him, stubborn, malicious, hateful. He disliked all passers-by — he disliked their faces, gait, movements. He would have spat on someone, bitten them, it seems, if anyone had spoken to him...

He suddenly stopped when he walked out onto the embankment of Malaya Neva, on Vasilyevsky Island, near the bridge.
