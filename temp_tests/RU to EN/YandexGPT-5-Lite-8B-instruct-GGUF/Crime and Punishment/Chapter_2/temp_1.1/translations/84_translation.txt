In two minutes, Nastasya returned with soup and announced that tea would be served soon. Two spoons, two plates, and the whole set appeared for the soup: a salt shaker, pepper shaker, mustard for beef, and other things that had not been seen in such order for a long time before. The tablecloth was clean. "It wouldn't be bad, Nastasiushka, if Praskovya Pavlovna would send two bottles of beer. We will drink it," — "Well, you sharp-nosed one!" mumbled Nastasya and went to carry out the order.
