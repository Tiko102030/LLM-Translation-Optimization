But what, what did he care now about the loan letter, about взыскания! Was it worth any anxiety now, in turn, even some attention! He stood, read, listened, replied, even asked himself, but all this mechanically. The triumph of self-preservation, salvation from pressing danger — that's what filled his entire being at this moment, without foresight, without analysis, without future guessing and solving, without doubts and questions. It was a minute of complete, непосредственной, purely animal joy. But at that very moment something like thunder and lightning occurred in the office. The lieutenant, still shaken by the impoliteness, flushed and, obviously wishing to support his wounded ambition, attacked the несчастную "stately lady" with all his might, who had been looking at him since he entered with the silliest smile. — And you, such-and-such and such-like, shouted he suddenly at the top of his voice (the mourning lady had already left), what happened to you last night?
