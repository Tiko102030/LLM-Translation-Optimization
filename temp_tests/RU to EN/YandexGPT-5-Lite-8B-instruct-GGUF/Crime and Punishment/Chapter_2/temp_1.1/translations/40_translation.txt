 The impulse was so strong that he had already got up from his seat to carry it out. “Shouldn’t I at least think about it for a minute?” — flashed through his head. “No, it’s better not to think and get it over with!” But suddenly he stopped as if stuck in the ground: Nikodim Fomich was talking enthusiastically to Ilya Petrovich, and words reached him:
— It can't be, both of them will be released! First of all, everything contradicts; judge for yourself: why would they call a дворник, if it were their case? To inform on themselves, what for? Or for cunning? No, that would be too clever! And finally, student Pestyakov was seen at the very gate by both the caretakers and the commoner woman right at the moment when he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted with them at the very gate and asked about living quarters from the caretakers, even in front of his friends. Well, would someone like that ask about living quarters if they were going there with such an intention?
