Having chosen everything, even turning his pockets inside out to make sure there wasn't anything else left, he carried all this pile to the corner. There, in the very corner, at the bottom, in one place the wallpaper torn away from the wall was ripped: immediately he began pushing everything into this hole, under the paper: “It went in! Everything is out of sight, and so is the wallet!” — he thought joyfully, standing up and looking stupidly into the corner, at the hole that bulged even more. Suddenly he shuddered all over with horror: “Oh my God,” he whispered in despair, “what’s happening to me? Is this what I hid? Is this how you hide things?”
