 This very present apartment I have forgotten; however, I never really remembered it because I didn't know it. Well, as for the previous apartment — all I remember is that it was near Five Corners, Kharlamov house. I looked for that Kharlamov house for so long — and it turned out later that it wasn't actually a Kharlamov house, but a Bukha house — how you sometimes stumble over sounds! Well, I got angry. I got angry and went, come what may, to the address bureau the next day, and imagine: they found you there in two minutes. You're registered there.
