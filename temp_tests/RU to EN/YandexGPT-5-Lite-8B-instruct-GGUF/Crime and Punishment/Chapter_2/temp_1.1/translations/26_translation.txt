 The clerk looked at them with a smile. The hot lieutenant was apparently perplexed. "This is not your business!" he shouted finally in an unnaturally loud voice, "but here please submit the review that's required of you. Show him, Alexander Grigorievich. Complaints about you! You don't pay money! Look at this bright falcon having flown out!" But Raskolnikov was no longer listening and greedily grasped at the paper, looking for a quick answer. Read it once, twice, and didn't understand.
