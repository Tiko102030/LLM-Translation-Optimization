 "There they are, the writers!" — and he shot a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. "The day before yesterday there was a story in the tavern too: had lunch, but doesn't want to pay; 'I, say, will describe you in satire for that.' On the boat also another one, last week, the respectable family of a statsky councillor, wife and daughter, called them the vilest words. The other day they were thrown out of the pastry shop by shoving. That's what they are like, writers, literati, students, heralds... ugh! And you went! I'll drop in on you myself... then look out! Did you hear that? Louise Ivanovna rushed to curtsy with hurried courtesy in all directions and, curtsying, backed toward the door; but in the doorway she bumped into an officer from behind, with an open fresh face and excellent thick blond sideburns.
