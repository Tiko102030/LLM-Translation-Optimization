 "The house is like Noah's ark," remarked the clerk, listening from his place. "It's clear, it's clear!" repeated Nikodim Fomich fervently. "No, it's very unclear," confirmed Ilya Petrovich. Raskolnikov picked up his hat and went to the door, but he didn't get there...

When he came to his senses, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that someone was supporting him from the right, that another person was standing on the left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nikodim Fomich was standing in front of him and staring at him; he got up from the chair. "What's this, are you sick?" asked Nikodim Fomich rather harshly. "They could hardly move the pen as they used to sign," remarked the clerk, settling back into his place and getting down to work on papers again. "And have you been ill for long?" shouted Ilya Petrovich from his place, also going through the papers.
