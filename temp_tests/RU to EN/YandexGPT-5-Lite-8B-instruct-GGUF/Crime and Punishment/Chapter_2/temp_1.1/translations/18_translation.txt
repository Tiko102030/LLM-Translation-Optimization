 There was terrible stuffiness because of that. Yardmen with books tucked under their arms, errand runners and various people of both sexes — visitors — were going up and down. The door to the office itself was also wide open. He entered and stopped in the hallway. Some men were standing and waiting here too. There was extreme stuffiness here too, and besides, fresh, still uncured paint on rancid boiled linseed oil in the newly painted rooms hit the nose until it made one nauseous. After waiting a while, he decided to move further ahead, into the next room. All the rooms were tiny and low-ceilinged. A terrible impatience was pulling him farther and farther. No one noticed him.
