 It was strange. — Well then, okay then, — concluded Ilya Petrovich, — we're not keeping you any longer. Raskolnikov went out. He could still hear how a lively conversation began after his departure, in which the questioning voice of Nikodim Fomich... could be heard the loudest... He came to his senses completely outside. "Search, search, there's a search right now! — he repeated to himself as he hurried to get there; — robbers! they suspect!" The same fear seized him again, from head to toe. II

"But what if there already was a search? What if I find them sitting there just in their place?"
