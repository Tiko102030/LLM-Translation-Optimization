 And he opened the window onto the canal and stuck his head out like a little pig, squealing; and that's shameful. And how can one stick one's head out the window into the street, like a little pig, squealing; and that's shameful. Fie-fie-fie! And Karl pulled him by the tailcoat from behind the window, and then, truly, sir captain, they tore his coat. And then he shouted that he had to pay a fine of fifteen целковых. And I myself, sir captain, paid him five целковых for his coat. And this is an ungracious guest, sir captain, and he made all the scandals! I, I say, will make a big satire about you, because I can write everything about you in all the newspapers. — From writers, then? — Yes, sir captain, and what kind of an ungracious guest is that, sir captain, when it's a noble house...
