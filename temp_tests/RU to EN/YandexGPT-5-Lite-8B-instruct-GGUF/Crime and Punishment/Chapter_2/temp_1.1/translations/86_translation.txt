 — Hurry up and pour the tea, Nastasya, because regarding tea, it seems one can manage without the faculty. But here's some beer! — he moved to his chair, pushed his soup and beef towards him and began to eat with such appetite as if he hadn’t eaten for three days. “I, brother Rodya, dine like this every day now,” he muttered, as much as a mouthful of beef allowed, “and it's all Pashenka, your little hostess, who is managing the housekeeping and honoring me with her hospitality. I certainly don’t insist, but I also don’t object. And here comes Nastasya with tea. She’s so quick! Nastenka, want some beer?” — “And, well, go to the prankster!” — “How about some tea?”
