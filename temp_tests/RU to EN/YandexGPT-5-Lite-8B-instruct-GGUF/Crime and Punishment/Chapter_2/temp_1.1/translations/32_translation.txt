 — Yes, Captain sir, and what kind of base guest is it, Captain sir, when in a noble house...
— Come now, enough! I already told you, I said, I did tell you...
— Ilya Petrovich! — the clerk said again significantly. The lieutenant took a quick look at him; the clerk gave a slight nod of his head. “...So here’s your last word, dear Laviza Ivanovna, and this is for the last time,” the lieutenant continued. “If there’s another scandal in your noble house even once more, I’ll drag you into a fight, as they say in high style. Heard that? So a literator, a composer, took five silver coins in a 'noble house' for his tailcoat. There they are, the writers!”
