 “Damn it all! — he thought suddenly in a fit of irrepressible anger. — Well, it started, so it started, the devil with her and this new life! How stupid it is, Lord!.. And how much I lied and acted mean today! How vilely I fawned and flirted just now with that awful Ilya Petrovich! But then, that's nonsense too! I don't care about any of them, or the fact that I fawned and flirted! It's not at all what it is! Not at all!..”

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question knocked him off balance and surprised him bitterly:

“If indeed all this was done consciously, and not stupidly, if you действительно did have a specific and firm goal, then how come you haven't even looked into the wallet yet and don't know what you got, why you endured all the torment and deliberately went for such a vile, гадкое, low deed?”
