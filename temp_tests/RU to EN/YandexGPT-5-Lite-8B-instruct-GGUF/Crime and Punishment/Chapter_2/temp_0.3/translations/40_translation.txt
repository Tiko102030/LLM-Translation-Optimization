 The urge was so strong that he got up to act on it. “Shouldn’t I at least think about it for a minute?” ran through his head. “No, it’s better not to think and just get it over with!” But suddenly he stopped as if rooted to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was speaking passionately to Ilya Petrovich, and words reached him:

— It can't be, both will be released! Firstly, everything contradicts; judge for yourself: why would they call a дворник if it were their business? To inform on themselves, perhaps? Or is it some trick? No, that would be too cunning! And finally, the student Pestryakov was seen at the very gate by both the yardkeepers and the commoner woman at the exact moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted with them at the very gate and asked about living quarters from the yardkeepers, even in front of his friends. Well, would someone like that ask about living quarters if they were going there with such an intention?
