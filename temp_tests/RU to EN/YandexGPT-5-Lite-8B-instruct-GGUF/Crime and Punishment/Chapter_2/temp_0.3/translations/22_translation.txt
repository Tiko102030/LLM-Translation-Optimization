Her light blue dress with white lace trim spread out around the chair like a balloon and took up almost half the room. There was a scent of perfume. But the lady was obviously робкая that she occupied half the room and that she smelled so strongly of perfume, although she smiled timidly and impudently at the same time, but with явным беспокойство. The траурная lady finally finished and began to stand up. Suddenly, with some noise, quite smartly and somehow especially twisting her shoulders with each step, an officer entered, threw his cap with a cockade on the table and sat in an armchair. The lush lady jumped up from her seat as soon as she saw him, and began to curtsy with some special enthusiasm; but the officer did not pay her the slightest attention, and she no longer dared to sit down in front of him.
