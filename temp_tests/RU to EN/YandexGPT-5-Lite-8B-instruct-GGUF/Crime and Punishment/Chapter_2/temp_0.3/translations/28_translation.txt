 — But isn't she my landlady? — Well, so what if she's the landlady? The clerk looked at him with a condescending smile of сожаление and also some triumph, as if looking at a newcomer who is just being обстрелян: “What, how do you feel now?” But what, what did he care now about the loan letter, about collection! Was it worth any anxiety now, in turn, even some attention! He stood, read, listened, answered, even asked himself, but all this mechanically. The triumph of self-preservation, salvation from pressing danger — that's what filled his whole being at that moment, without foresight, without analysis, without future guessing and solving, without doubts and without questions. It was a minute of complete, непосредственной, purely animal joy. But at that very moment something like thunder and lightning occurred in the office. The lieutenant, still shaken by the disrespect, all flushed and obviously wanting to support his injured ambition, lashed out with all his might at the unfortunate "stately lady", who had been looking at him since he entered with the silliest smile. — And you, this and that and the other, — he shouted suddenly at the top of his voice (the mourning lady had already left), — what happened to you last night?
