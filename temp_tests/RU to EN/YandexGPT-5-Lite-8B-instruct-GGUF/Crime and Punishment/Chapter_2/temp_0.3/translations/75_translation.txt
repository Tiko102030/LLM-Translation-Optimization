 “He asked, pointing to the guy. ‘Look at that, he woke up!’ she said. ‘He came to his senses,’ the foreman replied. Guessing that he had come to his senses, the landlady, who was peeping from the door, immediately closed it and hid. She had always been shy and found it difficult to tolerate conversations and explanations; she was about forty years old, and she was stout and fat, dark-browed and black-eyed, good-natured from her stoutness and laziness; and she was even very pretty in appearance. Shy beyond necessity. ‘You… who are you?’ he continued to question, turning to the foreman himself. But at that moment the door opened wide again, and Разумихин entered, bending down a little because he was tall.”
