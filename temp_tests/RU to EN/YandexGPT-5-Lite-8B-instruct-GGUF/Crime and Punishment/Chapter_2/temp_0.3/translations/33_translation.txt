 There they are, the writers! — and he cast a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. — The other day in the tavern there was also a story: had lunch, but doesn't want to pay; "I, they say, will describe you in satire for that." On the steamer too, another one, last week, the respectable family of a statsky советник, the wife and daughter, were called names in the foulest words. They kicked someone out of the pastry shop the other day by pushing him. That's what they are like, writers, literati, students, heralds...ugh! And you went along! I myself will come to see you... then look out! Heard?
