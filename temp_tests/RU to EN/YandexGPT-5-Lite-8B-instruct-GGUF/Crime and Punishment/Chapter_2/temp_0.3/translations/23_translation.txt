 The stout lady jumped up from her seat upon seeing him and began to curtsy with some special delight; but the officer did not pay any attention to her, and she no longer dared to sit down in front of him. It was a lieutenant, an assistant district supervisor, with reddish mustaches sticking out horizontally in both directions and extremely fine facial features that expressed nothing special, except for some impudence.
