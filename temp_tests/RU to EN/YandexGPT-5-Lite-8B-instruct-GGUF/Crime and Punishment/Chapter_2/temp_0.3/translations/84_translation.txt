 — I'll bring it. Raskolnikov looked at everything with deep surprise and stupid senseless fear. He decided to remain silent and wait: what would happen next? "I don't seem to be delirious," he thought, "this seems to be really happening..."

In two minutes, Nastasya returned with the soup and announced that tea would be served soon. Two spoons, two plates, and all the cutlery appeared for the soup: salt cellar, pepper shaker, mustard for beef, and other things that had not been in such order for a long time before. The tablecloth was clean. "It wouldn't be bad, Nastasya, if Praskovya Pavlovna would send two bottles of beer. We'll have a drink." "Well, you sharp-nosed one!" muttered Nastasya and went to obey the order.
