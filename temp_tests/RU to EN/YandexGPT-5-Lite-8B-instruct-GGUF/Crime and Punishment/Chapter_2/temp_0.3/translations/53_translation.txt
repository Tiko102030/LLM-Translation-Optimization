 “Oh, damn it all! — he thought suddenly in a fit of inexhaustible anger. — Well, it started, and that's how it started, the devil take her and this new life! How stupid it is, lord!.. And how much I lied and schemed today! How vilely I fawned and flirted just now with the awful Ilya Petrovich! But then, nonsense all that! I don't care about them all, or about my fawning and flirting! That's not it at all! That's not it at all!..”

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question knocked him off balance and surprised him bitterly:

“If indeed all this was done consciously, and not stupidly, if you really had a specific and firm goal, then how is it that you haven't even looked into the wallet yet and don't know what you got, why you went through all the муки and deliberately went to such an ignoble, vile, low deed?”
