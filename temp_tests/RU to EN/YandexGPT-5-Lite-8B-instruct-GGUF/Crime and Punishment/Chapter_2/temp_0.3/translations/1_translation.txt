 "It's been the third hour already!" He sat on the sofa — and then he remembered everything! Suddenly, in an instant, he recalled everything! At first he thought he would go mad. A terrible chill seized him; but it was also cold from a fever that had started with him in his sleep a long time ago. Now suddenly such a chills hit him that his teeth almost jumped out and everything inside him shook. He opened the door and began to listen: everyone in the house was completely asleep. He looked at himself and everything around in the room with astonishment and did not understand: how could he yesterday, having entered, not lock the doors with a hook and throw himself on the sofa, not only without undressing, but even in his hat: it rolled down and lay right there on the floor, near the pillow. "If someone came in, what would he think? That I'm drunk, but..." He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began to look at himself, all over, from head to toe, all his clothes: are there any traces?"
