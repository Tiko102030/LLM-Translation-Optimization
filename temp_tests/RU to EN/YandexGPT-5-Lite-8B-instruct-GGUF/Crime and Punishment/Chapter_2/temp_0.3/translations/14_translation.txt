 "Nastassia, therefore, could not discern anything from a distance, thank God!" Then he opened the summons with trepidation and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood. It was an ordinary summons from the precinct to appear today, at half past nine, at the office of the district constable. "But when has this ever happened? I don't have any business with the police on my own! And why именно today?" — he thought in painful bewilderment. "Lord, just hurry up!" He rushed to his knees to pray, but even laughed at himself — not at the prayer, but at himself. He hurriedly began to get dressed. "I'll go down that road, so be it! Put on a sock!"
