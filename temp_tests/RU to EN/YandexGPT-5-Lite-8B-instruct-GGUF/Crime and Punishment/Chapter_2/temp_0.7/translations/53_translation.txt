 “Oh, damn it all!” — he thought suddenly in a fit of inexhaustible anger. “Well, it started, and that's how it started, the hell with her and this new life! How stupid is this, Lord!.. And how much I lied and schemed today! How vilely I fawned and flirted just now with the awful Ilya Petrovich! But then, nonsense all that! I don't care about any of them, or about my fawning and flirting! It's not that at all! It's not that at all!..”

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question knocked him off balance and surprised him bitterly:

“If indeed this whole thing was done consciously, and not stupidly, if you really had a definite and firm goal, then how is it that you have not even looked into the wallet until now and do not know what you got, why you went through all the муки and deliberately set out to such a base, vile, low deed?”
