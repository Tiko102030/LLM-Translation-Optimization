 And where did these feelings come from? On the contrary, now, if suddenly the room were filled not with orderlies, but with his first friends, then even then, it seems, he would not have a single human word for them, his heart had become so empty all of a sudden. A gloomy feeling of painful, endless loneliness and alienation suddenly consciously manifested itself in his soul. Neither the meanness of his heartfelt outpourings before Ilya Petrovich, nor the meanness and triumph of Pouchikov over him had suddenly turned his heart like this. Oh, what does he care now about his own meanness, about all these ambitions, lieutenants, Germans, взыскания ( взыскания literally means reprimands or penalties but context suggests demands), offices, etc., etc.! If they even sentenced him to be burned at that moment, he would not have moved, he probably would not even have listened to the sentence carefully. Something completely unfamiliar, new, sudden and never experienced before was happening to him. Not that he understood, but he clearly felt, with all the power of sensation, that not only with sensitive expansiveness, as before, but even with anything at all, it was no longer possible for him to turn to these people, in the police station, and if they were all his own brothers and sisters, and not police lieutenants, then he would have absolutely no reason to turn to them in any case of life; he had never experienced such a strange and terrible sensation before this moment. And what is most painful — it was more a feeling than consciousness, than concept; immediate sensation, the most painful sensation of all those he had experienced in his life so far. The clerk began to dictate to him the form of an ordinary response in such cases, that is, I cannot pay, I promise then (sometime), I will not leave the city, I will not sell or give away my property, etc.
