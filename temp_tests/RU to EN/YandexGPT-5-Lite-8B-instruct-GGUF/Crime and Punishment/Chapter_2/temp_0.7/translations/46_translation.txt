 Nothing and nobody; no one looked in. Not even Nastasya touched anything. But, my God! How could he have left all these things in that hole earlier? He rushed to the corner, reached his hand under the wallpaper and began to pull out items and stuff them into his pockets. There were eight of them: two small boxes with earrings or something like that — he didn't take a good look; then four small morocco cases. One chain was simply wrapped in newspaper paper. Something else in newsprint, an order, it seems...
He put everything in different pockets, in his coat and in the remaining right pocket of his trousers, trying to make it less noticeable. He also took the wallet along with the items. Then he left the room, this time even leaving it wide open.
