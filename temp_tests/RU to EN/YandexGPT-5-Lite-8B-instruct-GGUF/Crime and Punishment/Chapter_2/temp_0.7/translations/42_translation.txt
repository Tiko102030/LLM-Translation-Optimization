 "The house is like Noah's Ark," observed the clerk, listening from his place. "It's clear, it's clear!" repeated Nikodim Fomich fervently. "No, the matter is very unclear," confirmed Ilya Petrovich. Raskolnikov picked up his hat and went to the door, but he didn't reach the door...

When he came to his senses, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that someone was supporting him from the right, that there was another person standing to his left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nikodim Fomich was standing in front of him and staring at him; he got up from the chair. "What's going on, are you sick?" asked Nikodim Fomich rather sharply. "They could barely write with a pen even when they signed their names," observed the clerk, settling into his place and resuming his work on the papers again. "And how long have you been ill?" shouted Ilya Petrovich from his place, shuffling through the papers as well.
