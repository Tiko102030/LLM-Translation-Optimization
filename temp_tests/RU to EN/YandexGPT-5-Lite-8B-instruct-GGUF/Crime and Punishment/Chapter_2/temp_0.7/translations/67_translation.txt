 It seemed to him that at that moment he had cut himself off from everyone and everything as if with scissors. He returned to his place only in the evening, which meant he had been away for about six hours. He did not remember where and how he walked back. After undressing and shaking like a загнанная horse, he lay down on the sofa, pulled his overcoat over himself and immediately fell asleep...

He woke up in complete twilight from an awful scream. God, what a scream! He had never heard or seen such unnatural sounds, such howling, wailing, grinding, crying, fighting and swearing. He could not imagine such cruelty, such frenzy. In horror, he raised himself up and sat on his bed, every moment trembling and tormenting himself. But the fights, screams and curses grew stronger and stronger. And then, to his greatest изумление, he suddenly heard the voice of his landlady.
