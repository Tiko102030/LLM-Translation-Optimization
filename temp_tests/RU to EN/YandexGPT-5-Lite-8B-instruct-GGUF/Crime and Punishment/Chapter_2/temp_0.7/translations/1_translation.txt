 "It's the third hour already!" He sat on the sofa — and then he remembered everything! Suddenly, in an instant, he remembered everything! At first he thought he would go mad. A terrible cold seized him; but it was also because of a fever that had started with him in his sleep long ago. But now such a chills suddenly struck that his teeth almost jumped out and everything inside him began to shake. He opened the door and started listening: everything in the house was completely asleep. He looked at himself and everything around him in the room with astonishment and did not understand: how could he yesterday, having entered, not lock the doors with a hook and throw himself on the sofa, not only without undressing, but even in his hat: it rolled down and lay there on the floor, near the pillow. "If someone had come in, what would he have thought? That I'm drunk, but..." He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began to look at himself, all over, from head to toe, all his clothes: are there any traces?"
