 A small depression formed under the stone; immediately he began to throw everything from his pocket into it. The wallet ended up at the very top, and yet there was still room in the depression. Then he grabbed the stone again, turned it over with one turn to its previous side, and it just fell into its прежнего place, perhaps a little higher, barely noticeable. But he raked up some earth and pressed down on the edges with his foot. Nothing was noticeable. Then he went out and headed towards the square. Again, intense, hardly bearable joy, as before in the office, seized him for a moment. “The ends are hidden! And who would even think of looking under this stone? It has been here, perhaps since the house was built, and will lie there for just as long.”
