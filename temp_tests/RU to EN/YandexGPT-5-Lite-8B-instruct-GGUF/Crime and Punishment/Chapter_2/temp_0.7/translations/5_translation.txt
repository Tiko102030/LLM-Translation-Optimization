 "Pieces of torn canvas will not arouse suspicion in any case; it seems so, it seems so!" — he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with painfully intense attention began to look around again, on the floor and everywhere, wondering if he had forgotten anything else? The certainty that everything, even memory, even simple reasoning was failing him, began to torment him unbearably. "What, is it really beginning, is this torture coming?" Indeed, scraps of fringe that he had cut from his pants were lying on the floor, in the middle of the room, for anyone to see! "But what's happening to me!" — he cried out again, lost. A strange thought occurred to him: perhaps all his clothes were bloody, perhaps there were many stains, but he just didn't see them, didn't notice them, because his reasoning had weakened, was fragmented... his mind was darkened… Suddenly he remembered that there was blood on his wallet. "Hey! So, there must be blood in my pocket too, because I put the still wet wallet into my pocket then!" He turned out his pocket in a flash, and sure enough — there were traces, stains on the lining of the pocket! "So, maybe reason hasn't left me yet, so, there must be reasoning and memory, since I caught myself and guessed it!" — he thought triumphantly, breathing deeply and joyfully with his whole chest — "it's just feverish weakness, delirium for a minute" — and he tore out the entire lining from the left pocket of his pants.
