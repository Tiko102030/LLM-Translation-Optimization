Guessing that he had come to his senses, the landlady, who was peeking from the door, immediately shut it and hid. She had always been shy and found it difficult to tolerate conversations and explanations; she was about forty years old, thick and fat, dark-browed and black-eyed, kind from being stout and lazy; and she was even very prettish. Shy beyond necessity. “You... who are you?” he continued to question, turning to the foreman himself.
