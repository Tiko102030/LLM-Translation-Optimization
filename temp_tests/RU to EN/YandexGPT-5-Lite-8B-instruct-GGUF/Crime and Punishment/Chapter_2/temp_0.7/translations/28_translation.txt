 — But isn't she my landlady? — Well, so what if she's the landlady? The clerk looked at him with a condescending smile of сожаление and also some triumph, as if looking at a beginner who is just being bombarded: "What, how are you feeling now?" But what, what did he care now about the loan letter, about взыскание! Was it worth any anxiety now, in turn, even some attention? He stood, read, listened, answered, asked himself, but all this mechanically. The triumph of self-preservation, salvation from the pressing danger — that's what filled his entire being at that moment, without foresight, without analysis, without future guessing and solving, without doubts and without questions. It was a minute of complete, непосредственной, чисто animal joy. But at that very moment something like thunder and lightning occurred in the office. The lieutenant, still shaken by the disrespect, all flushed and obviously wishing to support his wounded ambition, lashed out with all his might at the unfortunate "stately lady", who had been looking at him since he entered with the silliest smile. — And you, this such-and-such and so-and-so, shouted he suddenly at the top of his lungs (the mourning lady had already left),— what happened to you last night?
