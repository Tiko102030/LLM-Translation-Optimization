 The urge was so strong that he had already gotten up from his seat to act on it. “Should I at least think about it for a minute?” passed through his head. “No, better not thinking about it and get it over with!” But suddenly he stopped as if struck in his tracks: Nikodim Fomich was talking heatedly to Ilya Petrovich, and words reached him:

— It can't be, both will be released! Firstly, everything contradicts; judge for yourself: why would they call the janitor if it were their business? To inform on themselves, perhaps? Or is it a trick? No, that would be too cunning! And finally, student Pestryakov was seen near the gate by both janitors and a commoner at the very moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted with them right by the gate and asked about living quarters from the janitors, even in front of his friends. Well, would someone like that ask about living quarters if they were going there with such an intention?
