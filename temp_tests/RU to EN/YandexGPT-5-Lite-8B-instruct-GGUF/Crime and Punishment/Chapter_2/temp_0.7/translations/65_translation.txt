By their dress and appearance, they very well might have taken him for a beggar, a real street-corner penny collector, and the giving of a whole two-groat coin he probably owed to the stroke of the whip, which had touched their pity. He put the two-groat coin in his hand, walked ten steps and turned his face toward the Neva, towards the palace. The sky was without the slightest cloud, and the water was almost blue, which is so rare on the Neva. The dome of the cathedral, which from no point is outlined better than when looking at it from here, from the bridge, not reaching twenty steps to the chapel, shone so brightly, and through the clear air one could clearly make out even each of its decorations.
