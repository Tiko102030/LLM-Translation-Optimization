 There was a terrible stuffiness because of that. Sweeps with books tucked under their arms went up and down, porters and various people of both sexes — visitors. The door to the office itself was also wide open. He entered and stopped in the hallway. Some men were standing here too and waiting. There was extreme stuffiness here too, and besides, the fresh, not yet dried out paint on the rotten linseed oil of the newly painted rooms hit the nose to the point of nausea. After waiting a while, he decided to move even further forward, into the next room. All the rooms were tiny and low. Terrible impatience pulled him further and further. Nobody noticed him.
