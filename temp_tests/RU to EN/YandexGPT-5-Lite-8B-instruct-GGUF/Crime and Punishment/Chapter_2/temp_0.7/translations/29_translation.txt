 — And you, this and that and the other, — he shouted all at once with might and main (the mourning lady had already left), — what happened to you last night? huh? Shame again, making a racket in the street. Fighting and drinking again. You dream of being put in a straitjacket! After all, I've told you, I've warned you ten times that on the eleventh time I won't tolerate it! And you again, again, you this and that and the other! Even Raskolnikov's paper fell out of his hands, and he stared wildly at the stately lady, who was being so unceremoniously dealt with; but soon, however, realized what was going on, and immediately this whole story began to please him very much. He listened with pleasure, so much so that he wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh... All his nerves were jumping. — Ilya Petrovich!
