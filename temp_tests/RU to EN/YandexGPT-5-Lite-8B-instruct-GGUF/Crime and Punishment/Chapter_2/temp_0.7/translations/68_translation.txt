 And so, to the greatest изумление, he suddenly heard the voice of his landlady. She howled, squealed and lamented, hurrying, rushing, letting out words in such a way that it was impossible to make out what she was begging for — certainly for someone to stop beating her, because they were mercilessly beating her on the stairs. The voice of the one who was beating her became so terrible with anger and rage that he could only croak, but still the one who was beating her also said something, and also quickly, indistinctly, hurrying and choking. Suddenly Raskolnikov trembled like a leaf: he recognized this voice; it was Ilya Petrovich's voice. Ilya Petrovich is here and beating the landlady! He beats her with his feet, hits her head against the steps — it’s clear, it can be heard by the sounds, the howls, the blows! What is going on, has the world turned upside down? It could be heard as a crowd gathered on all floors, throughout the staircase, voices and exclamations were heard, they went up, knocked, slammed doors, rushed together. "But why, why, and how is it possible!" — he repeated, seriously thinking that he was completely crazy. But no, he hears too clearly!..
