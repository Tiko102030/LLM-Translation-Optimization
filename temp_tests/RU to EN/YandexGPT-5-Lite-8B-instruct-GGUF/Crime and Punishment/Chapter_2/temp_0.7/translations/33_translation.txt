 There they are, the writers! — and he cast a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. — The other day in the tavern there was also a story: had lunch, but doesn't want to pay; "I, they say, will describe you in satire for that." On the ship too, another one, last week, the respectable family of a state councilor, the wife and daughter, were called the most vile names. The other day they kicked someone out of a pastry shop by pushing him. That's what writers, litterateurs, students, heralds... ugh! Go your way! I myself will drop in to see you… then look out! Did you hear? Louise Ivanovna began to curtsy with hurried любезностью in all directions and, curtsying, backed up to the door; but in the doorway she bumped into the back of a distinguished officer with an open, fresh face and excellent thick blond sideburns.
