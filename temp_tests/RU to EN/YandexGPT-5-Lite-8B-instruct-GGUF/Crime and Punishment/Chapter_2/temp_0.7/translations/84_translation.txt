 — I'll bring it. Raskolnikov looked at everything with deep surprise and stupid senseless fear. He decided to remain silent and wait: what would happen next? "I don't seem to be delirious," he thought, "this seems to be real..."

In two minutes, Nastasya returned with the soup and announced that tea would be served soon. Two spoons, two plates, and all the cutlery appeared for the soup: salt shaker, pepper shaker, mustard for beef, and other things that had not been seen in such order for a long time before. The tablecloth was clean. "It wouldn't be bad, Nastasiuschka, if Praskovya Pavlovna would send two bottles of beer. We will drink it." "Well, you nosy one!" muttered Nastasya and went to execute the order.
