 What is this? He hastily began to take them out and throw them onto the table. Having chosen everything, even turning his pockets inside out to make sure there was nothing else left, he carried all this pile to the corner. There, in the very corner, at the bottom, in one place the wallpaper peeled away from the wall: immediately he began to shove everything into this hole, under the paper: “It went in! Everything is out of sight, including the wallet!” — he thought joyfully, standing up and staring blankly into the corner, at the hole that bulged even more. Suddenly he вздрогнул with horror: “Oh my God,” he whispered in despair, “what’s happening to me? Was this hidden? Is this how you hide things?”

Indeed, he had not expected any items; he thought there would only be money, which is why he had not prepared a place ahead of time — “but what am I happy about now? – he thought. – Is this how you hide things?”
