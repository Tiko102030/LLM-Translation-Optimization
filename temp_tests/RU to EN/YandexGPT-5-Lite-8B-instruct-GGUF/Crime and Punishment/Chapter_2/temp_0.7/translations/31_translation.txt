And how can you stick your head out of the window into the street, like a little pig, squealing; and that's shameful. Fie-fie-fie! And Karl was pulling him by the tailcoat from the window behind him, and here, it's true, Mr. Captain, his coat got torn. And then he shouted that he had to pay a fifteen target fine. And I myself, Mr. Captain, paid him five targets for his coat. And this is an ungracious guest, Mr. Captain, and he makes all the scandals!
