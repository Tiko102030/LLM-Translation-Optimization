 It was strange. — Well then, all right then, — Ilya Petrovich concluded, — we're not keeping you any longer. Raskolnikov left. He could still hear how an animated conversation began after he left, in which Nikodim Fomich’s questioning voice was the most audible... He came to his senses completely outside. “Search, search, it's a search now! — he repeated to himself, hurrying to reach; — robbers! they suspect!” His former fear seized him again, from head to toe.
