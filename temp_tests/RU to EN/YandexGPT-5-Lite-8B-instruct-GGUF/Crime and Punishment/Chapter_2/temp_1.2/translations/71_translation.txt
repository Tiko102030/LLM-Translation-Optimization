 He became very uncomfortable from this looking over, it even scared him. "Nastasia, why are you silent?" — he timidly said at last in a weak voice. "It's blood," she answered finally, speaking softly and as if to herself. "Blood!.. What blood?.." — he murmured, turning pale and moving away to the wall. Nastasia continued to look at him silently. "No one beat the landlady," she said again in a stern and resolute voice. He looked at her, hardly breathing.
