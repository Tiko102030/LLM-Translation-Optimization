 "Pieces of torn canvas will not arouse suspicion in any case; it seems so, it seems so!" — he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with an attention strained to the point of pain began to look around again, on the floor and everywhere, whether he had forgotten anything else? The certainty that everything, even memory, even simple reasoning was abandoning him, began to torment him unbearably. "What, is it really starting, is this punishment approaching now? There, there, so it is!" Indeed, the cuttings of fringe that he had cut from his trousers were lying on the floor, in the middle of the room, for anyone to see first! "But what's happening to me?" — he cried out again, lost. A strange thought occurred to him: that perhaps all his clothes were covered in blood, that perhaps there were many stains, but that he just didn’t see them, didn’t notice them because his reasoning had weakened, was fragmented...his mind was darkened… Suddenly he remembered that there was also blood on the purse. "Bah! So, it must mean that there's blood in my pocket too, because I put the wet purse into my pocket then!" He immediately turned out his pocket, and sure enough — there were traces, stains on the lining of the pocket! "So, reason hasn’t left me completely yet, so there’s still reasoning and memory if I caught myself and guessed it!” — he thought triumphantly, inhaling deeply and joyfully with his whole chest — “it's just a feebleness of feverishness, delirium for a minute” — and he pulled out the entire lining from the left pocket of trousers.
