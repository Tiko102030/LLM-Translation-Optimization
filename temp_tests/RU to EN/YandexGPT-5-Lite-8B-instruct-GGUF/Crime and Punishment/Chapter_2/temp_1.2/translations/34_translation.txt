 Louise Ivanovna started curtsying urgently in all directions and, as she curtsied, backed up to the door; but at the door she bumped into one distinguished officer, with an open fresh face and excellent thick blond sideburns. It was Nikodim Fomich himself, a quartier overseer. Louise Ivanovna hurried to присесть almost to the floor and in quick small steps, bouncing, flew out of the office.  
“Thunder again, thunder and lightning, tornado, hurricane!” — Nikodim Fomich addressed Ilya Petrovich любезно and amicably — “they’ve stirred up my heart again, I’m boiling again! Heard it from the stairs.”  
“Yes what!” - said Ilya Petrovich with noble negligence (and even not «shto», but somehow: «Da-a shta-a!»), moving to another table with some papers and dramatically shuddering with each step, — here you see, sir: the gentleman writer, that is, a student, former that is, does not pay money, gave promissory notes, does not clear out the apartment, there are continuous complaints against them, and you took offense when I lit a cigarette in their presence! They themselves are swindling, but here you go, please look at them: they’re now in their most attractive form!”  
“Poverty is not a vice, my friend, well, whatever!”
