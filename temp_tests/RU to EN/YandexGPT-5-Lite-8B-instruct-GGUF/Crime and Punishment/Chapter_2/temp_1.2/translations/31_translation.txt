 And he opened the window onto the canal and stuck his head out the window like a little pig, squealing; and that's shameful. And how can one stick out a window into the street, like a little pig, squealing; and that's shameful. Fie-fie-fie! And Karl pulled him by the tailcoat from behind the window there, it's true, Mr. Captain, he tore his clothes. And then he shouted that he had to pay a fifteen-silver coin fine. And I myself, Mr. Captain, paid him five silver coins for the clothing. And this is an ungracious guest, Mr. Captain, and he caused all the scandals! I, said, there will be a big satire on you, because I can write about you in all the newspapers. — A writer, then? — Yes, Mr. Captain, and what kind of an ungracious guest is this, Mr. Captain, when it's into a noble house...
