 The impulse was so strong that he got up to fulfill it. “Shouldn’t I think about it for a minute?” ran through his head. “No, it’s better not to think and get it over with!” But suddenly he stopped as if struck to the ground: Nikodim Fomich was speaking heatedly to Ilya Petrovich, and words reached him:

“It can’t be, they will both be released! Firstly, everything contradicts; judge for yourself: why would they call a janitor if it were their business? To inform on themselves, perhaps? Or for cunning? No, that would be too clever! And finally, student Peschtryakov was seen at the very gate by both janitors and the commoner woman at the very moment he was entering: he was walking with three friends and parted with them right at the gate and asked about living quarters from the janitors, even in front of his friends. Well, would someone like that ask about living quarters if they had such an intention?”
