 He was looking at her, barely breathing. “I heard it myself... I wasn't sleeping... I was sitting,” he said even more timidly. “I listened for a long time... The overseer's assistant came... Everyone ran to the stairs, from all the apartments...

“Nobody came. And this is your blood screaming. This happens when there’s no outlet and your insides start to bake, that's when it starts to seem... Are you going to eat something?” He didn't answer. Nastasya stood over him, gazed intently at him and did not leave. “Give me something to drink… Nastasya.” She went downstairs and returned in a couple of minutes with water in a white earthenware mug; but he no longer remembered what happened next. He only remembered taking a sip of cold water and spilling some from the mug onto his chest.
