 — And you, oh you, such and such and this and that, — he shouted suddenly at the top of his voice (the mourning lady had already left), — what happened to you last night? Eh? Again shame, a brawl on the whole street. Again fighting and drinking. You dream of being placed in a straitjacket! After all, I've talked to you about it, I've warned you ten times that I won't put up with it the eleventh time! And you again, again, oh you this and that! Even Raskolnikov's paper fell out of his hands, and he was looking wildly at the magnificent lady, who was being so unceremoniously lectured; but soon, however, he realized what was going on, and immediately he really liked the whole story. He listened with pleasure, to such an extent that one wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh… All his nerves were jumping. — Ilya Petrovich!
