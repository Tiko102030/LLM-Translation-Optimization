Guessing that he had come to his senses, the landlady, peering from the door, immediately closed it and hid. She was always shy and endured conversations and explanations with difficulty; she was about forty years old, plump and fat, dark-browed and black-eyed, good-natured from her thickness and laziness; and she was even very pretty in appearance. Shy beyond necessity. “You... who are you?” he continued to question, turning to the artel man himself. But at that moment the door opened wide again, and Razumikhin entered, bending down a little because he was tall.
