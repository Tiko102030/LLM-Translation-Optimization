 — Bring some tea quickly, Nastasya, because regarding tea, it seems, one can do without the faculty. But here's the beer too! — he moved to his chair, pushed his soup and beef closer to him and began to eat with such appetite as if he hadn't eaten for three days. — I, brother Rodya, dine like this every day now with you here, — he murmured as much as his mouth full of beef would allow, — and all this is Pashenka, your хозяйка, managing, honoring me with all her heart. I don't insist, of course, well, I don't object either. And here comes Nastasya with the tea. She sure is quick! Nastenka, want some beer? — And, go to the mischief-maker! — And some tea?
