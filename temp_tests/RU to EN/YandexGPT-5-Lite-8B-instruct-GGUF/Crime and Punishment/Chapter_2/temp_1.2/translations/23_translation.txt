 The stout lady jumped up from her seat upon seeing him and began to curtsy with some peculiar delight; but the officer paid no attention to her, and she did not dare sit down again in his presence. This was a lieutenant, an assistant district constable, with ginger-colored mustaches sticking out horizontally in both directions and extremely fine facial features that expressed nothing special, except for a bit of insolence. He glanced askance at Raskolnikov, somewhat indignantly: his costume was too bad, and despite all the humiliation, his posture still did not match the costume; Raskolnikov, through неосторожности, looked at him too directly and long enough that the latter was even offended. 
“What do you want?” he shouted, probably surprised that such a ragamuffin does not hide from his lightning glance. 
“Summoned… by повестку…” Raskolnikov answered somehow.
