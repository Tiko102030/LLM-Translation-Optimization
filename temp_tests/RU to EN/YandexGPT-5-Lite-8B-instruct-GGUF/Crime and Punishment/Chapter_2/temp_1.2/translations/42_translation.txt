 "The house is like Noah's Ark," observed the clerk, who was listening from his place. "It's clear, it's clear!" repeated Nikodim Fomich fervently. "No, it's very unclear," affirmed Ilya Petrovich. Raskolnikov picked up his hat and went to the door, but he didn't reach the door...

When he came to, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that someone was supporting him from the right, that there was another person standing to his left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nikodim Fomich was standing in front of him and gazing at him intently; he got up from the chair. "What's this, are you ill?" asked Nikodim Fomich rather sharply. "They could hardly write with a pen even when they signed their names," observed the clerk, sitting down in his place and starting to deal with papers again. "And how long have you been sick?" shouted Ilya Petrovich from his place while shuffling through the papers as well.
