 — Do not deign to shout!
— I'm not shouting, but speaking quite evenly, and it's you who are screaming at me; and I am a student and will not allow myself to be shouted at.
The assistant got so angry that for the first moment he couldn't say anything, and only some sparks flew from his lips. He jumped up from his seat.
— Please be s-i-i-lent! You are in the presence. Don't b-e rude, sir!
— And you are also in the presence, — Raskolnikov shouted, — and besides, while you are shouting, you smoke a cigarette, therefore you are snubbing all of us.
Having said this, Raskolnikov felt an indescribable pleasure. The clerk looked at them with a smile.
