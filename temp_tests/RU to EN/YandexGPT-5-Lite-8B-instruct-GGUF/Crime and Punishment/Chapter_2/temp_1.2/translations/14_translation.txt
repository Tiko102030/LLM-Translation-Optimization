 "Nastasia, therefore, could not notice anything from a distance, thank God!" Then he opened the summons with trepidation and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood. It was an ordinary summons from the precinct to appear today, at half past nine, at the office of the district supervisor. "But when has this ever happened? I have no business with the police on my own! And why именно today?" — he thought in excruciating bewilderment. "Lord, just make it faster!" He would have rushed to his knees to pray, but even laughed at himself — not at the prayer, but at himself. He hurriedly began to get dressed. "I'll be lost either way! But I must put on a sock!"
