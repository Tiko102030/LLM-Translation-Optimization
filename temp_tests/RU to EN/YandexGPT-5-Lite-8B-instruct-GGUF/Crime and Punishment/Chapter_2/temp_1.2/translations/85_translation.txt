Meanwhile, Razumikhin moved to sit with him on the sofa, awkwardly, like a bear, put his left arm around his head, even though he could have lifted himself up, and with his right hand brought a spoonful of soup to his mouth, blowing on it several times beforehand so that he wouldn't burn himself. But the soup was only warm. Raskolnikov greedily swallowed one spoonful, then another, a third. But after bringing a few more spoonfuls, Razumikhin suddenly stopped and announced that further actions should be discussed with Zosimov. Nastasya entered carrying two bottles of beer. — "Do you want some tea?" — "I do." — "Bring some tea quickly, Nastasya, because it seems like one can drink tea without consulting the faculty."
