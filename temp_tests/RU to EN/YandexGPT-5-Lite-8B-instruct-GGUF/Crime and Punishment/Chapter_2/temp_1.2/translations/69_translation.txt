 But no, he hears too clearly!.. But, therefore, they will come to him now too, if that's the case, "because... surely, all this is because of the same thing... because of yesterday... Lord!" He wanted to lock himself in with a hook, but his hand did not rise... and it was useless! Fear encircled his soul like ice, tormented him, made him stiff... But finally, after about ten minutes, all this racket began to subside gradually. The landlady moaned and groaned, Ilya Petrovich still threatened and swore... But finally, it seems, he too quieted down; now one can no longer hear him; "could he have left! Lord!" Yes, here the landlady is also leaving, still moaning and crying... and her door has slammed shut... Here the crowd disperses from the stairs to their apartments — they gasp, argue, call out to each other, raising their speech to a shout now, lowering it to a whisper then. There must have been a lot of them; almost the whole house had come running. "But god, is all this really possible! And why, why did he come here!"
Raskolnikov collapsed powerlessly onto the sofa, but could no longer close his eyes; he lay there for half an hour in such suffering, in such unbearable feelings of boundless horror, which he had never experienced before. Suddenly, bright light illuminated his room: Nastasya entered with a candle and a plate of soup. Looking at him carefully and seeing that he was awake, she put the candle on the table and began to arrange what she had brought: bread, salt, a plate, a spoon.
