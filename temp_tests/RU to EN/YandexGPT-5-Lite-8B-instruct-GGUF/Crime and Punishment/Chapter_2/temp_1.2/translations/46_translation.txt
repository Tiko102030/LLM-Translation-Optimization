 Nothing and nobody; no one peeked in. Not even Nastasia touched anything. But, my God! How could he have left all these things in that hole earlier? He dashed to the corner, reached his hand under the wallpaper and began pulling out items and loading them into his pockets. There were eight of them: two small boxes with earrings or something like that — he didn't take a good look; then four small satin cases. The chain was simply wrapped in newsprint. Something else in newspaper paper, an order, it seems...
He put everything in different pockets, in the coat and in the remaining right pocket of his trousers, trying to make it less noticeable. He took the wallet too along with the things. Then he left the room, this time even leaving it wide open.
