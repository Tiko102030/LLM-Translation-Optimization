In two minutes, Nastasya returned with the soup and announced that tea would be served soon. Two spoons, two plates and the whole set appeared for the soup: saltcellar, pepper shaker, mustard for beef and other things, which had not been seen in such order for a long time before. The tablecloth was clean. "It wouldn't be bad, Nastasya, if Praskovya Pavlovna would send two bottles of beer. We'll drink it." "Oh, you sharp-nosed one!" mumbled Nastasya and went to carry out the order.
