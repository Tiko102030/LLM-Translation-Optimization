 His entire room was of such a size that it was possible to remove the hook without getting out of bed. Sure enough: the janitor and Nastasya are standing there. Nastasya looked at him in some strange way. He glanced at the janitor with a defiant and desperate look. The latter silently handed him a gray piece of paper folded in half and sealed with sealing wax. “A summons from the office,” he said, handing over the paper. “From what office?..” “They're calling you to the police, to the office.” You know which office it is. “To the police!..”
