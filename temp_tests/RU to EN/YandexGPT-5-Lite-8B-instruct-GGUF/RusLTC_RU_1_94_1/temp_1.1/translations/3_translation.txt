However, such a general comparison of the concepts of property rights can be misunderstood because these rights largely limit the rights of others. Property rights give people state-approved privileges to monopolistically распоряжаться resources, the ability to contact authorized police agencies in order to limit the activities of other people. Thus, property significantly limits some advantages due to the fact that government authorities support property owners to a greater extent than other citizens. Therefore, it is not a dispute about the principles of natural freedom and justice, but rather discussions of property regulation that force us to make a choice about whose property needs to be protected and whose property rights have an advantage in the struggle for them. The discussion of democratic rights and their regulation has become so confusing because legal issues have been repeatedly discussed at various levels of government within the structure of the United States' legal system. Thus, according to the federalism system, one Federal Government is formed, and each state establishes its own legal system. Also at each level of government there is a clear separation of executive, legislative and judicial powers, each of which is призвана respect and ensure compliance with the norms of the Constitution, in which they find their interpretation.
