However, such a general comparison of the concepts of property rights can be misunderstood because these rights largely limit the rights of others. Property rights give people state-approved privileges for monopolistic management of resources, the opportunity to contact authorized police bodies in order to limit the activities of other people. Thus, ownership significantly limits some advantages due to the fact that government authorities support property owners more than other citizens. Therefore, it is not a dispute about the principles of natural freedom and justice, but именно discussions of property regulation that force us to make a choice about whose property needs to be protected and whose property rights have advantage in the struggle for them. Thus, under the federalism system, one Federal Government is formed, and each state establishes its own legal system. Also, at each level of governance there is a clear division of executive, legislative and judicial powers, each of which is призвана to respect and ensure compliance with the norms of the Constitution, in which they find their interpretation.
