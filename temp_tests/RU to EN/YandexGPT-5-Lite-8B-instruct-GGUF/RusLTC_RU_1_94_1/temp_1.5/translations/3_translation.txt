However, such a general сопоставление of the concepts of property rights can be misunderstood, since these rights largely limit the rights of other people. Property rights give individuals state-approved privileges to monopolistically распоряжаться resources, the opportunity to contact authorized police bodies in order to limit the activities of other people. Thus, ownership significantly limits certain advantages due to the fact that government authorities support property owners more than other citizens. Therefore, it is not a dispute about the principles of natural freedom and justice, but rather discussions of property regulation that force one to make a choice about whose property needs to be protected and whose property rights have преимущество in the struggle for them. The discussion of the issue of democratic rights and their regulation has become so unclear because legal issues have been repeatedly discussed at various levels of government in the structure of the legal system of the United States. Thus, according to the system of federalism, one Federal Government is formed, and each state establishes its own legal system. Also, at each level of administration there is a clear separation of executive, legislative and judicial powers, each of which is призвана to respect and ensure compliance with the norms of the Constitution, in which they find their interpretation.
