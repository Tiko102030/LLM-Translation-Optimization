On the other hand, the right of the majority to control economic activity, determine social goals and protect property owners from the consequences of external воздействия by polluters is considered. However, such a general сопоставление of the concepts of property rights can be misunderstood, since these rights, for the most part, limit the rights of other people. Property rights give individuals state-approved privileges to monopolize the disposal of resources and the ability to contact authorized police agencies in order to restrict the activities of others. Thus, ownership significantly limits certain advantages due to the fact that government authorities support property owners to a greater extent than other citizens. Therefore, it is not a dispute about the principles of natural freedom and justice, but discussions of property regulation that force us to make a choice about whose property needs to be protected and whose property rights have precedence in the struggle for them. The discussion of democratic rights and their regulation has become so unclear because legal issues have been repeatedly discussed at various levels of government within the structure of the US legal system. Thus, according to the system of federalism, one Federal Government is formed, and each state establishes its own legal system. Also at each level of governance there is a clear separation of executive, legislative and judicial powers, each of which is призвана to respect and enforce the norms of the Constitution, in which they find their interpretation.
